Story

With DAREU’s continuous innovation, your keyboard becomes more than just a tool — it evolves into your most trusted weapon on the digital battlefield.

Whether it’s a split-second trigger or a high-stakes combo, every keystroke is sharpened by our DAREU Hyper Algorithm to match your reflexes and playstyle. When your gear feels just right, your chances of winning rise with every round.

Built for Competitive Play, Tuned for Precision.


The A75 Pro isn’t just a keyboard — it’s a next-level gaming instrument engineered for peak performance. Powered by an ultra-fast 8000Hz polling rate and a blazing 0.05ms latency, every keypress is captured with lightning accuracy. With full-key adjustable magnetic switches, triple connectivity, and D.H.A, the A75 Pro delivers unmatched responsiveness and control — making it the ultimate weapon for serious gamers.

Built for next-gen performance, the Sky Magnetic Switches on the A75Pro replace traditional metal contacts with high-precision magnetic sensors, enabling lightning-fast actuation and per-key customization. You control the exact trigger point with 0.005mm fine-tuning accuracy, unlocking a personalized response curve for every keystroke.

Silent Gasket Structure — Game Hard, Stay Quiet

The DAREU A75Pro is built with a 3-layer dampening system and a soft gasket-mounted structure that absorbs vibrations and eliminates unwanted noise—so you can game or type with total focus.

No hollow echoes. Just a clean, muted “thock” on every press.

The DAREU A75Pro supports three versatile connection modes: Wired, 2.4GHz Wireless, and Bluetooth 5.1, giving you the flexibility to switch between devices effortlessly.

Whether you're seeking ultra-low latency for competitive gaming via wired or 2.4G, or need the convenience of Bluetooth for on-the-go setups, A75Pro adapts to your environment with ease.

With seamless mode switching and strong, stable connections, it’s built for gamers and multitaskers who demand performance—no matter how they connect.

With a built-in 8000mAh high-capacity battery, the DAREU A75Pro delivers ultra-long endurance that keeps up with your grind—whether it’s marathon gaming sessions or a week of work and play.

Thanks to its efficient power management system, you can enjoy hours of RGB lighting and wireless performance without constantly reaching for a charger. Say goodbye to low-battery anxiety—A75Pro powers you through, day after day.

The A75Pro features a CNC-machined aluminum top case that delivers exceptional durability with a premium feel. Its sleek, solid construction not only adds weight and stability but also gives your setup a touch of refined performance.

The A75Pro comes equipped with a dedicated volume knob, giving you instant, precise control over your audio without leaving the game. Whether you're adjusting in the heat of battle or fine-tuning your playlist, the tactile rotary dial makes it fast, intuitive, and satisfying.

Easily swap out your switches without soldering. Whether you're upgrading to a different feel or replacing a worn switch, the hot-swappable sockets make it simple to personalize your typing experience.

Light up your setup with 44 built-in RGB effects. From subtle gradients to intense reactive waves, customize the mood to match your game, your room, or your vibe. With so many options, your keyboard becomes a visual centerpiece.

Enjoy a rich, responsive typing sound that’s engineered to be both crisp and smooth. Thanks to the gasket mount structure and sound-absorbing foam layers, each keystroke feels solid and sounds just right.

Customize your A75Pro like a pro with the Dareu Web Configurator. Open it in Chrome, Edge, or Opera and plug in your keyboard to start remapping keys, adjusting actuation points, toggling rapid trigger, setting up macros, and tweaking RGB patterns — all from your browser. Save multiple profiles and update firmware with a click. No downloads, no hassle.
