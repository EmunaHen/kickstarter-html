Story

GenePro G1 helps you make smarter tactical decisions, speeds up your game, gives you insights into course conditions, and keeps you mentally steady. Take it to the course, sharpen your skills, and enjoy golf to the fullest!

Shows golfers the distance to the front, back, and center of the green to enhance their on-course confidence and help them plan their shots more effectively.

Every swing counts on the course. Customize your Driver Arc to plan the best route and accurately identify obstacles within your ideal hitting range. 

The Pin Pointer tells you the right direction to the pin, even when you can't see it from your current position.

Use the Layup Feature to plan each shot ahead to eliminate the guess work and avoid landing the ball into hazards.

Connects GenePro G1 to the paired Mileseey Golf App for more detailed statistics of your shot data, making post-round analysis a breeze.

With its industry-leading Ball-to-Pin™ mode, the GenePro G1 empowers golfers to measure any two points on the course from anywhere, delivering a decisive edge in club selection.

The GenePro G1's patented SmartSlope™ technology incorporates elevation, temperature and humidity factors, providing golfers with the most precise compensated distances based on environmental data.

Dual-color metalic finish with anodized treatment ensures distinctive indication of the slope function, allowing golfers to easily toggle it off to stay tournament legal.

GenePro G1 features an IP65 protection rating and advanced Rain & Fog Mode that ensures consistent and accurate laser readings even on rainy or foggy days. With up to 24 hours of battery life, GenePro G1 delivers reliable performance round after round, whatever the conditions.
