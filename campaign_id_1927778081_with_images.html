Story

🔹 Our Mission

At Navbot, we believe hands-on learning drives innovation.
We created EN01 to make robotics education fun, affordable, and open for everyone — students, makers, hobbyists, and developers.

🔹 Why EN01?

Most educational robots today are either too simple, too expensive, or too limited.
We designed EN01 to break that mold:

🛞 Hybrid Movement — Combines wheels and legs for driving and walking.

🔧 Modular Architecture — Add modules, sensors, or custom builds easily.

🎞️ Screen expression design —  makes the robot more vivid and dynamic.

😘 Expression-motion sync —  actions match corresponding expressions.

📡 Robot Control — App, Wi-Fi Web Control, or Voice Commands.

💻 Open-Source Friendly — Full access to firmware, CAD files, and documentation.

💵 Affordable — High quality without breaking your budget.

Environmental commitments

Visit our Environmental Resources Center to learn how Kickstarter encourages sustainable practices.

Long-lasting design

I've designed EN01 to be durable by using modular construction, high-quality materials, and easily replaceable components. Users can upgrade or repair specific parts without needing to replace the entire robot, significantly extending its life and reducing waste.

Reusability and recyclability

I plan to implement a take-back program with my backers by offering spare parts and repair kits, encouraging users to repair and reuse rather than replace the entire unit.

Sustainable materials

I plan to use upcycled materials including recyclable plastics and environmentally friendly packaging materials for shipping and assembly kits.

Environmentally friendly factories

I'll select a plant that minimizes its own impact by following environmentally conscious manufacturing processes, such as waste reduction programs and energy-efficient operations.

Sustainable Distribution

I will optimize my shipping routes by partnering with logistics providers who offer carbon-neutral delivery options and by minimizing packaging size and weight to reduce environmental impact.

Something else

I am committed to creating detailed online assembly and repair guides to extend product life and reduce waste, allowing users to upgrade and maintain their robots easily over time.
