Story

Hello!

Hiya! I'm Straton. As a user experience (UX) designer, I'm passionate about the beauty and usability of great design. Naturally, this makes me an Apple fanboy. I'm also a nerdy pin collector and creator.

Over the last four years I've launched successful Kickstarter campaigns for WWDC 2021, 2022, 2023, and 2024 pin sets. I am blown away by the amount of support from people who are also passionate about everything Apple. I received a ton of great feedback from my backers and those who have purchased the pins after the campaigns.

Now, I'd like to introduce you to my fifth Kickstarter campaign for a set of WWDC25-inspired pins.

This set of six (6) unofficial enamel pins pays tribute to iconic Apple software and commemorates Apple's WWDC 2025 conference, including Liquid Glass!

Designs

Hello: 2.5" x 0.82", silver nickel plated with UV printed gradient

Liquid Glass: 2.0" x 1.7", silver nickel plated with clear transparent stained glass effect

Camera: 0.75" x 0.75", silver nickel plated with inner UV printed lens and epoxy dome

Window Controls: 1.25" x 0.5", silver nickel plated with epoxy dome

App Icon: 0.75" x 0.75", blue anodized coating with inset template lines

Tahoe Finder: 0.75" x 0.75", black nickel plated with epoxy dome

Risks and challenges

Lessons Learned: My previous Kickstarter campaigns taught me a lot about backer pledges, working with manufacturing partners, fulfilling orders, and international shipments. This time around, I've optimized the pledge options and fulfillment processes. I'll be working with my same manufacturing partner, which has been a helpful and smooth process. Manufacturing defect rate: As with any manufactured product, I am expecting at least a few pins that won't turn out as desired. With that in mind, I will be ordering enough pins to cover a 20% defect rate. Based on my previous orders with my manufacturing partner, the actual defect rate is much lower. Manufacturing and shipping delays: As with any new product, it is reasonable to expect that there may be some delays in manufacturing and shipping due to circumstances out of my control. If the quantity of backers exceeds expectations, the larger order may take slightly longer to manufacture. I'll try my darnedest to get all the rewards out on time. I'll send frequent updates as the project moves forward. Thanks for your understanding!
