Story

Tired of endless piles of unsorted cards? Meet CardMill™—a compact, intelligent device that scans, identifies, and sorts your collection automatically. Whether you're organizing bulk commons or preserving high-value pieces, CardMill™ handles the heavy lifting—hands-free—across all your favorite trading card games. Just set your preferences, walk away, and let CardMill™ do the rest.

CardMill™ is a hands-free desktop device that scans, identifies, and sorts your trading cards automatically—freeing up your time, your table, and your sanity. With the push of a button, it loads each card, uses AI-powered image recognition to identify and catalog it, and places it into trays based on your preferences—alphabetically by 1st, 2nd or 3rd letter, by set, by color, card type, rarity or price-based (up to 5 tiers). We built CardMill™ for collectors and players who are tired of the manual grind and want a smarter, faster, and more accurate way to manage their collection.

CardMill™ was born out of frustration with expensive machines that monopolize your workspace and charge you a monthly fee just to do the thing they were built for. We knew there had to be a better way—there wasn't—so we made one.

Using CardMill is easy - load cards, press the button, walk away. CardMill™ is built to handle it all—from freshly cracked booster boxes to massive bulk lots you bought online months ago.  

We've taken a lot of care to ensure the safety of cards processed through CardMill™. We use a non-abrasive rubber to move the card from the hopper to the chamber. Then, a conveyor system glides the card into position for identification. After that, gravity will then gently place the card into the discharge tray. The tray itself is lined with a soft felt to protect the card when it's placed into position. Our goal from day 1 of CardMill™ has always been the protection of cards. And with a lot of tweaking, we've been able to achieve this. 

Everyone's asking "will CardMill™ damage my cards?" We heard you, and now we'll show you...by using an Unlimited Black Lotus, a Time Walk, and some OG Duals. For those that don't know, them some expensive MTG cards!

Long-lasting design

CardMill™ is built to last. We’ve chosen high-quality components and a sturdy frame to ensure years of reliable, daily use. Whether you’re scanning a few cards a week or a thousand, CardMill™ is designed to stay in service long-term. Fewer replacements mean less electronic waste—and more time spent organizing, not upgrading.

Reusability and recyclability

CardMill™ isn’t a disposable gadget—it’s a long-term tool for collectors. When the time comes, we’re committed to responsible end-of-life practices. The device is designed for easy disassembly, and key components are recyclable. We’ll publish teardown guides and recycling instructions to support eco-conscious disposal.

Sustainable materials

We’re actively working with our partners to minimize CardMill’s™ environmental footprint. That includes using recyclable packaging, reducing box sizes to cut down on shipping emissions, and eliminating unnecessary plastics. Our goal: compact, protective, and plastic-free packaging wherever possible.

Sustainable Distribution

While certain components require high-performance materials that can’t yet be recycled, we’re exploring alternatives—like recycled ABS plastic for external housings and cardboard-based inserts for shipping. We're also evaluating shipping strategies that group orders and minimize carbon output, balancing durability with sustainability.

Something else

We know manufacturing has an environmental cost. That’s why we’re exploring additional ways to reduce our impact—such as carbon offsetting, tree planting, or partnering with sustainability-focused organizations. We believe building powerful tools doesn’t mean ignoring our footprint—it means taking responsibility for it.
