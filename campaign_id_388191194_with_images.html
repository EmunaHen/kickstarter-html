Story

Letting your pets outside is an essential part of pet ownership. Not only does this give your pet a place to do their business, but it also gets them out of the house to experience the freedom of the outdoors.

But any reasonable pet owner is also thinking about their pet’s safety!

If your pet gets too excited and runs off, they may get lost or even become missing. In the worst case scenario, they may even get hurt.

According to the American Humane Society, 10 million pets go missing every year and at least 1 in 3 pets will go missing at least once in their lifetime.

And among the animals that do go missing, up to 29% of dogs and 47% of cats were unable to reunite with their owners.

That’s why we wanted to create an easy way for pet owners to prevent their pets from getting lost and track them down when should they actually go missing.

Introducing Bremen Pet Tag—the pet tracker that is not only equipped with RFID and Bluetooth chips, but also sports LED lighting for nighttime walks. Our pet tag also comes with a community app, Petstapic, for better interaction with your local pet community.

With Bremen Pet Tag and Petstapic, you can harness the power of technology and the community to ensure your pet’s safety.

Bremen Pet Tag comes equipped with an internal Bluetooth tracking chip.

The Bluetooth chip connects to your smartphone and has a detection radius of up to 87 yards indoors and 43 yards outdoors.

If your pet leaves the chip’s detection area, a push notification alert will be sent to your phone. And in the Petstapic app, a marker showing your pet’s last detected location will appear.

If your pet goes missing, users in a 12 mile (20 km) radius will instantly receive a missing alert with your pet’s information.

Plus, the Petstapic community platform lets you easily request help from fellow pet owners in the area.

Once installed, the Petstapic app will automatically scan for nearby tags. If a lost pet is nearby, the owner will be notified of their pet’s location so they can take immediate action.

When someone does find your pet, the Bluetooth and RFID chips in Bremen Pet tag will contain your pet’s info to help you recover your lost companion.

To keep our pets safe, we’re in the process of securing FCC (USA), CE (Europe), and KC (South Korea) certifications for safe usage.

For homes with multiple pets, you can connect up to 10 different Bremen Pet Tags to one account, and each tag can be registered to up to 5 different users across the whole family.

If you’ve ever tried walking your dog at night, you’ll know how easy it can be to lose track of them.

Bremen Pet Tag comes with LED lighting that helps you track your pet’s location in low-visibility areas.

With this increased visibility also comes increased safety. If someone’s riding their bike or running past you at night, they’ll be better able to avoid running into your pet.

Make your pet glow with 1 of 5 brilliant hues whenever you want to add some color to your walk. No need to buy any addons! One tap from your smartphone will change the color in an instant.

Just like how each pet has its own personality, Bremen Pet Tag is customizable for a tag that’s just as unique as your pet.

Bremen Pet Tag comes with a silicone case that makes it easy to place your pet tag in a harness, collar, leash, or other accessory that your pet is already wearing.

We specifically designed Bremen Pet Tag for comfort and weightless wear for dogs big, small, or anywhere in between. If your pet might feel burdened by extra weight on the leash, feel free to put your tag in the harness instead.

The silicone case is automatically included with your backing and is highly durable and practical.

You can also add a custom engraving to the back of your pet tag. Try engraving your pet’s name and your phone number to the back of your tag for extra assurance that your pet will make it home okay.

You can even engrave your very own company or event logo for a personalized souvenir. If you’re giving away a pet tag as a gift, feel free to personalize your very own message to the receipient.

Thanks to our special Kickstarter event, you can create your own custom tag engravement at no extra cost! Time for you–and your pet—to express yourselves!

We deliberately designed the Bremen Pet Tag with a circular, minimalist design that goes well with cats and dogs of all shapes and sizes.

Weighing in at less than 0.1 pound, Bremen Pet Tag is light enough to be worn with ease by even the smallest cats and dogs.

And with a USB-C charging port, Bremen Pet Tag is a fast-charging device with no need for extra batteries.

Bremen Pet Tag’s companion app, Petstapic, isn’t just for tracking your pet. It’s also a social network for local pet owners.

If you ever lose your pet, Petstapic users in your area will be able to see your missing pet post and contact you directly.

But Petstapic is also a joy to use when your pets aren’t missing. Try using Petstapic to learn more about the furry friends that live in your community.

If your pet is shy around strangers or other pets, you can let other pet owners on their Petstapic profile so others can give your pet space when you’re out for a walk.

And you can even use the Petstapic marketplace to trade or give away pet goods, pet food, and other accessories with the local community.

Petstapic and Bremen Pet Tag are the perfect choice for anyone who wants to keep their pet safe while creating a better community for pet owners.

Join Petstapic with your neighborhood pet lovers today!
