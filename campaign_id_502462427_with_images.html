Story

🌿Avocadogrow – by Cuaderno Limón

Hi, I'm Carolina, the creator of Cuaderno Limón, a creative brand that transforms everyday objects into beautiful, functional pieces full of personality. I believe that even the most ordinary things can become decorative, joyful, and useful.

That’s exactly where the idea for Avocadogrow comes from.

It all started one day, staring at a glass with an avocado pit floating between some crooked toothpicks. That improvised, fragile and not-so-pretty setup made me think:
What if we could make this process practical… and beautiful?

And so Avocadogrow was born:
a collection of 3 elegant 3D-printed holders designed to germinate avocado pits (or plant cuttings) in a stable, clean and decorative way.

🥑 Elegant, colorful, and made to fit any glass

Each piece is designed with a universal fit, meaning it adapts perfectly to almost any standard glass or container.
I’ve tested it with real setups — and it works with all of them!

Each holder also features a carefully designed slot on the bottom, allowing the roots to grow freely and to be gently removed later if you want to transplant the plant or switch holders.


It’s a thoughtful design — for growth, and beyond.

The result: a little water garden full of life, color, and style.

Long-lasting design

A long-lasting and functional life Each piece is designed to be useful, durable, and beautiful. The goal is for you to form a connection with the object — to care for it and keep it — avoiding disposable or improvised supports.

Reusability and recyclability

Designs optimized for sustainable materials I recommend printing the models in PLA or PETG, two widely available 3D printing plastics that are more environmentally friendly. PLA, for example, is made from renewable resources like corn and is biodegradable under industrial conditions.
