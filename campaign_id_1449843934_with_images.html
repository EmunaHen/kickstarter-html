Story

AI Vision replaces wires, RTK, and LiDAR with pure camera-based intelligence. M1 sees your lawn, maps it automatically, and navigates like a self-driving car — no setup, no weak signals, no stress.

While traditional RTK mowers require base station calibration, RTK antenna setup, and manual mapping, Anyonebot M1 is ready to mow in just one minute .

Smart Blade Sensing gives M1 the ability to feel the grass — detecting grass type and density in real time. It knows exactly where to mow and how to mow, saving battery and protecting your lawn.

Together, this Dual Perception system — powered by a 5 TOPS AI chip — enables M1 to see, think, and adapt like a human gardener, learning your lawn and getting smarter with every pass.

M1 uses computer vision to identify your lawn type — from Tall Fescue to Bluegrass — and adjusts blade speed in real time for the healthiest cut.

By recognizing your grass type, M1 selects the optimal cutting speed to protect roots, reduce stress, and keep your lawn thriving

The M1’s blade is crafted from premium 65Mn steel, a high-carbon manganese alloy known for its exceptional hardness (HRC 60) and tensile strength (180 MPa).

M1’s blade motor reaches up to 5000 RPM, with cutting speed automatically adjusted by AI in real time — ensuring optimal performance, efficiency, and lawn health.

M1 uses AI vision to scan and understand your lawn layout right out of the box. No RTK, no wires, no manual input — just switch it on and let it build the map for you.

With real-time obstacle detection, M1 glides through your lawn with the awareness of a watchful gardener — ensuring safe, clean navigation every time.

M1 comes equipped with two intelligent mowing modes, each designed for real-life lawn conditions — and both easily switchable via the app.

Systematic Mode: For clean, open lawns, M1 follows precise, efficient paths — no zigzags, no wasted motion. It maps your lawn and executes straight-line coverage with surgical accuracy.

Smart Seek Mode: For more complex, obstacle-rich yards, M1 switches to adaptive behavior — using AI vision and smart blade sensing to find uncut grass zones and navigate organically around flower beds, trees, and other landscaping features.

Whether your lawn has paths, multiple areas, or disjointed zones, M1 recognizes them and transitions automatically. You never have to lift a finger.

M1 visually recognizes No-Go Zones and avoids them with centimeter-level accuracy. Control each zone through the app — turn them on or off as needed.

M1 is built on a pure vision AI system，intelligence that grows. M1 gathers data about your lawn’s layout, grass types, and growth patterns. With cloud-based OTA updates, M1 evolves over time — improving its navigation, adapting to your specific lawn, and delivering a smarter, more personalized mowing experience with each update.

The Anyonebot app puts your lawn on autopilot. Start, stop, or send M1 home with a tap. Easily manage multi-zone and No-Go Zone, track mowing history, and set your preferred daily mowing schedule. You’ll also receive the latest improvements via seamless OTA updates — so M1 gets smarter over time.

With a built-in rain sensor, M1 automatically returns to charge when it starts to rain or the battery runs low — and picks up right where it left off once conditions improve. It knows when to pause and when to resume so your lawn care never misses a beat.

Powered by dual 500W brushless DC motors on the rear wheels, M1 confidently tackles inclines up to 45%.Built for real-world terrain, it climbs hills and handles uneven lawns with ease — delivering clean, consistent cuts even on a slope.

With edge-cutting as close as 5cm, M1 leaves no untrimmed borders. It gets right up to paths, patios, and fences for that crisp, finished look.

M1’s compact design and vision-based pathfinding allow it to pass through tight spaces effortlessly. Perfect for yards with gates, garden paths, or segmented zones.

Built-in lift sensors instantly stop the blade if the mower is raised — even slightly. Whether it’s a child, pet, or curious hand, M1 cuts power before anything goes wrong.

Anyonebot M1 's IPX6 waterproof design allows for effortless cleaning with water, making regular maintenance simple and convenient.

M1’s low-noise operation lets it mow safely and discreetly after dark — with optional indicator lights and built-in camera illumination for clear nighttime vision. Let your lawn get groomed while you sleep, without disturbing your peace.

Anyonebot was founded by robotics veterans with deep expertise in AI vision and product engineering.The core team previously led the development of the EcoFlow Blade and contributed to flagship robotics at DJI. Since December 2022, they’ve spent over 2.5 years developing M1 from the ground up: from vision navigation and smart blade sensing to OTA learning. With full control over engineering, supply chain, and manufacturing, Anyonebot is ready to deliver.
