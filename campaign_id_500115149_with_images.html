Story

Hey Friends! We’re WLKATA, the creators of Mirobot and HARO380 – two crowdfunding hits that helped makers learn and build with robots. Now we’re back with our most advanced project yet: WLKATA MT4, a powerful desktop robotic arm designed to bring real industrial automation into classrooms, labs, and maker spaces.
MT4 packs 4+1 DoF, 0.1mm precision, and 600g payload into a compact, industrial-grade build. It supports Arduino, ROS, and graphical programming within the Arduino ecosystem, making it perfect for both beginners and pros. More than a robotic arm, MT4 is your portable smart factory—ideal for low-cost prototyping and hands-on experience with real industrial tools.
Let’s build the future, together.
Our components are miniaturized versions of industrial structures, perfect for smart factory validation, cutting costs for both learning and real-world applications.
We offer various accessories to satisfy your needs, allowing you to build more minifactory scenarios.

Risks and challenges

This is our third project and we are confident in saying that we are already putting what we learnt from the previous project into good use. We have discussed the supply of necessary components in detail with our partner companies, and our process and production schedule for MT4 is going smoothly. As with the first & second projects, we are prepared for foreseeable risks during processing and assembly testing, we are mindful of the fact that unknown problems may emerge. That is why we have accounted for potential issues and difficulties that could arise within the timeline and promise to keep our backers informed of any complications and how they are being resolved.
