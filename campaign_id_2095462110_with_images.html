Story

We created this special bundle with practical needs in mind:

Perfect for Sharing or Dual Use

With two magnetic charging clips, one charging dock, and two batteries, this set allows two people to charge their Ray-Ban smart glasses on the go. Great for couples, friends, or anyone who wants to share the power!

Backup in Case of Damage

Accidents happen — having a second charging clip means you’re always covered. Use it as a spare to ensure continuous charging without interruption.

Comes in an all-new packaging layout — compact, organized, and ready for gifting or travel.

Or worse—cables dangle across your face, block your view, and ruin the look. 
It breaks your flow, slows you down, and turns seamless moments into frustrating ones.
Unlike traditional charging cases that force you to remove and dock your glasses, Transnovo SnapCharge powers your Ray-Ban Meta smart glasses while you wear them. 
It's a patented, magnetic system—modular, seamless, and built for real life.
Our dual battery swapping system and built-in power charging case keep you going all day.

Use of AI

This project does not utilize artificial intelligence in the development, design, or functionality of the product, nor is it seeking funding for any AI-related technology.
