Story

I wanted to create more than just a docking station — I envisioned a device that not only solves the problem of limited laptop ports but also brings everyday moments to life on your desk. That’s why I designed this special Dock with a built-in Mini-Display.

ViewDock—a vertical 5-in-1 USB-C hub with an embedded 10×8 cm display, smart auto-sleep behavior, and a suite of high-speed ports including SD/TF, USB 3.0, and 100W PD charging.

Not Just a dock —It is a Smarter Workflow Companion 

With ViewDock, you can mirror your device or expand your desktop on a clean, vertical screen. You can display your calendar, weather, photos, videos, or real-time lyrics—all while powering your device and connecting peripherals. 
And when you're done for the day, ViewDock’s auto-sleep feature ensures the screen powers off when your system sleeps—no more glowing screens all night. 
This is more than a dock. It’s a smarter, more elegant way to work, create, and live.

Smart Sleep Sync — Automatic Power-Off That Works With You

ViewDock features an intelligent sleep linkage system that automatically powers down the built-in screen when your connected device shuts down or enters standby. Unlike many external displays that stay lit all night or require manual shutdown, VertiDock syncs seamlessly—no extra steps, no glowing screens at midnight. Just plug in and go; when your work is done, VertiDock knows it’s time to rest too. It’s true plug-and-play, designed to simplify every session from start to finish.

About the Team

We are a passionate team of engineers, product designers, and manufacturing experts with over 20 years of experience in building high-performance electronics and accessories. From precision circuit integration to industrial-grade quality assurance, our factory background gives us the unique ability to turn bold ideas into polished, production-ready products.

The concept for ViewDock came from real-world frustrations we faced as mobile professionals: too many cables, too little space, and not enough screen real estate on the go. So we set out to build something different — a vertical docking station with a built-in display that looks great, works anywhere, and eliminates clutter from your setup.

Every detail of ViewDock — from the all-in-one design to the smart sleep logic — was developed in-house by our R&D team, prototyped with precision, and iterated based on real user feedback. We’re not a faceless factory, but a hands-on innovation team that believes tools should be functional, beautiful, and inspiring to use. With ViewDock, we’re proud to deliver a solution that reflects both our engineering roots and our user-first philosophy.
