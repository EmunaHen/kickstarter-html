Story

Air quality at your fingertips—just like plugging in your headphones! No power button, no hassle. Plug InstanSense into your phone or charger, and instantly monitor AQI, humidity, temperature, CO₂, and VOCs.

Whether you're at home, in the office, or outdoors, InstanSense makes it easy to monitor your environment. Say goodbye to complicated setups and enjoy truly instant air quality monitoring!

Air quality plays a critical role in our health and safety, yet indoor air pollution is often overlooked. According to experts from the Johns Hopkins Bloomberg School of Public Health, the effects of indoor pollution can be even more harmful than outdoor pollution. Research shows that pollutants like harmful particles from wood stoves and candles can significantly elevate CO₂ and toxin levels—sometimes surpassing those of busy city streets. Since we spend most of our time indoors, ensuring clean, healthy air is vital for our long-term well-being.

Have you ever felt tired, experienced headaches, or noticed a drop in concentration? These symptoms are often overlooked, but they could actually be warning signs of poor air quality around you. Air pollution isn't just an outdoor issue anymore; it can lurk in your office, at home, or even in the space where you relax the most.

But here's the catch—we can' t always perceive the air we breathe.

That' s why, in your daily life, you need a convenient device that gives you instant insights into the quality of every breath you take, ensuring health risks are always in plain sight.

InstanSense makes air quality monitoring a breeze. With its sleek, compact design, simply plug it into your phone, laptop, or power bank, and you're good to go—no setup required. Instantly get real-time air quality data anytime, anywhere, without the hassle.

InstanSense offers powerful air quality monitoring capabilities, providing real-time tracking of key parameters such as AQI, CO₂, PM2.5, PM10, temperature, humidity, and VOCs, ensuring the ultimate protection for your health.

In enclosed spaces like meeting rooms or bedrooms, especially when filled with people, CO₂ levels can climb rapidly, causing fatigue, headaches, and loss of focus. With InstanSense, you can monitor CO₂ levels in real time and take action to refresh your space with clean air. Stay sharp and energized, whether you're working hard or winding down, thanks to InstanSense.

Have you ever been bothered by the strong smell of new furniture or freshly painted walls in your home? These colorless and odorless volatile organic compounds (VOCs) are hidden health risks. Found in cleaning products, paints, and plastics, their levels can spike after cleaning or renovations, causing headaches, fatigue, and respiratory issues. With InstanSense, you can monitor these invisible threats and ensure a safe, healthy environment for you and your family!

In busy cities, PM2.5 and PM10 are ever-present, penetrating deep into the lungs and posing health risks. Whether you're outdoors or cooking at home, these tiny particles can surge to hazardous levels. InstanSense monitors air quality in real time, helping you detect potential threats and ensuring every breath you take is safe and fresh. Let it be your family's trusted guardian of health!

The built-in smart alert system instantly notifies you when air quality falls below standards, helping you take timely action to avoid prolonged exposure to pollutants and ensuring that you and your family breathe fresh, clean air at all times.

InstanSense is equipped with a high-definition LCD screen that displays real-time data clearly, allowing you to quickly monitor air quality without the need for a smartphone—making the information easy to read at a glance.

Weighing just 80 grams and featuring a compact design with a 90° rotating connector, this device is highly portable and easy to carry. Whether you slip it into your pocket or toss it in your bag, you can take it with you wherever you go.

Note:

Long press the button for 2 seconds to switch the screen orientation. The screen rotates in the order of 0° → 90° → 180° → 270°.

Air Quality Detection Standards:

When AQI < 50, the air quality is good; when 100 < AQI < 150, the air quality is normal; when AQI > 150, the air quality is poor.

When PM2.5 < 35, the air quality is good; when 35 < PM2.5 < 115, the air quality is normal; when PM2.5 > 115, the air quality is poor.

When PM10 < 50, the air quality is good; when 50 < PM10 < 150, the air quality is normal; when PM10 > 150, the air quality is poor.

When VOC < 100, the air quality is good; when 100 < VOC < 280, the air quality is normal; when VOC > 300, the air quality is poor.
