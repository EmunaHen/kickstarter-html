Supports All Major High-Speed Cards

Most multi-card readers only support one high-speed format. MagicLink lets you use multiple at once without speed loss:

  • CFexpress Type B: 2GB/s (Dedicated PCIe 16Gbps)
  • CFexpress Type A: 1GB/s (Dedicated PCIe 8Gbps)
  • SD/microSD Cards (UHS-II SD 4.0): 300MB/s (Dedicated USB 10Gbps)
  • Max total transfer speed: 2.7-3GB/s when all slots are occupied.

To match the capabilities of MagicLink 4-in-1, you would need:

  • 1× SD/MicroSD UHS-II Reader (e.g. Anker USB-C SD 4.0) — $32
  • 2× CFexpress Thunderbolt Readers (e.g. OWC Atlas FXR) — $160
  • Total Cost: $192
  • Total Ports Used: 3× USB-C

MagicLink does it all — for just $119 and using only 1 port.

Less clutter. More efficiency. Same blazing speed.

Unlike plastic-based competitors that trap heat and compromise performance, MagicLink is crafted with a precision CNC aluminum body, ensuring superior thermal conductivity.

 This means cooler operation, consistent maximum speeds up to 2790MB/s, and long-lasting reliability — even during heavy workloads. No slowdowns, no worries — just full-speed performance, anytime you need it.

Simply connect to your Thunderbolt 3/4 or USB4 port, insert your memory cards, and start transferring instantly.

Traditional Thunderbolt card readers allocate the full PCIe bandwidth to a single card, which often can't utilize that capacity, leading to both speed waste and the need for multiple expensive readers.

MagicLink 4-in-1 Card Reader changes the game. It’s the world’s first to apply Thunderbolt PCIe bandwidth allocation technology in a reader. By intelligently distributing bandwidth based on each card’s maximum read speed, MagicLink enables every card to run at full speed—simultaneously—through one port, saving both cost and performance.
