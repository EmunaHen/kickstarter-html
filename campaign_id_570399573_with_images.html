Meet TRU: stylish, accurate and goes with any look

Smartwatches, fitness trackers and pedometers don't track your steps when your wrists are stationary. Worse, they are often bulky, unattractive, and don't complement formal or stylish outfits.

TRU was designed to solve both problems: tracking every step and looking good while doing it.

Designed to go where the action is, TRU counts every step - even when others fail. With a modular fashion forward design, you can swap bands to match your outfit, from workout gear to evening wear.

A sleek, lightweight step tracker that blends seamlessly into any look with multiple stylish bands. Worn on the ankle, it delivers significantly better step-tracking accuracy, ensuring every step counts - without sacrificing style or convenience.

Getting to know our community and answering their most burning questions

The idea for TRU started with a simple complaint. I (Temi) was walking on my treadmill desk, trying to get my steps in while working, and realized my smartwatch wasn’t tracking anything. Super annoying. When I told Tomi during a visit in March 2024, she laughed and said she had the same problem -but hers also didn’t count steps when she was pushing the stroller with her kids.

That little moment sparked something. We started noticing all the ways our smartwatches were failing us. I even started wearing mine on my ankle just to get accurate data. Then I went online and saw so many other people complaining about the same thing.

We’ve always been the resourceful type, our parents are entrepreneurs, and our dad was an engineer. So we grew up tinkering and figuring things out. And with our backgrounds, we knew we could build something better.

So we did.

Our Timeline

We've spent a year prototyping and refining until we landed on TRU, a simple, accurate, and stylish step counter that actually works with your lifestyle. Whether you’re walking on a treadmill, pushing a stroller, or just living your life, TRU is built to count every step.

Does TRU only count steps?
Yes, for now it only counts steps. This allows us to focus on accuracy, reduce the size and have the best battery life to provide the best experience.

What are the metallic bands made of?
Our metallic bands are made of a 14K PVD coating on nickel-free stainless steel. This reduces the chances of tarnishing and increases durability as our products are designed for 24/7 wear. Water, shower, steam and ocean proof.

Will TRU fit my ankle?
TRU comes in multiple sizes or will have extenders (depending on the band type). We are working to ensure that TRU fits every ankle.

I already have a fitness app/tracker, will TRU work with it?
Yes! TRU will work with Apple, Google, Samsung and Android Health out of the box. This should allow it to work with most other apps. You can also prioritise TRUs data in your settings, which will ensure that you get the most accurate numbers.
We're also planning custom integrations with other popular health apps and are always taking suggestions from our community.

Will I need a subscription for the app?
No. There's no required subscription. The app will be free to view your data, but there will be an optional monthly premium for some features e.g Cloud Sync, Groups and Communites etc. We’re still fine tuning the app so that may change so make sure to sign up to get the latest info.
