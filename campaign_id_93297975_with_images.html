Story

Introduction
Want to be able to express yourself on stage more easily, or have more flexibility behind the scenes?

I sometimes think about this quest because I am a band performer and stage/theater production staff. In these activities, I often need to use MIDI/Show Controller. Sometimes during performances, I want to use Show Controller to change sound effects/instrument settings/cues anytime and anywhere. Traditional wired controllers limit the range and space of operation, and the common wireless controllers on the market have only short-distance and unstable operation ranges due to the principle, and may not be able to achieve multiple controllers to operate independently with custom channels.

This is the first time I developed a device totally on my own. The original purpose of designing this controller was to test whether there is a device that can perform Show Control/Playback more flexibly and for my personal interests. Now I want to share the idea with you. I searched the market and found that there was no controller that could provide relevant operations. Therefore, I designed the "FreeTroller" Wireless MIDI Show Controller myself. It can switch 4 channels with UHF (the similar theory as wireless MIC), and multiple controllers can operate independently with custom channels at the same time. 

Key Features:
Transmitter - 
MIDI message buttons x4
Powered by two AA batteries under 5V
Prototype size: ~8cm(W) x 3.5cm(D) x 7cm(H)
UHF: 433-434.79mhz
Max output power: 10mW
A screen display selected channel, transmitted MIDI message and estimated remaining battery.


Receiver - 
Half-U size, operated under 5V (Bus power)
USB output x1
MIDI output x1 which can be connected to the MIDI input of the Interface, and is powered by a 9V adapter
(It will be transfer to 5V internal) 
Half-U size, operated under 5V. (USB/ MIDI cannot output MIDI Message at the same time) 
A screen display selected channel and received MIDI message.

Housing - In the prototype, the housing is a 3D Print case that I designed and printed myself. The actual cases is expected to use custom plastic cases depending on the cost. If the cost is too high, in order to save costs, it may be possible to use a further designed 3D Print case (ABS).

Application: Events/ Theatre Production, Show Control and Temporary Control during Rehearsal.
I have tested and used "FreeTroller" for activities and performances in different indoor and outdoor environments many times, and the results are very stable and satisfactory.
 

Some photos of using "FreeTroller" during performance.

Risks and challenges

1. This product uses 433MHz to transmit and receive wireless signals, different places have different regulations on the use of 433MHz. Please pay attention to whether your place is compliant and does not require a license. "FreeTroller" may be not allow to use in some countries. The wireless frequency band and transmission power of "FreeTroller" are adjusted according to Hong Kong's laws: Class Licence for Short Range Device. The finished product will also be submitted to the Hong Kong government for evaluation to confirm further sales criteria. 2. Certification and Authorization: This is an electronic product, a series of certifications may be required before the it is launched. Certification and other related procedures take a certain amount of time. In summary these factors may affect the delivery time. 3. Housing cost: In the prototype, the housing is a 3D Print case that I designed and printed myself. The actual cases is expected to use custom plastic cases depending on the cost. If the cost is too high, in order to save costs, it may be possible to use a further designed 3D Print case (ABS).
