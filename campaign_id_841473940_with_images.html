Story

Meet Magix Keys: Type Smarter, Not Harder

What if your phone or tablet could whisper directly into your computer’s keyboard — without ever touching a key?

Introducing Magix Keys — a versatile smart input solution that transforms your smartphone into a powerful, wireless text-entry tool for any computer, TV, or device with a USB keyboard port or Bluetooth interface.

Whether you're scanning documents, dictating emails, entering barcodes, or automating text with macros — Magix Keys lets you type anything, anywhere, without actually typing.

One Tool, Endless Input Possibilities

Magix Keys doesn’t just add convenience — it actively replaces a whole collection of devices you might already use, combining their functions into one compact, smart solution:

Standalone App, Dongle Optional. Simple by Design.

At its core, Magix Keys is an app-based solution. It connects to your computer or device using Bluetooth — just like a wireless keyboard. No need to install any additional software on your connected device.

If your phone/tablet supports the required Bluetooth mode (many Android devices from popular manufacturers like Samsung and Google do) and your target device supports Bluetooth keyboards (e.g., PCs with Windows, macOS, Linux, etc.), you don’t need any additional hardware.

But even if these conditions aren’t met, there’s a simple solution: The Magix Keys Dongle.

You only need the Magix Keys Dongle if:

  • Your Android phone/tablet doesn’t support Bluetooth Human Interface Device (HID) mode
  • You're using iOS (iPhone/iPad) — iOS requires the dongle due to strict Bluetooth limitations
  • Your target device has no Bluetooth interface (e.g., some TVs, kiosks, embedded devices)
  • You want convenient plug-and-play support, with no Bluetooth pairing setup

What is the Dongle Exactly?

The Magix Keys Dongle is based on the widely available M5Stack AtomS3 Lite DevKit — a compact and powerful ESP32-S3-based microcontroller with built-in USB.

iOS Support – A Stretch Goal

While the Android app is already fully functional and available for testing, iOS support is planned as a stretch goal.

Why is iOS different?

Apple restricts the use of Bluetooth peripheral mode on iOS, which means iPhones and iPads cannot emulate Bluetooth keyboards directly. As a result, iOS devices must use the Magix Keys Dongle to communicate with the target device.

Because of this limitation, it only makes sense to invest time and resources into the iOS version of the app if there’s sufficient demand for the dongle-based workflow.

Snap It, Type It

Use your phone’s or tablet's camera to scan documents—receipts, barcodes, tracking numbers, forms, or even handwritten notes, extract just the text you need and it will "type" the text for you automatically.

Effortless Quoting from Books, Articles, and Tables

Need to quote a sentence from a printed book, grab a definition from a magazine, or pull numbers from a statistical table? The Magix Keys App makes it incredibly easy.

Just open the app, point your phone’s camera at the page, and tap to scan. The built-in text recognition (OCR) instantly extracts the text. After that, select the parts of the text you want the Magix Keys App to type for you and transfer it with a single tap to your word processor, email or spreadsheet as if you entered it by hand.

Perfect for:

  • Students and researchers quoting academic texts
  • Writers and bloggers referencing printed materials
  • Journalists pulling quotes or figures from reports
  • Professionals digitizing financial or scientific data from paper

Whether it’s a block of prose or a dense chart of numbers, Magix Keys helps you skip the typing and get straight to the point. 

Targeted Text Capture – Scan Just What You Need

Sometimes you don’t want to scan an entire page - just a specific part of it. With the Magix Keys App’s Quick-Scan feature, you can draw a rectangle directly on the live camera feed to precisely select the text you want to extract. Whether it’s a product number, serial code, or just one total from a long receipt, you’re in control.

Once selected, the Magix Keys App types that snippet into your connected device with one button-tap - no copy-paste, no typing, no clutter.

Perfect for:

  • Picking out tracking numbers from receipts
  • Grabbing serial numbers or product codes from devices or labels
  • Extracting just the total amount or invoice number from financial documents
  • Copying reference numbers from shipping documents or forms
  • Digitizing ingredients or nutritional info from packaging

Need to enter a barcode or QR code into a system that doesn’t have a scanner? The Magix Keys App turns your phone or tablet into a smart scanner—and makes your PC think you typed it yourself.

Just point your phone’s camera at the code. The Magix Keys App instantly reads the barcode or QR content and “types” the data into your device—exactly as if you were using a keyboard.

Perfect for:

Speak Smarter – The Intelligent Dictaphone

Running late on that email? Dictate it into your phone or tablet, and watch it magically appear on your computer screen.  The Magix Keys App transforms your phone into a smart dictaphone with live speech-to-text conversion and types it as if you did it yourself.

As you speak, your words are instantly converted into text. And when you want to add punctuation - like commas, periods, question marks, line breaks, etc. - you simply tap a button on the screen. This is far faster than saying "comma" or "period" out loud and lets you focus on your ideas and rhythm of speech - without interrupting your flow

If you make a mistake or want to revise something, the app offers a smart undo function: With just one tap, you can delete the last few spoken words, without ever needing to touch your computer keyboard. This makes editing on the fly effortless and distraction-free.

Tap to Automate – Your Personal Macro Keyboard

Tired of typing the same things over and over? The Magix Keys App gives you a fully customizable macro keyboard right on your phone or tablet. Each button can be programmed to send a specific sequence of keystrokes to the connected device - just like a hardware macro pad, but way more flexible.

You can create multiple workspaces, each tailored to a specific application, task, or workflow. Switch between them instantly to stay organized and efficient.

Examples of what you can do:

  • Insert frequently used email signatures, phrases, or boilerplate text
  • Launch complex command-line sequences with one tap
  • Automate login sequences for test systems or kiosks

Stay Awake – Smart Mouse Movement to Keep You Online

We’ve all been there: You step away for a few minutes, and your PC goes to sleep. Or worse, your status in MS Teams, Slack, or Zoom turns to "Away" even though you're still actively working - just not at the keyboard.

Magix Keys solves this with a built-in configurable mouse mover feature. It sends subtle, periodic movement signals - just enough to keep your device awake and your status "Online".

Key Benefits:

  • Prevents your PC from going to sleep during long downloads, renderings, or data processing
  • Keeps your status active in Teams, Slack, Zoom, and other apps
  • Simulates presence during long meetings or calls when you're listening but not typing
  • Ensures uninterrupted remote desktop sessions and VPN connections
  • No need for sketchy software or admin rights - Magix Keys behaves like a real Bluetooth or USB mouse

Fully configurable - use the Magix Keys App to adjust:

  • Movement frequency and pattern
  • Active/inactive schedules
  • On/off toggles at your convenience

Perfect for transferring:

Why the Dongle? Plug-and-Play Power When You Need It

The Magix Keys Dongle uses the USB keyboard standard — meaning it works instantly, without drivers or software.

Use it when your target device:

  • Doesn’t support Bluetooth
  • Has strict IT or security policies
  • You’re an iPhone/iPad user
  • Your Android device doesn’t support Bluetooth HID mode

Since it acts like a physical keyboard, it works universally — with Windows, macOS, Linux, smart TVs, point-of-sale systems, embedded devices, and more.

And yes — it’s secured with AES-256 end-to-end encryption, ensuring private and tamper-proof data transmission between the app and dongle.

In short, the Magix Keys Dongle gives you full flexibility, zero setup hassle, and maximum device compatibility - with the kind of simplicity only hardware can deliver.

Use of AI

Our product uses AI to extract text from images and audio recordings, functioning similarly to an OCR scanner and a voice dictation tool.
