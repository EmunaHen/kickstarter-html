Story

Welcome to a new era of creativity—where tools work around you, not the other way around. Whether you're carving, soldering, drilling, sanding, or just tinkering with your next big idea, VersaCarve gives you the stability, accuracy, and portability that traditional setups can’t match.

Say goodbye to the chaos of scattered tools, tangled cords, and cluttered workspaces. VersaCarve consolidates your maker must-haves into a clean, modular system you can carry in your backpack. No more tool-switching, no more mess—just focused creation with professional-grade results.

Tired of switching between tool types and stations? VersaCARVE’s intuitive modular system lets you change functions in seconds—without tools or fiddly setups.

With our wide range of modular parts, you can get the right setup for the right job without breaking a sweat.

With an internal battery, PowerGrind and SolderPro support you to work for hours wirelessly with one charge, and if you are working for a longer time, simply connect your SolderPro to a power source with a USB-C cable.

With any power bank or power station with USB-C charging port, you can craft anywhere—at your desk, in the garage, on the road, or even outdoors.

Every piece fits into a clean, labelled storage system that travels with you. From workshop to backpack, it’s your entire bench in a box.

VersaCarve is accessible yet Powerful, whether you’re installing heat-set inserts, engraving glass, trimming 3D prints, or soldering a sensor, VersaCARVE is ready.

VersaCARVE isn’t a single tool—it’s a complete, expandable platform designed to adapt to your needs and grow with your skills. Each tool set can function independently or combine seamlessly for more demanding, large-scale, or precision-driven projects.

Here’s what you’ll unlock with each part of the VersaCarve ecosystem.

PowerGrind offers 3 precision speed settings from 5000 to 15000RPM

The kit also comes with 36 pieces of bits and parts for various projects.

Accessories included in the PowerGrind kit: Screw clamp, polishing wheel, cutting wheels, sanding bands, sanding drum, diamond grinding bits, diamond grinding wheel, mandrels, drill bit, screw mandrel, saw blade, carving bit, collets

Add more function as you grow, we offer 3 accessory kits specifically designed to empower your workflow with PowerGrind and SolderPro

VersaCarve can handle a wide range of materials, and you can adjust the power output when working with different materials.

VersaCARVE is engineered for durability and reliability, crafted through countless prototypes and rigorous testing to ensure it performs flawlessly across your creative projects. From our extensive prototyping phase (May–June 2024) to the final design (July 2024), every component has been thoroughly vetted for safety and consistency, so you can trust it to handle demanding tasks with ease.

Using premium materials, VersaCARVE delivers professional-grade precision and longevity that outshines DIY alternatives, making it a dependable partner for makers of all levels—whether you're a beginner exploring new skills or a pro pushing creative boundaries.

From dorm room creators to professional engineers, VersaCARVE meets you where you are. Whether you're just starting out or refining your skills, this system is designed to evolve with your journey.

Risks and challenges

We’ve taken great care to design, prototype, and test VersaCarve through countless iterations to ensure performance, safety, and reliability. However, as with any hardware product involving multiple precision-manufactured components and electronics, unexpected production or supply chain delays may arise. We’re working closely with vetted suppliers and manufacturing partners to keep timelines tight and quality high—but global conditions, material sourcing, or logistics constraints could potentially affect delivery schedules. Our modular design and all-in-one system bring together several tool categories—soldering, rotary carving, heat-insert pressing, and more—into one seamless platform. Coordinating production of these diverse components requires attention to both mechanical tolerances and user safety. We're fully committed to rigorous testing and quality control across each unit, and we’ve built in fallback options with suppliers to avoid bottlenecks. Still, as we scale, ensuring consistent output will remain a challenge we’re prepared to tackle head-on. Most importantly, we promise to keep our backers informed every step of the way. Transparency is part of our commitment to the maker community. If any delays or issues arise, you’ll be the first to know—and we’ll work around the clock to resolve them. With your support, we’re confident we can bring VersaCARVE to your hands-on time and with the quality you expect.
