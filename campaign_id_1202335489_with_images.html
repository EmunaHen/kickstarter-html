How it works

  • Download the STL files, pre-arranged build plates, and PDF instructions for up to 10 unique science and engineering kits.
  • 3D print, following the PDF build guides, and assemble with ease.
  • Proudly display your 3D printed kits. 

Download - 3D Print - Build - Learn - Display

Print-A-Kit is a cool new kind of experience that is both easy, interactive, and fun for all ages. But what makes it better than your average science and engineering kits? First of all, you get to 3D print them yourself, which is more interactive and fun then just getting pre-made parts. Plus, you can choose what color to print the kits in to match you own unique style or preferences. 

Print-A-Kit is perfect for all ages, whether you are retired and looking for something new to print, simply appreciate science and engineering, are just getting into 3D printing, or are looking for a family-friendly experience where you learn by doing. Print-A-Kit STL are thoroughly tested to make the whole experience seamless. 

The 3D Printable Kits

Kit 1: Insanely high gear ratios (very popular)

Kit 2: Power transmission. The rise of Ultra High Voltage DC transmission

Kit 3: How clocks work: Escapement mechanisms. 

Kit 4: Manuel transmissions. (New and improved!)

Kits 5-10: Top Secret! (in development, more info as the campaign progresses) 

Each digital kit includes all the STL files needed to print the project, PDF guides with interesting facts and history, and exclusive build videos.
