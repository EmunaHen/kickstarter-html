Story

Introducing Repeatly — Personalized Language Learning Tool

Hi! I’m Volodymyr, the creator of Repeatly, a new kind of language learning app born from my own journey of learning Polish.

Like many, I started with popular tools like Duolingo, Memrise, and Quizlet. While Duolingo and Memrise gave me a great start, I quickly ran into limitations — they’re great for beginners, but don’t let you go further with custom content. Quizlet was my next step, but it lacked the core ingredient of long-term learning: spaced repetition.

That’s when I decided to build my own solution — something smarter, deeper, and truly customizable. Repeatly was created to fill that gap.

What Is Repeatly?

Repeatly is a smart, customizable language learning platform focused on building your vocabulary through spaced repetition, engaging exercises, and rich content features.

Current Features:

  • Personal dictionary: Add your own words with translations, images, and usage examples.
  • AI-powered example generation: Get automatic example sentences for your words.
  • Spaced repetition system: Words are scheduled for review based on your learning settings.
  • Learn activity: Learn new words with varied exercises (typing, audio, multiple choice, etc.).
  • Review activity: Review already learned words with varied exercises (typing, audio, multiple choice, etc.).
  • Repeat activity: Practice pronunciation with timed repetition and speech-focused drills.
  • Flexible settings: Customize intervals, exercise types, and word batches to match your pace.

The app is already live and available at repeatly.ai — but it’s still in beta, and that’s where you come in. With your support, we can polish the experience, expand its capabilities, and bring smarter language learning to more people.

Use of AI

I plan to use AI-generated content in my project.

What parts of your project will use AI generated content? Please be as specific as possible.

Repeatly uses AI-generated content in a limited and optional way. Specifically, when a user adds a new word to their personal dictionary, they can choose to generate an example sentence using AI. This helps provide context and show how the word is used in real language.

Do you have the consent of owners of the works that were (or will be) used to produce the AI generated portion of your projects? Please explain.

All AI-generated content used in Repeatly is created using publicly available language models and resources. We do not use any copyrighted works without consent. The AI-generated example sentences, for instance, are created from general linguistic data and are not derived from proprietary sources.
