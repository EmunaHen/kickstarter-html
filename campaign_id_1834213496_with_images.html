Risks and challenges

Creating an innovative platform like the Digital Architecture Museum comes with both technical and strategic challenges. We‚Äôre committed to tackling them transparently and responsibly. 1. Technical Complexity Risk: Integrating a smooth 3D viewer for BIM files, moodboard features, and blockchain IP protection can be resource-intensive. Mitigation: Our development will follow a phase-by-phase structure, allowing us to test and optimize core features before expanding functionality. We‚Äôre also working with experienced developers who have background in AEC tech. 2. Mitigation: We will allow only non-downloadable models, use viewer-only access, and implement smart licensing workflows in Phase 3 via blockchain tracking and contributor agreements. 3. Community Engagement Risk: A vibrant user base is critical, especially for moodboards and social features. Mitigation: We‚Äôre offering free access for students, partnering with universities, and involving early backers as ‚ÄúFounding Members‚Äù with visible roles. 4. Sustained Funding Beyond Kickstarter Risk: Platform maintenance and development beyond the MVP may require further funding. Mitigation: We will explore strategic partnerships, early sponsorships, and subscription models for firms and educators to ensure long-term sustainability. 5. Data Privacy and Security Risk: Users need confidence that their moodboards, preferences, and model interactions are safe.
