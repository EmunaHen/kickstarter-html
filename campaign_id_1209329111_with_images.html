How It Works

Fact Hub scans the internet 24/7 to gather and verify data in real-time. There isn’t a moment where verification stops. We take guesswork out of the equation, so that you can get reliable facts hassle-free. 

Every fact-check begins with the fundamental 6Ws: Who? What? When? Where? Why? How? They form the Factual Barebones of every investigation.

From there, every fact is rigorously cross-referenced using a broad and diverse set of sources—including global and local media, government records, academic research, satellite imagery, scam pattern repositories, and more—to ensure you get the most accurate information. Whether it’s debunking misinformation or exposing scams, Fact Hub leaves no credible trail unexplored.

Lastly, we provide a full list of verification sources and the fact-checking process for every question. Nothing is hidden. We want the process behind getting information to be accessible, immediate, and to elevate the standard for truth on the internet.

Fact-Checked Feed vs. Other News Sources

The Fact-Checked Feed is different from current news sources. We are the only place that verifies and updates continuously in real-time.

At Fact Hub, every story begins with fact extraction, broken down into the 6Ws. From there, our system initiates a real-time global scan, pulling in data from:

  • Credible media outlets across countries and languages
  • Official government records and regulatory filings
  • Criminal databases and court documentation
  • Think tank reports, NGO briefings, and academic research
  • Public open data repositories and independent fact-checking networks
  • Social media platforms, where we monitor public claims, viral trends, and patterns of misinformation

Once collected, every fact is put through multi-source cross-verification. We check for consistency across databases, analyze discrepancies, and score source reliability to ensure the information you see is accurate, comprehensive, and free from manipulation.

What makes Fact Hub truly unique is what happens next: context reconstruction. 

We use advanced methods including natural language processing, knowledge graph modeling, and temporal-spatial analysis to rebuild the full picture behind every story. This allows us to trace how events unfold, map the relationships between people and entities, and connect each fact to a broader, verified context.

You're not just reading headlines, you’re seeing how everything fits together. The Fact-Checked Feed is more than just a news feed. It’s a dynamic, trust-first information system designed to give you clarity in a noisy world.

Facts Unveiled, A New Strain of Journalism:


Facts Unveiled isn't just opinion-based commentary. It's media system analysis built on a structured methodology designed to expose how narratives are constructed, shaped, and distributed across today’s information ecosystem. We reveal the political and corporate agendas that are hidden from you to raise the media’s standard of truth.

We begin with fact extraction, isolating confirmed events and data points from emotional framing, speculative language, and editorial bias. Using content parsing and linguistic analysis, we strip stories down to the core facts—what actually happened.

From there, we examine narrative architecture through methods such as:

  • Framing Analysis: How language, imagery, and emphasis influence public interpretation.
  • Agenda-Setting Theory: What topics are chosen for coverage, and whose priorities they reflect.
  • Source Tracing: We map the origin and echo chain of claims across outlets, influencers, and networks.
  • Ownership Influence Modeling: We analyze corporate, political, and ideological relationships that may bias editorial decisions.
  • Comparative Signal Analysis: We compare how the same event is portrayed across international, independent, and state-aligned media.

We also include coverage deltas—what’s missing, buried, or distorted compared to known facts—and identify coordinated narrative trends, such as emotionally polarizing keywords or synchronized headline cycles.

The result is a clear, evidence-backed breakdown of not just what a story says, but why it was told that way, who benefits from that version of events, and what truths may be systematically excluded.

Facts Unveiled is designed for critical readers who want more than just the news cycle. It’s an X-ray for the logic, incentives, and systems behind modern media.
