Story

Ever struggled to capture the perfect shot?
Fumbling with your phone, trying to zoom in, only to end up with a blurry, shaky mess?
Lugging around a bulky camera, adjusting settings while the moment slips away?

With KINGOPT Optical Zoom Binoculars, capturing stunning photos and videos is as effortless as watching the scene unfold. Simply track your subject through the eyepiece, press a button, and instantly save high-resolution shots—all in one seamless motion. No missed moments, no distractions—just pure immersion. And with built-in sharing, your best captures go from the lens to Instagram in seconds.

KINGOPT utilizes a sophisticated design that integrates the objective lens, focusing group, and imaging sensor in a single optical path, enabling “single optical path with dual outputs” (eyepiece observation + sensor imaging). This eliminates optical distortion effectively, preventing field-of-view shifts and image quality degradation, whilst ensuring 100% optical path efficiency. Through “single focus adjustment for precise synchronization of observation and photography focus,” you can rest assured that you won't miss critical moments due to focus delays.

View targets up to 1400yds away with the 8x optical zoom of the KINGOPT Optical Zoom Binoculars.It delivers superior clarity and precision, magnifying distant objects without compromising image quality. Instead of digital zoom, which often sacrifices detail and clarity, optical zoom maintains the vibrancy and clarity of each image, ideally suited for birdwatching, outdoor adventures, or enjoying the landscape.

Featuring large 42mm objective lenses, the KINGOPT is capable of capturing more light, ensuring bright, sharp images in all conditions, even in challenging low-light environments such as dusk, dawn, or cloudy skies. From vibrant sunsets to cloudy mountaintops, KINGOPT binoculars deliver flawlessly, presenting the finest details with exceptional clarity, contrast and color fidelity.

KINGOPT binoculars are equipped with ED (Extra-low Dispersion) optics, which minimizes chromatic aberration and fringe distortion to ensure true-to-life color reproduction. This innovative optical technology keeps every image you see as vibrant and sharp as nature intended, regardless of whether you're looking at distant landscapes or observing wildlife up close and personal.

The BAK4 prism in KINGOPT's optical zoom binoculars improves image quality with superior light transmission, sharper detail and vivid color accuracy. It maximally reduces aberrations and color fringing, guaranteeing a clear and bright field of view even in low-light conditions. Perfect for a premium viewing experience in all conditions.

KINGOPT's designed for seamless functionality.Switch seamlessly between 16MP still shots and 2K video recording with a single button. Enjoy full-resolution captures (16MP) in standalone mode,with intelligent resolution adjustment (6MP) for stable real-time transmission via mobile app. Instant playback and settings adjustment through Viipulse interface.

All captured media can be analyzed by Merlin system, delivering species identification within 3 seconds.

KINGOPT binoculars are supplied with integrated 2.4G Wi-Fi, allowing you to easily synchronize with your smartphone or other device to transmit a live view directly from the lens. This seamless real-time sharing feature boosts the experience of watching wildlife or recording video by sharing your view with others instantly and with high quality.

KINGOPT binoculars support up to three devices (Android and iOS) to be connected at the same time within a range of 10 meters. Once the first device is connected, it is possible to control the binoculars to take photos or videos, allowing other users to experience the same view and capture memories together. This highly interactive feature is well-suited for group hikes, family outings, or collaborative explorations.

The KINGOPT is packed with a powerful 4000mAh battery that lasts for a long time, keeping you powered up throughout your adventure. From hiking and camping to enjoying a day of bird watching, the battery will ensure that you never run out of power. Shoot and explore for hours on end with the device on standby so you can rest easy.

The nitrogen-filled, IPX7 waterproof design keeps the telescope fog-free and clear even in wet or rainy environments, preventing moisture buildup or temperature fluctuations from affecting the internal lenses. The durability of this product is designed to ensure a consistent and reliable viewing and photographing experience in wet environment. Note: The electronic components are splash-resistant.

The included 32G TF card lets you instantly store all your photos and videos, so it's easy to start sharing your moments right away. Videos are saved directly to the card and can be transferred to your phone or computer with just a few taps. What's more, the TF card slot supports up to 128G of expandable storage, as well, ensuring that you'll never run out of space, even on your longest adventures.

The optical zoom binoculars offer two convenient options for use: a carrying strap for hands-free portability, perfect for outdoor activities like hiking or birdwatching, and a 1/4-inch tripod thread for stable, hands-free viewing, ideal for extended observation or photography.

KINGOPT binoculars are well-crafted to complement a variety of activities. They're sophisticated enough to enrich every experience, whether you're immersed in nature, on the go, or indulging in birdwatching. From capturing razor-sharp, vivid views at sporting events and concerts to providing stunning clarity in outdoor adventures, these binoculars ensure unrivaled visual excellence.

KINGOPT OPTICS is the comprehensive production base of precision optical lens elements and binoculars & rifle scope.  Products cover precision optical component (precision lens), outdoor sport optics binocular & Rifle scope items. 

In 2010 Kingopt optics was established and engaged in precision optics products exporting.

In 2011 Kingopt established Nanyang Kingopt optical & electronics Co.ltd factory to produce optical prisms & optical lens.

In 2012 Kingopt worked with well-known brand from Japan and built binoculars production lines.

In 2015 Kingopt worked with high-end projectors manufacturer from Europe and became their largest supplier of optical lens elements.

In 2017 Kingopt started to construct new factory building and office building.

In 2017 Kingopt established new factory Nanyang Kingopt optical instrument co.ltd.

In 2020 New factory building and office building was put into use.

In 2020 Kingopt optics acquired new ISO9001 quality management system certification and ISO14001 environmental system certification. 

Our goal :Establish “Kingopt”brand enterprise and become the best optics company in CHINA.
