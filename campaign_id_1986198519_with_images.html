Story

Anti-Reflection Design

Traditional teleprompters often suffer from glare during use, which not only affects the quality of the recorded footage but can also distract the audience. This teleprompter features a well-engineered design that effectively eliminates reflection issues. As a result, photographers can shoot freely under any lighting conditions without worrying about light interference.

No Black Border Design

Many teleprompters produce black borders when shooting vertically, which not only affects visual aesthetics but also adds to the editing workload. The new teleprompter features an optimized screen frame design that ensures seamless content display, eliminating the issue of black borders.

Magnetic Quick-Release Design

For both the filming phone and the teleprompter display phone, we use a practical magnetic quick-release design — no obstructions, fast switching! Supports teleprompter phones up to 8 inches.

Compatibility

Currently, we only support the full range of iPhone models for filming. Each iPhone model comes with a compatible lens mount, and the reflective surface measures 9.25 inches. We also provide a handheld tripod. In addition, we offer a dedicated app and remote control to adjust font settings.

Effortless Control and Customization with App & Remote 

In addition to its innovative physical design, our Magnetic Portable Teleprompter offers its dedicated app and remote control. Whether you're filming a long presentation or a quick social media clip, the ability to customize your viewing experience is at your fingertips. The app allows you to effortlessly adjust the font size, ensuring that the text is perfectly visible no matter the distance between you and the teleprompter. Need to switch to the next page of your script? With a simple click of the remote control, you can smoothly flip pages, eliminating the need to pause your recording or disrupt your flow.
