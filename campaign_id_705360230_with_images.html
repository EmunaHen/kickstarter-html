Hear With You

A storytelling app that keeps the voices we love alive.

👨‍👧 A Familiar Voice is Everything

I’m building Hear With You because I know the power of a familiar voice. As a dad, I’ve seen how much comfort my daughter finds in hearing me read her bedtime stories,  especially during the moments I can’t be there in person. That feeling of connection, safety, and love is something every child deserves, no matter where their loved ones are.

🧓 Where It All Began

As my father approaches his 84th year, I’ve found myself holding tightly to the sound of his voice, knowing one day, it will become a memory. What struck me most wasn’t just my own fear of losing that voice, but the thought that my daughter would no longer hear her granddad tell her stories, make her laugh, or pass down his wisdom and warmth through his words.

🔊 The Idea: Preserve the Voice That Matters

That’s when Hear With You was born, a way for families to preserve the voices of the people they love, and continue experiencing their presence through stories.

Whether it’s a parent reading a bedtime story, a grandparent sharing a family tale, or a loved one sending a comforting message, our voices are more than sound. They’re memory, emotion, and legacy.

📱 What is Hear With You?

Hear With You is a mobile app that uses AI voice technology to let anyone record a short voice sample, then use it to read personalised stories aloud, replicating the warmth, tone, and rhythm of their natural voice.

✨ Core Features:

Record just 30–60 seconds of your voice

Choose from a growing story library

Personalise characters and names

Generate narrated stories in your own voice

Listen anytime, even offline

Share with family or save for special milestones

Voice setup

Story selection

Playback screen

👪 Why It Matters

This project isn’t just about bedtime stories, it’s about presence, memory, and emotional continuity.

For military parents, long-distance families, terminally ill loved ones, or just those juggling work and travel, Hear With You keeps your voice in the room when you physically can't be.

Risks and challenges

As with any tech-based project, there are a few risks to be mindful of. We have already secured the patent for Hear With You and developed a working prototype. The next challenge is refining / finalising the app. A key focus is ensuring the AI voice cloning remains safe, private, and emotionally accurate. We are working with experienced developers and ethical AI advisors to fine-tune this process and uphold the highest level of data protection. There is also the risk of timeline adjustments. Although we have carefully mapped out our development schedule and budget, unexpected technical issues may arise. I am committed to being transparent with backers and will provide regular updates throughout the journey. This project is built with heart, backed by a skilled team, and supported by a clear plan. With your help, we can bring Hear With You to families everywhere.

Learn about accountability on Kickstarter

Use of AI

We will leverage ai voice cloning and prompt generating technology to allow users to create customised stories, in their own voice or loved ones voice. This will be integrated via API in our software. The part of our project that will use AI will be when users clone their voice and also when they want to generate a custom story. The customisable story will need human creativity to be completed, however we will offer pre produced prompts for people who need ideas with their stories. This is the only part of the app that will use generative AI.
