MetricSip Equinox: The Smart Water Bottle That Thinks For You

The Problem: We Know We Should, But...

Let’s be honest. We all know hydration is crucial – for energy, focus, overall health, and peak performance. Doctors tell us, articles remind us, and our bodies signal us (though often when it’s already too late).

But life gets busy – work piles up, workouts demand attention, and errands keep us on the go. Remembering to drink enough water is one thing; accurately tracking it is a completely different challenge.

Manual logging apps require constant attention. Pushing buttons on a bottle? Easy to forget. Guessing your intake? Often wildly inaccurate. The result? We either give up tracking altogether or stress over unreliable data, never truly knowing if we're meeting our body's unique needs.

The Solution: Hydration Tracking That Finally Just Works.

The concept of the Equinox is quite simple - it tracks everythink you drink. Everything will be done automatically. You just have to check the results in the app.

Elegance Meets Intelligence:

We believe technology should integrate beautifully into your life. Equinox features a minimalist, elegant design crafted from premium, durable materials like 100% recyclable borosilicate glass and a high-quality, BPA-free lid. The design is based on a common water bottle. The looks itself will be in different options, for now we plan a white and grey frosted glass as well as a transparent glass bottle. Since everything making the bottle smart is placed in the lid, we will be able to offer a great flexibility for the bottle itself.
It looks and feels great in your hand, complementing your style whether you're at the gym, in the office or on the go. It's smart hydration, without compromising on aesthetics.

How Does the Magic Happen?

Equinox learns your unique hydration needs (based on factors like your profile and activity levels) and automatically tracks every sip you take throughout the day.

No buttons to push. No apps to open before you drink. No manual logging.

Sophisticated sensors embedded in the lid handle everything: they measure your intake precisely and sync the data effortlessly with your smartphone. Just live your life, drink when you’re thirsty (or when Equinox gently reminds you), and gain powerful insights into your habits – all without lifting a finger, except to lift the bottle!

To ensure accuracy, you can briefly press a sensor in the lid to indicate refilling or anything else. If an incorrect value is recorded, the app allows manual adjustments. Common scenarios are autonomously detected and processed using built-in sensors. A quick tutorial during setup will guide you through proper usage and showcase all features.

Unlock Your Potential, Effortlessly:

Beyond Basic Tracking: Unlock Deeper Insights

Discover your hydration patterns over days, weeks, and months. Set personal goals, monitor your progress, and receive tailored recommendations to help you stay on track. Instantly visualize your drinking habits with intuitive charts and unlock trends that empower you to make healthier choices, every day.

Take the guesswork out of hydration. With Equinox and Metricsip, your journey to better health is guided by data that matters — and a bottle that’s always got your back.
