Risks and challenges

While we’ve invested significant time in the development, design, and testing of the JIYUAIRING Smart Ring, a few potential risks remain as we move into mass production and global fulfillment. First, although our prototype has been thoroughly tested, scaling up manufacturing may bring challenges such as component delays or quality control issues. We’ve partnered with experienced suppliers and factories to minimize these risks and ensure consistent production standards. Second, software updates and app optimization may require ongoing refinement. Our engineering team is committed to continuously improving performance, stability, and compatibility across different smartphones and operating systems. Third, unexpected shipping delays or customs-related issues could affect delivery times, especially for international backers. To address this, we’ve carefully planned logistics and will keep backers informed throughout the fulfillment process. Lastly, as with any wearable technology, user experiences may vary depending on skin tone, finger size, or wearing habits. We will provide detailed usage guidelines and responsive customer support to ensure optimal performance. We’re confident in our timeline and preparations, but we believe in transparency. Should any delays or issues arise, we promise to communicate openly and work hard to deliver a product that meets or exceeds your expectations.
