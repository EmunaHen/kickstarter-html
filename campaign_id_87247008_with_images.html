Story

Startups Area: Crowdfunding the Future of Startup Collaboration.

Overview

Startups Area is a mobile-first professional social network for startup founders, cofounders, investors, advisors, and service providers — launching soon on Android and iOS.

It’s designed to help people connect around real, actionable startup ideas and build serious ventures from the ground up. Unlike noisy or generic platforms, Startups Area offers a focused space to find cofounders, validate ideas, get expert feedback, and pitch to investors.

The app includes a curated database of 2,200 verified investors, advanced filters, private document sharing for pitch decks, and a powerful search system based on stage, sector, and skills.

Whether you're building, joining, or investing — Startups Area is where startup collaboration begins.

User Roles

Users can sign up under specific roles:

· Startup Creator – Share your vision and build your team.

· Cofounder – Find a project where your skills matter most.

· Investor – Discover promising startups and access private decks.

· Advisor – Help founders grow and build your network.

· Service Provider – Offer value to startups looking for legal, tech, or marketing help.

Startup Management
Showcase your startup and attract the right attention.

Each user can create one Startup Card, which includes:

  • A short pitch to quickly explain your idea or solution.
  • The sector(s) your startup operates in.
  • The current stage of your startup: Idea, Validation, Prototype, Traction, or Scaling.
  • An optional pitch deck, with the ability to upload supporting documents — visible only to verified investors for confidentiality and trust.

Privacy and Trust

  • Private documents (e.g., business plans) visible only to verified investors
  • Corporate email required for roles such as investor
  • Blocklist of suspicious email domains to prevent fraud

Advanced Search and Filters

Users can search and filter the database of 2200 investors by:

  • Investor name
  • Investment criteria (Pre-Seed, Seed, Series A, …)
  • Location (country, region or state, city)

Internal Messaging

Chat only available after a connection is stablished.

  • 10 premium messages per month for PRO users. Premium messages can be sent to anyone on the platform.

Advantages for PRO users
Unlock powerful features to boost your visibility and opportunities.

  • Advanced filters to help you find the most relevant people and opportunities.
  • Highlighted visibility on your profile to stand out in the community.
  • 10 premium messages per month — contact anyone on the platform directly, even if you're not connected.
  • Exclusive access to a curated database of over 2,200 verified venture capital firms.

Risks and challenges

We’re working with a small, focused team of builders. While we’ve already built the core features, some risks remain: 1. Tech Matching Accuracy — We’re iterating based on user feedback to improve match relevance. 2. User Growth — Like any network, value grows with the number of users. 3. App Store Delays — Apple/Google approval can take time. We’re allowing buffer periods, but will keep you updated every step of the way.
