Story

Introducing DealDish

Imagine an app that simplifies grocery shopping, saves you money, and helps you eat healthier—all in one place. Meet DealDish, the AI-powered meal planning app designed to transform how you plan, shop, and cook.

What We’re Raising Funds For

We’re building DealDish to:

  1. Save Money: Use AI to analyze local grocery flyers, finding the best deals on ingredients.
  2. Track Nutrition: Help you meet your dietary goals with tailored nutritional insights.
  3. Cook Better: Generate recipes based on your preferences, budget, and cooking skill level.

Your funding will help us:

  • Finalize app development for iOS and Android.
  • Enhance AI capabilities for personalized meal and recipe recommendations.
  • Launch a beta version to test and improve user experience.

Why We Care

We’re passionate about helping people eat well without overspending. In today’s fast-paced world, balancing health, cost, and time can feel overwhelming. DealDish was born out of our desire to make healthy living accessible and affordable for everyone.

How We’ll Make It Happen

With a small but dedicated team, we’ve developed a prototype using AI to analyze flyer data and recommend personalized recipes. Funding will allow us to:

  • Refine our user interface for seamless interaction.
  • Expand recipe databases and nutrition tracking features.
  • Launch across Ontario, with plans for future expansion.

Who We Are

We’re a passionate two-person team based in Ontario, blending expertise in AI technology and a commitment to healthier, more affordable living. With your support, we’ll make DealDish the go-to app for grocery savings and personalized meal planning.

Risks and challenges

Creating DealDish has been a passion project from day one—driven by determination, late nights, and a real desire to make a difference. I’ve done everything myself: from designing the interface, developing the app, building the backend, and even researching and implementing the AI for recipe generation. It’s been an all-in journey, funded by landscaping jobs and built during every spare moment I had while in college. This isn’t just an app—it’s a mission to help Canadians battle rising living costs, save money on groceries, and eat better without the stress. Here are a few of the challenges I’m tackling head-on: Budget and Scaling: As a solo, self-funded developer, every bit of support goes directly toward infrastructure, AI improvements, and expanding features. Your backing helps keep DealDish fast, accurate, and growing. Development Timeline: The core is nearly finished, but refining the app for a seamless experience across all devices takes time. I’m committed to quality and will keep you in the loop with every milestone. Data Accuracy: DealDish uses AI to scan flyers, match ingredients, and generate meals. While the system works well, small errors may appear in early versions. A beta phase will help iron these out before full launch. Reaching More People: I’m launching first in Ontario to focus on local users and fine-tune the experience. Expanding across Canada will require more data integration and community feedback—but it’s absolutely part of the plan. Despite the challenges, this project is built with heart and purpose. With your support, DealDish can become the go-to app for Canadians looking to eat smarter, save more, and make food decisions with confidence.

Yes, the data used in DealDish is sourced from publicly available grocery flyers, recipe databases, and nutritional information. Any data requiring specific consent will either be excluded or used only after obtaining the necessary permissions.
