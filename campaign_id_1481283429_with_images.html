We’re a team of engineers in Control Science and Engineering—with an average age of just 30—who are passionate about redefining how we move through the ocean. For years, we’ve been immersed in developing intelligent marine tech, especially in unmanned surface vehicles. Along the way, we’ve built up deep expertise in propulsion systems, smart controls, and hydrodynamic design.

The idea behind AQUATICJETPACK started with a simple yet bold vision: what if we could design a sleek, powerful jetpack for underwater adventures—something totally different from what’s out there today? We wanted to create a device that straps intelligence and thrust to your back, sets your hands free, and lets you glide through the water like a dolphin. It had to be powerful, fun, and accessible to everyone.

But something amazing happened after we built it.

Once the prototype came to life, we realized the possibilities were even bigger than we imagined. The AQUATICJETPACK opened up unexpected ways to explore, play, and interact with the water. That surprise confirmed what we’ve always believed: great technology should excite its creators and empower its users—and when that connection happens, real innovation begins.

That’s the kind of tech we live to build.
