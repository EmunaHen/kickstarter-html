How it all began

Our story begins in 2019 with the founding of Neunziggrad. Our goal back then: industrial 3D printing and development for companies in Germany. But the deeper we delved into the world of product development, the more often we came across an issue that many makers were missing - a simple, reliable and affordable way to create vacuum formed parts. The problem, however, was that the equipment on the market was either outrageously expensive or made of haphazardly assembled wood or plastic. None of these options met our requirements in terms of quality, functionality and value for money.

So we set about developing our own vacuum forming box - robust, efficient and tailored precisely to our needs. Without unnecessary technology, but with maximum effect.

What began as a solution for ourselves quickly developed into a larger project. Because we realized that the box could also help others.

That's why we are now launching the ZeroPoint9 on the market - so that everyone has access to professional vacuum forming technology. For makers, hobbyists, architects, designers, small businesses - in short: for you.

Why the name ZeroPoint9?

At some point, we were faced with the question: What should our vacuum box be called?
We didn't want just any name - but one that felt right to us and created a connection to our company name NEUNZIGGRAD. So we started to think about it. We thought about the number 90 and the angle - and came up with the negative pressure that can arise during vacuum forming: -0.9 bar.
There it was, this parallel between ninety and zero point nine.
So we started experimenting. We wrote down all the combinations: -0.9, M0.9, minus zero point 9 - until we finally had a name on paper that we knew immediately: 
that's it - ZeroPoint9.

The vacuum box 

A special feature are the four protruding flags in combination with the cleverly recessed areas in the frame. These ensure that the film always remains precisely in position during the vacuum forming process and does not slip. The film is pulled evenly and straight over the workpiece. This ensures that it covers the entire vacuum bed cleanly and seals it perfectly - for reliable results with every application.

The vacuum box has been designed to be particularly space-saving and flexible. As soon as the guide rods are removed, the box can be easily stored on the shelf - compact and tidy, without taking up valuable space. At the same time, it is always ready to hand when it is needed.

Tenter frame:

The ZP9 tenter frame is designed to fit perfectly into a standard oven with its dimensions (445mm x 341mm). Thanks to the quick-release screws supplied, a new foil can be inserted and securely fixed in a very short time - without any tools or great effort. This not only saves time, but also makes the entire process efficient and reliable.

Particularly practical: slots are integrated into the upper part of the frame, which serve as a template. This allows the film to be marked and cut easily and precisely - without the need for a folding rule or ruler. This means you are not tied to expensive, manufacturer-specific films, but can use inexpensive yard goods and cut them to size individually.

Vacuum cleaner connection

There is a connection for standard vacuum cleaners (Ø 35mm) on the back of the ZP9. As a rule, any standard hose will fit - simply plug it in and get started.

  • If, contrary to expectations, your vacuum cleaner does not fit exactly, this is not a problem: inexpensive adapters are available online or in DIY stores for just a few euros.

No special appliances required - your household vacuum cleaner is all you need!

Exchangeable perforated plate

The ZP9 is not only robust, but also flexible. If you need a different vacuum bed for a special project, the supplied perforated plate can be easily replaced. This means you can easily use a customized vacuum bed that is precisely tailored to your application.

A major advantage of this is that your mold can be directly and firmly connected to the box. This ensures maximum stability during the vacuum forming process - no slipping, no shifting of position. You can rely on everything staying in place while the film is pulled cleanly over the component. Precision becomes the standard

Considerations for future updates

To make the vacuum forming process even more convenient and efficient in the future, we are currently working on several optional extensions for the ZP9.

One particular focus is on the development of a foot pedal that enables the entire vacuum forming process to be carried out independently and even more precisely. The idea behind it: As soon as the heated film touches the component on the vacuum bed, the vacuum cleaner can be conveniently activated by foot pressure. This leaves both hands free to position the film precisely and hold the workpiece securely in place. We are currently testing various pedals and are looking for a solution that meets our high standards in terms of quality, ergonomics and reliability. Of course, we will keep you up to date here on Kickstarter - as always, you will be the first to hear about new developments!

We are also thinking about a mechanical alternative: A solution in which the vacuum cleaner runs continuously and the vacuum is specifically triggered by an integrated flap. This opens at exactly the right moment and allows the air to escape in a controlled manner - the vacuum forming process thus starts automatically without the need to manually operate a pedal or switch.

Another exciting item on our development agenda is an adjustable frame. This would make it possible to flexibly stretch smaller or particularly large film formats - a real added value for our customers.
