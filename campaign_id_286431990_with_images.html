Product 1 GoFlex

Measure with intelligence and ease with the Go-Flex tape measure. Artificial Intelligence in your revolutionary new Go-Flex with “Bucky” will give you voice commands that will make it easy to take measurements, automatically store them, and disengage and associate them with the measured items. Just point the laser or use the tape measure, and talk to “Bucky”.

With the natural language processing that “Bucky” has, he will be your partner builder that will increase your productivity, improve your time, and be able to use voice commands.

-Bucky” stores this measurement.
-Bucky” takes a capture.
-Bucky” adds this measurement with the previous one.
-Bucky” takes a note about this measurement, room, room, ect.
-Bucky” creates a new group of measurements called, kitchen, bedroom, ect.
-Bucky” take a reminder to adjust this measurement and send it to the supervisor.
Bucky” generates a supervision order.

GoFlex is designed with the goal of bringing innovation to construction using cutting-edge contech. It features a 20 MP camera, built-in microphone, rechargeable battery via USB-C or wireless charging, laser distance meter, replaceable blades and reels, and Bluetooth connection with the GoCont app, which provides access to 'Bucky,' the revolutionary virtual construction assistant (VCA).

The combination of “Bucky”, GoFlex and the GoCont app will give us the possibility to generate an interactive work group in real time and the opening of voice commands and voice synthesis, for coordination, measurement and progress control in construction. Synchronizing 2 or more GoFlex to take measurements faster, in tasks such as stakeout, execution control or volume calculation.

Would you like to have a visual record of your work and measurements? With Go-Flex, the smart device that makes it possible, you can. Go-Flex has an integrated camera that allows you to photograph the sections or pieces you measure and save them in the internal memory or in the Go-Cont application, so you can have a quick and accurate record.

Product 3 GoBook

GoBook will take monitoring, planning, updating and execution control to the next level of innovation thanks to the “Bucky” VCA and character recognition. Giving you the ability to update your notes in real time, configure the pages according to the needs of the project, all this through Bucky and the GoCont app.

GoBook is the first smart notebook for builders, incorporating Bluetooth connectivity, microphone and speaker in its durable leather-covered body, which can be increased in number of sheets as needed. The innovation in field notebooks for the builder and professional will provide you with voice commands and practical manual or digital note taking. An intelligent bridge between analog and digital.

Use character recognition, take a photo with the GoCont app and synchronize all your manual notes with your construction site records. O Use voice commands with Bucky.
-Bucky” changes the record of the progress sheet number 7.
-Bucky” the schedule needs to be changed, there was a counter time.
-Bucky” I have notes in the GoBook that I need to take to an area calculation.
-Bucky” I have the delivery signature on page 4 of the GoBook, record it in project 17.
-Bucky” record the attendance in the GoBook notes an adjustment to the progress.
-Bucky” record my notes and transfer them to the work team.
These voice commands give dynamism to the traditional notebook, making GoBook and the GoCont app usable without manual user intervention. Increasing productivity and introducing AI in the processes of supervision and control of construction sites.

All via voice commands in desktop or mobile mode.

-Bucky” generate me a rendering with standard materials.
-Bucky” generate me a rendering with materials chosen in the project.
“Bucky” analyzes the progress of the work with a 20% increase in labor.

GoCont the application specialized in construction. It will offer you project creation, time planning, cost analysis, execution diagrams, list of suppliers and professionals.
