Story

My name is James. I have been building and fixing things my entire life. Along the way I kinda became jack of all trades, master of none but better then some as the saying goes. I come from a different time when televisions where more a piece of furniture then a screen. The tv sat low off the floor and was not the over-powering presence in the living space that it is today. The tvs of old where beautiful wood cabinets with stereos and record players built in with hand made speakers. I have saved a couple of those old tv cabinets and put in modern electronics. I have one in my home now.  It's time to transform Television back into an Art form!

I have seen a couple flat screen televisions framed over the years, custom jobs done by carpenters. When I was putting together my media room I knew I wanted to frame my tv.  I had some cove molding left over from a job and decided that will work just fine. A few hours later and presto magico a framed television.  As it turned out the framed tv was to big for the space I wanted to put it in so it ended up in my dining room as more of a digital art frame. As I turned it on I was taken aback. The frame created a sense of warmth and changed the entire perspective of the viewing experience.

Which would you rather have?

It's ok I already asked the magic eight ball it said the framed one!!!

 In that moment I knew I had created something so beautifully obvious that everyone wants this in their home!! Now I know what your thinking Framed tvs its already a thing, weeeell it's not. How many have you seen in real life, my guess is none! Yes you can buy a tv called the frame and yes you can then buy the frame that goes on the frame tv or you can buy an after market frame for said tv but it's the only one period full stop. The frames are plastic or metal and well lets just say not pretty. That set up will cost you between $1500.00 to $3000.00 depending on the frame tv model and frame you choose. Of course you could find a custom wood shop/mill and have them make you one. The local mill will charge you $2500.00 to $3000.00 for one! No wonder it's not a thing. At first I thought I will buy tvs and frame them then sell them. Nope that's not going to work. I need to find a way to frame any tv easily. Ok baby Jesus I need some help and wolla like magic out of the ether came the design for the universal television frame. The design looked good on paper but I needed a proof of design. In came the magic of the 3D printer.

Not real pretty, but it did it's job proof of design done!!

Now it was time for wood.

Well hello beautiful! You could say the outcome is stunning. Pictures do not do justice for the presence this frame creates. In person the frame has an effect on the way your mind perceives the television. It creates a very warm inviting almost comforting emotion. Once you see it with your own eyes you will never see a television the same again.

The frame is 5 1/2 inches wide and 2 inches thick made from solid wood. LED multicolor backlighting powered off tv usb and controlled by app on your phone. The frame installs so simply a trained monkey can put it on the tv and it ships easily. These frames are G.rown in America, E.ngineered in America, M.ade in America, S.old in America. We are a G.E.M.S. company we are not just made in America we are made of America. Tooling for the the wood mill is done. Prototyping is done. The funding needed will be used for large lumber orders and finishing higher volume production facility. We will be offering 250  frames in this campaign. Reward tier is based on tv size. End user simply provides the television make and model a couple measurements and we take it from there. The market for television accessories is astronomical. At $500 a unit .5% of the market is $311,875,000.00. That does not include possible commercial applications such as Art galleries, forward facing customer lobbies, high end boutique hotel rooms and restaurants just to name a few. There have not been many television accessories through the evolution of the TV.  VCR/DVD, satellite receivers, cable boxes, sound bars, fire sticks and gaming consoles those combined have made trillions of dollars. The first 250 units will come with a referral code that when future orders are made will pay a referral fee of $25. This code will allow the new customer to receive a 10% discount on their order. These 250 codes will be the only ones ever issued will be perpetual and marketable. Meaning on future orders people will have to seek out 1 of the 250 codes to get their discount. I hope you will join us on this journey.

 Picture This is transforming television into an art form and changing the perspective of TV one frame at a time..

Risks and challenges

We are after people and places that appreciate craftsmanship and quality. There is also the possibility that this project will not fund and we take a slower road to success. Projects are not successful because people give up much to easily.
