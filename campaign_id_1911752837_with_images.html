Story

Lettre: A Social Letter-writing App

Built by friends, funded by Community

Bringing Back the Art of Letter Writing - Together

In a world of fleeting texts, ignored emails, and algorithm-driven feeds, we built Lettre as a home for something real: letters. The kind that slow you down, make you reflect and create meaningful connections across the world.

No fleeting messages. No endless scrolling. Just the joy of writing, collecting beautiful stamps and discovering new penpals - without distractions.

And the best part? Lettre.app is free.

But keeping it that way isn’t easy - and that's why we are here today

Features You Will Love

- Write & Send Handwritten Letters: Share thoughtful, handwritten (digital) messages with other Lettre users or any email address under the sun. No bots or generative-AI; only authentic, human feels.

- Find Your Global Pen Pal: Meet real people and build deep meaningful connections with pen pals around the world - a refreshing alternative to instant messaging.

- Collect Unique Digital Stamps: Collect and exchange master-crafted digital stamps designed by Artists from all over the world that celebrate philately in the digital age.

- Full Apple Pencil Support: Write naturally and expressively with full Apple Pencil integration for an authentic handwriting experience on iPads (maybe even on iphones too if you try to push the limits of what's possible - let us know how it goes).

- Premium Digital Stationery: Choose from elegant digital paper and envelope designs that mimic the feel of traditional correspondence (yes, even on iphones).

- No Ads, No Distractions: Enjoy a peaceful, uninterrupted experience of personal expression without the clutter of ads or spam. Only delightful scribbling bliss!

No Ads. No Paywalls. No Data Selling. Ever.

Like you, we hate intrusive ads. We refuse to sell user data. And we won’t let corporate sponsors dictate what we build.

We started Lettre as a small group of friends who love letters, technology and collectibles - and we want to build it with all the nice-stuff we have seen across spaces we love ourselves, independent, and truly free. Instead of being backed by investors who’d push us to compromise, we’d rather be backed by something far stronger: you, our community.

What's Next?

With the launch of our iPhone version and the community's help, we are hoping to cross 200,000 downloads this year - unlocking opportunities to build some truly exciting features.

We’re working on a brand-new Canvas experience that will support videos, GIFs, audio files, and - one of our most requested features - Additional Pages for longer, more expressive letters.

Beyond that, we're designing more immersive community-building tools, including Writing Clubs where users can create and join groups centered around shared interests - from mental health support groups to film appreciation societies and more. 

If we exceed our funding goals, our 5 year plan can become a 1 year plan

We’re aiming for more than just product updates - we’re hoping to spark a culture shift, where writing letters becomes a new standard of communication in a world increasingly shaped by bots. In this new era, we believe authenticity is what will truly set us apart.

Risks and challenges

Crossing 200,000 downloads means that even with a modest 10% conversion to subscribers, a relatively small group of passionate patrons could help keep Lettre.app truly free for everyone in the community. While the app itself is free and always will be - the reality is that keeping it running isn’t. From server costs to development to supporting our small team pay rent, there are real costs behind every handwritten moment. If we’re unable to reach sustainability through the support of our community, then sadly, we won’t be able to keep the lights on forever. This isn't about scaling endlessly - it's about preserving something meaningful in a noisy digital world. If you believe in what we’re building - a slower, kinder, more intentional way to connect — now’s the time to become a part of it.

Reusability and recyclability

At Lettre.app, everything we build is grounded in intentionality, sustainability and reuse. From the stamps you collect to the stationery you write on, our ecosystem is designed to be circular, not disposable - honoring the spirit of real correspondence. We also want to be transparent about what we don’t use: We do not rely on generative AI tools in our development process - not only because of the ethical concerns surrounding authorship and exploitation but also because they are resource-intensive and contradict our values around mindful tech and environmental responsibility. Lettre is about real people writing to real people. No shortcuts, no bots - just the joy of genuine, human expression.
