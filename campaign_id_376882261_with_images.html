Story

When we first launched Eilik, our little desktop buddy quickly found its way into people’s hearts. To our surprise, many of you took Eilik far beyond the desk — to cafés, beaches, even around the world in travel bags.
That's when the idea bloomed: What if Eilik could come with you everywhere, a little easier?
Then, Eiliko was born — designed as an adorable bag charm you can clip onto your bag, bringing that same happy vibe wherever you go.
Also, something else comes to our minds together. Many of you loved dressing up Eilik, giving it a personality all its own. Why not create swappable themed bodies for Eiliko, that's perfect for your makeover fun and matches your unique style.

Risks and challenges

Hello, we are Energize Lab. Eiliko is our completely new product, designed from the ground up with its own unique identity. It is currently in the late stages of development, with ongoing small-batch testing and functional refinement, and is ready to move into mass production. While challenges may arise during the manufacturing process, we’re confident in our ability to deliver high-quality Eiliko units on time. Our first Kickstarter project, Eilik, is now trusted by over 300,000 users. That journey gave us valuable experience, technical insight, and operational confidence as we take on something entirely new. Over the past few years, we've invested heavily in Eiliko’s design, engineering, and testing. We've completed EVT, DVT, and PVT phases, made multiple refinements based on real user feedback, and ensured that Eiliko meets our standards for long-term reliability. We’ve partnered with a manufacturing facility that operates a 47,000㎡ production base and has worked with leading drone and robotic vacuum brands. Their experience in high-precision, large-scale production ensures that Eiliko is in good hands. Thanks to the lessons learned from supporting Eilik’s large user base, we’ve also been able to proactively improve Eiliko’s hardware and software for greater stability at scale. We will only ship units that pass strict quality control. If any unforeseen issues arise, we’ll communicate with full transparency and act quickly to resolve them—because your trust means everything to us.

Use of AI

Our project does not fund AI tools, nor does it use AI-generated content. However, our project uses AI in other forms. Specifically, we have connected to the AI big model, which enables our product to have interesting AI chats with Eiliko when the network is successfully configured and the network environment is good.
