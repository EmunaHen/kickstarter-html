Story

My Story

I still remember the feeling I felt when I realized I had lost over $500 — not just in money, but in momentum. Every mistake I made while trading felt heavier than the last. Missed entries, mismanaged exits, forgotten expirations — all buried inside messy spreadsheets and scattered notes.

I wasn’t just losing trades — I was losing confidence.

Trading started as a passion — something I believed could unlock freedom, discipline, and maybe even financial independence. But somewhere along the way, it became overwhelming. My spreadsheets grew out of control. I was tracking dozens of trades manually, jumping between tabs, calculating profits, logging dates, and hoping I didn’t overlook something important.

And then one day, I made another mistake — a simple one.
I forgot about a trade I had open, and it cost me.

 The Spark: Where It All Changed

That night, I stepped away from the screen and really asked myself:
“Why am I doing this the hard way?”

I realized something powerful — I wasn’t the only trader struggling to stay organized. There had to be a better way. I didn’t need another spreadsheet. I needed a system. A tool designed specifically for options traders, built from the ground up with the clarity and control we all need.

That’s when TradeBinder was born — not just as an app, but as a solution built on lessons learned the hard way.

Features

💻 Trade Entry, Made Simple

This is a screenshot of the Trade Entry screen in TradeBinder, the all-in-one options trading journal I've been building for everyday traders.

At the top, you can input your starting balance, stock symbol, strategy type, net premium, and the credit received — so you stay organized and in control of every trade.

Below that, you can build out your custom trade with multiple legs — selecting Buy/Sell, Call/Put, strike price, and expiration date with just a few clicks. Whether you're entering a basic covered call or a complex multi-leg spread, TradeBinder makes it intuitive and fast.

Once your trade is set up, hit Add Trade and it gets logged instantly into your portfolio.

On the right side, you’ve got access to all your major actions:
✅ View your Journal
✅ Close an open trade
✅ Save or Load your portfolio
📊 View a Performance Chart of how you’re doing over time

Risks and challenges

Building a tool like Trading Binder comes with real challenges — and I want to be upfront with you about them. 🛠️ Development & Testing This is a one-man project, which means every feature, bug fix, and update takes time. There’s always a chance of unexpected issues during development, especially when adding new tools like strategy scanners or app store integration. How I’ll overcome it: I’m keeping the design simple, functional, and focused — and I’ll be testing it every step of the way with real traders to make sure it works. 📱 App Store & Google Play Approval Getting listed on both stores involves reviews, requirements, and delays outside of my control. How I’ll overcome it: I’ve already started preparing the app with those requirements in mind. I’ll be transparent with backers if any delays come up. 💾 Cross-Platform Compatibility Making the app run smoothly on different devices (Windows, macOS, Android, iOS) takes extra time and attention. How I’ll overcome it: The first release will focus on desktop (Windows/Mac), with mobile versions rolling out afterward. Feedback from early backers will shape what comes next. 💡 Scaling with Features As more features are added, things can get complex — especially keeping the interface clean and intuitive. How I’ll overcome it: I’m sticking to a “simple but powerful” design philosophy and building the app in modular pieces that grow with users’ needs. Your support helps me bring this vision to life — and I’m fully committed to finishing strong. If challenges come up, you’ll hear it from me first. Let’s build something real together.
