Story

Bonder & Long Jin: Scented Journeys, Illuminating Drive Time.

Industrial designer Bonder and his perfumer wife Long Jin are outdoor enthusiasts and dreamers. Amid modern life’s fast pace, Long Jin envisioned soothing souls through natural fragrances. ​

For every vehicle, both air fresheners and trash solutions are essentials. Yet current offerings fall short: car fragrances come in disposable bottles or canisters demanding constant replacement. Generic car bins suffer from awkward positioning that obstructs cabin space and chronic instability causing spills during drives.If the purchased trash can or garbage bag is too large and often forgotten to be disposed of in a timely manner, it can cause a very unpleasant odor inside the car.

ZhouShangPromisedLand Z1 redefines in-car essentials, it is a small trash can for your car that also has an air freshener built in.  Its PCB-embedded base unit combines a quiet fan with long-lasting scent blocks, Magnetic cover ，while the strategically designed vents circulate freshness throughout the cabin instantly. The hermetically sealed trash compartment eliminates odor leakage completely—transcending disposable air fresheners and unstable mini bins for unparalleled convenience. 

The Z1 revolutionizes compact car spaces by merging a trash bin and fragrance system into one multi-functional marvel. Its space-smart engineering transforms your cup holder into an elegant design accent—delivering utility with uncompromising sophistication.

Z1 with Two Custom-engineered natural scents, the one is Leisure Aroma Travel. Top is Sparking cardamom pierced by icy lemon-mint.Heart is Velvety coconut-lavender swirls through tobacco gold.And the drydown is Machined earth—vetiver roots gripping aged sandalwood, locked by leather-patchouli gears.Where glacial sparks ignite mindful calm—a journey from clarity to depth, wherever you drive.

The other is Soothing Shadow Aroma. Top is Rain-drenched greens or Bergamot spark.Heart is is Jasmine sambac layers and Tuberose. And the drydown is Virginia cedar . From dewy awakening to zen stillness—a clean oasis for your journey.

Unlock scent freedom with your FREE premium diffuser wood— Drip oils directly or soak it in any essential oil or designer perfume, pop it into your Z1, and instantly transform your cabin into a personalized oasis. Create your signature scent.

Compact and portable by design, it fits effortlessly into any space—from cup holders to countertops—making premium ambiance universally accessible.

Crafted entirely in Aluminum Alloy—from shell to lid—our bin achieves 95% recyclability while radiating premium durability. The minimalist architecture, enhanced by a PulseRing button and magnetic lid actuation, transforms every disposal into a sensorial ritual.

Disposable air fresheners waste resources, while traditional diffusers demand constant restocking yet fail to sustain fragrance. Low-end car bins cheapen your ride with clunky plastic. The Z1 shatters these compromises—engineered as the market’s only truly integrated solution.

Just open, use, and snap the magnetic lid shut—effortlessly one-handed. That’s all.

Simply twist off the base knob, lift out the inner bucket, and rinse—no tools needed.
