Story

What is VFX Oasis?

VFX Oasis is an industry focused visual effects asset library website featuring Hollywood level footage, HDRI's, plates, and other VFX assets. We want to give every artist cheap and free high quality VFX assets that they can use to boost their careers. Become a Kickstarter today and save big on our library once we release!

The Specifics

An Example Product

Below is an example of what a product would look like. You can pick between if you want the footage alone or if you want to bundle together the HDRI and denoised version together. HDRI and denoised plates are crucial to Hollywood-level vfx and so it's recommended to bundle them. We also provide accurate information about the camera, lens, and color data so that you can use them in your workflow.

Pricing

No Subscription

VFX Oasis will not launch with a subscription model. As an artist myself, I don't like how everything has become a subscription and so once you make a purchase on VFX Oasis, it's yours forever. 

Free Section

There will be an ever growing free section of the site. This will be just a taste of some of the footage that we will offer, but will still offer a nice place to start for most artists. Don't worry, the resolution and quality will still be the exact same as the paid options :)

Individual Downloads

To put the power in your hands, you can buy products individually if you'd like. Each product will be offered in different variants depending if you just need the footage, HDRI, and/or denoised footage. Footage starts at $5 per video.

Bundled Packs

VFX Oasis will feature packs which are bundles of footage, assets, and/or files. Some examples of packs include The Camera Tracking Pack, Superhero Pack, and Beginner VFX Pack. Packs will include everything from footage to HDRI's to other files representing a discount compared if you were to buy the products individually. Packs start at $25-50 per pack.

Why I Started VFX Oasis?

Like many others, I started my VFX journey with my phone's camera and a dream. It wasn't until many years into my career that I could touch cinema level footage and it was a game changer. Since then I've cultivated an online community of artists of over 65000 and I teach over 1500 students on my digital courses. I want all VFX artists to be able to have experience with things such as log, proper cinema footage, and HDRI's regardless of their financial position.
