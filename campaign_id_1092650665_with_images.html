Story

Our mission with this app is to unlock the full potential of every drummer, transforming practice from a boring chore into an exciting journey of discovery. We will achieve this by:

-Provide intelligent technology that can check the exercise execution and give precise feedback to improve.

-Ignite the fire of ambition with gamified progress tracking, ensuring every practice session is a successful step towards realizing your Drumming Dream.

-Leveraging established musical proficiency to deliver the most effective teaching method available. 

Ultimately, following the right learning path, coupled with the energy and drive for consistent practice, your drumming skills and prowess will inevitably grow immensely. And the better you play, the more your soul will sing and the higher you will fly!

The Unique Advantage of a Pro Musician Founder

A professional musician since 2006, Pietro Valente has toured extensively across 15 countries in Europe, Asia, and America, collaborating with hundreds of artists in renowned venues and festivals. His diverse musical background spans jazz, rock, funk, pop, Latin, world, and avant-garde music. 

Awarded prestigious prizes in Italy, including the Umbria and Siena Jazz scholarships, 'Circolo Jazz Verona' best new talent 2009, and the Golden Melody Award in Taiwan, Pietro has also recorded over twenty albums, two of which as band leader featuring his original compositions.

Risks and challenges

A key risk for this project is achieving consistent audio detection performance on every device. We anticipate a significant challenge in calibrating the sound recognition system to various hardware configurations, particularly integrated microphones. Continuously improving the effectiveness of our technology is a core, long-term commitment. While initial performance may not be flawless, we are dedicated to diligently refining our detection system to provide the most reliable and user-friendly experience.
