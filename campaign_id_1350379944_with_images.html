Story

Are you sick of sticking and peeling off markers for 3D scans? 

Then, meet Revopoint Trackit! It's an advanced optical tracking and blue laser 3D scanning system designed for marker-free industrial scanning that ensures volumetrically accurate measurements while significantly reducing cumulative errors. 

Whether you're scanning coins or cars, Trackit simplifies your process, making it more efficient than ever.

For more Beta Testers’ cases, please visit the forum.

Supercharge your 3D laser scanning efficiency for workpieces ranging from 10 mm to 6 meters with Trackit's dual-camera base station. It tracks the scanner's real-time spatial movement, letting you create accurate 3D models with no markers and no clean-up.

Effortlessly capture large workpieces by leapfrogging* Trackit's base station around them as you rapidly capture their surfaces at up to 1.5 million measurements per second. Trackit's anchor block system ensures flawless frame stitching with every leapfrog, enabling you to efficiently create professional 3D models. The highly adjustable tripod lets you easily position Trackit to meet multiple capture scenarios.

Maximum Tracking Frame Area: 2800 x 2800 mm @ 4000 mm

Easily Maneuver: 6-meter cable

Tracker Working Distance: 1.5 to 4 m

*Moving the base station around large objects to fully capture them.

Create detailed 3D models ready for dimensional inspection in quality control or scan-to-CAD conversion for reverse engineering.

Volumetric Accuracy: 0.025 + 0.04 mm × L (M)

Scanner Accuracy: Up to 0.02 mm

Reference documents for the test: JJF 1951-2021 C.S. for Optical 3D Measuring System Based on Structured Light Scanning

Certificate No. GFJGJL1001251723746

Skip complex and lengthy calibration processes and get perfect multi-pose calibrations free from human errors. Trackit’s smart auto-calibrating dual-axis gimbal system does all the hard work with total precision, so you'll always have a reliable, accurate, and easy calibration process.

Ensure consistent scan data every time with Trackit's durable one-piece carbon fiber frame, eliminating accuracy errors due to frame deformation shifting markers. Designed for demanding environments, its sturdy yet lightweight construction is comfortable for long scanning sessions.

Adapt to multiple scanning scenarios by switching between Trackit’s dual blue laser capture modes on the fly in both optical tracking and standalone handheld 3D scans. Easily capture obscured or interior areas that the base station can't see using Trackit’s standalone capabilities.

Create photorealistic 3D models and exportable standardized texture reports, integrating with Revo Measure. After the point cloud scan, use the Scanner's high-resolution RGB camera to capture the object's surface color. The images align with the 3D model, ensuring accurate texture alignment and color details.

Trackit's high-sensitivity temperature sensors monitor fluctuations, showing the Tracker and Scanner's current temperature in real time. This ensures optimal operation and prevents temperature-related accuracy drift, maintaining high 3D scanning accuracy.

Revo Trackit is easy-to-use and free software for scanning, editing, processing, and calibrating.

Scan

Granular scan settings let you control every aspect of the scanning process, ensuring optimal point cloud capture. Always ensure you have the perfect scan with real-time point cloud monitoring, showing if you've captured enough data.

Process

Process your raw point cloud automatically with one-click editing or fully fine-tune it with fusion, and meshing tools.

Edit

With versatile selection and clean-up tools, you can swiftly remove unwanted data, fill holes, smooth, and simplify your 3D model.

Merge

Scan large complex projects in sections, then combine the scanned data automatically or manually to create a complete model.

Export

Export your scans in formats supported by all major quality control, CAD, 3D slicing, and 3D modeling professional software for seamless integration into your existing workflows.

Output Formats: PLY, OBJ, STL, ASC, 3MF, GLTF, FBX

Every Trackit unit backed on Kickstarter comes with a free one-year license (worth $499) for Revopoint Measure. It's 3D metrology software that analyzes the shape, dimensions, and quality of objects by comparing point cloud or mesh data to CAD models or drawings.

Ensure your project is within tolerances with Revo Measure's high-accuracy measurement of point clouds and mesh models, which enables the extraction of dimensions, angles, and volume measurements. 

Efficiently compare scanned data with CAD design models and analyze discrepancies, including overall and local deviations. Helping you to quickly identify any issues between the actual object and the intended design.

With robust tools for mesh editing, freeform and parametric modeling, and scan-to-CAD workflows, Revo Design is ideal for reverse engineering, product development, and industrial design.
