Story

Supports instant translation across 138 languages with high accuracy in both text and voice. Communicate fluently no matter where you are.

Seamlessly translates conversations inside WhatsApp, Telegram, Messenger, Instagram DMs, and more — without switching apps.

Enables real-time interpreting during calls, video conferences, and voice chats. Ideal for business meetings or long-distance family calls.

Offers near real-time interpretation for face-to-face conversations, mimicking a personal interpreter — perfect for meetings or travel.

Snap signs, restaurant menus, or printed documents — get instant translations with camera input and smart OCR.

Automatically converts spoken words into written text and translations — ideal for lectures, interviews, and meetings.

Engage in bilingual conversations with auto-switching voice detection — perfect for seamless dialogues between two speakers.

Supports up to 20W of fast wired charging and recharging through USB-C. Whether you're charging your phone or topping up the device itself, the process is quick and efficient — ideal for travelers and power users on the go.

MagSafe-compatible with powerful N52-grade magnets, ensuring a secure snap-on connection for your iPhone or Qi-enabled Android phone. Supports up to 15W wireless output with auto-adaptive power for optimal efficiency.

Compatible with phones, wireless earbuds, and tablets — both wired and wireless. Charge multiple devices seamlessly without needing bulky adapters or cables.

Free on iOS & Android. Translates in chat apps, live calls, social media, and more. Dual-mode: voice + text translation with speech playback.

180° rotation + 90° foldable stand made from premium aluminum alloy. Perfect viewing angles for meetings, videos, or maps.

Risks and challenges

Bringing the Intelligent Language Translator & Portable Charger to market comes with challenges, including ensuring accurate real-time translation, optimizing battery performance, securing regulatory approvals, and managing production logistics. Supply chain delays, software updates, and international shipping complexities may impact timelines, but our team is dedicated to overcoming these hurdles with rigorous testing and reliable partnerships. We’re committed to transparency and will keep our backers informed every step of the way.
