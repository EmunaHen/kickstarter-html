Story

Enter a new world of AR technology. With a stunning 220-inch micro OLED screen, these sleek, ergonomic glasses boast the largest AR display in the world. Everywhere you go - enjoy a full HD 1920x1080 resolution full of vivid imagery and hypnotizing colors right in front of your eyes.

No more need for glasses - adjust your vision instantly! Our AR glasses are designed to innovate convenience and comfortability and make perfect vision effortless with its 0-600° dioptric adjustment wheel. By simply twisting the wheel, instantly fine-tune the display to match your definition of perfect clarity so that you can focus on the future.

Stream the unlimited heights of your drone feed! The Envision AR Glasses are equipped with Drone Eagle Eye Image Transmission - allowing you to experience a direct, real-time feed of your drone footage on its massive 220-inch display. Bring the views of the sky right in front of your own eyes  - seamlessly streamed, effortlessly controlled.

With the help of our backers to reach our goal, the NPS Envision AR Glasses will come with 1 out of your choice of 3 stylized, customizable attachable visors. Designed for ultimate immersion, the visor darkens backgrounds for a more complete cinematic experience. Simply attach and detach the visor by its magnets at your convenience.

The NPS Envision AR Glasses are equipped with an ultra-bright 1500-nit display to help provide the ultimate cinematic experience. Colors burst to life with stunning vibrancy, sharpness, and true-to-life clarity - even in sunlight! We have carefully designed our HD display so that you can experience augmented reality at its most vivid, wherever you go, without limitations! 

The NPS Envision AR Glasses deliver an unrivaled 52° field of view - the widest on the market - creating an all-encompassing window into augmented reality. Watch your favorite shows and stream your favorite games with rich HD clarity and deep, vivid colors. It’s not just augmented reality - it’s cinematic excellence, right before your very eyes.

As the perfect complement to the Envision, the AR station is your personal entertainment hub. Powered by Android 14, the AR station is certified with Google TV so that you can watch movies and TV shows on all of your favorite streaming services, such as Netflix, AppleTV, Amazon Prime, and Hulu. Power up your favorite games with Xbox Game Pass and Nvidia GeForce Experience. Take your personal cinema and video game setup with you anywhere you go with the Envision’s AR Station.

See how the brand new Marvel Rivals game works on the Envision AR Glasses!

Invest in innovation that lasts. Our AR glasses, equipped with cutting-edge Apple 3D decoding technology, process 3D content with stunning depth and realism, making movies, games, and augmented reality experiences feel truly lifelike. Designed to stay ahead of the curve, this technology ensures seamless performance for years to come with continuous software updates and unrivaled hardware support.

As well as the AR Station, the Envision AR Glasses can seamlessly connect to any device with a USB Type-C connection, such as Samsung smartphones and Samsung Dex, Apple computers, Windows devices, gaming consoles, and gaming streaming services such as Nvidia GeForce Experience and Xbox Game Pass. Simply plug the AR glasses into your desired device and create the ultimate ecosystem for entertainment, work, and gameplay!
