Introducing the Micro Wallet Finder

Total Wallet Security

A Digital Multi-Tool for Safety & Security

The Micro Wallet Finder is a Certified 'Find My' Device with the worlds first RFID Skimming Alarm offering Smart RFID™ Protection and Active Jamming Technology.  

Find your wallet instantly around the globe or under the couch. Keeping your valuables safe with left behind alerts is not only smart, it's super convenient!

RFID Skimming Alarm! 
Exclusive to the Micro Wallet Finder

The Micro Wallet Finder performs digital Jiu-Jitsu by harvesting the Radio Frequency waves of any skimmer/reader and temporarily jumbling them making your card impossible to read while alerting you with an audible alarm.

Active & Passive Wallet Finder

Our Apple Certified "Find My" device works on the Apple Mesh Network* to find your item quickly. Get left behind alerts instantly and track down your valuables wether around the globe or around the corner. 

*See our stretch goal to add Google "Find My" for Android during the live campaign. 

All the functionality of an AirTag but a wallet friendly credit card size makes the MWF an ideal Every Day Carry for digital protection and convenience. 

Custom QR Code Powered by ScanKeep™

Every Micro Wallet Finder includes a custom-printed QR code with a Lifetime ScanKeep Lite Membership—that lets anyone reach your chosen contact info (name, email, or phone) instantly and can be edited 24/7 from your ScanKeep dashboard. 

ScanKeep™ allows your finder to find you even when your finder battery is dead by instantly sharing your contact info when your QR code is scanned. If your wallet is lost you can update your contact info 24/7 or upgrade your plan to receive geolocation notifications of any scans of your QR code. 

Use Your Custom QR Code Anywhere

Download and reuse your QR code in countless ways: add it your phone’s lock-screen for a “scan-if-found” retrieval tool, stick it to a pet collar, laptop sleeve, water bottle, or bike helmet—anything you’d hate to lose. Leave a print out in your car when parking in a questionable spot or you need to be contacted to move your EV after charging. 

ScanKeep Premium 

Level up your connections with ScanKeep Premium* and unlock up to 5 MB of data storage per scan.

Perfect for sales agents, creative freelancers, coaches, educators or realtors who want to add videos, PDFs, photos to every scan. You can also add multiple contact personas, so you can tailor what persona you want to use at any time.

Premium plans also adds GPS scan-location logs for every scan so you know where your cards last scan location was. Anonymous chat is also unlocked on the ScanKeep™ Premium plan. 

Whether you stay on the Lifetime Lite plan or go Premium, ScanKeep™ turns your Micro Wallet Finder's QR code into a fail-safe recovery tool—even if the Finder’s battery is depleted.

Google Find My Stretch Goal - Status Update

We’ve had a lot of interest and questions about the Google Find My™ stretch goal, so here’s where things stand:

There are 2 parts to this process. We’re currently in active discussions with our factory to negotiate the lowest possible minimum order quantity (MOQ) required to enable Google Find My integration. This quantity will determine our goal and it's our hope to set a realistic and achievable stretch goal based on the final MOQ.

We've already made encouraging progress in securing a substantially lower MOQ for this order, but negotiations take time and there are still some technical and cost constraints we’re working through to make this feasible at a reasonable unit cost.

Adding to the complexity is that just to apply for the license includes a $2,000 fee, adding to the unit cost no matter what the MOQ. Dynomighty has been a Case Study of many Google products over our 20+ years and have written a very compelling application to ensure our best chances of success.

While we can’t guarantee approval, we’re confident our chances will be excellent. The truth is that the more support we can gather for the project will help us build the case — and take the Micro Wallet Finder to Android users everywhere!

Rest assured, we’re doing everything we can behind the scenes to make this feature a reality for Android users — and we’ll announce the official stretch goal as soon as we can lock in the lowest production threshold.

Thanks again for your support and enthusiasm — it means the world!
