Story

Following the successful delivery of our first Kickstarter campaign (the Foldable Wireless Charging Case), we are proud to introduce the revolutionary NeoClip Wallet, your next generation everyday carry solution.

Do you often forget your wallet and have to rummage through your drawers?

The world’s first Find My-enabled hybrid that merges a sleek card case with a full-function wallet, engineered to fit perfectly in your palm.

Why choose between a minimalist card holder and a functional wallet? 

📌 Single-function frustration: Card cases  lack cash storage; wallets  force bulkiness.  
🔍 Lost item anxiety: Many people lose their wallets every year and have no way to track them.
📏 Bulky compromise: A “slim” wallet still feels like a brick in your pocket.

We combine the best of both worlds:

✅ Card holder core: translucent 6-card holder with Find My (high-decibel speaker（75db-85db）, rechargeable and with a battery life of half a year).

✅ Expandable Wallet: Zippered Coin Pocket + Hidden Card Slots for 3 Extra Cards/Cash! ).

NeoClip's trigger system delivers your top card in one click – no wallet opening needed.

The spring-loaded mechanism pops cards to perfect grabbing height while keeping others securely stored.  

The NeoClip Wallet features a 170mAh built-in battery with magnetic cable charging  - just snap on the magnetic charging cord for quick power-ups.

A single charge delivers 6 months of continuous Find My tracking, thanks to our ultra-low-power Bluetooth chipset. No more fiddling with coin-cell batteries like other trackers! 

The NeoClip Wallet blends a translucent cyberpunk aesthetic with everyday practicality.

Its ultra-compact form factor (can fit in one hand) slips easily into a pocket while making a bold statement - perfect for those who value form and function.

The NeoClip Wallet's smart layout fits 9 cards max (6 in the transparent case + 3 in the folio) or 7 cards + cash in slim mode. 

The zippered coin pocket holds multiple coins or keys - and is smaller than a passport.

Your security comes first. 

The NeoClip wallet features RFID blocking to eliminate electronic pickpocketing and eliminate unwanted scans, with comprehensive protection built into every layer.

While other products force you to compromise, NeoClip offers better value:
✓ Dual-function design (wallet + card holder)
✓ Rechargeable battery, no battery replacement required (extra cost)
✓Cyberpunk transparent design（Focus at checkout）
