Story

MicroCam X1: One Twist. Unlimited Discovery.

Imagine uncovering the fine hairs on a butterfly’s wing, then twisting your camera to freeze a sweeping mountain vista—all with one rugged, 160g gadget. MicroCam X1 combines a 400× microscope lens, 3 cm macro view, and 4K/12 MP camera into one seamless device. Designed for adventurers, educators, creators and curious minds, it reveals hidden worlds and everyday beauty in vibrant detail––no extra lenses or bulky gear required.

MicroCam X1 delivers three powerful views in one compact device. With a simple twist, zoom from 24 mm to 0.01 mm—capturing everyday moments, stunning macro shots, and even the microscopic world in crisp 12MP photos and sharp 4K video.

400x Microscope: See What the Naked Eye Can’t

The MicroCam X1 offers up to 400× magnification in  4K ultra-HD resolution, revealing details as small as 0.01 millimeters—far beyond what the naked eye can detect. Bring it close to any object and uncover a hidden universe, from the fine hairs on an insect to the intricate structure of its compound eyes, or even the ridges of a coin. Every inspection becomes an exciting journey where the tiniest details come to life.

3cm Macro: Close-Up Wonders, No Extra Lens Needed  

Get astonishing close-ups from just 3 cm away. Turn small natural wonders—ice crystals, feathers, petals, even tiny insects—into dramatic, high-definition art. MicroCam X1 lets you sharpen focus on the details without changing or adding lenses, transforming inconspicuous scenes into vibrant, breathtaking images.

Capture in 4K: Preserve Every Detail in Ultra-HD

The MicroCam X1’s standard 4K camera captures crisp, vibrant images and videos—perfect for everyday moments, from sweeping landscapes to close-up , all without the hassle.

Creative Capture: Time-Lapse & Slow Motion

Zoom in to 0.01mm, slow down the rapid movements of insects, or witness the flowers bloom in time-lapse. With its flexible, wrap-around mount, you can secure it to branches, poles, desks—almost anywhere—for hands-free creativity，Every frame tells a new story

Simple 180°Twist-to-Switch Lens: From Visible to Unseen 

Most cameras force you to carry multiple lenses or compromise on magnification. Traditional microscopes demand fragile setups and never leave the lab. MicroCam X1 solves these with a twist: switch instantly between micro, macro, and standard views. You get extreme magnification, crisp close ups, and full resolution video/photos—affordable, durable, and ultra portable.

View Big, See More

Easily connect MicroCam X1 to your computer or larger display via USB for a full-screen, real-time view of what you’re exploring. Perfect for classroom demos, content creation, or families sharing  in ultra detail—no extra software or setup needed.

Compact, Tough, and Ready to Explore 

Weighing just 160 grams, MicroCam X1 is ultra-portable and built to last. Its shockproof, durable body stands up to real-world adventures, while expandable storage up to 512GB ensures you never miss a moment—no matter how far you roam.

Powered by 4K Imaging and Precision Optics

Microcam X1's optics blend precision glass with digital zoom for 10×–400× magnification, and its Sony 4K sensor locks in every nuance with true 4K video and 12 MP photos. Together, they transform hidden worlds into crystal-clear discoveries, revealing textures and patterns you’d never see otherwise.

So Simple, Even Kids Can Use It

Switch between views in just one second with a simple twist—no complicated menus, no bulky lenses. The bright 3.5" IPS display (264 ppi) offers instant visual feedback, making it easy for anyone even for kids to explore—no professional skills or technical knowledge needed.

Vivid 3.5″ Display for Instant Clarity

See your discoveries come to life on a sharp 3.5″ IPS screen with 264 ppi resolution. The high pixel density delivers rich color, crisp detail, and real-time visual feedback. Perfect for capturing reviewing, and sharing every moment.

Long-lasting design

Our MicroCam X1 is built with high-quality ABS+PC composite materials for the body, ensuring durability and a long lifespan. This design helps reduce waste and minimizes the environmental impact of our products over time.

Reusability and recyclability

We’ve invested considerable effort into developing packaging that is 100% recyclable. We replaced traditional polymeric foam with an organic material derived from cellulose, which offers the same protective qualities but is fully recyclable, reducing waste.

Sustainable materials

The MicroCam X1 lenses are made from high-strength aluminum alloy, and the high-quality glass used is also 100% recyclable. Both materials ensure the product’s durability while contributing to environmental sustainability.
