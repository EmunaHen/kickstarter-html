Pan Tilt Chip

The Pan Tilt chip is an optional add-on circuitry inside of the Roll Cam hardware. It allows you to use the app to remotely pan and tilt your camera, independently from the motion of Roll Cam. This means your camera can pan/tilt in one direction, while Roll Cam moves in a different direction.Â 

This is great for giving your videos a smooth dramatic and cinematic parallax effect to appear more creative. It works with the Bescor motorized pan/tilt head (not included). Having the independent pan/tilt capability also has many other creative uses too.

The Idea!

We got the idea for Roll Cam when we noticed that the face tracking gimbals on the market were stationary and fixed to a tripod. They only allowed you to move around within the radius of the tripod.Â 

So we got the idea to create Roll Cam, a device that can track and follow the user as they walk from place to place, giving them more freedom than fixed face tracking Gimbals. We couldn't find any device like it on the market, so we started designing it right away.

Optional Add-On Accessories

Extra Re-chargeable Batteries Add-On

Roll Cam uses a dual (5V/12V) 18000mAh (combined) re-chargeable / removable / replaceable battery, basically two batteries in one to power the electronics, motors, and servos. Ordering extra batteries will ensure you are able to quickly swap batteries to keep Roll Cam rolling for a long time between charges. Roll Cam already includes one battery and charger, getting another one (charger included) is recommended to keep you rolling without delays.

Roll Clamp Add-On

Designed to work with Roll Cam, it helps keep your cameras, batteries, and Roll Cam itself attached to your tripod. Its very handy to have, and can be used not only with Roll Cam, but for other uses too. Getting at least 2 is recommended.

Pan/Tilt Chip Add-On

Adding the Pan/Tilt chip option to the Roll Cam controller allows you to plug in a Bescor MP101/360 motorized pan/tilt head into Roll cam. This allows you to remotely pan or tilt your camera while Roll Cam is being operated. This gives you a lot more flexibility to make more creative shots, where Roll Cam is moving in one direction while the camera is panning in a different direction, like a parallax effect.

With this chip added, all you need to do is plug in your Bescor Motorized head directly into Roll Cam controller, then control it remotely via the app. Bescor Pan/tilt head itself is not included. This chip is an added capability to the Roll Cam controller.
