Story

🎉 Introducing Axmac – the revolutionary 8-key macro dock that’s more than just a keyboard. It’s your portable automation center, smart control panel, and real-time system monitor. Whether you’re a streamer, developer, designer, or gamer — Axmac puts power at your fingertips.

Modern digital workflows are full of distractions and repetitive steps. Switching apps, launching tools, checking system performance — they slow you down and break your flow.

We wanted something more powerful, more modular, and truly cross-platform.

1️⃣USB Side Display – Real-Time System Monitor

Axmac A1 can be connected to a computer as an independent secondary screen to display CPU, GPU, memory usage, temperature, and other system monitoring data in real time. No need to install drivers or additional software, plug and play, easily control the system operation status.

2️⃣One-Click Actions – Customize Your Workflow

Customizable button functions enable one-click app launch, folder opening, automatic account/secret entry, web page loading, and even multi-action combination tasks. Compared to traditional macro keyboards, Axmac A1 offers greater flexibility and freedom, allowing you to tailor every button to your unique workflow.

•💻 Run terminal commands, scripts, or batch files

3️⃣Virtual Input Sequences – Full Mouse & Keyboard Emulation

It can simulate complex sequences such as clicking, dragging, typing, key combinations, delayed operations, etc., and is suitable for automated testing, script execution, repetitive work or game operations.

4️⃣Programmable Macros – 40 Keys, Driver-Free, Cross-Platform

Axmac features a built-in ESP32-S3 chip with storage support, allowing you to create, save, and run advanced macro scripts without the need for external software or drivers. Configure it once, and it works across any Windows, macOS, or Linux system.

You can customize up to 8 keys per page, and store 5 profile pages — totaling 40 programmable macros that can include hotkeys, text strings, mouse movements, command sequences, or even complete workflow automations.
This makes Axmac an ideal tool for developers, automation engineers, power users, and anyone who wants full control over repetitive tasks or complex operations.

5️⃣Dual Display Expansion – Boost Your Visual Workspace

Axmac supports HDMI + DP dual video output interfaces, can connect two monitors at the same time, and supports up to 8K/30Hz resolution, allowing you to easily expand your workspace and perform multi-tasking, streaming monitoring, editing and arrangement, and other scenarios.
