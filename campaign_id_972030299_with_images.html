Story

CEAD Marketplace – A Smarter Way to Buy & Sell Locally 
We're building a local marketplace that makes buying and selling second-hand and retail items faster, safer, and cheaper. With integrated delivery and secure payments, CEAD helps people save money, support local businesses, and avoid risky meetups or arranging shipping and pickup options. Whether you're a student, a family, or a local shop - CEAD is here to simplify your local commerce experience.

CEAD Marketplace is a local platform designed to solve real problems in everyday buying and selling across Ireland.

Too many people face high delivery costs, meet strangers in unsafe places, or get stuck with unreliable second-hand platforms. Local shops struggle to bring their goods online affordably. We're changing that.

With CEAD Marketplace, users can:

Buy and sell locally with integrated door-to-door delivery with no risky pickups.
Pay securely - sellers receive payment only after delivery is confirmed.
Support local businesses with easy-to-use tools and fair pricing.
Save money and reduce waste through sustainable reuse.

We’ve already built a working MVP and begun talks with Irish partner companies. Now, we need your help to bring CEAD to life - from app development and marketing to building a strong local community.

Your pledge supports not just an app, but a mission to create safer, more sustainable, and more affordable commerce for Ireland and beyond.

Risks and challenges

1. Development Delays: App development and integration with payment and delivery systems might take longer than expected. We’re committed to maintaining transparency with our backers and have contingency plans in place to tackle potential roadblocks. 2. Market Adoption: While we believe there’s significant demand for local, sustainable commerce in Ireland, market adoption might take time. We plan to mitigate this risk with targeted marketing campaigns and strategic partnerships with local retailers and delivery companies. 3. Competition: The online marketplace space is competitive. We plan to stand out through our unique focus on local, sustainable selling and delivery, along with our secure payment system. We will continuously improve our platform based on user feedback to ensure it meets the needs of our customers. 4. Funding Requirements: We are raising funds to complete the necessary development and marketing for the platform. If we do not meet our funding goal, we will reassess and explore other options such as loans, grants, or further investor support. We’re fully committed to overcoming these challenges and delivering a platform that will make local buying and selling easier, safer, and more affordable for everyone.

Environmental commitments

Visit our Environmental Resources Center to learn how Kickstarter encourages sustainable practices.

Long-lasting design

Promote durable, high-quality, and second-hand products to extend their lifespan. Offer repair tips and resources to keep products in use longer. Educate users on sustainability with guides on care and maintenance. Ensure regular platform updates for a smooth, long-lasting user experience.

Reusability and recyclability

Encourage the use of recyclable and biodegradable packaging for all transactions. Promote reusable packaging options to reduce single-use waste. Partner with local eco-friendly delivery services that use sustainable materials. Support second-hand sales to extend product life and reduce the need for new production. Provide customers with options for returning packaging for reuse or recycling.

Sustainable Distribution

Promoting second-hand goods to reduce the need for new manufacturing, lowering overall resource consumption and waste. Encouraging local transactions to minimize the environmental impact of long-distance shipping. Packaging made from recycled or biodegradable materials, keeping waste to a minimum. Partnering with eco-conscious delivery services that prioritize low-emission or electric vehicles for local deliveries.

Something else

Digital-First Approach: Minimizing paper usage by focusing on digital transactions and receipts, which reduces paper waste. Local Partnerships: Collaborating with local businesses to reduce transportation emissions and encourage sustainable practices across our supply chain. Sustainability Awareness: Educating our customers on the benefits of buying second-hand and the impact of sustainable shopping choices.
