Story

A starter is precious to bakers, but it takes a lot of care.
SourFriend is the smart baker’s buddy that looks after your starter for you.
From beginners to pros, anyone can grow their starter easily—without worry or stress.

From feeding to fermenting, scheduling to storing—this is the endgame of starter care!

SourFriend has been performance-tested and PoC-tested by KPDSC (Korea Product Development Support Center)—a government-backed organization in South Korea with a proven track record of supporting award-winning hardware startups. Their rigorous validation process ensures that our product meets high standards for both engineering precision and user experience.

Following successful PoC testing, we are now transitioning to pilot production with trusted partners in Korea. All of our products are planned to be manufactured domestically, leveraging the country’s world-class manufacturing infrastructure, skilled workforce, and globally recognized quality control systems. From precision electronics to durable housing, every element of SourFriend is designed to deliver a reliable, high-performance experience you can trust.

We believe that everyone should have access to great sourdough.
The process of making sourdough shouldn't be difficult. This journey began with a simple hope: that anyone could easily make and enjoy healthy sourdough bread at home.

Our first product, Sourfriend, simplifies and automates the sourdough fermentation and maturation process. This idea was met with excitement from the baking community — from seasoned professional bakers to beginners just starting their home baking journey. The ability to create great sourdough easily, regardless of skill level, was something everyone welcomed.

Thanks to the incredible support, we were able to develop and complete a working prototype. Our engineers and designers worked tirelessly to bring this product to life — one that combines innovative features with beautiful design. And now, we’re ready to introduce Sourfriend to the world through Kickstarter.

Creating a smart kitchen device with IoT functionality is no small feat. But we’re proud of what we’ve built, and now we need the help of the Kickstarter community to bring it to completion and mass production. There’s still work to be done — PCB production, mold tooling, software refinement, certification, and final manufacturing — but much of it is already in progress.

We believe Kickstarter is more than just a funding platform. We’re also looking forward to your feedback, ideas, and insights. The best suggestions will be considered for implementation in the final product.

We need your support and encouragement to take this next step. With your help, we’ll bring truly excellent sourdough into more homes — so that everyone can enjoy fresh, delicious bread made with love.

Thank you.

Risks and challenges

Toasterz has developed a working prototype and holds two core patents: one for our integrated cold fermentation chamber, and another for our fermentation prediction and management algorithm via app connectivity. While we’ve laid a strong foundation and begun collaborating with manufacturing partners, potential delays related to supply chains or certifications may occur. We’re committed to full transparency and will keep you informed every step of the way. Your support helps bring Sourfriend to life—with the quality and care it deserves.

Long-lasting design

We've designed our product to remain timeless and functional for at least five years. The aesthetic is intentionally minimal and kitchen-friendly, allowing it to blend into various environments without feeling outdated. As long as the device remains operational, there's no need to replace it — ensuring long-term usability and reduced waste.

Environmentally friendly factories

As part of a government-supported initiative in Korea, Toasterz produces only the necessary quantity of products based on actual demand. We do not mass-produce or hold excess inventory, which helps us avoid wasteful overstocking. This made-to-order approach allows us to reduce environmental impact while maintaining responsible manufacturing practices.
