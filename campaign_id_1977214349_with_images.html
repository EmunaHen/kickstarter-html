Story

Spray Gun Y02. equipped with laser-guided precision—redefining professional painting standards once and for all!

The laser system marks the spraying area boundaries in real time, eliminating errors from manual visual estimation.

Uneven spray painting? Always worried about going too thick or too thin? Introducing the Laser-Focus Electric Paint Sprayer – a global first with dual laser alignment tech! Every stroke delivers pro-level precision!

The laser distance measurement module continuously monitors the distance between the spray gun and the surface, ensuring optimal spraying distance to prevent uneven coating or sagging caused by improper distance. It also reduces overspray, saving 20%-30% on paint consumption.

Y02 features Rapid-Dry Technology, delivering faster curing for efficient creativity. Perfectly balancing speed and quality, every stroke is precise and smooth—keeping your inspiration flowing effortlessly!
