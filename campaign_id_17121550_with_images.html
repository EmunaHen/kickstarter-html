Story

Imagine a world where building, running, and visualizing your tabletop roleplaying game campaign is as easy as opening an app. Loomlite is here to make that dream a reality—a powerful, intuitive app designed to bring your TTRPG adventures to life, whether you’re at the table or on the go.

Loomlite is more than just another tool for Game Masters. It’s a complete campaign management suite that helps you:

  • Design campaigns from scratch or using templates.
  • Keep your notes organized with a simple and easy-to-use note system which you can tie to your campaign, players, and each specific session.
  • Create characters and NPCs with rich profiles and dynamic relationships.
  • Build custom items and manage inventories. Let your players craft their own items through our own innovative item crafting system.
  • Customize every hex of your maps and visualize your world like never before.
  • Design and run encounters with integrated initiative tracking and encounter flow.
  • Keep track of your story. Visualize your campaign as a flowchart, making it easy to track branching storylines, player decisions, and plot twists.

At Loomlite, our aim is to provide high-quality tooling and features for every GM and players need. Tired of another note-taking system for campaign management? We're so much more!

Keep track of your campaign: Take notes, not just for your campaign, but for each individual session too. Add notes to specific quests and locations, and query for later in case you need it!

Deep dive into your campaign setting: With Loomlite, you don't have to just write a campaign. You can design worlds, countries, cities. Go into as much detail as you want! Then create and assign your campaign. Tie your quests directly to specific landmarks and locations, so you are never scrambling to figure out where to go next.

Never forget another session: Tired of always forgetting when your next session is? Or trying to find a time that works for everyone? Use our calendar system to help co-ordinate your entire group!

Tired of the plain old encounter system? Loomlite's encounter system is designed to be simple to use, modular based on the RPG System, and easy to run and modify. Rolling initiative and choosing monsters are the bare minimum; get recommendations on which monsters to choose based on biome and environment! Easily scale your encounter difficulty with just a few settings. At our advanced tier, we can even suggest the more personalized encounters for your campaign and party

Never scramble to find a roll table again: Setup and save your roll tables with ease. You can set what dice to use, what the results are, and link the table across your campaign

Don't just look at a map, interact with it: We don't want to be a VTT; let's make that clear. We do however, want to improve how maps are handled. Don't just look at a map, set up your encounters, NPC's, loot, etc. right on the map itself. Click on a grid and view what that grid contains! You can even link your roll tables straight onto the grid so you can easily roll your encounters without having to switch screens.*

*Our free tier will only allow roll tables and NPC's. Our Pro tier will have access all described features. 

Keep your items organized: With Loomlite, you can create items that are as varied and diverse as your imagination. You can define your own custom attributes and effects. Organize these items by type, effects, collection; and assign them to your players and NPC's.

We've gone one step further, allowing you to take advantage of your items by assigning them to dedicated shops, letting your players interact with your world without you having to remember and explain every detail (unless you want to that is!). You can also create randomized treasure chests for your players to find!

We also have a slew of other features planned that don't have screenshots or UI/UX for yet. This includes:

  • Item Forging: Let your players forge items using recipes and ingredients! Use our own forging system, or design your own and watch your players brew up trouble
  • Story Flow: Don't just read your campaign notes, visualize your campaign and your player's progress through it with an interconnected flow chart. Create and edit your campaign with a new perspective
  • Backstory Generation: Tired of trying to come up with an interesting backstory? We've done the hard work for you! Choose settings for your character, and watch as we write a backstory for you from the 100+ of backstories painstakingly written by our resident DMs. We plan to update our list regularly as well, adding even more potential options.

Be the master of your campaign!

Not a GM or running your own game? No problem! Loomlite is also meant for players! Create and manage your characters with our easy-to-use tools. Interact with your GM's campaign and world at your own pace. You can suggest actions, write notes, and store your character assets on our system.

Loomlite is built for both online and offline users, with plans to integrate with major Virtual Tabletop (VTT) platforms like Foundry. This means you can seamlessly move between Loomlite and your favorite VTT, streamlining your workflow and making game night smoother than ever.

Use of AI

Loomlite includes opt-in AI tools designed to help inspire creativity and get past writer’s block when it may occur. All features laid out below require a direct opt-in and are 100% optional within our site. The following features have optional AI attached: - Name Suggestions - Item Creation Ideas - Plot Point Prompts - Roll Table Generation - Quick Loot Chests Our current plan is to use the Perplexity Sonar models for these features, which can work with an offline mode that doesn't crawl through the internet. If these plans change, we will notify users before any changes occur so that user's can make conscious and ethical choices. WE WILL NOT be using AI-generated images, videos or large AI-written creative content in our site, Kickstarter, or in any content that is directly published or distributed by Loomlite. Every image/video you see that is published by us is directly created or commissioned by us, or officially licensed for commercial use from artists.
