Why THA3?

・55 m/s Wind Speed 

Tired of weak blowers?
Meet THA3 — the ultimate cordless air blaster delivering a staggering 55 m/s (124 mph) wind speed, up to 3× more powerful than standard blowers. Clear leaves, dust, dirt, and even standing water from patios, driveways, garages, and pools in seconds — all with zero hassle.

Built for serious performance, THA3 features an advanced aerodynamic turbine system that delivers massive airflow without the ear-splitting noise. Its weather-resistant, ergonomic design ensures a comfortable grip and reliable use, rain or shine. Whether you're cleaning up after a storm or prepping for a barbecue, StormForce gets it done — fast.

・Infinite gear adjustment

In stepless adjustment mode, the THA3 delivers air pressure ranging from 30 to 830 grams. With one-touch turbo mode, it can reach up to approximately 1400 grams of pressure—far beyond what ordinary blowers can achieve.

・Instant Max Power

In addition to stepless speed control, we know sometimes you need full power — fast. That’s why we added Turbo Mode. Just one press and you go straight from 0 to 55 m/s (12,992 ft/min) in an instant. No ramp-up. No wait. Just wind.

・Real-Time Wind Speed Display

Stay in control with a built-in display that shows your current wind speed in real time. Whether you're fine-tuning airflow or pushing max power, you'll always know exactly how much force you're working with.

・Fast and Flexible Charging

THA3 supports both USB-C and DC 30W fast charging, powering up the 4000mAh battery quickly and efficiently. With fast charge technology, you can get a full recharge in as little as 2.5 hours, minimizing downtime and keeping you ready for action.

Flexible Power for Every Need:

  • Stepless Speed Adjustment: Easily tailor airflow from gentle to powerful for any job.
  • Extended Runtime: Get up to 80 minutes on low settings or about 6 minutes of intense Turbo mode on a single charge — perfect for every task.

・Power On the Go

Equipped with 30W power output, this device doubles as a reliable power bank to charge your phone or other gadgets anywhere. Stay connected and powered up whether you’re working, traveling, or exploring the outdoors.

・Built-in Light for Precision in the Dark

The THA3 is also equipped with a built-in light, allowing you to aim and operate the blower accurately even in low-light conditions.
Turning on the light is simple—just a single press is all it takes.

・Rugged & Weather-Ready

Built for real-world use, the TH64A3 combines durable ABS and silicone materials to withstand tough environments and everyday wear. The body features a sleek metallic paint finish for added style and protection, while the silicone grip ensures a comfortable, non-slip hold — even in wet or rugged conditions.

・Cordless Freedom

Say goodbye to tangled cords and limited range. The TH64A3 is completely cordless, giving you the freedom to operate it anytime, anywhere — whether you're cleaning the garage, drying gear at a campsite, or clearing debris in the backyard. With no cables to hold you back, you're free to move, work, and explore without limits.

・All-in-One Tool 

From drying camping gear and cleaning delicate electronics to fanning your BBQ flames or clearing workshop dust, the THA3 handles it all. Compact yet powerful, it’s the perfect companion for outdoor adventures, home projects, emergency kits, and everyday use. One tool, countless uses — always ready when you are.
