💡Meet SolarioX

NEXT-GEN Solar-Powered Power Bank for Your Every Adventure⬇️

⚡️Power Through Every Adventure

Massive 42,000mAh/155.4Wh Capacity

With a 42,000mAh / 155.4Wh battery, SolarioX is built to support all your essential devices—phones, tablets, cameras, drones, laptops, Nintendo Switch, Steam Deck, and more. 

Its high-capacity lithium battery is optimized for energy efficiency and stability, delivering sustained power across multiple devices while remaining portable. 

☀️Solar Tech Integration

Integrated ETFE Mono Solar Panel  

Unlike traditional PET or polycrystalline panels, SolarioX features an integrated ETFE-coated monocrystalline solar panel, offering a higher efficiency and longer durability. 

💡ETFE (Ethylene Tetrafluoroethylene) allows more sunlight to penetrate the solar cells, resulting in conversion efficiencies of up to 22%-24%, outperforming standard panels that typically range between 15–18%. 

With Integrated ETFE Mono Solar Panel, the features of self-cleaning, weather resistance, and high durability make SolarioX ideal for rugged environments. 

🔌Multi-Device Powerhouse 

Charge 6 Devices at Once

✨With intelligent power distribution, SolarioX balances output across multiple devices,delivering maximum efficiency and safety.

✅ 140W USB-C PD 3.1 - Powers large laptops like the 16" MacBook Pro or Dell XPS.

✅100W USB-C - Ideal for Phones, DJI drone, or more.

✅ Two 22.5W USB-A - SuperVOOC/Huawei SCP supported.

✅ DC-UPS Port - powering routers, LED lights, DSLR batteries, mini fridges, and other voltage-sensitive equipment.

✅ 15W Magnetic Wireless Dock - auto-aligns for perfect contact.

🛠️Versatile for DIY and Pro Gear

Adjustable DC-UPS Output (5V-28V)

DC-UPS port with output from 5V to 28V, makes SolarioX ideal for powering LED lights, DSLR batteries, mini fridges, and other voltage-sensitive equipment.

UPS Smart System provides smart voltage regulation, management, and short-circuit protection.

🌍Power for Every Scenario

Your Emergency Power Lifeline

📍Stay charged at home, on the road, or completely off-grid, designed to meet the demands of modern life and outdoor exploration.

In the event of a power outage, SolarioX can ensure you stay connected with loved ones and emergency services, making it an essential part of your emergency preparedness kit.

💨Performance Without Overheating

Built-in Active Cooling System 

⚠️⚠️Power banks with large capacities often struggle with heat management, especially during fast charging. 

📍SolarioX solves this with a Built-in Cooling Fan that activates during high-power operations to prevent overheating and maintain optimal performance. 

Whether you’re running multiple devices simultaneously or using high-wattage outputs, the internal fan keeps temperatures in check, extending the lifespan of both the battery and your devices. 

📊Real-Time Charging Insights 

Track Stats with a Full-color TFT Display

SolarioX features a TFT display that provides real-time charging stats, including wattage output per port, input levels, temperature, battery status, and more. 

The easy-to-read interface gives you full control and visibility, empowering you to optimize your power usage and power management.

🎒Adventure-Ready Portability

916g – Fits in Your Bag

SolarioX weighs just 916 grams —making it one of the lightest high-capacity solar power banks on the market. 

With a backpack-friendly design, it's portable enough for everyday carry and powerful enough for adventures. You can take it on a flight, a hike, or a road trip.
