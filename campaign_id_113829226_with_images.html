🧠 So… what are binaural beats?

Great question! Binaural beats aren’t a playlist or a music genre—they’re an auditory illusion your brain creates when it hears two slightly different frequencies, one in each ear. That difference becomes a third tone, called a binaural beat, and this magical mental remix helps shift your brain into more restful states.

For example:
👂 Left ear hears 300 Hz
👂 Right ear hears 310 Hz
🧠 Brain goes: “Cool, let’s vibe at 10 Hz” — and that’s the binaural beat.

Science has shown that specific frequencies (like 2–8 Hz) can help promote deep sleep, reduce anxiety, and even make waking up a little less painful. It's like hacking your brain... gently.
