Story

Imagine a world where your AI, machine learning, robotics, home labs and edge computing projects run seamlessly, without limitations, without compromise. A world where high performance computing meets efficiency in a single, compact solution.

Meet Cerebro. A clusterboard created for innovators, builders and visionaries like you. Designed to fit effortlessly in a micro ATX case or a sleek 1U server chassis, Cerebro unifies what others fragment, bringing raw power, intelligent design and limitless scalability right within your reach. 

Stop struggling with scattered components, inefficient setups or space-consuming alternatives. Choose a single solution that supports up to 4x NVIDIA Jetson NX modules, alongside Raspberry Pi CM4/CM5 and Radxa CM5 compatibility.

Cerebro isn’t just a motherboard. It’s the missing piece in your ultimate setup. The tool that will accelerate your projects, refine your workflow, and push your innovations further than ever before.

It’s time to supercharge your potential. It’s time to introduce you properly to Cerebro.

Development boards on today’s market didn’t fully meet our needs. When we set out to build our own AI and cluster computing setup, we quickly ran into frustrating limitations. The available development boards were powerful, but they weren’t designed to work together seamlessly. We needed multiple carrier boards, separate networking solutions, and additional peripherals just to get a functional system running. The result? A cluttered, inefficient setup with too many cables, too much power consumption, and constant compatibility headaches.

That’s why we engineered Cerebro. It’s the board we wished we had from the start. With support for up to four Jetson NX, Raspberry PI CMx, Radxa CM5 modules, it eliminates the need for multiple separate carrier boards, reducing complexity while maximizing power. Its M.2 expansion slots give you the flexibility to add WiFi, USB, Bluetooth, NPUs, and more, without worrying about compatibility.

But most importantly: Cerebro integrates seamlessly with NVIDIA Jetson developer kits, so you can focus on building, experimenting, and innovating, without wasting time troubleshooting hardware conflicts.

We came up with Cerebro because we needed it. Now, we’re bringing it to you!

We’ve named our product “Cerebro”, a subtle nod to pop culture. In the comics, Professor Xavier used a device by that name to locate others like him. Cerebro also means brain, and this links neatly to the many use cases this board can occupy. So we thought the name Cerebro completely fits the bill.

Building a system with Cerebro’s capabilities using off-the-shelf components would cost you around €850,- to €905,- and that’s before factoring in the space, power, and setup time needed to make everything work together.

With Cerebro, you get seamless integration, high-performance scalability, and clean, compact efficiency all for up to €450 less than piecing it together yourself.

More power. More flexibility. No headaches.
