Story

When size goes down, performance goes up

Say goodbye to bulky, clunky air pumps that rattle in your trunk like unwanted passengers. The aigo Mini Inflator is a palm-sized powerhouse engineered to inflate everything from bike tires to car wheels — without weighing you down. Compact, quick, and cleverly designed, it’s your pocket-friendly pit crew for the road, the trail, or anywhere pressure calls.

Unmatched Portability, 50% Smaller Size

Thanks to advanced structural design, the aigo Mini Inflator shrinks the traditional pump size by 50% without sacrificing efficiency. Lightweight and ultra-compact, it becomes the perfect addition to your riding gear — more power, less burden

4500mAh High-Capacity Battery

Equipped with a high-performance 4500mAh lithium polymer battery, the aigo Mini can fully inflate up to four R16 car tires on a single charge. Whether on the road or trail, reliable power is always within reach

🔧 Built Like a Tank — Military-Grade Battery Meets Beastly Motor

Under the hood, the aigo Mini is powered by three military-grade 14500 lithium cells, delivering explosive energy reserves that put ordinary pumps to shame. Paired with a 19,000 RPM pure copper permanent magnet motor, this compact inflator unleashes serious power for high-pressure tasks. Whether you’re topping off a tire or reviving a flat in the wild, you’ll feel the torque — and the confidence — of industrial-grade engineering in the palm of your han

Maximum Pressure up to 150 PSI

Delivering a maximum pressure of 150 PSI (approximately 10 bar), the aigo Mini makes inflating car tires, motorcycles, and bicycles effortless. Tackle high-pressure needs with confidence and ease

Go Big, Pump Longer

Equipped with a 4500mAh lithium battery and a powerful 150 PSI output, the aigo Mini handles serious inflation with ease. Inflate 3 full 195 car tires, 8 high-pressure 700C road bike tires, or over 100 basketballs from flat to 8 PSI — all on a single charge. Wherever you go, it’s ready to deliver, again and again.

Dual Valve Compatibility – Schrader and Presta

Featuring a built-in dual valve system compatible with both Schrader (American) and Presta (French) valves, the aigo Mini eliminates the hassle of frequent nozzle changes. Seamless switching saves time and effort.

Built-in High-Brightness Light and SOS Mode

The integrated LED provides bright illumination for nighttime emergencies. With an SOS flashing mode, you gain extra safety and visibility when you need it most.

Multiple Preset Modes for Safer, Smarter Inflation

Select from various preset modes tailored for bicycles, motorcycles, cars, and balls. Fine-tune the pressure with micro-adjustments and rest assured — the pump automatically stops once the target pressure is reached.

Type-C Fast Charging, 3 Hours to Full Power

Recharge quickly and safely using the Type-C port with a 5V/2A adapter. Fully charges in about three hours, ensuring you're always ready for your next journey.

Low Noise, Powerful Performance

Despite its compact size, the aigo Mini runs with noise levels under 80dB at one meter. Enjoy a more comfortable and less disruptive inflation experience — perfect for quiet environments.
