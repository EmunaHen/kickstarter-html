Story

Why ZzzRing

ZzzRing is the world's first solar-powered smart ring that solves battery life challenges through its innovative technology. Combining an advanced chip and 6-axis sensors, it delivers precise health tracking, gesture-controlled photography and video switching, and one-tap ChatGPT activation - all without subscription fees. This revolutionary device redefines wearable technology.

At the same time, ZzzRing is also committed to inspiring everyone to form good health habits and pledges to use 30% of its proceeds to incentivize its users.

The smart ring utilizes high-efficiency flexible perovskite solar cells for power supply, combined with low-power design, intelligent power management. Supporting both indoor and outdoor light charging, it reduces charging frequency, enhances wearing experience.Say goodbye to battery anxiety and never miss a data point - even when you forget to charge.

ZzzRing sets up two incentives:

  • Sleeping incentive
  • Exercise Incentive

Users community to initiate any challenge, if you win the challenge or adhere to the clock, then will be awarded ZzzRing tokens, tokens can be used for the exchange of the actual goods or cash rewards.

After solving battery life challenges, ZzzRing utilizes the high-efficiency Apollo chip to achieve a sleep monitoring frequency twice as high as other smart rings.

With its advanced 6-axis sensor, ZZZRing responds to your gestures instantly - snap photos mid-air with just a wave.

Hands full while scrolling? ZzzRing lets you swipe videos with a wave—hands-free freedom!

ZzzRing monitors your daily stress levels through heart rate analysis, and provides personalized mood reports and improvement tips powered by AI—nurturing your emotional well-being

ZzzRing also provides personalized mood reports and improvement recommendations powered by artificial intelligence - for your emotional health!

Whether you are sleeping or exercising ZzzRing is able to track your health data throughout the day under any conditions , whether you are running or riding ZzzRing will summarize your exercise data every week and every month, so that you can see the results of your exercise at a glance!

ZzzRing accurately recognizes the changes in body temperature at different stages through multiple temperature sensors to identify the time of the onset of menstrual period, and once abnormalities occur, ZzzRing can give feedback to the data in the first place.

ZZZRing monitors key health metrics including heart rate (HR), heart rate variability (HRV), and blood oxygen levels (SpO₂), then uses AI to generate regular cardiac health reports from this data.

Whether it's sleep, exercise and stress tracking is to make us more aware of our health status, ZZZRing will give you a regular summary of your health status based on all the data aggregated and provide you with guidance, so that you can feel more at ease and live healthier!

You can't imagine that ZzzRing can surround your life so comprehensively, that is, now ZzzRing in the case of thin and light design NFC function, even if you can't spare your hands to still be able to unlock the door of your home through ZZZRing.

Gently touching each other's rings can send your messages face-to-face to new friends, and all these functions that require multiple products to be realized can be brought together in the ZZZRing!

Now you can touch ZZZRing to invoke ChatGPT and keep AI waiting for service!

About Us

As an industrial designer with 8 years of experience who is also a smart wearables enthusiast, I found that both smartwatches and smart rings have battery life problems, and they also often suffer from missing data because they forget to charge or the charger can't charge in time.
In order to solve this problem we designed ZzzRing's first smart ring that can be charged by solar energy, thus solving the problem of battery life and power outage of smart rings, and based on this, we added a lot of features to make life more convenient, so that every user can get more accurate health tracking and efficient life through ZzzRing.
In the future, we will keep updating and iterating as the demand increases, so let's unlock more possibilities of ZzzRing together!

Risks and challenges

As with any innovative product, ZzzRing may face unforeseen challenges during production and delivery. While we have extensive experience and strong manufacturing partners, potential delays or technical adjustments may arise. Additionally, global logistics can be unpredictable, but we are committed to working with reliable partners to ensure smooth fulfillment. We pledge to maintain transparent communication, providing regular updates and prompt solutions if any issues occur.
