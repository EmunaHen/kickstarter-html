🧭 What We're Creating

We’re about to build a cultural website — MAX Discover — to showcase original documentaries, photography, and travel resources about Tibet.

This site will feature:
🎥 Short documentary films we create, capturing authentic Tibetan life, beliefs, and landscapes
📸 Original photography, offered as downloadable wallpapers, postcards, and digital photobooks
🗣️ An online forum where supporters can connect, discuss, and share experiences
🌍 Custom travel services that directly benefit Tibetan communities

We’re already sharing our work across YouTube, Instagram, Facebook, and TikTok — but this website will bring it all together in one home, offering backers a richer, more permanent way to engage.

🏔 What Tibet Gives Us

Tibet — the Third Pole of the world!
With an average elevation of over 4,000 meters above sea level, it stands alongside the Arctic and Antarctic as one of Earth’s three extreme regions. Often called “the roof of the world” or “the summit of the Earth,” Tibet is a place of high altitude, low oxygen, and fragile ecosystems — known to scientists as a “High Pole.”

Those who come here are deeply moved by its unmatched beauty, down-to-earth people, sacred beliefs, dazzling culture, and truly unique way of life. Their souls are uplifted.

🫶 Why We Created MAX Discover

As a designer and a passionate admirer of Tibetan culture, I’ve traveled deep into Tibet many times — as an artist, explorer, and friend.

I’ve seen beauty beyond words… and also realities that few outsiders witness: poverty, isolation, cultural pressure. 
Tibetans often have no platform to share their voices or stories with the world.

So we asked:
What can we do? How can we help protect this endangered culture — and support the people who carry it?

Our social media presence was only the beginning. Now we’re building a lasting digital home for everything we create. The website will include:
💻 Technical infrastructure — a dedicated domain, branded email system, and high-performance servers to support media content and forum traffic

🎞️ Our original short documentaries, downloadable for supporters
🖼️ Photography collections — including wallpapers, postcards, and digital photobooks
🧭 Travel stories and cultural guides, curated by experts who truly know the land
📈 Regular updates, showing our impact and sharing voices from the Plateau

Your support will help us take this further — giving MAX Discover a permanent home, and Tibet a stronger voice.
