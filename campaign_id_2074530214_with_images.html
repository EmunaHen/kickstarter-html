Story

Tired of the hassle of constantly switching windows?
Struggling to find the perfect visual angle because of limited adjustability?

FaSuH 14.5-inch Portable Touchscreen Monitor — Redefining efficiency and immersion!
One screen, effortless control — Seamlessly switch between work and entertainment, unlocking your full productivity and creativity with just a touch!

Multitasking made easy, with multiple applications running simultaneously without the need to constantly switch between interfaces, ensuring a smooth workflow.

Optimize your workflow with smart schedules and task management, eliminating messy paper notes. Easily plan and organize your tasks for improved productivity.

Not only is the screen touchable, but the volume adjustment can also be controlled via touch, making your user experience more intuitive and convenient, enhancing smoothness and ease of operation.

The FaSuH monitor allows you to effortlessly drag tasks from your computer screen to the display, enabling precise and smooth window switching and content adjustment to enhance work efficiency.

Supports multiple gaming platforms including PS4, XBOX, SWITCH, PC, and TV BOX, allowing you to fully enjoy the immersive gaming experience on a large screen!

Ultra-low latency and smooth visual response ensure no lag or screen tearing during gameplay, delivering a flawless gaming experience.

Adjustable angles from 45° to 135°, ensuring comfort during long hours of use without fatigue. The kickstand requires no extra accessories, making it the ideal choice for work, entertainment, and creativity. Sturdy and durable, it provides secure support, freeing your hands for a better experience.

The FaSuH monitor supports easy switching between landscape and portrait modes. Landscape is ideal for multitasking, while portrait mode is better for reading long documents and coding. You can effortlessly adjust the display angle according to your work needs, ensuring a more efficient and comfortable work experience.

Easily connect from either side, making it perfect for different work environments without the need to flip or adjust the device. Simultaneously connect multiple devices to handle all your expansion needs, including external monitors, headphones, and power banks. With 3.5mm audio, dual Type-C, and Mini HDMI ports, it offers enhanced compatibility for audio, office tasks, and gaming.

Ultra-Wide Viewing Angle (ADS Technology) – Clear, vibrant visuals from any angle, perfect for multi-angle use.

Projection-like Display, Vibrant Colors – Higher brightness and precise color reproduction, ensuring visibility even in well-lit environments.

FaSuH boasts a 14.5-inch ultra-wide screen with a 32:9 aspect ratio, delivering a near dual-screen experience. The expansive display boosts multitasking efficiency and offers an immersive gaming experience with sharp details and precise control.

With a 2560×720 resolution, it delivers exquisite 2K quality, providing clear images and true-to-life colors, making the details come to life.

The 60Hz refresh rate ensures smooth and seamless screen transitions, reducing lag and stutter, enhancing the experience in office work, image editing, and gaming, making every action precise and accurate.

With support for 100% sRGB color gamut, it delivers precise color reproduction and vivid details. Whether for design, photography, or video editing, it ensures accurate color performance to meet professional needs.

With a 16mm ultra-thin body, slimmer than a pencil, and lightweight design, it’s easy to slip into your backpack for effortless travel and commuting.

Dual-Side Plug-and-Play Ports: Featuring Mini HDMI 2.0×1, two full-function Type-C×2, and a 3.5mm audio jack, supporting plug-and-play with PS4, Xbox, Switch, Android phones, PCs, and more.

One-Cable Compatibility: Directly connect Type-C 3.1 phones from brands like Samsung, Huawei, and Sony to instantly transform into a productivity workstation.
