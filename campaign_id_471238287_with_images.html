Story

Kenpio is a kid and adult friendly Sudoku console packed with over 12,000 puzzles across 3 grid modes (4x4, 6x6, 9x9) and 4 difficulty levels—perfect for minds to grow through play. Designed with a bright, adjustable screen and soft button feedback, it offers sound prompts, mistake hints, a timer, and a favorites feature so you can save and replay puzzles you love. 

With a compact, rechargeable design, Kenpio is ideal for screen-free entertainment on road trips, at home, or on the go. Whether you or your child is just starting out with logic games or ready to master advanced grids, Kenpio makes learning fun, portable, and rewarding. 

Say goodbye to paper waste and scattered game pieces. Kenpio delivers clean, digital fun—reusable,and eco-friendly.

Choose the play style that fits you best! You can tap a square first, then select a number, or pick a number first and place it where you want. Both ways are intuitive and responsive—designed for smooth, frustration-free gameplay.

Enjoy endless brain workouts with over 12000 built-in Sudoku puzzles! Whether you're a casual player or a logic master, Kenpio offers fresh challenges every day—no Wi-Fi needed, just pure fun at your fingertips.

Choose how you play: 4x4 for beginners, 6x6 for a step up, and 9x9 for classic Sudoku fans. Kenpio adapts to your skill level and grows with your progress—perfect for all ages.

From easy to expert, select your preferred challenge. Whether you're warming up or seeking a real brain teaser, Kenpio’s 4 difficulty levels make every session rewarding and fun.

Master your Sudoku experience with intuitive, easy-to-use controls. From the central game board to clearly labeled number keys, every button is designed for effortless play. Adjust brightness or volume, set a timer, or jump to your favorite puzzle in seconds. Easily switch grid modes, choose difficulty levels, or check answers—all with a simple tap.

Track your progress and challenge yourself with the built-in timer. Whether you're beating personal bests or practicing for speed, Kenpio helps keep your mind sharp and focused.

Made a wrong move? No problem—Kenpio instantly flashes the incorrect number, helping you learn and improve without breaking the flow. If it just stay still after your filling in a number, then congratulations! You got the right answer!

Get audio feedback as you play! Kenpio features subtle sound prompts to confirm inputs or alert you of errors, making gameplay smoother and more intuitive.

No need to worry about replacing batteries—Kenpio is fully rechargeable for endless play sessions. A single charge powers 6 hours of gameplay, making it perfect for travel, commutes, or relaxing at home. Just plug it in when needed and keep the fun going anytime, anywhere!

Found a puzzle you love or want to retry later? Kenpio lets you bookmark your favorite puzzles with a simple tap. Your personal puzzle library is just one click away—perfect for tracking tricky challenges or replaying the ones you enjoy most. Smart, convenient, and made for true Sudoku fans!

Play comfortably anywhere—day or night. With adjustable screen brightness, Kenpio protects your eyes and adapts to different lighting conditions so you can play longer, stress-free.

Sudoku isn’t just a solo challenge—it’s a chance to bond. Sit down with your child, solve puzzles together, and spark meaningful conversations while boosting logic and focus. Learning becomes play, and play becomes quality time.

Compact, stylish, and easy to carry—Kenpio fits in your bag or even your pocket. Take it on the go and enjoy Sudoku anytime, whether at home, on a plane, or during a coffee break.

Risks and challenges

We’ve spent months prototyping and testing Kenpio to ensure a smooth and enjoyable user experience. Our manufacturing partner has extensive experience in producing handheld electronic devices, and we’ve already finalized key components and suppliers to minimize production risk. However, as with any hardware project, there are potential challenges: Production Delays: Unexpected component shortages or supply chain disruptions may affect our delivery timeline. We’re working closely with our suppliers to build buffer time into our schedule. Quality Control: Ensuring consistent quality at scale is always a challenge. To address this, we’ve implemented multiple quality checks at each stage of production. Shipping & Logistics: Global shipping remains unpredictable due to changing regulations and regional customs procedures. We’ll keep backers informed of any updates and work to minimize delays. We’re committed to transparency and will provide regular updates throughout the process. With your support, we’re confident we can deliver Kenpio on time and at the quality you expect.
