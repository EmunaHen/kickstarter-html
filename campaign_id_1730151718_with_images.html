Story

Solderix 

Too many kids get sidelined by sharp tools and expensive and complex kits — and too many curious minds are turned away by tech that’s sealed, locked, or just too difficult to understand.

Hobby and maker culture are dying, as current tech is an expensive magic black box, difficult to disassemble or figure out how it works without breaking it.

Older generations learned by the natural process of exploration and discovery, and they became the masters of DIY culture and future engineers, problem solvers, or even astronauts!

That is why we have created Solderix!

Introducing Solderix, a fun, LEGO-like system made of real circuit boards that lets kids (and curious adults) build, explore, and truly understand electronics, robotics, and engineering.

Completely modular and reusable, you can use the same boards in many different projects that we will be releasing over time as we expand our offer.

Solderix is easy to use!

Use our custom metal stands to connect different boards quickly! Only a screwdriver is required.

Don't forget, when you join the boards using metal standoffs, you can always disassemble them and use them in a different project.

Solderix incentivises using fine motor skills, planning, and visualising in 3D space when assembling our models.

So, what are you getting with Solderix?


Zoom, our basic car kit made of several different boards like main board that has motor drivers and a place for our SolderBit main controller based on ESP32, or if you want, use it with Microbit if you have it, it is fully compatible with it.
You will get four motor adapter boards for N20 motors at 250 RPM, they are fast and agile enough to go outside or inside.
Wheels with rubber tires will ensure good traction on any surface.
Our simple joystick is made of 3 boards and has everything you need to drive Zoom and also comes with Solderbit, the same one as in the car, also, you can use Microbit with it too.

If you wish, you can add different modules as add-ons. In the future, we will have many more!

Zoom, our basic car kit.

Joystick SRT, our basic joystick powered by our SolderBit.

Joystick SRT is powered by 2 AAA batteries.

Our SolderBit is powered by powerful ESP-32 chip giving it good processing power, range, memory and it has USB-C for connecting to your computer and is fully compatible with Microbit connector. To enhance your experience, we are suggesting adding the Line follower module as an additional reward to your purchase.

Our Zoom Exploration Pack is a massive upgrade to the basic pack. It will give you the ability to mount a servo motor for better maneuvering, as well as mounting different sensors and even our Sideye camera for direct video streaming to our Pro Joystick and SolderBit Pro module with LCD screen.

Our big joystick and SolderBit Pro with screen and extra buttons.

Our big joystick has many more buttons, enabling more control over various projects (upcoming robotic hand), and is fully compatible with regular SolderBit.

Difference between SolderBit Pro and SolderBit.

Exploration expanded kit modules, forklift servo adapter, LCD module, or acoustic sensor for proximity, laser proximity for precision, servo addon, QWIIC expansion module, line follower, Lithium battery module supporting 18650 or our standard 2500 mAh battery pack and many more upcoming structural, mechanical and electronics modules.

Also, for those who want, Zoom can transform into a terrain truck with our custom shell, and more shells will be available as we develop them, all compatible with your Zoom.

Only thing left for you is to be creative and play!

It will all come in a box with instructions, online material, and our online visual programming tool called SolderBrix.

Risks and challenges

We have shipped over 70,000 products to our customers in our main line of business. With every new model, we nail the technological and manufacturing process, optimize the supply chain, simplify the building process for the user, making our assembly manuals better. We have applied all our experience and knowledge to make the Solderix platform and Zoom as cool and fun as possible. We have worked extremely hard to make this product ready for full-scale manufacturing and fulfillment, but here are the risks we see and what we plan to do about it: MANUFACTURING: We have dedicated and trusted suppliers and manufacturers for each part of the Solderix platform. And quality assurance people will be on-site during production. FULFILLMENT: Sometimes it gets a bit crazy and things don't go as planned: a supplier misses a deadline or a shipment gets lost. Those things can cause a delay. We are using an experienced logistics team to get our products to you, so we will do our best to tackle every issue. We’ll keep open and honest communication with you through our process so you know where your product is and when it’s going to get to you. Our logistics partners are reliable and after your product is shipped, you will receive a notice from them.
