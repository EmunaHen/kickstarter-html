Story

***DIGITAL REWARDS ONLY // no physical products will be shipped***

Welcome to my largest Kickstarter! With this campaign you will be able to recreate the battlefields of the American Civil War with up to 793 highly detailed STL miniatures! 

3D print a formidable army complete with infantry, cavalry, artillery and units such as Zouaves and the Iron Brigade.

All miniatures include pre-supported files and have been test printed with an ANYCUBIC Photon Mono 4K resin printer. These models are optimized for 6mm wargaming, but can comfortably be scaled up to 15mm without a loss in quality.

About Me

I am a graphic designer who dabbled in Blender 3D as a side hobby. Back in 2020 I discovered the world of 3D printing and was instantly hooked. I used the earnings from my very first Kickstarter campaign to buy a resin printer, and I’ve been sculpting and designing miniatures ever since. 

In particular my focus is on 6mm miniature wargaming. I love the visual of thousands of soldiers marching across battlefields while still fitting them all comfortably in a shoe box.

Don't forget to follow me on Instagram.

No Stretch Goals?—Here's Why

I believe in providing a finished product. Not rushing the process to meet a deadline. Instead of locking rewards such as the artillery, Zouaves and Iron Brigade behind stretch goals that may or may not unlock, I have included them from the start. What you see is what you get regardless of how many back the campaign.

Distribution

This campaign is for digital STL files only. No physical products will be shipped. All files will be distributed via MyMiniFactory using a redemption link. Be sure to check your email (not your kickstarter inbox).
