Story

WOOASK A9, a product that perfectly combines the functions of mobile phone, CHATGPT and translator ,translator earbuds, can be used independently without relying on the mobile phone. It can not only translate and listen to music, but also insert SIM card to make calls and share WIFI hotspot. Combined with CHATGPT, you can always use voice to ask all kinds of questions about attraction introduction, travel planning, recommended restaurants, hotels and other various issues, you will always have a virtual intelligent assistant.

WOOASK A9 is beautifully designed, compact and portable, it is the best choice for you to travel around the world and will bring you an unprecedented translation and travel experience!

What's more, the A9 doubles as regular TWS Bluetooth earbuds, seamlessly connecting to smartphones for everyday use. 

At Hongkong Global Sources exhibition on April 18th, 2025, Wooask CEO Andy Lee introduced the innovative features of the A9, showcasing the global appeal of Wooask's latest innovation.

During the exhibition, Visitors from all over the world were very interested in the A9. They learned about and experienced the A9 product. Many customers praised the A9 for not only its beautiful appearance but also its excellent translation experience. At the same time, it can insert a SIM card to make calls and share WIFI hotspot. It is a very good multi-functional smart terminal product and is very helpful for global travel,study and business meeting.

The A9 now offers enhanced flexibility with both Wi-Fi and 4G network support. You can seamlessly switch between Wi-Fi and 4G, depending on what's available, ensuring you're always connected. Whether you're in a remote area without Wi-Fi or traveling abroad, simply insert a SIM card to access the 4G network and stay online for translations, communication, and more. This dual connectivity option makes the A9 more adaptable and reliable, offering enhanced convenience and keeping you connected anytime, anywhere.

The A9's innovative touchscreen design distinguishes it from typical translator earbuds. Unlike other devices that require a smartphone connection, the A9’s intuitive touchscreen interface allows users to easily control the device and view translation results directly on the screen, eliminating the need for a smartphone app.

The A9's collaboration with ChatGPT takes intelligent voice interaction services to a new level. By tapping into ChatGPT's extensive knowledge base and natural language processing power, users can enjoy more than just translation help. Need recommendations for the best nearby restaurants? ChatGPT has you covered. Its ability to understand and respond to user queries transforms the A9 into a personal virtual assistant, significantly enhancing the travel and communication experience.

Wooask A9 takes communication to a new level by enabling voice calls and text messaging. You can now make and receive calls directly, as well as send and receive texts.

With the A9’s hotspot sharing feature, you can easily share your 4G connection with other devices. This allows you to keep your smartphone, tablet, or laptop connected, even in areas with limited internet access.

The A9 introduces a revolutionary feature with its integrated high-definition camera. Simply point the camera at text, signs, menus, or documents, and instantly receive accurate translations through the device.

Wooask A9 supports seamless real-time translation, allowing two people to engage in fluent multilingual conversation. Whether it's for business, travel, or everyday interactions, the A9 ensures that communication flows smoothly, with translations delivered instantly through the earbuds.

A9‘s lightning-fast online translation service offers extensive coverage across 144 languages and accents, ensuring seamless communication when connected to Wi-Fi or using mobile data.

The offline translation mode supports the 16 most widely spoken languages worldwide, allowing you to translate without the need for an internet connection.

Whether you’re having a private conversation or in a noisy environment, the A9’s translation modes have you covered. The in-ear mode provides discreet, seamless translations, while the speaker mode ensures that you can hear translations even in noisy environment.

At the heart of the A9 lies the Qualcomm quad-core chipset, which drives its translation capabilities, ensuring both speed and stability. With translation speeds of under 0.5 seconds and an impressive 98% accuracy rate, the A9 operates efficiently while maintaining low power consumption. This ensures uninterrupted performance, offering users a seamless and reliable translation experience.

The A9’s advanced dual microphone setup, paired with Environmental Noise Cancellation (ENC) technology, ensures crystal-clear translations, even in the loudest of environments. Stay focused and engaged in conversations without distractions, no matter where you are.

With a 40mAh battery in each earbud and a 1500mAh charging case, the A9 offers up to 5 hours of continuous translation. When combined with the charging case, you get an impressive 36 hours of total use. Plus, the A9 boasts a standby time of up to 30 days, ensuring you're always ready when you need it.

Enjoy effortless, instant pairing. Simply open the charging case, and the A9 earbuds will automatically connect to your device — no complicated setup needed. It's quick, seamless, and always ready for use.

The A9 is not just a translation device — it also lets you enjoy music and videos on the go. You can listen to music on Spotify, Melon, or import your own files, and watch videos in MP4 format.

Whether you're exploring the globe, attending business meetings, or just chatting with friends, the Wooask A9 is the ideal companion. It streamlines communication, making it smoother and easier than ever.

Use of AI

Our project does not raise money for AI tools, and we do not use content generated by AI. Our project uses AI in other ways, in the case of our product that allows users to ask questions by voice (for example, what's the best place to visit in New York?). The question is answered by the text content generated by CHATGPT, and then the text is sounded by speech synthesis technology (TTS) so that the user can hear the voice.
