About PoleShine

PoleShine is the ultimate palm-sized companion for any outdoor adventure. Housed in a precision-machined all-aluminum body, it delivers up to 1200 lumens of crisp white light that reaches 200m, while integrated Maximum 5mW red and green lasers extend your signaling range to 2 km. On the underside, a COB RGB ambient lamp offers red, blue, and white color modes to set the perfect campsite mood or serve as a discreet emergency light. Three tactile rear buttons keep all modes at your fingertips, and a built-in magnet on the base lets you free your hands by sticking PoleShine to any metal surface.
With IPX6 water resistance, 1.5 m drop-proof durability, and an internal 1,500 mAh battery, PoleShine is as tough as it is versatile—ready for hiking, camping, vehicle repairs, or any situation where reliable, multi-mode illumination matters.
Don’t let its size fool you—PoleShine measures just 75×45×20 mm, roughly the size of a USB power bank or two fingers side by side. 
Slip it easily into your jacket pocket, backpack, toolbox, or even clip it inside your tent. Whether you’re hiking, repairing a flat tire, or setting up camp in the dark, you’ll barely notice it—until you need it.
Only 117 grams with a full metal body and battery included. That’s less than a smartphone and perfectly balanced for one-handed use.
Flat-sided shape: Won’t roll away on uneven surfaces like rocks or car hoods.
No wasted space: Every millimeter is functional—light, battery, magnets, and buttons are compactly integrated.
PoleShine is equipped with a three-source lighting system, giving you the flexibility to adapt to any environment—whether you're navigating in the dark, setting up camp, or signaling for help.
PoleShine may be pocket-sized, but it packs a serious punch with its 1200-lumen high-intensity LED. Whether you’re hiking in the woods, searching around your campsite, or inspecting a dark workspace, this beam cuts through darkness with ease.
Engineered for Outdoor Precision. Custom TIR (Total Internal Reflection) lens: Focuses the beam for maximum reach without wasting light. Up to 200 meters of clear throw distance, with a balanced hotspot and wide spill — giving you both depth and peripheral visibility.

Dual Laser Modules – Precise Signaling with Safety in Mind

PoleShine features integrated red and green laser beams, built into each side of the main light. Designed for long-distance pointing and signaling, these lasers are visible up to 2 kilometers in optimal conditions—perfect for navigation, team communication, or marking distant objects in the dark.

🔴 Red Laser: Preserves night vision, ideal for low-light environments.

🟢 Green Laser: Ultra-visible, great for daytime use or far-range signaling.

Both lasers are Class 2M, capped at the Maximum 5mW output, offering a safe, effective signal light that complies with global visibility standards.

Double press activates both lasers for instant long-range signaling. 

Auxiliary COB Light

Sometimes, you don’t need a blazing spotlight—you need a light that’s soft, steady, and easy on the eyes. PoleShine’s side-mounted COB light delivers a smooth 180-lumen flood beam, ideal for close-up tasks and ambient lighting.
Whether you're cooking at camp, reading a map, or navigating your tent, the COB light provides a wide-angle, flicker-free glow without overpowering your surroundings.

When things go wrong, visibility can save lives. PoleShine’s COB light isn’t just for ambiance—it features a flashing red/blue warning mode designed for emergency situations, making sure you’re noticed even in the dark or bad weather.
PoleShine features a built-in high-strength magnetic base, turning any metal surface into an instant mounting point. Whether you're in a garage, on the road, or deep in the woods, you can attach it to your car, gear, tools, or tent frame—and free your hands for what really matters.
PoleShine is built to withstand the elements, not avoid them. With an IPX6 waterproof rating, it shrugs off rain, mud, snow, and high-pressure water spray. Its solid aluminum body and reinforced internal structure allow it to survive drops from up to 1.5 meters (5 feet)—onto concrete, rocks, or gravel—without skipping a beat.

Our product utilizes laser technology and is classified as Class 2M, with a maximum output power of 5mW. Devices in this classification emit visible laser light and are considered safe under normal usage conditions when handled as intended, though direct viewing with optical instruments may be hazardous.

Pursuing appropriate certifications (such as FDA, CE, and others as required for our major shipping regions)

Ensuring proper labeling, user instructions, and safety precautions are included in the final product

Inspiration

Our journey began on a frosty winter camping trip when our only source of light was a dim phone torch and a bulky spotlight that hogged our hands. We found ourselves constantly juggling tasks—trying to hold the light just right while cooking, setting up the tent, or checking maps. And when we set the torch down, it rolled away or failed to illuminate exactly where we needed it most.
That moment sparked a simple question: What if one tool could do it all?
Pocketable yet powerful: Slip it into your pack without adding bulk, but still light up trails at 200 m.
Hands-free versatility: A magnetic base to stick on metal surfaces, so your hands stay free when you need them.
Multi-mode lighting: From a 1200 lm white beam to red/green lasers for signaling, down to a COB RGB lamp for ambiance or map reading.
Rugged reliability: Waterproof and drop-proof to weather unexpected storms and knocks.
That spark of frustration transformed into our mission: to design a flashlight that adapts seamlessly to any outdoor challenge. And thus, PoleShine was born.
