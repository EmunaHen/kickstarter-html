Story

BOYA Magic, the world's first AI-powered transformable wireless microphone, ushers in a new era of audio recording. 
Unlock multiple usage setups (vlogging, podcasting, interview, livestreams, and so on) with only one device: Lavalier, Handheld, Desktop, and On-Camera. 
Backed by an advanced AI chip and trained on over 700,000 real-world noise samples, BOYA Magic delivers noise cancellation you never thought possible, up to -40 dB noise suppression.Truly restores pure and natural vocals.
Developed over 3 years with 20,000+ R&D hours, BOYA AI Noise Cancellation employs AI neural networks trained on 700,000+ noise samples. It analyzes sound in milliseconds, intelligently distinguishing human voices from noise through deep learning. The system identifies and suppresses 40dB of background interference while preserving vocal clarity via spectral analysis and adaptive algorithms.
BOYA Magic delivers studio-level voice clarity even in noisy environments, utilizing real-time processing with virtually no latency. With instant noise suppression, it eliminates the need for post-production editing, providing a more efficient recording experience.
BOYA Magic also offers multiple levels of noise cancellation that adapt to your environment: Strong mode cuts up to 40 dB for loud noises like honks and waves, while Light mode reduces 15 dB of white noise in quieter settings, preserving natural ambience.
BOYA Magic is a versatile all-in-one microphone designed to adapt to various usage needs with exceptional flexibility. No more dealing with complex, time-consuming microphone setups, making it perfect for situations where quick sound capture is needed or for on-the-go use. Enjoy the freedom of multi-scenario creation.
BOYA Magic, with its ultra-slim 13mm thick and 7g design, completely eliminates the bulk of traditional wireless microphones. Its compact and discreet appearance ensures that only clear vocals remain in the frame—free from visual distractions or cluttered equipment.
BOYA Magic offers 48kHz/24-bit recording and the 20Hz–20kHz wide frequency response, precisely capturing high-frequency details and a dynamic range of up to 144dB, faithfully reproducing every detail of vocals and instruments.
BOYA Magic combines the smart limiter with the -12dB safety track to effectively prevent audio clipping and distortion, significantly reducing the need for post-production fixes, thereby providing maximum recording reliability.
BOYA Magic is engineered for maximum flexibility, offering wide-ranging compatibility with nearly all devices. Whether you're Creating with an iPhone, Android, DSLR, Action Camera or laptop, BOYA Magic pairs effortlessly with just one click.
The BOYA Magic Wireless System transforms your audio experience with a smart and user-friendly design. Its case-to-mic control technology allows the charging case to directly control the transmitters inside. With one-tap pairing and instant auto-reconnection, it’s truly grab-and-go — no setup hassle.
With the BOYA Central app, adjust AI noise cancellation, custom EQ, and camera presets right from your phone. Create broadcast-quality audio before you even hit a record.
