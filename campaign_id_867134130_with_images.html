My Passion & Vision

SoulMuse was born from my own journey, one filled with over a decade in corporate America, where I often gave everything to my job and left very little for myself. Like so many others, I reached a point of burnout, disconnection, and emotional exhaustion. I had the titles, the meetings, the success on paper… but inside, I felt lost.

What saved me wasn’t a single moment, it was a series of small, sacred ones. Lighting a candle before journaling. Saying an affirmation aloud when I didn’t believe it yet. Breathing deeply and simply being for a moment. These rituals became my anchor. They reminded me of my worth, my power, and my soul.

I created SoulMuse because I knew I couldn’t be the only one craving something deeper, a place to return to yourself when life gets overwhelming. A space that blends the healing magic of mindfulness, spirituality, and intentional living with the intuitive power of technology.

My vision is to make SoulMuse more than just an app, I want it to be a sanctuary.
A digital home where women and seekers of all kinds can feel seen, guided, and supported.
A platform that evolves with you, with AI-powered features that listen to your energy and respond with care.

Risks and challenges

Risk: As we approach the final stages of development, there may be unforeseen delays related to debugging, user testing, or app store approval. How We’re Handling It: We’ve built SoulMuse on a solid technical foundation using modern tools and best practices. Our development roadmap is clearly defined, and we’ve budgeted extra time and resources for final testing, optimization, and deployment. If delays arise, we’ll communicate transparently and keep our community updated every step of the way. Scaling as a SaaS Platform Risk: Scaling SoulMuse to handle growing user demand, while maintaining performance, personalization, and (data security) takes careful infrastructure planning. How We’re Handling It: We’ve already implemented secure backend architecture and encryption protocols. Your support will help us expand our server capacity, optimize performance, and ensure a seamless experience as we grow. Our team is focused on long-term stability, not just launch-day success. Emotional & Spiritual Integrity Risk: SoulMuse is more than an app, it’s a space for healing and personal growth. With that comes a deep responsibility to ensure the tools we create are inclusive, supportive, and safe. How We’re Handling It: We’ve built SoulMuse with intention, care, and lived experience. We’re also collaborating with wellness experts and our user community to ensure SoulMuse remains a safe, respectful, and empowering space for all backgrounds and beliefs.

What parts of your project will use AI generated content? Please be as specific as possible.

In my project, I will be using AI-generated content in the form of visual assets (such as icons, illustrations, wallpapers, and select promotional materials), written copy (such as guided affirmations and journaling prompts), and light AI functionality within the SoulMuse app, including mood tracking and personalized wellness suggestions. These tools are designed to enhance the user experience in a mindful and ethical way.

Do you have the consent of owners of the works that were (or will be) used to produce the AI generated portion of your projects? Please explain.

Yes, I have ensured that any AI-generated content used in this project was created either by me directly using tools that license their training data ethically (such as OpenAI and other platforms that ensure no copyrighted or unauthorized training materials were used), or from royalty-free platforms with proper commercial usage rights. No content used violates copyright or relies on unconsented intellectual property. For visuals, I also ensure all base images or inspirations used are original or licensed.
