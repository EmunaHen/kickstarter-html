Story

 Ti-Scout—— A triangular hard-core EDC tool that subverts imagination. It is a collision of outdoor survival aesthetics and wilderness instinct. It is your ultimate outdoor survival tool.

The main body of Ti-Scout is made of titanium/special stainless steel/brass, achieving a perfect combination of strength and functionality. Ti-Scout is designed based on the "triangle rule" and is a super-dimensional outdoor survival tool that combines practicality, geometric aesthetics, and decompression fun.

Measuring just 58 mm L x 62 mm H x 19 mm D, the Ti-Scout is rugged enough to withstand whatever life throws at you. Whether you’re navigating the city or exploring the great outdoors, the Ti-Scout is there for you, rain or shine. Built for the ultimate experience.

In short, Ti-Scout is a compass, thermometer, level, magnifying glass, bottle opener, flint, crowbar, stress relief toy and other functions in one. It's time to revisit the industrial beauty of metal and machinery.
