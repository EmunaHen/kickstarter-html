Use of AI

I plan to use AI-generated content in my project.

What parts of your project will use AI generated content? Please be as specific as possible.

The P91S AI Tattoo Stencil Printer leverages AI-generated content specifically in the design creation process through our proprietary PeriMonkey app. The AI component is integrated into the app to transform user inputs—such as text prompts (e.g., "dragon with flames"), voice commands (e.g., describing a floral pattern), or uploaded images (e.g., a sketch or photo)—into tattoo-ready line art. This AI-driven feature uses advanced algorithms to analyze inputs and generate precise, customizable stencil designs in seconds, which are then sent wirelessly to the P91S printer for output at 304 DPI resolution. No other parts of the project, such as the physical printer hardware or marketing materials, rely on AI-generated content; the AI is exclusively used to enhance the creative workflow by producing unique tattoo stencils tailored to user specifications.

Do you have the consent of owners of the works that were (or will be) used to produce the AI generated portion of your projects? Please explain.

Yes, we have ensured that all works used to train the AI model in the PeriMonkey app are either fully licensed or created by our team, with explicit consent from the respective owners. The AI was developed using a combination of: Licensed Datasets: We partnered with professional tattoo artists and digital illustrators who provided their original designs under a licensing agreement, granting us permission to use their artwork to train the AI. These agreements specify that their works can be used to generate new designs for the P91S project. In-House Creations: Our design team produced a substantial portion of the training data, consisting of original line art and tattoo sketches, which we own outright and have full rights to use. Public Domain Works: Where applicable, we incorporated public domain images to supplement the training, ensuring no copyright infringement. We do not use any unauthorized or scraped content from the internet. Our AI model is built to generate original outputs based on these consented works, and we maintain documentation of all licenses and consents, available upon request to verify compliance with intellectual property rights.
