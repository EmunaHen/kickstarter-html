Story

We’re building Custom Character Creator (CCC) — a browser-based tool that lets you design 3D characters in your style, anytime, anywhere (as long as you’ve got Wi-Fi). Your characters live safely in the cloud, ready to be accessed, edited, or dropped into your next project at a moment’s notice. Creativity shouldn’t be chained to a device — and now, it doesn’t have to be.

For a while, comics seemed like relics of the boomer era—overshadowed by the rise of online and mobile games. But then came the plot twist: Webtoons. These mobile-optimized digital comics captured the attention of MZs craving quick, bite-sized entertainment during their downtime—no long movies or binge sessions required.

Webtoons didn’t just stop at being a snackable delight; they became a goldmine for adaptations, inspiring hit dramas and films. As visual storytelling skyrockets in demand, the problem is clear: supply can’t keep up. Technical skills and long hours still block aspiring creators from entering the scene, while professionals are stuck grinding through time-consuming, repetitive tasks.

We think it’s time for a change.

As creators ourselves, we zeroed in on a problem that professional artists know all too well: the crushing weight of repetitive production. In the fast-paced world of webcomics or webtoons, creators are buried under weekly deadlines, thousands of hand-drawn panels, and a serious lack of helpful tools.

To survive the grind, many turned to a surprising savior — 3D assets.

3D mannequins quickly proved to be a game-changer. With just a few tweaks, artists could test poses, visualize proportions, and eliminate hours of redrawing. It was a leap forward — but not quite the final answer.

Why? Because while 3D pose references helped scratch the surface, creators were still stuck doing the heavy lifting — manually redrawing textures, tailoring costumes, and adapting proportions to match their unique art styles. The iceberg was still there. They’d just chipped off the tip.

Source: Unicorn Process by Saige199 on DeviantArt

Learning 3D tools themselves seemed like a logical next step — but anyone who’s ever opened one knows the pain. Complex. Expensive. Time-devouring.

That’s why we’re building something radically different — a service laser-focused on custom character creation. Simple, creator-first, and powerful enough to finally unburden visual storytelling.

Meet Custom Character Creator — the tool that lets you bring your signature style to life without ever touching a 3D textbook.

Webcomics, of course. But the use of visual content is rapidly expanding beyond traditional comics, as shown in the examples below. Visual storytelling is now being applied to websites for engaging guides and tutorials, webcomics and publications for immersive narratives, posters and illustrations for marketing and artistic expression, campaign materials and etiquette guides to communicate messages effectively, and even short-form videos and reels for social media. These diverse applications highlight how visual content has become a powerful, versatile medium for conveying stories, ideas, and information across industries and platforms

Custom Character Creator (CCC) can now empower creators to meet this growing demand for diverse visual content by making it easier than ever to create unique, high-quality 3D characters. Whether for webcomics, campaigns, short reels, or publications, CCC allows creators to design characters in their own style without requiring advanced 3D skills — saving time and enabling consistent, customized visuals across all formats. By lowering the technical barrier and providing ready-to-use assets, CCC helps creators deliver more content, faster, and expand their creative possibilities into new platforms and industries.

💡 Where Did This Project Come From?

As former startup operators and creators, we’ve seen firsthand how demanding visual content production is — especially in the webtoon and manga world. Artists are stuck redrawing similar panels every week under insane deadlines.

We launched Toonit Studio in early 2024 and quickly learned from our users that custom character creation was the biggest unmet need. CCC was born out of hundreds of conversations with creators — who wanted to save time without sacrificing style.

Long-lasting design

We designed the Custom Character Creator (CCC) to be a long-lasting, evolving platform rather than a one-off tool. By building it as a browser-based service with cloud storage, creators’ characters and assets remain accessible and usable anytime, without relying on specific devices or software versions. We also plan regular content updates — such as new presets, styles, and features — informed by creator feedback, ensuring CCC stays relevant and valuable as creators’ needs and trends evolve.

Sustainable Distribution

Since Toonit Studio provides digital content only, there is no need for physical shipping, packaging, or transportation. This significantly reduces carbon emissions and supports a more eco-friendly method of delivering rewards to our backers.
