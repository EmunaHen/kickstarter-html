Story

Hey fellow guitar lovers and music creators! Imagine strumming your acoustic guitar and instantly unleashing the power of a full band—drums pounding, echoes resonating, and mixes blending seamlessly. That's Sonicoal, the multifunctional guitar pickup we've poured our hearts into for over two years.

As a team of passionate musicians and engineers, we've tested and refined this device to bridge the gap between solo play and epic band vibes. No more settling for basic pickups; Sonicoal turns your acoustic into a versatile powerhouse, delivering richer expression than even electric guitars. Ready to rock your world?

We've all been there: lugging extra gear for effects, battling noisy environments during recordings, or feeling limited on stage. 

Sonicoal changes that. Built with cutting-edge hardware and software integration, it empowers you to perform, record, and create like never before. Whether you're a street busker, YouTube creator, or stage performer, this pickup gives you professional tools in a compact, guitar-friendly design.


Tired of pickups with just a few bland drum sounds? Sonicoal revolutionizes this with 100 diverse drum patterns, from thumping rock beats to groovy jazz rhythms and everything in between.

with a crisp 1.4-inch LCD screen displaying your choices in real-time. Our real-world tests show it rivals a live drummer's feel, adding explosive energy to your solos. Perfect for fingerstyle grooves or high-energy strums—your music will pulse with life!

Create lush, professional echoes that elevate your sound. Sonicoal's sound delay lets you dial in customizable reverb and delays, turning simple chords into immersive soundscapes. Whether you're crafting ambient folk tunes or soaring ballads, this feature adds emotional layers without extra pedals.


Seamlessly mix your guitar tones, vocals, and drum effects on the fly. Sonicoal's mixing controls give you balanced, dynamic output, ensuring every element shines. No more muddled sounds—get that polished, band-like harmony right from your acoustic.

Flexibility meets convenience:
Magnetic Mounting: Featuring a powerful N52 magnetic ring (Magsafe-sized), Sonicoal snaps onto a matching patch on your guitar's side for quick attachment and removal. Ideal for nomad musicians who switch setups often.

Screw Mounting: For a permanent hold, use our provided template to drill precise holes and secure it with screws. Only the four buttons and LCD panel show, keeping your guitar sleek and playable without hogging space.


Sonicoal grows with your needs:
1. Pure Acoustic Mode: Switch off for unamplified, natural tone—perfect for intimate sessions.
2. Built-in Speaker Mode: Activate for amplified resonance and volume, with booming body vibrations that fill small venues or street spots.
3. External Speaker Mode: Plug into a high-power amp for piercing clarity and stage dominance, ideal for big gigs where you need to cut through the crowd.
