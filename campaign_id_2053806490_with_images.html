Story

A compact, smart nitrogen infuser for smooth, creamy nitro cold brew – anytime, anywhere.

Nitropop pro transforms your regular cold brew into a rich, velvety nitro coffee with just the push of a button. No gas cylinder, no waiting – just café-quality coffee in seconds. From nitro tea and cocktails to sparkling fruit infusions or even non-alcoholic drinks like kombucha, this little powerhouse takes your favorite beverages and gives them a creamy, smooth texture with a rich, cascading foam. If you love experimenting with drinks, Nitropop Pro will be your new favorite tool.

Nitrogen turns regular cold brew into something special. When nitrogen is infused into coffee, tea, or cocktails, it creates a rich, creamy texture and a naturally sweet taste — without adding milk or sugar. The result is a smooth, velvety drink topped with a thick, cascading foam that looks as good as it tastes.

Nitropop features an integrated nitrogen generator using advanced PSA technology, extracting high-purity nitrogen (≥99.5%) directly from the air and always fresh nitrogen on demand. There’s no need for bulky external gas cylinders or costly nitrogen cartridges — ever. PSA works by using a special filter material that selectively absorbs oxygen from the air, allowing only nitrogen to pass through. In just seconds, Nitropop Pro separates and concentrates food-grade nitrogen from the ambient air, delivering it straight into your cold brew with no delay.

With a strong 500kPa nitrogen boost, this machine fully infuses your cold brew to create a thick, silky foam that makes every sip feel special. Its 500ml volume means you can pour 5 to 8 cups per batch, making it easy to share the nitro love with family or customers.

With a single tap, Nitropop starts working its magic. It’s designed so anyone can use it quickly and effortlessly, even if you’re new to nitro coffee.

Cleaning Nitropop Pro is a breeze. It doesn’t leave any sticky residue behind, so you don’t have to worry about buildup. Plus, all the parts are dishwasher safe, making cleanup super easy and stress-free. It’s that easy to keep your machine fresh and ready for the next cup.

Love nitro coffee at home? Want it on the road or at your outdoor hangouts too? Nitropop Pro works great both at home and outdoors. Thanks to its built-in 4000mAh battery and USB-C charging, you can easily take it camping, to a picnic, or anywhere you want delicious nitro cold brew — no need to worry about plugging in.
