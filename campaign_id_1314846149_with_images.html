Story

Introducing ProHighlight – The Last Highlighter You'll Ever Need

ProHighlight is the ultimate organization tool. Whether you're taking notes, planning your week, studying, or highlighting important text, ProHighlight lets you do it all efficiently—without juggling four different highlighters at once. It helps you stay focused and finish your tasks twice as fast.

What Problem Does It Solve?

  • No more scattered highlighters or losing one of them.
  • No more messing with four different caps.
  • No more repetitive, meaningless movements between highlighters.
  • Ergonomic and comfortable for long study sessions.
  • Long-lasting ink.
Just pure efficiency.

The Story Behind ProHighlight

This project was born from numerous late nights balancing school and work, which made me realize just how precious our time is—and how much design can help us reclaim it. I created ProHighlight to help students become more productive with their weekly tasks and build the future they dream of. Especially for teenagers and young adults that are shaping their future lives with their own hands.
Currently, I have a handcrafted prototype of ProHighlight. I also have detailed mechanical plans and a 3D model ready for manufacturing. The funds raised will help me partner with a manufacturer, refine the design and packaging, and produce the first batch of ProHighlight. The goal? Launch on Amazon before the start of the school year by August 2025.
