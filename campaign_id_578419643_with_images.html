What is CurbCue

CurbCue is the combo of a GPS device, a cloud server and a mobile app. They work together to detect your parked location, look up corresponding street cleaning schedules, and automatically set reminders.

How it works

It’s perfect for people who:

  • Often park in different areas
  • Don’t want to think about reminders or alarms
  • Want something that just works—without friction

App Features

  • Confirmation of reminder set: Receive confirmation via push notification that reminders are set, after you park your car.
  • View location: View car’s current parked location and corresponding active reminders.
  • Update location: Manually update car’s current parked location.
  • Battery status: See GPS device’s current battery status.
  • Low battery reminder: Receive reminder via push notification that GPS device battery is low.
  • Customize reminder schedule: Tailor the default reminder schedules to suit your personal needs.
  • Customize reminder methods: Choose to receive reminders via app push notification, SMS or email by default.

Born from Personal Need

Technical Design

After you park your car, the GPS device sends the car location to CurbCue’s cloud server via its built-in cellular network. The cloud server then checks the city’s street cleaning schedule of this location, and automatically sets a reminder on the mobile app. The mobile app then sends a confirmation push with you. Later, when the previously set reminder time comes, the mobile app sends alerts via push notification, SMS or email based on your customized settings. 

The entire process is autonomous and doesn’t require any action from you.

Roadmap

We will focus on making street cleaning stress-free for San Francisco car owners, and we prioritize implementing and shipping to pledgers once we hit the goal. After the shipments, we will focus on gathering feedback and iterating the app.

Once the app is in a stable status with satisfying UX, we plan to expand the product with more features shown below, and prioritize based on the population’s interest:

Risks and challenges

CurbCue uses the official street cleaning schedule dataset maintained by the City of San Francisco. This creates a dependency on how promptly the dataset is updated. That said, street cleaning schedules tend to remain stable over time, and the dataset itself was last updated on April 21, 2025.
