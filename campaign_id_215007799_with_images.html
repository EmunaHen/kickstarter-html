💡 Project Introduction

What if job hunting didn’t mean applying, guessing, or getting ghosted?
At Vettify.careers, we’re building a privacy-first job platform that ends the chaos of job boards.

❌ No more apply buttons.
✅ No more fake jobs.
🔒 No more giving your details to strangers.

Vettify is a new kind of job platform — one where employers express interest first, jobseekers stay anonymous, and AI makes real career matches.

✨ Project Story

We’re raising funds to launch Vettify.careers, a platform that puts trust and transparency back into job search.

🚨 The Problem

Job boards are outdated. You apply. You wait. You never hear back — or worse, you get scammed.

💡 The Vettify Solution

We flip the hiring model:

  • Jobseekers join intent-based talent pools
  • Employers get verified before posting
  • AI matches both sides based on skills, interests & intent
  • Only when both sides are interested, a connection is made

🔐 Privacy & Verification First

Jobseekers stay anonymous until they choose to connect.

Every employer/recruiter is verified. Every job is tracked. Your contact info is never shared without consent.

Risks and challenges

We believe in building transparent, ethical technology — and that means being open about what could go wrong, and how we plan to overcome it. ⸻ 1. AI Model Development & Training Time Vettify’s smart matching engine is powered by custom-trained AI/ML models that match jobseekers and employers based on intent, skills, and compatibility. The risk: Training high-quality, ethical models takes time — especially since we’re working with anonymised, privacy-first datasets and ensuring bias mitigation. How we’ll manage it: We’re using a phased approach — launching with rule-based matching and progressively introducing trained models as data grows. We’re also collaborating with domain experts to fine-tune for quality. ⸻ 2. Building Trust at Scale Vettify is a platform where trust is the product — which means strict employer verification, job audit trails, and data privacy. How we’ll manage it: We’ve already implemented a verification system and privacy-by-design structure. We’ll launch with verified partners and scale trust systems gradually. ⸻ 3. Market Resistance Not every employer is ready to give up spray-and-pray hiring. Vettify flips the flow: employers express interest, not jobseekers. How we’ll manage it: We’ll start with mission-aligned partners who value intent-based hiring. Our early adopters will shape the experience with us. ⸻ 4. Timeline Risks As with any tech product, there’s a risk of delay — especially while refining AI logic, multi-tenant systems, and privacy flows. How we’ll manage it: We’ve built in development buffers and will be honest with updates. Our priority is quality and user trust. ⸻ 5. Sustainability After Launch Kickstarter helps us launch, but long-term growth needs a clear business model. How we’ll manage it: We’re introducing sustainable revenue streams like subscription-based access and B2B tools — always respecting our privacy-first mission.

We are incorporating AI in Vettify.careers through the development of a proprietary AI/ML model designed to improve job matching. The model will analyse intent, skills, and compatibility signals between jobseekers and employers to generate smarter, fairer matches than traditional keyword-based job boards. Our approach prioritises ethical design, transparency, and privacy — jobseeker data is anonymised, and all AI interactions are reviewed and tested by our human team. This AI is developed in-house and serves to assist human decision-making, not replace it.
