The Bear Country Jubilee

This Kickstarter aims to fund the restoration of an animatronic show called The Bear Country Jubilee (The predecessors to The Rock-afire Explosion from Showbiz Pizza Place), specifically getting them in perfect working condition and producing new cosmetics for them. Funding this will also open many doors to new possibilities past that, such as putting together The Rock-afire Museum and digitizing things like original showtape audio, footage, and photos. 

The Bear Country Jubilee was created in 1978 by Creative Engineering, the same company that eventually produced The Rock-afire Explosion.

This specific band comes from Morey’s Piers, Wildwood, New Jersey, and consists of 7 animated pneumatic characters in the main band and 4 extra characters originally located in a different part of the building.

Once funded, Creative Engineering will mechanically restore the characters and create brand-new cosmetic pieces for them, and they will become working display pieces in The Rock-afire Museum located in Florida.

This funding is crucial for the show’s restoration, given the high costs and extensive work required for a band of this size. Every cosmetic piece is custom-made, and there are many specialty parts that add up once spread across multiple characters. The restoration will involve creating new latex masks, repairing and cleaning fabric and fur, fitting new seals to pneumatic cylinders, remaking and repairing mechanical components, cleaning and repairing valve banks, and much more. This will be a very long and expensive process, so it is very important that we can have the funds set aside for Creative Engineering to be able to focus their time on bringing these characters back to life.

Not only will this allow us to restore the characters, but it will also help us construct a dedicated place for displaying the band in the museum.
