Story

The Tri-Screen 3 is the ultimate portable workstation that lets you add two more screens to your laptop with just one cable! Designed by us here at Xebec, the creators of the original Tri-Screen. 

Reimagine the way you work on the go, with the Tri-Screen 3.

In just 10 seconds you have triple the screen space. Our fastest setup yet!

Simply expand the Tri-Screen 3 frame to mount it on the back of your laptop screen using our tried-and-true, patented tensioning design. No magnets or adhesives. Deploy the new and improved aluminum kickstand for ultimate support. 

Connect with a single, embedded USB-C cable. One cable for two extra screens, that’s it. Mac laptops require the single cable Xebec Tri-Screen 3 Mac Adapter, more details below.

Open your screens and enjoy seamless screen expansion and over 40% boost in productivity.

Introducing the world’s first plug-and-play Tri-Screen with a single embedded USB-C cable. Just connect one cable to your laptop and go. No setup headaches, no more digging for the right cables, no separate power cable required! 

This isn’t just fewer cables, it’s fewer compromises. Instantly add two extra screens to your laptop with one seamless connection. Whether you're working remotely, presenting on the go, or powering through tasks at your favorite café, this single cable does the heavy lifting so you can stay focused, efficient, and untangled.

Mac users, quick heads-up: due to macOS limitations, the Tri-Screen 3 Mac Adapter add-on is required to enable Tri-Screen support, along with a one-time DisplayLink® driver installation (more details below). It’s fast, safe, and only takes a minute for the one-time setup and allows you to still take advantage of the single cable solution. After that you’re fully set, just plug in and go, every time.

Level up your workflow with two additional 13.3” Full HD screens. With vivid 1080p resolution, you get crisp text, lifelike colors, and sharp visuals that make multitasking effortless.

Whether you're comparing spreadsheets, editing media, coding, or managing multiple apps at once, you can fit it all in the same view. And with more screen real estate, you can boost your productivity by over 40%. No more alt-tabbing, window shuffling, or squinting. Just smooth, focused efficiency from edge to edge.

The Tri-Screen 3 is designed for the modern, mobile professional. Lightweight, ultra-slim, and protected by the included EVA-padded travel case, it slides easily into your backpack or laptop bag.

Whether you're jumping between coworking spaces, hopping on a plane, or setting up at the kitchen table, your full multi-screen setup goes with you. It's durable enough for travel, compact enough for tight spaces, and always ready when you are. Freedom to work where you want, without sacrificing screen space or productivity.

The Tri-Screen 3 is compatible with Windows, Mac, Chromebook, and Linux laptops that contain at least one USB-C port with DP Alt mode or Thunderbolt. 

Xebec's patented mount expands to clasp to the back of any 13”-18” laptop screen without magnets or adhesive so you can enjoy worry-free use, even on company laptops

The Xebec Tri-Screen 3 Mac Adapter is required for all Mac users. It connects directly to the frame and screws in so it becomes part of the unit overall, no need to remove and attach each time. The single cable solution still applies, even utilizing the same embedded cable management system to keep it all in place.

It's NOT a dongle... it's a one time connection so you can truly set it and forget it.

Installation of the free DisplayLink® driver is required.

NERDY ADAPTER DETAILS BELOW...

The Adapter is required because Apple does not support MST, which is the technology that allows for a single cable to drive both additional screens. Our custom built adapter makes this possible due to the DisplayLink chips embedded into it's PCB.

The Tri-Screen 3 features a fully embedded USB-C cable, integrated directly into the Tri-Screen’s frame. No more rummaging through your bag or realizing you left your cable at home because now, you can't. 

It tucks neatly into a custom cable track for clean storage and quick deployment. When you’re ready to connect, just pull it out and plug in. When you’re done, it stows securely, no tangles, no clutter, no lost cables.

It’s a small detail that makes a huge difference: effortless setup, always-ready convenience, and one less thing to worry about.

The Tri-Screen 3's Presentation Mode lets you rotate your display up to 270°, so you can easily swing a screen around to face a colleague, client, or collaborator. It takes just a second to flip a screen into view, no unplugging, no repositioning your laptop.

It’s a smarter, smoother way to collaborate in meetings, at cafés, or on the go. Your screen becomes their screen, instantly.

The Tri-Screen 3’s built-in aluminum kickstand has 10x the number of contact points delivering unmatched stability and strength, giving your laptop a sturdy foundation wherever you work.

Crafted from durable aluminum, it protects your laptop’s hinges from strain and wear while holding your screens steady on any surface, from your office desk to your lap while sitting on the couch.

Pass-through charging allows you to charge your laptop even if your charging port is occupied by the Tri-Screen 3. Simply plug your laptop's supplied USB-C charger into the dedicated pass-through charging port on the back of the Tri-Screen 3 and allow up to 60W of power to pass through and keep your laptop powered up!
