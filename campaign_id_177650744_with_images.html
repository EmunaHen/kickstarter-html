Story

Say goodbye to ordinary neck fans. ArcticLoop combines dual semiconductor cooling plates with quad-turbo brushless fans to deliver a powerful, wearable cooling experience that goes far beyond airflow. In just 10 seconds, the cooling plates drop to 25°C (77°F), directly targeting the heat-sensitive back of your neck for rapid,refreshing relief.At the same time, four precision turbo fans create a 360° wind tunnel effect — stronger, quieter, and more efficient than any dual-fan device. 

With 200-level wind control, multiple airflow modes, and  voice commands, ArcticLoop puts cooling at your fingertips. Whether you're commuting, hiking, or working outdoors, its long battery life, quiet operation, and lightweight neckband design make it the perfect way to stay cool anytime, anywhere.

At the heart of ArcticLoop are two high-performance semiconductor cooling modules, each powered by a dedicated thermal management chip.These chips utilize the Peltier effect, a cutting-edge thermoelectric technology that actively transfers heat away from the skin. Unlike passive fans, these modules are electronically controlled and can drop surface temperatures to 25°C (77°F) in just 10 seconds, creating a rapid and direct cooling sensation.

When switched on in hot, humid environments, the surface of the cooling plates quickly develops a visible mist — a thin layer of frost-like condensation, proof of the rapid heat extraction. The cooling is dry, safe, and skin-friendly — no water drips, no discomfort. You don’t just feel the cold — you can see it.

While most neck fans simply circulate hot ambient air, ArcticLoop actively lowers the temperature of your skin. In high temperatures (90°F+/32°C+), traditional fans can end up blowing warm air, offering little relief. ArcticLoop, by contrast, uses real thermoelectric cooling to combat heat stress — especially important in extreme conditions, outdoor labor, or heat-sensitive users.

Another core component of the ArcticLoop is the advanced Quad Turbine System – four high-performance turbo fans working together to provide powerful 360° airflow. Unlike traditional neck fans, this advanced device generates powerful, focused wind from multiple directions, ensuring fast and even cooling of your neck and face.

Powered by a durable, low-noise brushless motor, ArcticLoop offers not just power, but precision. Brushless technology means higher efficiency, longer lifespan, and whisper-quiet operation—so you stay cool without the noise.


Our custom-designed spiral air duct optimizes the direction and pressure of airflow, ensuring cool air reaches your skin faster and further. Less turbulence. More chill.

No more hot spots. ArcticLoop’s 360° airflow structure wraps your neck in cooling wind from every angle — perfect for intense heat or humid conditions.

ArcticLoop offers 0-200 wind speed adjustment, and the intuitive joystick control makes wind speed adjustment smooth, responsive and enjoyable. Whether you like a gentle breeze on your face or a strong gust of wind to dispel the summer heat, you can fine-tune the air volume and find the most comfortable temperature through true tactile interaction.

Control your fan with voice commands — switch between Breeze, Gentle, Hurricane, and Storm modes, or power it off hands-free.

Front, back, both, your airflow, your choice. Whether you want to cool your face while exercising or relieve neck pressure while sitting, ArcticLoop can meet your needs.

ArcticLoop is equipped with a 5500mAh lithium battery, delivering up to 15 hours of runtime in Breeze Mode and 3–4 hours with semiconductor cooling activated — so you can stay cool all day without worrying about recharging.

ArcticLoop's integrated smart LED screen displays real-time wind speed and battery level information, giving you at-a-glance information without the guesswork.

ArcticLoop features a soft, flexible neckband made from food-grade silicone that’s gentle on the skin, hypoallergenic, and built for long-term wear. Its highly elastic design naturally adapts to different neck sizes and shapes, providing a custom, secure fit for all users. It also helps wick away sweat, keeping you cool and comfortable even in extended use or high-heat environments.

Constructed with reinforced ABS plastic, ArcticLoop resists slipping even during workouts, and handles minor drops with ease. Light enough to carry, tough enough to endure.

From daily commutes to outdoor adventures, from focused work to relaxation, the ArcticLoop neck fan is always by your side — your most thoughtful personal air conditioner for the summer.
