Your Devices Are Smart.

Your Power Bricks Are Stuck in the 90s.

You’ve got modern tech, but you’re still stuck hunting down the exact voltage and connector for each device. It’s draining, messy, and frustrating.

Electronic devices over the past 50 years have relied on AC to DC power supplies with a litany of output voltages and connectors.  The combinations are endless. 

Finding the right one can be close to impossible.

How VFLEX works

VFLEX solves the problem in a clever way.  It has one base that is configurable for the right voltage and a family of low cost adapters to find the right connector.

If we don't have your right adapter, talk to us and we’ll build it!

New adapters will be continuously added to expand the uses for VFLEX. Our supply chain is setup to quickly make new cables and stock them for the entire community to benefit.

Power Supply Options

VFLEX is compatible with most USB-C power supplies that support USB-C Power Delivery.  Each device has variability in what modes are supported and what maximum voltages are allowed.

In this campaign Werewolf is offering 4 options for Power supplies.  Two that are powered by an AC wall outlet and 2 that are Battery powered.  These supplies are tested and verified to work with VFLEX out of the box.

They are also very useful devices for your everyday life, use them to charge your phone, laptop or any other native USB-C device.

VFLEX Specifications

VFLEX is designed to operate at voltages up to 50volts and currents up to 5A.  These are the maximum specifications of USB-C.  This means that it will work in any configuration and is only limited by the USB-C Power Supply that you plug it into.

The VFLEX App

To make VFLEX as easy to use as possible, it is incredibly important to have a convenient and easy to use app.  For this reason we are using cross platform development tools to offer an app for iOS, Android and the Google Chrome Web Browser.

So far we already have a working version of the Google Chrome Web app that works on PC and Android. 

We believe that in order to support USB communication with VFLEX on iOS, we will need to ship an iOS app to the Apple App Store.  We are beginning this process now and will have more updates to share about it.

The VFLEX App will always be Free.  At launch it will have a simple interface where voltage can be manually programmed.  over the time, we plan to implement additional help and support features to assist everyone with determining the correct voltage settings and suggested adapter.

No more searching for the “right” charger. If it runs on a barrel plug — VFLEX powers it.

Something unique about many musical accessories is that they are “Center Negative” or “Reverse Polarity”. The distinctive Orange color indicates this “Center Negative” configuration.

The molded "Lever Nut" connector in the DIY edition offers a tool-less, solder-less method to conveniently connect wires to VFLEX.  

  1. Lift the color coded levers
  2. Insert your wires.
  3. Snap the lever back to the locked position.  
  4. A spring loaded clamp grips onto the wires and provides a robust connection.

Long-lasting design

VFLEX is manufactured with high quality materials like ABS, Aluminum and TPE. The are carefully selected to maximize the number of bend cycles and continuous use.

Reusability and recyclability

We hope that adoption of VFLEX in future products can reduce the amount of eWaste contributed from AC/DC power supplies that are shipped with products. If we can increase the reusability of USB-C based power supplies, then they can be higher quality, more reliable, more multipurpose and reusable.

Environmentally friendly factories

The VFLEX Base is built at our factory in Green Bay WI. We work very hard to minimize waste and transportation of parts.
