Story

Eat My Carbon – Let’s Plant a Better Future

We're raising funds to build a mobile app that makes it easy for everyday people to take climate action by helping to plant trees and restore forests.

Eat My Carbon connects landowners with underused land to volunteers and teams who want to get their hands dirty and make a real environmental impact. Our mobile app will make organizing and joining planting projects as simple as tapping a button — whether you're a local landowner, a keen volunteer, or a passionate eco-group.  You can checkout our current website at https://eatmycarbon.com/ 

Our Goal

Our goal is to enhance environmental sustainability by establishing trees and forests, in particular:

  • to rapidly reduce the level of CO2 by assisting people to plant as many trees as possible on marginal land.
  • Reducing erosion, improving water quality, focusing on at-risk catchments and/or restoring natural forest
  • Enhancing indigenous biodiversity by restoring natural forest

Eat My Carbon is a website platform (https://eatmycarbon.com) that provides a place for landowners and helpers to connect and manage planting activities, so that the right trees are planted in the right place, and looked after as they grow.  Our ambition is to scale this into a worldwide program. 

Why We Care

Climate change isn’t just a global issue — it’s local too. We're passionate about giving people real ways to contribute, especially in rural communities with land that could be restored.

We believe the response to climate change should be credible, community-driven, and nature-based — not just a buzzword. That’s why we want to build a tool that brings people together on the ground to create living carbon sinks, one tree at a time.

How We’ll Make It Happen

We already have:

  • A working website connecting users and showcasing our vision: www.eatmycarbon.com
  • A team of developers ready to start building the mobile app
  • A working concept tested with landowners and helpers in New Zealand and Australia
  • Have over 200 community planting groups who have run more than 500 events and planted over 200,000 trees through the Eat My Carbon system

Now we need your help to fund the mobile app, which will:

  • Let helpers join planting events in their region
  • Allow landowners to list available planting areas
  • Track and show the carbon impact of each planting project
  • Verify and build credibility through photos, GPS, and community reviews

Risks and challenges

We already have a live website that has been operational for more than 24 months, and experienced development team. Although this round of funding to build a mobile app brings additional technical challenges, we already have a proven web-based platform - we mainly need the app for additional speed of data entry, and to allow offline access in remote areas. We have already proven the concept with the website, so this project is about having an alternative delivery mechanism to optimise work completed onsite. Technical integration with our existing system has some complexities, but we’ve planned for this with phased development and regular testing. Timeline delays are always a risk, so we’re starting with a clear minimum viable product (MVP) and a roadmap for improvements. Ensuring smooth performance across devices takes time, but we’ve allocated resources for thorough QA and user testing. Finally, as an environmental project, we know credibility matters. Our platform is grounded in transparent actions—real landowners, real planting, real impact—and we’re committed to rigorous reporting and verification as we grow. We’re confident we can deliver a quality, meaningful app that helps people take real climate action.
