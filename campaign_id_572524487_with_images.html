Story

Late-night focus sessions. Quiet open offices. Shared workspaces - you deserve a keyboard that performs without noise. 

That’s why we created the MCHOSE UT98 - the silent mechanical keyboard engineered to enhance concentration, comfort, and creativity.

“When MCHOSE approached me to co-design a keyboard, I knew we had to create something special.”   — Lewis Hilsenteger, Unbox Therapy

After the success of the GX87—our first collaboration with Unbox Therapy—we knew there was more to create. Lewis called it one of his favorite keyboards, but this time, we set out to push the idea further.

Mechanical keyboards offer a great feel, but their noise can be disruptive in shared spaces. So we partnered with Unbox Therapy to create a keyboard that keeps the comfort, feel, and customization, just quieter. 

This is more than just a product—it’s the result of months of hands-on collaboration between the MCHOSE engineering team and one of the most trusted voices in tech. 

Designed in close collaboration with Unbox Therapy, the UT98 isn’t just another keyboard with a logo—it’s a shared vision brought to life through thoughtful design and detail.  From the cool grey tones to the ESC key’s cap element and aluminum knob, every detail reflects thoughtful collaboration and premium craftsmanship.

It's not just built to perform—it's built to reflect who you are.

When we set out to build the UT98, we didn’t just want it to look good—we wanted it to be your everyday keyboard. That meant choosing switches not from a catalog, but from real-world needs.

Designed for low-noise workspaces, this switch keeps sound under 35dB thanks to silicone-gel dampening and factory-lubed rails. It’s quiet without compromising feel, offering crisp linear motion, a stable 5-pin structure, and long-lasting PA66 + POM materials.

Made for speed and feedback, it features a POK stem, BOX structure for dust and water resistance, and a 1.3mm actuation distance for quick input. The gold-plated extended spring delivers a snappy rebound, while the standalone PC diffuser brings out vivid RGB lighting.

No matter how you work or play, the MCHOSE UT98 adapts to your setup. Effortlessly switch among the above 3 modes - all with ultra-stable performance and broad compatibility across different devices.

Smart touches make daily work smoother—like a quick mute in meetings, fast volume control without fiddling, or a bigger 2U0 key that’s easier to hit when you're typing fast. Thoughtful details, right under your fingers.

Enjoy ultra-smooth, quiet keystrokes with MCHOSE’s refined gasket mount design. Built with five layers of premium dampening material, it absorbs vibrations, softens impact, and delivers a pure, consistent typing sound. The result is enhanced stability, comfort, and a satisfyingly responsive feel with every press.

Designed for multi-device users, the UT98 supports seamless switching across Windows, macOS, Android, and iOS - perfect for creators, developers, and multitaskers alike.

Whether you're editing videos on a MacBook or gaming on a Windows desktop, the UT98 adapts effortlessly. It even comes with extra keycaps like Command for a smoother Mac experience.

Whether on desktop or in your browser, MCHOSE HUB makes it effortless to personalize every detail of your UT98. Remap keys, create macros, and fine-tune lighting effects—all from one unified, self-developed platform.

With onboard memory support, your custom settings are saved directly to the keyboard through MCHOSE HUB—so your configurations stay with you, even when you switch devices.

Designed for seamless control across the UT98 and most MCHOSE devices, MCHOSE HUB is your go-to companion for a consistent, customizable experience.

Let your desk come alive with over 16 million colors of soft, diffused lighting. The UT98’s MCHOSE® SoftLight RGB isn’t just bright - it’s ambient, refined, and easy on the eyes. Customize freely with dynamic effects, brightness levels, and reactive modes via MCHOSE HUB (Web or App). Whether you're working late or winding down, your keyboard sets the tone.

*Note: The keycaps feature non-shine-through legends for a clean, minimalist look. 

Features a low front height and dual-stage feet for wrist comfort and prolonged use.

Every aspect of the MCHOSE UT98 is carefully crafted to elevate daily use - combining elegant design with smart, user-focused features that make typing more intuitive, comfortable, and satisfying.

Risks and challenges

While the UT98 is already in a mature pre-production phase, bringing a premium mechanical keyboard to the global market still comes with certain risks. We want to be upfront with our backers and share how we’re actively managing these challenges. 1. Manufacturing and Component Availability Custom switches, premium cases, and precise keycaps require carefully sourced materials and consistent manufacturing standards. While we’ve already secured our factory partners and most core components, global supply chain fluctuations could still cause short delays. To mitigate this, we’ve built buffer periods into our production timeline and lined up alternative suppliers to minimize impact. 2. Quality Control and Pre-Shipment Testing Every UT98 keyboard will undergo rigorous quality control—testing everything from keystroke consistency and wireless stability to switch actuation and battery health. However, as we scale to meet demand, ensuring this high standard at volume remains a core challenge. We’ve implemented layered QC processes at both the assembly and final packaging stages, with extra attention to mechanical tolerances and firmware calibration. 3. International Fulfillment & Customs We’re partnering with experienced global fulfillment providers to ensure your keyboard reaches you quickly and securely. We’ll provide tracking, guidance, and responsive support to help resolve any region-specific issues efficiently. We’re deeply grateful for your support. Your trust empowers us to bring the UT98 from vision to reality—delivering a quiet, powerful, and thoughtfully crafted mechanical keyboard for real work, real setups, and real users.
