Use of AI

For checkpoint validation, AI is used to analyze videos and photos submitted by users as proof of mission completion. Computer vision algorithms compare user uploads with reference images or locations, confirming authenticity and context while detecting potential fraud or manipulation. This ensures that real-world actions are accurately verified without relying on manual review or easily gamed GPS data alone. Beyond validation, AI will also help personalize the experience for each user. Recommendation algorithms will suggest missions based on individual interests, location, and participation history, ensuring that every user receives relevant, motivating challenges. AI-powered moderation will flag inappropriate content, maintain community safety, and streamline support. As the platform grows, AI will be used for analyzing engagement data, identifying patterns in successful missions, and optimizing both the reward system and onboarding process. This allows the team to continually adapt Jurnit to user behavior, maximizing participation and impact without compromising privacy or transparency.
