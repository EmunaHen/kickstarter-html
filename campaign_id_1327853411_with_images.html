Story

WheelKeep is an invisible security system that helps cyclists to protect their bikes from theft and enjoy the freedom of everyday cycling. It includes a smart hardware device that is integrated into the bike, and a mobile application. 

Thanks to motion sensors, the system sends a push-notification on every movement of the bike. If you react fast enough, you are able to scare a thief away and prevent a theft!


But don't worry! Even if you couldn't prevent a theft, WheelKeep will track the bicycle location on a map in real time.


WheelKeep works over 3 months on a single charge and is easily recharged with USB Type-C.


After charging, make sure to put the anti-vandal cap on top of the device, so it's concealed and protected from water & dust.
