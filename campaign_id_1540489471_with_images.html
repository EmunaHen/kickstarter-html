Story

Introducing Starlight Night Vision — exclusive technology that transforms how you capture moments in the dark. By harnessing minimal ambient light, FOSSiBOT F107 Pro delivers clear, full-color photos and videos even in total darkness. 

Unlike traditional infrared night vision, which only produces black-and-white images using infrared light, SNV uses minimal ambient light(like moonlight or starlight) to create vibrant, color-rich visuals. This advanced technology detects both visible and infrared light, offering clearer, more detailed shots than standard night vision systems.

While infrared requires a separate light source (like IR LEDs) and thermal imaging focuses on heat signatures (without color), Starlight uses what’s already available in the environment to give you natural, high-quality images, even in low-light conditions. 

The FOSSiBOT F107 is built for serious outdoor photography, featuring a powerful 200MP main camera that captures every detail with stunning clarity—even in rugged, natural settings. Whether you're shooting wide mountain views, close-up textures in nature, or vibrant sunsets, the 50MP wide-angle lens and macro camera give you the flexibility to frame your shot perfectly. And with a 32MP front camera, your selfies and vlogs stay sharp and vivid, even on the go. From vast landscapes to tiny details, the F107 lets you capture your adventures exactly as you see them.

With Electronic Image Stabilization (EIS), the FOSSiBOT F107 Pro ensures your footage stays smooth and steady, no matter the adventure. Whether you're cycling down rough trails, hiking through rocky landscapes, or vlogging on the go, EIS eliminates shake for clear, professional-quality content. Conquer the wild and record every moment without compromise.
