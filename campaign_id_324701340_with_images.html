Introducing the FX-D: Your First Digital Film Simulation Camera

Proudly introducing our latest camera: YASHICA FX-D. Perhaps you've never had the pleasure of shooting with film, or maybe you're just getting started with digital cameras and the art of photography itself. That's precisely why you're here to discover Your First Digital Film Simulation Camera!

FX-D stands for Film Experience Digital, precisely capturing a genuine Film Experience delivered through a Digital platform. Unlike other film or digital cameras, YASHICA FX-D uniquely bridges both worlds. It offers the convenience of digital capture while bringing the soulful aesthetic of film to your fingertips through in-camera film simulations.

Since 1949, the soul of YASHICA has lived in every captured moment.

In the past, embracing both film and digital meant carrying two cameras and managing distinct workflows. But now, YASHICA FX-D offers a unified solution.

Inspired by the legendary YASHICA FX-3, YASHICA FX-D achieves a remarkable duality by expertly blending both film simulation and digital photography into a single camera. Every element is designed for a truly immersive photographic journey. This perfect integration means you no longer have to compromise. 

It's an invitation to a more mindful and emotional photographic journey. It encourages you to pause, to reflect, and to deepen your connection with the stories your photos tell.

Analog Soul: Film Simulation Mode

At YASHICA, our legacy in film photography stretches back to the 1950s, and we've continued that journey by launching new film cameras in recent years. This passion for analog aesthetics culminates in YASHICA FX-D's Film Simulation Mode. 

Here, you'll discover how YASHICA FX-D allows you to infuse your digital images with our built-in film simulations and your own custom recipes.

Photos taken with FX-D 100.

The film advance lever is an active part of your YASHICA FX-D creative workflow. 

In Film Simulation Mode, after every single shot you capture, you will need to fully wind the film advance lever: guide the lever ahead until it reaches its stop and you hear a soft click. Without this winding action, the shutter will not release for your next shot.

By reintroducing this classic analog motion, FX-D invites you to slow down and be truly present in the moment. Each pull of the lever transforms fosters a deeper connection with your subject and the scene before you. It's a subtle yet powerful reminder to pause before every shot, which significantly enhances the unique "film experience" at the very heart of this innovative digital camera.

Conveniently located on the left side of the camera body, a dedicated film simulationdial allows you to effortlessly switch between the film simulations. A simple rotation of the dial is all it takes to instantly change the character of your images.

But the creative journey doesn't end there.

YASHICA FX-D empowers your creativity beyond its pre-set film simulations by saving your own customized film recipes. You are encouraged to fine-tune various settings to your heart's content, including grain, white balance shift, saturation, contrast, sharpness and hue.

Once you've perfected your signature aesthetic, you can easily save it as a custom recipe directly in the camera. This ensures your personalized styles are always ready for immediate recall, maintaining a consistent look across your work.

While YASHICA FX-D celebrates the art of film, its digital mode ensures you also have the uncompromised speed and convenience of modern photography at your fingertips.

In Digital Mode, capture swiftly and effortlessly with instant shooting. This shooting mode is perfect for those moments when you need quick reactions, or when you prefer the straightforward workflow of digital photography. 

Located at the right side of the camera’s top plate, you will find a Mode dial that allows you to switch between various digital shooting options, such as Manual (M) and Program (P), so you can select the ideal setting for your shot. 

YASHICA FX-D series offers 3 camera models perfectly suited for every photographer. You can always find one that best fits your needs and style:

FX-D 100 — Compact, intuitive, and powered by a 13MP sensor. Ideal for everyday creators who want the charm of analog with the speed of digital, all at a competitive price. Available in the classic silver & black combination.

FX-D 300 — 50MP high-resolution, built for professionals who demand detail, dynamic range, and flexibility in post-production. Available in a silver & black combination.

FX-D S300 — 50MP high-resolution with an EVF, built for professionals who look for a complete and immersive digital-analog-simulated camera experience. Its camera body comes in an elegant, sleek, full black design.

Regardless of which FX-D model you choose, each camera offers you the full, integrated analog-digital experience. You will always gain the power of one camera, featuring two distinct shooting modes, to tell your story creatively without compromising.

Introducing the YASHICA FX-D S300 with Electric Viewfinder (EVF)

From day one, our YASHICA journey has been about co-creation: building cameras FOR and WITH our backers community.  After receiving overwhelming interest and thoughtful feedback from backers, we’re proud to introduce a new model born from that dialogue: YASHICA FX-D S300, now featuring an electronic viewfinder (EVF).

In fact, incorporating an EVF was a key consideration at the earliest stages of our design. However, a high-quality EVF significantly increases the cost of production. Therefore, on FX-D 100 and 300, we prioritized what we believed would bring the most value to the widest audience, while always keeping the door open to evolve.

Now, the addition of the EVF in FX-D S300 is a sensory enhancement that embodies the very soul of analog photography. As you raise the camera to your eye, the LCD screen turns off and you can have a more focused connection with your subject through the EVF. You’re immersed in the present and the story you are about to tell.

The birth of YASHICA FX-D S300 is a celebration of our community’s voice. It wouldn’t exist without your contribution!

YASHICA was founded in 1949 in Japan. With the sales of 5 million YASHICA Electro 35 film cameras in the 1970s, YASHICA has been playing a vital role in the analog photography world.

In 2017, YASHICA introduced the game-changing YASHICA Y35 Camera. Its ground-breaking, world-first digiFilm system revolutionized the film camera industry by bridging the gap between tradition and technology. The innovation was launched globally through a crowdfunding campaign and became the highest-funded project in Asia, both in terms of amount raised and the number of backers.

YASHICA continues to push the boundaries of innovation by expanding its product range. The brand ventured into the world of night vision with the launch of two groundbreaking night vision goggles, YASHICA Vision and YASHICA Explorer, featuring cutting-edge patented technology that paved the way for YASHICA’s success.

International brands like Miu Miu, Supreme, Jägermeister, and Sanrio are captivated by YASHICA. Together, they created unique, trendsetting collaboration products that further enhanced YASHICA’s global presence.

YASHICA has reached new heights by reinventing film and digital photography for a new generation and building a loyal community of passionate photographers. We are committed to driving a new wave of innovation while honoring our rich legacy in photography. Additionally, we strive to create affordable cameras and make photography accessible to everyone — empowering more people to tell their stories through timeless imagery.
