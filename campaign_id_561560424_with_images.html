What makes IORI Timer stand out?

Feature 1: High-Precision Timing

This timer delivers outstanding accuracy, providing precise time measurement down to 1/100 of a second. Whether used for timing sprints, races, or other athletic events, this feature ensures exact tracking of performance, making it ideal for professional sports where every fraction of a second counts. It guarantees reliable and consistent results, critical for competitive environments where timing precision is paramount.

Feature 2: No Reflector Required

Unlike traditional systems that rely on reflector for proper functioning, this device operates without the need for such accessories. This design simplifies setup and reduces the potential for errors caused by misalignment or other issues related to reflective surfaces. It streamlines the user experience, making the system more convenient and user-friendly.

Feature 3: Wireless Connectivity

This timer offers robust wireless connectivity with a range of over 200 meters, covering the entire track. The wireless system supports more than 5 simultaneous connections, enabling segmented timing across multiple stations, perfect for split timing in races. This feature enhancing flexibility and ease of use in competitive athletic events.

Feature 4: Ultra-Simple Operation

Designed for ultimate ease of use, this timer features a single-button interface so simple that even a child can operate it. With no complex menus or settings, it’s intuitive and user-friendly for all skill levels, ensuring anyone can start timing with minimal instruction. This ultra-simple design reduces the learning curve and guarantees a smooth experience for coaches, athletes, and event staff alike.

Feature 5: Automatic Recording

This timer also functions as a camera shutter remote control, compatible with both iPhone and Android devices. It allows users to easily trigger video recordings from a distance, making it perfect for capturing races or events without having to manually operate the camera. 

Feature 6: Long Battery Life

Equipped with a powerful battery that lasts for over 10 hours on a single charge. It’s ideal for long events or all-day competitions, ensuring that the device remains operational without needing frequent recharges. This extended battery life provides reliability and peace of mind, especially in during lengthy events, allowing continuous operation when it's needed most.

How it works?

Eye-safe infrared laser

The timer utilizes eye-safe infrared laser, eliminating the need for reflector. By default, it operates over a trigger distance of 1.3 meters, easily covering the width of a standard track. For different sports or scenarios, the trigger distance can be adjusted to 2.3, 4.3, or 6.3 meters, offering flexibility for a variety of environments.

If necessary, any physical object can serve as a makeshift reflector to reduce the triggering distance. Objects like a wall, a chair, or even a person can be used to tailor the distance to specific needs.

Wireless Transmission Over 200 Meters

The IORI timer uses a long-range wireless protocol to transmit data over distances greater than 200 meters with stable and reliable control. It supports simultaneous connection of up to 5 devices, making it ideal for creating a split timing system. This ensures accurate timing across different points on the track, providing flexibility and precision for various sporting events.

Multiple Mode Configurations

Multiple test modes are available and can be easily set up on the device itself. Here's an overview of the available modes and settings:

Option Modes:

  • TAP: Used for start/finish timing. Hold and release to start and stop.
  • SPT: Spilt timing mode, placed between start and finish points.
  • KEEP: Pause/continue mode. Short press to pause/continue, long press to stop.
  • SHTT: Shuttle run mode. Stops after a set number of passes are triggered.
  • LAP: Lap timing mode, ideal for BMX, roller skating, and similar activities.
  • SET: Starting signal mode, where the gun fires randomly 1.5 to 3.5 seconds after the "Set" command.
  • ADD: Multi-person timing mode, suitable for long-distance race.
  • CNT: Counting mode, useful for exercises like push-ups, where counting is needed.

Option Settings:

  • TWIN: Grouping setting, where devices in the same group automatically connect to each other.
  • DIST: Laser trigger distance configuration.
  • BT: Bluetooth settings for controlling smartphone video recording.

These flexible options cater to a wide range of sports and activities, providing accurate and convenient timing solutions for different scenarios.

Group Settings

Our system supports 11 different groups to ensure that multiple teams can operate simultaneously without interference. This allows for reliable and independent wireless connections, making it ideal for events where multiple timing systems are being used in close proximity. Each group operates on a separate channel, ensuring smooth and uninterrupted performance for all users.

How We Built the IORI Timer

When I worked part-time as a soccer coach, I wanted a fast and reliable way to evaluate my players’ athletic performance. But I couldn’t find a tool that met my needs — so I built a prototype myself.

I shared it with a few fellow soccer coaches, and they loved it. Their positive feedback inspired me to keep improving the product. I extend our gratitude to UK coach Sean Quigley, one of our earliest reviewers, who shared his positive experience.

Then, I added features like laser-based triggering and multi-device connectivity. 

Over time, it became easier to use and more powerful. Many coaches — especially in track and field — started using it in their daily training. It’s been amazing to see how it helps athletes make consistent progress.
