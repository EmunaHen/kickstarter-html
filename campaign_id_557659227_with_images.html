Story

WATCHMAN BY ESCT: Modern Security Management in the Palm of Your Hand

We’re building the future of security guard management and we’re inviting you to build it with us.

The Vision

At Enterprise Security Consulting and Training Inc. (ESCT), we’ve spent nearly four decades protecting people, properties, and communities. With firsthand experience managing field operations, we understand the frustration of outdated systems, paper logs, and disconnected tools.

That’s why we’re launching Watchman by ESCT—a powerful mobile and web app designed to help security companies operate smarter, faster, and safer.

We’ve completed the Scope of Work, mapped the full feature set, and are now seeking funding to begin development.

The Problem

Security companies are still relying on fragmented systems for scheduling, reporting, and communications. This leads to:

  • Delayed response times
  • Missed or incomplete reports
  • Poor accountability and oversight
  • Inefficient payroll and billing
  • Lost productivity and revenue

The Solution: WATCHMAN BY ESCT

Watchman by ESCT will be an all-in-one platform built by security professionals, for security professionals.

It will include:

  • Real-Time Guard Tracking: GPS-based patrol logs with checkpoint verification
  • Digital Incident Reporting: Report events instantly with photos, videos, and notes
  • Shift Scheduling & Timekeeping: Eliminate manual schedules and no-shows
  • Emergency Alerts: Trigger alerts with one touch, notifying supervisors instantly
  • Payroll & Billing Integration: Export time and attendance data directly to Paychex, Intuit WorkForce, and more
  • AND... Many other powerful features that make Watchman by ESCT a must-have solution for any organization that values safety, accountability, and operational excellence.

Risks and challenges

1. Development Delays: Custom app development can take longer than expected due to technical complexities or integration hurdles. How we'll overcome it: We’ve developed a detailed Scope of Work and will work with experienced developers using milestone-based timelines and agile project management to stay on track. 2. Integration with Third-Party Systems: Connecting with payroll systems like Paychex and Intuit WorkForce can involve evolving APIs or restrictions. How we'll overcome it: We’re prioritizing early communication with these platforms, budgeting for expert developers with API experience, and building flexible architecture to accommodate changes. 3. Budget Constraints: Development, testing, and marketing require capital. If we fall short of funding goals, the project could slow down. How we'll overcome it: We’ll prioritize core features for initial launch and pursue additional grants, partnerships, or phased rollouts to manage financial sustainability. 4. Market Adoption: Convincing other security companies to adopt a new platform may take time. How we'll overcome it: We’re leveraging our 37+ years of credibility in the industry, offering pilot access to early adopters, and focusing on real-world usability to build trust and generate word-of-mouth momentum. 5. Ongoing Maintenance and Support: All apps require updates, bug fixes, and support post-launch. How we'll overcome it: Part of our fundraising will be allocated toward building a lean support team and establishing a sustainable licensing model for long-term growth.
