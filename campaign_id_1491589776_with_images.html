🔇 50–60 dB

Print Without the Noise.

Largeo is designed to stay quiet, even when tackling large-scale prints. With motors and motion systems tuned for low noise operation, Largeo maintains a sound level between 50 and 60 decibels — about the same as a silent library.

Whether you're printing from your studio, workshop, or living space, Largeo won't break the silence.

Risks and challenges

At Lumha, we are fully aware of the challenges that come with delivering a high-quality product on time. That’s why we’ve made a clear choice: we’ve limited the number of backers for this campaign. This decision allows us to: - Reduce the risk of delivery delays by keeping production under control - Ensure high-quality support and after-sales service for every customer - Guarantee a smooth and reliable experience from purchase to first print Additionally, please note that some of the early offers include pre-production prototypes. These units might experience minor issues or require adjustments. However, they are essential to help us move faster and collect valuable feedback from our early users. Your input will directly contribute to refining and improving the final version of Largeo, scheduled for release in March. By focusing on a manageable volume, we’re committed to delivering not only a powerful 3D printer but also the attention and care that every maker deserves.
