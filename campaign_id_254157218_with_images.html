Story

In January of this year, our federal government without due process or performance review, abruptly began terminating employment, stripping away federal funding for education programs, and ended important resources which provided critical healthcare and shelter for our world's most vulnerable communities.  These devastating actions are still laying off employees and displacing innocent hardworking people 6 months later.  The ripple effect continues.

StartWorkOrder.com is a platform to start this work in the right direction again.  We're bringing together the thousands (if not millions) of people adversely affected or otherwise impacted by these, in some cases, life-threatening actions.

We're building social media profiles, in-app messaging, news articles, Candid Conversations - a podcast, and with your help, so much more.

The world wants to hear from us, see what we're doing about it, and know that we are OK.  Launched last week, when boosted, our Instagram reels clock over 100,000 views in 1 day.

Who Are We

Launched just last week by a former USAid/NIH/Dept. of Ed/Dept. of Labor contractor, this platform has traction and so much potential.  We want to hear from you and create a safe space to meet, greet, and re-build.  This project is a Social Media platform where colleagues can create profiles focused on the vast community of professionals putting in and the world's ordinary everyday people taking out.

We are linked with other Social Media platforms to gain traction, develop a strong following, and be sure our voices go unheard.  The world wants to hear from us.

  • Blog Posts of real stories from real people like YOU and how to overcome recent challenges.
  • Spotify Channel
  • Social Media campaigns
