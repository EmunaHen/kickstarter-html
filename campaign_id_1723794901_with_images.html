Story

2084: A Wake-Up Call for Humanity
Howard Atkins is a futurist and visionary fiction author known for his thought-provoking work on the direction of human civilization. His debut novel, 2084, received praise for its optimistic portrayal of a future shaped by peace, unity, and human potential. Blending social criticism, political insight, and futurism, Atkins offers readers a compelling contrast between utopian possibility and dystopian consequence.

Howard Atkins draws from a broad spectrum of sources, including: His own lived experience. Scientific research on climate change and emerging technologies. Historical patterns of political division and social unrest. His work challenges us to reflect on our current reality and confront uncomfortable truths.

2084: The End of the Beginning

2084 presents a stark and sobering vision of what our near future could become if we fail to address the urgent crises facing our world, environmental degradation, political polarization, and unregulated political decisions.
While 2084 envisioned a world healed and united, 2084 flips the lens to reveal the devastating consequences of inaction.

This book isn’t just a work of fiction, it’s a mirror held up to today’s world.

The question is what happens if humanity continues down its current path of neglect and division? 
You don’t need to wonder, Howard Atkins provides the answer in 2084.

Historical and Societal Context

Set in the not-so-distant future, 2084 is deeply rooted in our present-day reality. The book imagines a world unraveling due to the very issues we grapple with now:

  • Climate change and resource depletion
  • Mass migrations and rising global conflicts
  • Authoritarian governments and surveillance overreach
  • Human right exploitation and erosion of personal freedom
    The events of 2084 are unsettling because they feel so close, because they are.

Key Events & Turning Points in 2084

  • Global environmental collapse caused by relentless exploitation of natural resources, leading to widespread conflict over food and water.
  • Authoritarian regimes rise to power, using fear and propaganda to suppress dissent and control the population.
  • A scattered resistance movement inspired by the ideals of 2084, struggling to unite and take a stand against systemic decay.

2084 offers a brutally honest glimpse into a world on the brink, one that may already be in motion.


Author’s Interpretation & Themes

Atkins presents a carefully balanced narrative, blending speculative fiction with grounded realism. His analysis is supported by historical precedent, political theory, and current events. He challenges readers to consider their own roles in shaping the future and whether we can still alter the course of history.

Rather than offering blind hope or despair, 2084 aims to inspire action through awareness.

Strengths of 2084

  • Deeply relevant themes tied to current global crises
  • Rich world-building grounded in reality
  • Urgent, emotionally compelling storytelling
  • Clear philosophical contrast to 2084
  • A powerful call to self-reflection and societal accountability


A Conversation Starter, Not Just a Novel

2084 goes beyond entertainment. It opens the door for meaningful dialogue about the future we’re creating and how we can still change it.

Risks and challenges

Risks and Challenges Creating 2084: A Wake-Up Call for Humanity is a deeply personal and ambitious project, driven by a commitment to spark reflection and inspire change. However, bringing this book to life involves several risks and challenges, which I am prepared to address with transparency and dedication. Production Timelines: Writing, editing, and publishing a thought-provoking novel like 2084 requires meticulous attention to detail to ensure the narrative resonates with its intended impact. Unforeseen delays in the editing process, design of the book, or printing could push back delivery timelines. To mitigate this, I’ve established a clear schedule with experienced editors and a reliable printing partner, and I’ve built buffer time into the production timeline to account for potential setbacks. Funding Goals: The success of this Kickstarter campaign is critical to covering the costs of professional editing, cover design, printing, and distribution. If the campaign does not meet its funding goal, it could limit the scope of the project or delay its completion. To address this, I’ve carefully calculated the budget to prioritize essential elements and have contingency plans, such as exploring alternative funding or scaling production to match funds raised, while maintaining the quality of the final product. Balancing Vision and Accessibility: 2084 aims to blend complex themes of futurism, social critique, and environmental urgency into an accessible and engaging narrative. Striking this balance during the writing and revision process is challenging, as it requires ensuring the story resonates with a broad audience without diluting its message. I’m working closely with beta readers and editors to refine the manuscript, ensuring it remains true to its vision while being relatable and impactful for readers. External Factors: Unpredictable events, such as supply chain disruptions or global economic challenges, could affect printing or shipping timelines. To minimize these risks, I’ve partnered with reputable suppliers with proven track records and will maintain open communication with backers about any potential delays, providing regular updates on progress. My experience as an author, combined with the support of a dedicated team, gives me confidence in overcoming these challenges. I am fully committed to delivering 2084: A Wake-Up Call for Humanity as a high-quality, thought-provoking work that honors the trust of my backers. Thank you for joining me on this journey to confront the future and inspire change.
