Story

Meet DEFENDER — the World’s 1st Super.
Track your items, send instant SOS alerts, protect yourself in emergencies, and even snap photos remotely. Compact, powerful, and made for real-life protection.

Crafted into a sleek, ultra-light design, Defender delivers 4-in-1 functionality to simplify your day and safeguard every step. From tracking lost items to sounding alerts, lighting your way, and snapping photos remotely — it’s the compact companion built for your safety, convenience, and peace of mind.

Defender features multiple light modes — from bright flashes for emergency alerts to soft glows for nighttime visibility. Whether you're out for a run, walking alone at night, or simply need a quick flashlight, Defender lights the way to keep you seen and safe.

Defender features a glow-in-the-dark casing for fast retrieval in low light. Whether you're fumbling in a tent, a dark room, or your bag, it’s always easy to find. No more wasting time searching when you need it most. Visibility meets design in a practical finish.

Snap perfect selfies and group photos from any angle using Defender’s Bluetooth shutter. Effortlessly switch positions, frame your shots, and control the timing without touching your phone. Whether you're shooting alone or with friends, enjoy a smoother, hands-free photography experience.

Recharge with a USB-C cable and enjoy up to 3 months of use per charge. Say goodbye to frequent charging or bulky batteries. It’s made for people on the go—reliable, low-maintenance, and always ready. Just one charge powers weeks of peace of mind.
