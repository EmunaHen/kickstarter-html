Story

Final moment was born of the pandemic when we lost so many, in my own life I was facing my own mortality, my own life in pieces I decided to change my perspective, rearrange the pieces and use them to create something that could change the world.

I've had epilepsy since I was a child and for much of my life that has defined its story, it still does and one day will bring about my end, I've exceeded my life expectancy by a few years now but at the time I conceived of Final Moment I had a fascination with cloud storage and having just learned about my shorter than average life expectancy I wanted a memorial that reflected my unique life story and shared with those left behind what I loved and learned along the way. A memorial that reflected my unique life journey, something I felt everyone deserves, but not everyone gets.

As I worked on myself and made a plan for what I wanted to do with the remainder of my life I came to the realization that at my core I wanted to help others at scale which is something that's very hard to do when your poor, in fact it's hard to do most things when your poor so I knew I had to change that.

I created a plan to change that and utilizing 2 stimulus checks I founded Urban Alchemy soap and skincare taking an idea I conceived at the age of 15 and using it to create a foundation on which Final Moment would be built, A company with a focus on making the world better. I knew I had to think big and after two years focusing on Urban Alchemy creating what became the world's largest selection of shaped soap bars and finding it hard to scale, I knew I had to pivot to Final Moment.

Have you ever attended a funeral where you realized the person who was being remembered didn't really match the person you knew?

Final moment is unique, A cloud-based video application where you record the things you want to say, the things you want to share and the best moments of your life as you go through it. It offers a wide range of features including message delivery after death, remote viewing of Final Moment funerals via the app and stored moments where you can share what made your story unique with those you have left behind. Perhaps the most interesting aspect of it is that it serves as a in our own words history of who we are as a people the first of its kind to bring perspective to those who continue when we are gone, so that no one's story is lost. 

Final moment has been designed to meet a lot of needs but more importantly it's designed to make enough money after launching to be self-sustaining, produce profit for investors and create a large philanthropic wing to address many of the problems we all face in this world, taking on hunger, homelessness and empowering others to chase their own dreams. 

 At the backend it simplifies the end-of-life planning process, unifies the end-of-life industry and improves quality of service while reducing cost for providers with a dedicated app for providers to download users' final moment, activate after death features and connect with new users.

Final moment began development in March of 2024 and has completed its beta phase, and I am currently working at getting it on the apple and play stores, I am running this Kickstarter to help me cover the cost associated with doing that and any other expenses that come with launch.

Final moment brings a lot of value to the world and in the journey bringing it to the world I've had to endure a lot of hardship and suffering including homelessness to make sure that happens. I have risen to the challenge and thanks to a few believers who have supported me I am approaching launch on the Apple and Play stores within the next two months. 

In the course of this journey, I've done far more than anyone in my family ever expected writing my first book with shark tank founder Kevin Harrington telling my audacious story to the world and letting the world know that Final moment is coming and it's here to make a profound impact on the world.

Today I invite you to be a part of my story by supporting this project, helping me create the changes we all want to see in this world and setting a standard for others in the business world to give back to the people with the understanding that with great power comes great responsibility.
