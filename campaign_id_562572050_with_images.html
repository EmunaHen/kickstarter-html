Story

What is Mailbolt.io?

Mailbolt.io is a cutting-edge SaaS platform designed to give you full control over your email marketing—without restrictions, blocks, or hidden limitations. Unlike traditional platforms like Mailchimp, Mailjet, SendGrid or Brevo, Mailbolt lets you send emails directly from your own servers, ensuring 100% autonomy, privacy, and deliverability.

This is not just another email sender. It’s a new way to own your email campaigns, built on real user needs and frustrations with existing tools.

From Email Verifier to Full SaaS: How It All Started

I'm Raúl Vega Domingo, CEO of Heartize™, a fullstack designer and developer with over a decade of experience creating scalable, creative, and impactful digital tools.

Mailbolt was born from the success of our free online email verifier, a simple but powerful tool that helped thousands clean and validate their email lists. The demand was so overwhelming, we decided to take things further.

Over time, and thanks to feedback from real users, Mailbolt has grown into something much bigger:
A full SaaS solution that combines powerful email verification with autonomous sending capabilities, making it easier than ever to run campaigns without relying on third-party systems.

Risks and challenges

Like any ambitious tech project, Mailbolt.io faces certain challenges—but we’ve anticipated them and are prepared to respond with agility and transparency. 1. Scalability under demand If the project attracts more users than expected, infrastructure costs and delivery optimization may spike. We are prepared to scale progressively using load-balanced servers and proven cloud systems. 2. Third-party service limitations While Mailbolt.io lets users send emails through their own servers, we still rely on some third-party APIs (DNS checkers, blacklist lookups, etc.). If any of these services change policies, we will adapt quickly with in-house fallbacks. 3. Evolving compliance requirements Laws and regulations around email marketing (GDPR, CAN-SPAM, etc.) may evolve. We’re committed to ensuring Mailbolt.io stays compliant, and we’ll update the platform accordingly. 4. Feature delivery timeline While the core features are stable, some advanced features (e.g., detailed analytics, autoresponder logic) are still being finalized. We’ll always keep backers informed of milestones and delays. 5. User misuse Because we offer autonomy and power, there’s a risk of users misusing Mailbolt.io for spam. We’ve integrated safeguards and will enforce strict use policies to preserve our IP reputation and fairness. Our Promise: We’ve already built and validated the foundation of Mailbolt.io. With your support, we’ll deliver a powerful, ethical, and user-first platform—with transparency at every step.
