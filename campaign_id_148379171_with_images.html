Story

Cerakey's ZEN75 is a big step into the world of low-profile mechanical keyboards, offering a typing experience that blends minimal aesthetics with deep and satisfying acoustics.
The ZEN75 is a compact 75% keyboard with ceramic keycaps and durable PC stems. Its gasket mount reduces vibrations, enhancing the deep, pure sound of each keystroke. Combined with Gateron Low Profile linear switches, it offers smooth, consistent typing. Whether for work or play, the ZEN75 delivers a premium feel and amazing sound in a sleek design.
 

ZEN75 delivers a unique typing experience with its ceramic keycaps, producing a deep, resonant sound that is both satisfying and pleasant to the ear.

Our ceramic keycaps integrate advanced glazing techniques with the inherent properties of ceramic materials. This combination results in a silky smooth texture and a naturally cool touch, significantly enhancing your tactile comfort.

Ceramic's superior light transmittance enables bright, vivid RGB illumination on the Zen75. With over 40 color effects and adjustable intensity, you can effortlessly set your preferred lighting mode to match your mood.

Made from industrial-grade alumina ceramic, the Zen75 ceramic keycaps are durable, impact-resistant, and resistant to oil, stains, and dust, ensuring long-lasting freshness.

The low-profile ceramic keycaps come with a durable plastic stem design, ensuring optimal compatibility with low-profile switches and enhanced stability.

The Zen75 uses a gasket mount structure with a PC plate, IXPE, and Poron foam isolating switch vibrations while enhancing keystroke smoothness, delivering a smoother and comfortable typing experience.

The ZEN75's full hot-swappable design lets you replace switches easily and customize your typing experience to meet your personal preferences.

The ZEN75 features a dual-material construction with a sleek aluminum top case and a transparent PC base, seamlessly combining durability and modern aesthetics.

The ZEN75 has a dual-angle adjustable kickstand (5.5°and 7.6°), which is ergonomically designed to enhance comfort and boost productivity.

Seamlessly transition between ​USB-C Wired (Type-C), ​2.4GHz Wireless, and ​Bluetooth 5.0 via its ​tri-mode switch button.

ZEN75 seamlessly integrates with Windows, Mac, iOS, and Android. Effortlessly switch between phones, tablets, and computers for a flawless multi-device experience.
Experience the seamless multi-device compatibility with quick, effective, and stable connections designed for your ease.
Unlock the total command of your ZEN75 via QMK/VIA, enabling full key remapping, dynamic function layers, backlight customization, and creating macro/shortcut commands.

CERAKEY was born from a passion for keyboards and a vision to redefine their potential. Recognizing the limitations of plastic, our team of enthusiasts turned to ceramics, leveraging their unique properties to create a new standard in typing. 

In 2022, our first ceramic keycap set on Kickstarter became the most-funded keycap project ever, praised for its unique texture and sound. This success inspired our community and led to more innovations, like the Blue and White Ceramic Keycaps, Lacquered Ceramic Keycaps, and the Panda Keyboard, all of which were highly acclaimed.

Inspired by our success, we embarked on a new challenge: ZEN75, the world's first low-profile mechanical keyboard with ceramic keycaps. This keyboard is a testament to our commitment to innovation, combining sleek design with the enduring quality and rich acoustics of ceramic.
