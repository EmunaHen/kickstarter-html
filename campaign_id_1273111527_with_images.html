Story

Why Are We Building This?

Introduction:

Welcome to Web3C— Your Gateway to Crypto Intelligence! In the ever-evolving world of cryptocurrencies and Web3 technologies, our mission at Web3C is to empower individuals by creating a secure, mobile, accessible, and resourceful platform for the Web3 (Crypto and NFTs) community.

Our Vision:

Web3C aims to be the central hub for real-time updates, secure investment tracking, accessible education, and community engagement within the Web3 space. We are building Web3C to provide:

  • Real-Time Updates: Stay informed of the latest news and developments in the crypto and NFT world.
  • Crypto Insights: Access crypto predictions, sentiment analysis, and other valuable insights in one place, offering a comprehensive market view. This feature will be available as a premium offering.
  • Accessible Education: Learn about Web3 technologies through Gimo AI, our dedicated AI Web3 degen, designed to help you navigate the Web3 world with ease.
  • Community Engagement: Connect with like-minded individuals and experts to share knowledge and grow together in the Web3 ecosystem.

What We've Built So Far?

Milestone Achievement:

Since our launch on October 11, 2023, Web3C has made significant strides in the Web3 community:

  • Achieved 396 downloads.
  • Garnered 297k Apple app store impressions.
  • Secured our first 3 paid subscribers.

Current Core Features:

  • Real-Time Web3 News Updates: Stay updated with the latest articles and videos in the Web3 and crypto world.
  • Gimo - The AI Chatbot: Get personalized education and investment guidance with our AI-powered assistant.
  • Initial Crypto Prediction: An advanced algorithm that analyzes both market data and social sentiment to forecast crypto movements — offering clear signals to Buy, Sell, or Hold on Bitcoin.

What We Want to Build?

Focus Areas:

1. Create an Android App for Universal Access:

  • Expand our platform to Android, ensuring universal access to real-time Web3 updates and features.

2. Advanced Crypto Insight Feature:

  • Unlock a dedicated insights page featuring crypto predictions, sentiment analysis, and key market signals — designed to help users make smarter, data-informed decisions.

2. Engaging Social Feature:

  • Launch an engaging social feature within the app, creating a dynamic community where users can share insights, discuss trends, and learn from each other.

3. Amplifying Awareness and Marketing Efforts:

  • Increase marketing initiatives to raise awareness.
  • Engage with the Web3 community through X (formerly Twitter) and Reddit to foster organic interest.
  • Run targeted ads and promotions to grow our user base and enhance our platform's visibility.
