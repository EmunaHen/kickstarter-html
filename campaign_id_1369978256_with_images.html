Story

Compact, powerful, and designed for versatility, SparkO is your go-to lighting solution for camping, photography, and everyday tasks. With adjustable brightness, a long-lasting battery, and multiple mounting options, it’s ideal for both professionals and casual users. Light up your world—hands-free!

No More Handheld Burden

Tired of juggling flashlights while trying to work, create, or explore? SparkO is your ultimate hands-free lighting solution, designed to shine exactly where you need it, so you can focus on what matters most.

Clip it, magnetize it, stand it up, or capture the moment—SparkO transforms instantly to deliver perfect lighting for every situation.

SparkO is born to redefine hands-free lighting. By fusing cutting-edge technology with thoughtful design, we challenge every stereotype of traditional illumination. Transcending age and gender boundaries, SparkO empowers everyone to discover their own light—your way, your story.

Our compact multi-mode light features CSP LEDs delivering 300 lumens with adjustable color temperature (2700-6500K) and four lighting options, offering versatile illumination for any scenario in a portable design.

True Color Light (CRI 95+) reveals authentic colors with daylight accuracy, making it perfect for precision tasks such as photography, makeup, art, and detailed repairs.

SparkO's breakthrough silicone-embedded wiring withstands endless bends while keeping perfect conductivity. This exclusive, flexible circuit design offers exceptional durability and a premium, soft-touch feel.

SparkO delivers exceptional longevity without sacrificing style. Every curve and joint reflects our commitment to precision engineering, because true quality stands the test of time.
