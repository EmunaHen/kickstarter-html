Story

Tiny Tire Pump 200 is Flextail's brand new top-tier product. Packing robust performance into a compact design, this game-changer meets all your cycling inflation needs. 

At the same time, we creatively put the taillight function into the bicycle pump, which not only protects you from accidents, but also ensures your safety when riding at night. Truly become your riding companion.

Ever had a flat tire in the middle of nowhere? Sure, you can use a manual pump or CO2 pump - but it often ends up being too much trouble, too inconvenient, or just plain exhausting.Now, these problems have all been solved.

With Tiny Tire Pump 200, all you need to do is just take out the pump, click, and let it inflate — no effort, no stress.

Stop counting seconds. The Tiny Tire Pump flies through inflation at double speed. Your tires are pumped before you even notice.

We have conducted comprehensive tests on different tire models, optimized the air pump's performance under both low and high pressure conditions, ensuring you have the best experience.

While others struggle with the basics, Flextail leads with 10 years of expertise and exclusive high-pressure piston pump tech—something only a few brands in the world can claim.

Powered by a precision brushless motor and a short-stroke, large-bore piston, the Tiny Tire Pump 200 can hit 130 PSI. Less friction, more efficiency, and way fewer breakdowns—this is pro-level inflation, built to perform.

With the product's LED digital display screen and many thoughtful features, you can control tire pressure more accurately and enjoy a more professional and easier inflation experience.

In addition to the above functions, we hope that Tiny Tire Pump 200 can provide you with more help and support. Therefore, we innovated in the structure of the product and integrated the taillight function into the pump. 

From then on, carrying the pump is no longer a burden, but makes it your riding companion.

Taillights are important accessories for riding users, so we have put a lot of effort into them. 3 light modes and up to 12 hours of battery life make your night riding safer.

There is no need to worry about the tail light function consuming too much battery power. Even if you use the tail light function for 4-6 hours, it only consumes half the battery of the Tiny Tire Pump 200, which is still enough to help you deal with emergencies.

Despite its powerful performance and rich features, we have successfully reduced the weight of the Tiny Tire Pump 200 to an astonishing 130g. 

Considering that you can replace the existing traditional tire pump and tail light combination with it, you have the opportunity to reduce the weight by up to 60%.

In addition to weight, how to carry it is also an important issue. We have designed and included an adapter, allowing it to be directly installed on your seat tube, no longer occupying any additional space.

The compact size of the Tiny Tire Pump 200 allows you to put it in almost any pocket without barely feeling its presence.

Though compact and lightweight, the Tiny Tire Pump 200 is tough enough to work in harsh environments with its IPX4 waterproof rating, so whether you're on a hot summer road trip or a rainy night's ride, it's ready to accompany you.

The stable use of the product is crucial, especially in emergency situations, where you need reliable equipment. Unlike other brands' portable air pumps, which always face a high failure rate and significant performance degradation, our product boasts excellent stability, which is why we have confidence in offering a 2-year warranty.

High temps kill performance. The Tiny Tire Pump 200 uses a unique centrifugal cooling system to circulate heat away— protecting core parts and ensuring stable, long-lasting use.This is why we are confident in the performance and stability.

During the development process, we have conducted 50 + tests on the products, just to make the products meet our stringent quality and stability standards, so that you can use them with confidence.

Our premium battery module is the core of its high performance. High-density battery for compact size, low power loss, and long-lasting, stable performance.
