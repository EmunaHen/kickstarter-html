Story

Time to Redefine the Shower

It’s time to break free from monotony, inefficiency, and the lack of innovation and new experiences in our showers. 

Where to start?

A common solution is simply replacing the shower head — addressing only the tip of the iceberg. If you truly want to build in efficiency and deliver new experiences from the ground up, you need to replace the water heater, adapt the plumbing, and install additional equipment like lights and sensors. But that quickly becomes expensive and difficult to modify.

Blanya OpenShower changes all that.

Elevate your shower experience without spending thousands of dollars or touching anything in your bathroom. Make your shower the star of your bath with our plug-and-play, all-in-one system, ready to use right out of the box. No complicated setup. No hidden costs. Just a simple, complete solution.

Vision:

Owning a premium shower system should be as simple as buying a coffee machine or a vacuum — a single solution that brings together sophistication, efficiency, and innovation to help you control your comfort, your routine, and your resources.

Join us in redefining what a shower can be.

The New Water Saving

1.- Water Limit: 

Showerheads reduce waste. Water limits eliminate it.

With a 19-liter (5-gallon) tank, Blanya OS introduces a natural limit that encourages a reconfiguration of habits without sacrificing comfort. 

2.- Hands-Free:

No Water, No Time, No Focus Wasted

With a fixed water supply, every drop counts — and every distraction needs to go. Blanya OS is designed to eliminate the most common sources of waste and let you focus fully on your shower experience.

  • No wasted water while heating
    Water is pre-heated and stored in the tank. It only travels 2 meters to the showerhead, so you get hot water instantly — no need to let it run.
  • No need to adjust temperature
    Forget fiddling with knobs. The system delivers your perfect temperature instantly. Just press the button and enjoy.
  • No manual pressure control
    The system automatically adjusts water pressure based on your profile. There's no need to pause, tweak, or touch anything mid-shower.

3.- Profiles: 

The best way to save water is to plan ahead — and enjoy doing it.

With thousands of possible combinations, Profiles are your personal memory presets. They coordinate water pressure, temperature, and even light intensity and color — giving you full control with zero effort during your shower.

Take the “Barista Profile”, for example:
It delivers full pressure for the first minute — perfect for rinsing — then reduces the flow while you soap up, increasing it again in a loop during the first five minutes, and maintains a steady flow for the rest of the shower. Light colors shift along with the pressure, helping you intuitively follow each stage of your routine.

Create your own, or choose from curated presets. As we discover amazing combinations, we’ll share them with you.

4.- Invisible Waste Ends Here

Now, you can finally see what was once hidden.

Blanya’s transparent cylinder reveals how water is used at every stage of your shower — creating a new sense of awareness and connection.

Plus, the top display shows real-time information about your shower that you’ve probably never seen before.

Saving Energy

From day one, Blanya OS heats only the exact amount of water you need — at the precise temperature you want — giving you a perfectly comfortable shower with less waste, lower energy bills, and a smaller environmental footprint.

Want to save even more? Switch between Profiles or slightly lower your shower temperature — even by 1°C — and, thanks to the built-in sensor, you’ll always know exactly how hot it is, helping you save by using less water and heating it less. Review how the calculations are done here.

Modularity

The system follows a Right to Repair philosophy.

Modularity means savings — broken or outdated parts can be reprinted or updated whenever needed.

The electronics are designed for easy replacement and upgrades through separate modules like the main board, battery, and power unit. Some components follow a LEGO-like structure, making repairs even more accessible.

For non-3D printed parts, standard, universally available components were prioritized — so you can find replacements.

Safety & Operation

Safety: 

The shower only needs to be connected to main power to heat the water. For safety, it cannot be used while plugged in. Once the water is heated, two batteries power your shower experience.

A built-in temperature sensor monitors the water temperature, and you can also check it by simply touching the water tank. All electronics are isolated from the water to support durability and a long lifespan.

Operation:

  • Fill the tank with water.
  • Connect to main power to heat the water.
  • Press the button once and wait until the target temperature is reached.
  • Disconnect the shower from main power.
  • Press the button again to start the shower

One Place for Sensations

See:

Most of the shower is made of transparent materials, allowing you to observe both the water quality and the amount of water being used — encouraging awareness and conservation. This design also makes it easy to visually inspect the structure and spot any residue buildup. 

Complete your experience with the calming effect of bubbles, illuminated by an LED light at the bottom of the tank, offering a wide range of colors to set a soothing and personalized mood. The bubbling water creates gentle waves that reflect light across your entire bathroom.

Hear:

The soft sound of hundreds of bubbles enhances the relaxing atmosphere. The motor, positioned at the center of the electronics cylinder, is carefully isolated by a 3 mm acrylic wall. During the shower, it operates quietly, ensuring a peaceful and uninterrupted experience.

Touch:

You can rely on the displayed temperature — or simply touch the water tank, which maintains a uniform warmth thanks to the circulating bubbles. You can also feel the fine droplets from the shower head at the pressure you prefer, ensuring every moment is tailored to your comfort.

Expand Your Experience

Sensors & Data:

This version includes two sensors at the bottom: one for pressure and one for temperature. 

All readings are accessible, offering valuable insights to better understand and optimize your shower routine.

There’s also space reserved for an additional pressure sensor at the top, allowing for future upgrades or more advanced monitoring.

3D Printed:

Most parts are 3D printed, giving you the freedom to improve or replace components as you like — allowing your shower to evolve with your needs.

UI & UX:

Mounted at the top of the shower, a 1.28-inch round, watch-style LCD screen displays real-time information.

  • 1. Water Temperature: Displays the current water temperature in Celsius.
  • 2. Shower Profile: This icon changes based on the selected profile.
  • 3. P.Bott: Measures water level before and pressure during the shower.
  • 4. Start/Pause: Displays “Start” before and “Pause” during the shower.
  • 5. Battery: Indicates the current battery level charge status.
  • 6. Bluetooth: Gray means not connected; yellow indicates a successful connection.
  • 7. Desired Temp: The target temperature for your shower.

Shape:

An elegant, uniform cylinder is designed to minimize its footprint while combining form and function. The entire shower uses a single pipe diameter — making it easier to build, repair, and replace, since one size fits both the electronics and the water tank.

Filling the Tank

The first step to start using your shower, which is why there's a dedicated section explaining the two available methods.

Hose: The fastest method. You can choose the water source—for example, by attaching a quick connector to a showerhead or using an adapter like the Water Bandit on a faucet.

Funnel: If a hose isn’t available, you can fill the tank manually with a funnel. It works, but it’s slower.

Prototype Specs

Other Details

  • Batteries: 2x 18650
  • Battery Charging: Happens while the shower is connected to main power.
  • Heating Element: The base power is 1000 W, but it can reach up to 2000 W.
  • Connectivity: Bluetooth-enabled. Settings can be adjusted using the nRF Connect app on your smartphone.
  • Working Principle: Pressurization.
  • Bubbles: Air is added from the bottom of the tank to create bubbles, pressurize the system, mix with the water, and generate soothing waves and sounds for a relaxing experience..
  • Shower Arm: The shower arm length is a standard 82 cm but can be modified based on your height or preferred measurements.
  • Water Flow Range: 1.3 – 1.8 L/min
  • Operating Pressure: 0.5 - 1 psi
