Story

We believe that having a pet isn't just about daily care-it's about the love of home. Just like cooking for family is more than just filling stomachs, it's a way to express love with a sense of ritual.

We noticed that more and more pet parents enjoy making cakes, cookies, and treats for their furry companions, yet there is no kitchen appliance on the market truly tailored for pets. This sparked an idea: Why not create a dedicated pet treats maker?

A multi-functional appliance that can blend, air-dry, steam, simmer, cool, defrost and self clean, combining all-in-one capabilities to meet diverse food textures and pet dietary needs. It empowers pet parents to effortlessly prepare delicious treats for their beloved pets.

All Food-Contact Materials are Completely BPA-Free.

  • Blending Cup---Tritan (BPA-FREE)
  • Air-Dry Sheet---Food Grade AS Material (BPA-FREE)
  • Steaming Basket---Food Grade PP Material (BPA-FREE)

Every bite is a moment of pure joy for your pet. From the soft texture to the rich smell, each bite is simply irresistible. Treat them to their favorite flavors and make every day filled with happiness and satisfaction.
