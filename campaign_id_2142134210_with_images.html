Why Choose PaintStation?

1. Purpose-Built Design:

PaintStation is Design exclusively for model making enthusiasts, providing everything you need in one convenient set up. Whether you’re painting minis, airplanes, or intricate models, we’ve got you covered!

2.Air Filtering Ventilation System:

Breathe easy with our high-performance air filtering system. It effectively reduces harmful fumes and particles, ensuring a safe and healthy workspace while you paint. The system features two types of filters: a Carbon + PM2.5 filter for use during painting, and a PM2.5 +VOCs filter that removes chemicals and odors while your models dry. Enjoy peace of mind knowing that you're working in a cleaner environment!

Compressed Recycled P.E.T. Board for Airbrush Painting

The compressed recycled P.E.T. board is an excellent material for airbrush painting due to its superior paint adhesion. Paint particles stick effectively to the board, ensuring that the application is seamless. Additionally, cleaning is hassle-free; a simple wipe with a wet cloth is all that’s needed to remove any paint residue.

Our P.E.T. boards are designed for durability, lasting for months of extensive painting sessions. When it comes time for a replacement, the process is straightforward and efficient.

3. Dust-Free Drying Chamber:
 

Protect your masterpieces with our dedicated dust-free drying chamber. Keep your painted models pristine as they dry! The PM2.5 + VOCs filter ensures the space is free from dust and debris that could ruin your hard work. The chamber also features temperature control and fan airflow control, allowing you to create the perfect drying environment for optimal results. 

Inside the Dust-Free Drying Chamber, you can easily attach your parts using our magnetic clips. Enjoy peace of mind knowing that your creations are cared for from start to finish!

Built-in 6000K Daylight LED Strip 

The PaintStation is equipped with a built-in 6000K daylight LED strip that enhances the painting experience by providing optimal color accuracy and visibility. This lighting setup ensures the best observation for dust and imperfections, allowing for precise detailing. Additionally, the design eliminates hand shadows during airbrushing, ensuring a clear view of the artwork throughout the process.

4. Painting Preparation Deck:

No more searching for your brushes, paints, and tools! PaintStation features a built-in organizing station to keep all your painting supplies at your fingertips—everything in its place for maximum efficiency. Plus, the modular design of the organizer in the drawer allows users to set it up according to their preferences, giving you the flexibility to customize your workspace as you see fit!

Modular Design Paint and Tools Organizer

Experience maximum flexibility with our modular design paint and tools organizer! Tailor your setup to fit your unique needs and preferences, ensuring that all your painting supplies and tools are easily accessible and neatly arranged.

6. Extended Work Surface:

"Need to paint larger objects? No worries. Our extended work surface provides plenty of space to handle big projects comfortably, turning your workspace into a versatile zone for all sizes of models."

6. Convenient Lower Storage Space:

Tired of clutter? Our design includes a lower storage area specifically for your air compressor and other equipment, allowing for easy access while keeping your workspace clean and organized. Inside the storage unit, the sliding shelf provides effortless access to all your tools, making organization simple and efficient!

7. Polishing and Assembling Station:

The PaintStation isn’t just a paint booth—it also serves as a workstation for polishing and assembling your models. This flexibility allows you to streamline your workflow and make the most out of your creative time.

Development and production process
 

The PaintStation is designed and developed at UFM Design Studio in Hong Kong, led by our chief designers, Arb Leung and Ritchie Yau. Arb Leung is the former lead designer for Steelcase, and Ritchie Yau is a former product designer for Herman Miller, both with over 20 years of experience in furniture design and production. The manufacturing takes place at our production facility in Dongguan, China, which specializes in office furniture and boasts 30 years of experience in providing high-quality steel furniture to renowned brands such as Steelcase and Herman Miller.

Long-lasting design

PaintStation is designed with longevity in mind. We utilize high-quality powder-coated steel, ensuring that your paint booth not only looks great but also stands up to the test of time. This robust material offers exceptional resistance to scratches, corrosion, and wear, making it the perfect choice for hobbyists who want a reliable and durable workspace.

Sustainable materials

At PaintStation, we believe in sustainability without compromising on quality. That’s why we incorporate recycled PET panels into our design. These panels not only contribute to a reduced environmental footprint but also provide outstanding durability and flexibility.
