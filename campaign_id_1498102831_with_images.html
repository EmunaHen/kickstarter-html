We know that a good tool is not just a collection of features, every detail of it should consider the infinite possibilities of outdoor exploration. That's why strong/lightweight/durable materials are a must for Ti-ALLY, and we hope Ti-ALLY accompanies you on your journey.

We have equipped this tool with interchangeable accessories that allow it to instantly transform into different functions depending on your needs. One tool for all your exploration needs. It will be your most capable partner, ready to solve every challenge you face.

With the adjusting ring, you can easily adjust the telescopic length of the tool to make it longer and more powerful, or shorter and more flexible. When changing tools, you can simply unscrew the screw on the adjusting ring and switch between different functional tools as you wish - Ti-ALLY will always provide you with a precise solution.

Carburised titanium alloy knife:

The black body of the knife is cold and tough, its surface has been treated with a special carburising process to have a higher hardness, cutting as if the blade cuts through the air, easily solving all cutting tasks in the field.

TIMASCUS (Titanium Damascus)bayonet：

Deep black and grey intertwine as if the strength of the metal is jumping on the blade, and TIMASCUS brings a unique texture that is the perfect combination of toughness and art.

TIMASCUS(Titanium Damascus)machete：

Still with the TIMASCUS texture, a mix of dark and light grey, it's easy to handle no matter what it's chopped up to.

Crowbars/wrenches/bottle openers：

When strength is needed most, Ti-Ally's crowbar and spanner will be your solid backbone. Pry open a can, crack open a beer or tighten a bolt, it's as solid and reliable as one of nature's pillars.

Saw:

The saw tool is designed to tackle tasks that require fine cutting, whether it's chopping branches, repairing gear, or cutting through other materials, Ti-Ally's saw can handle it with ease. Its titanium alloy material delivers excellent durability, and the teeth of the saw are sharp enough to complete cutting tasks efficiently, so you're ready to tackle whatever comes your way in the outdoors.

Comb:

In the wilderness, where warm details are often needed, Ti-Ally's combs bring a touch of ease and comfort. Whether you're camping in the wind or in the extreme cold of the morning, comb yourself and stay fresh to feel confident every time you set out.

The versatility of the Ti-Ally is not limited to the many variations of the blade, but the main body itself has been cleverly designed to extend the versatility!

Ruler：

The Ti-Ally is also a ready-to-use precision tool. Measuring items in the field, Ti-Ally's ruler will be ready to help you with your measuring needs.

Window Breaker：

In a sudden emergency, the window breaker hammer can help you quickly escape or save yourself. Cleverly designed on the handle and ready to go, it can open up a way out for you in critical moments.

Screwdriver：

The bottom of Ti-ALLY is equipped with a screwdriver fixing hole, you can fit different types of screwdriver heads, so that you can easily perform all kinds of repairs or disassemble/assemble all kinds of complicated parts. A small screwdriver tool box can meet your various needs, and from now on you don't need to prepare various complicated models of screwdrivers.
