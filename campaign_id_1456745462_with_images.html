Story

When traveling, most people carry both a toothbrush and toothpaste. But traditional toothbrushes often leave the bristles exposed, touching everything in your bag — a perfect setup for bacteria. It’s unhygienic and gross.
Full-size toothpaste takes up too much space and adds unnecessary weight. And hotel toothbrushes? Cheap, flimsy, and ruin the brushing experience.

That’s why we created Dentrekx — the next-generation travel toothbrush. It’s compact, portable, and hygienic, built to support healthier teeth on the go — a perfect fit for any journey.
We also invented the world’s first magnetic toothpaste pod — a true original that redefines convenience for travelers.

Dentrekx is ultra-compact and easy to carry — it fits right in your pocket, or you can place it anywhere when you're on the go.

Stowable Design

Just pull the silicone loop to remove the brush handle, snap it into place — and you're ready to brush!

Magnetic Toothpaste Pod

It was created for ultimate portability — with it, there's no need to carry bulky toothpaste anymore. The Magnetic Toothpaste Pod attaches to Dentrekx, offering simple operation and long-lasting reusability. After it's used up, just refill it with your favorite toothpaste.

The Magnetic Toothpaste Pod has a highly precise structure. Thanks to its internal spiral mechanism, you can easily dispense toothpaste by simply twisting the bottom knob — a simpler way to squeeze toothpaste. 

Two twist methods. The bottom of the toothpaste pod features a serrated structure. When magnetically attached, it forms a firm connection — allowing for one-handed operation.

Usage Count. If used twice a day, one full Magnetic Toothpaste Pod can last up to two weeks. It covers the needs of 90% of short trips, so there’s no need to carry a bulky full-size toothpaste tube.

Once the pod is empty, you can add more toothpaste at any time — simply open the top cap and refill it with your favorite toothpaste before you go.

More Surprises from Dentrekx

The inner wall is coated with nano-silver, which releases silver ions to kill bacteria — effectively eliminating 99.9% of germs.

First-Ever Use of Titanium in a Toothbrush

Dentrekx is the first travel toothbrush to feature a titanium tube body — bringing ultra-durable, high-end performance to everyone. According to our test data, only the exceptional wear resistance of titanium allows Dentrekx to reach its full potential.

To meet different user needs, we also offer a limited quantity of aluminum versions.

The brush head on Dentrekx is fully replaceable. By swapping out the brush head, you can significantly extend the lifespan of Dentrekx — lasting dozens of times longer than a regular toothbrush.

We've also developed two specialized functional bristle options, available as add-ons. You can choose freely based on your personal needs.

If you're carrying an extra brush head, you can even share Dentrekx with others. After brushing, simply remove your own brush head and let someone else insert a new one — then it's ready to use again. The whole process is safe and hygienic — a surprisingly useful feature.
