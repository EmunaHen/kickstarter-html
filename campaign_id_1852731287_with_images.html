Story

More and more fitness enthusiasts and Pilates practitioners are finding it difficult to attend gym classes regularly due to their busy lifestyles. They're limited by time, cost, hygiene concerns, and restricted access to equipment.

Hyperformer seamlessly combines the structured training systems of professional gyms with the convenience of home workouts. Compact yet powerful, it integrates a Pilates reformer, modular strength training, and smart AI technology into one versatile machine. Supporting cardio, strength, stretching, sculpting, fat burning, flexibility, and balance exercises, it eliminates the need for multiple pieces of equipment.

Hyperformer’s strength training module simulates the free-weight zone of a professional gym. It supports a variety of resistance exercises such as squats, deadlifts, and presses, helping users, systematically build muscle strength and endurance, boost metabolism, and develop a stronger, more defined physique.

Building on classic Pilates moves, this module incorporates strength and cardio elements to intensify workouts. By engaging major muscle groups through compound movements, it increases heart rate and accelerates calorie burn, resulting in a toned and sculpted body.

This full-body cardio module simulates rowing and activates both upper and lower body simultaneously. It’s ideal for boosting heart rate, increasing endurance, and enhancing metabolic efficiency to support fat burning and stamina.

As the core Pilates bed module, the Reformer features a sliding platform and adjustable spring resistance to deliver precise stretching and controlled movements. It effectively improves flexibility, enhances joint mobility, relieves muscle stiffness, and strengthens posture and core control.

Designed specifically for glutes and lower body training, this module uses multi-angle resistance exercises like hip thrusts and leg presses to precisely activate the glute muscles. It helps users sculpt firm, lifted curves and improve overall lower body symmetry and posture.

Hyperformer features an advanced digital motor system that offers a 0–70kg resistance range with 0.5kg precision—far exceeding the limitations of traditional spring- or weight-plate-based equipment. Say goodbye to clunky manual adjustments and the hassle of swapping springs.

With no physical weights or springs to change, Hyperformer ensures smoother transitions, safer workouts, and a more personalized training experience. Hyperformer makes it effortless.

Hyperformer offers three intelligent resistance modes to elevate your strength training experience. These smart modes adapt to your goals, whether you’re aiming to build muscle, boost athletic performance, or improve muscle coordination.

Delivers a steady, uniform resistance throughout the movement, perfect for controlled muscle building and improving form.

Mimics the dynamic resistance of free weights—resistance increases as you move—helping develop explosive power and functional strength.

Focuses on the lengthening phase of muscle contraction, enhancing muscular endurance, control, and overall strength gains.

Hyperformer accurately simulates the natural resistance curve of water rowing, delivering a smooth and realistic rowing experience. It effectively improves aerobic capacity, builds endurance, and efficiently activates multiple muscle groups. Bringing the benefits of on-water rowing training right into your home environment.

Hyperformer’s AI Coach combines real-time motion tracking with video demonstrations to ensure proper form and reduce injury risk. It also offers smart guidance by tracking your strength curve and providing clear data visualization, allowing you to monitor progress accurately and optimize your training effectively.

Hyperformer’s powerhouse library packs 500+ on-demand classes designed to sculpt, energize, and transform. From heart-pumping HIIT and precision Pilates to zen-inducing yoga and core-crushing strength sessions.

Expands to 1.7㎡ (18.3 sq ft) and folds to 0.23㎡ (2.5 sq ft)—about the size of a small coffee table. Train at home anytime to save time and avoid gym hygiene risks.

Equipped with a noiseless motor and precision-machined aluminum alloy, it offers a premium feel, smooth and silent operation, perfectly blending into home environments.
