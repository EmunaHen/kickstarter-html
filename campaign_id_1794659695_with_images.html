Story

Introducing SnapMag: a magnetic camera kit that turns your smartphone into a pocket-sized cinematography rig.
Filters, grip, lighting, and control—SnapMag gives you the creative tools to shoot like a pro, without the bulk of a DSLR.

With SnapMag, every shot becomes a story, and your phone becomes your most powerful creative companion.

Most smartphone accessories just make shooting easier.
We wanted to make it truly professional.

Not just for iPhone 16 Pro Max - we offer perfectly fitted cases for every smartphone model.

Apple (iPhone Series) /  Samsung (Galaxy Series) / Huawei Series /  vivo Series/ Xiaomi Series / OPPO Series / Google Pixel Series

Aespresso is a company focused on the development of intelligent imaging equipment, with its own intellectual property rights. They have launched the world's first handheld multifunctional motorized slider, SMART-1, which has been granted intellectual property patents in several countries. The successful development of the SMART-1 handheld multifunctional motorized slider offers a new and efficient way for global video and film creators to move their cameras.

The Aespresso design team consists of followers of cutting-edge technology, as well as passionate enthusiasts of visual arts and classical music. They believe that the perfect presentation of these ultimate audio-visual arts is the ultimate goal for all imaging equipment researchers. These artistic essences touch the human spirit and provide people with profound enjoyment. Therefore, in future developments, Aespresso will continue to integrate aesthetic elements from the spiritual realm, such as mechanical aesthetics and fashion art, into every aspect of their imaging equipment design. Their goal is to provide global video creators with stylish, innovative, and intelligent products, contributing to cross-cultural and cross-regional artistic exchanges in various forms.
