Story

Intro

CableMate: Never fumble for the right cable again. Having so many electronic devices, I found it a hassle looking for the right cable every time I needed to recharge a low battery.

As someone who values organization and convenience, I created New York-based CableMate in collaboration with Robo-Design for help with design and development. CableMate is your all-in-one charging station to power all your different devices - no other cables necessary! CableMate contains three retractable charging cables (option of all three USB-C or USB-C plus Lightning plus Micro USB) that can be seamlessly stored when not in use. It also comes with adapters and a built-in storage compartment for more options in charging different devices.

3D Model

Below is an interactive 3D model of CableMate that can be viewed from all angles. The CableMate version in this model has a USB-C, Lightning, and Micro USB cable, but a version with all three cables being USB-C is also available.

Benefits

Imagine you are having a group of friends over. They want to listen to music, but your rechargeable speaker has zero battery power. Instead of rummaging around for a Micro USB cable you hardly use, simply plug your speaker into CableMate with the included cable or adapter. If a couple of your friends need to charge their phones, plug those into CableMate too - no worries about whether they have an Android or iPhone!

I have been using my CableMate prototypes for over a year now and cannot imagine switching back to single-purpose cables that become easily tangled.

Features

I have gone through 10+ prototypes to painstakingly develop a product that is easy to use, keeps your cables organized, and can charge any combination of devices.

Easy Setup - simply plug into any USB-A outlet

Three Retractable Cables easily store when not in use and extend up to 24 inches

Built-In Adapters to charge many types of devices

Storage for Adapters to prevent loss

Rubber Feet to prevent scratching and sliding

Optional Sticky Pad to keep in place on any surface

Fast charging & surge protection

Design

CableMate is thoroughly engineered to last a long time. Each of the three cables has its own reel within the main shell for extra protection. The cables can be easily pulled out and retracted many times without wearing.

Every Day Uses

CableMate can be seamlessly placed in a variety of locations to make charging more convenient - I personally use it on my desk, kitchen counter, when friends visit, and when I go on vacation.

Dimensions

CableMate is 3.70in (9.4cm) x 2.77in (7.0cm) x 2.77in (7.0cm) and weighs 8.3 ounces. The cables extend up to 23.62in (60cm).

Who we are

I am a CPA who currently works as a financial accountant in New York City. Prior to that, I worked at a start-up that owned and operated Amazon brands. I am very entrepreneurial and have always been interested in inventing new products.  

I was first inspired to create CableMate when friends would visit and there would not be enough cables nor the right type of cable to charge all their phones. I would also get annoyed having to search for less frequently used cables when I wanted to charge speakers, portable batteries, rechargeable lights, etc. Digging up a Micro USB or USB-C cable was particularly inconvenient. As someone who is very organized, I did not like having cables lying around.

Risks and challenges

Manufacturing - there is a possibility we will encounter challenges with manufacturing the product. However, we already have a finalized prototype and a manufacturer ready to produce the product. We also made the design as simple as possible to limit issues in production. Supply Chain - all parts required for CableMate are being sourced from trusted suppliers. Samples for all parts have been received and tested to make sure they are of good quality and fit together to create a quality product. Design flaws - there is always the possibility a product may not perform as intended. However, we have gone through 10+ different prototype iterations with only incremental changes to the last few to remove any flaws. We have also extensively tested the product including distributing prototypes to potential customers to obtain feedback. Fulfillment - there is always the possibility of delays in shipping the product. To mitigate this, we will have contracts in place for the manufacturer and shippers to adhere to clear deadlines. We also have a plan in place to ship the products directly to customers. Post-fulfillment issues: Should any issues arise once awards have been distributed, Daniel the CableMate founder will address any issues promptly and fully. Safety - CableMate contains safety features including surge protection. We are working with a manufacturer that has met all necessary safety requirements.
