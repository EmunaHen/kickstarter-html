Story

Introducing the Devee Tag, a product that transcends the ordinary pet accessory!

The Devee Tag is an innovative pet identification solution that seamlessly combines functionality with personalization. This smart tag enables pet owners to input essential information about their animal, including personalized contact details, which can be effortlessly managed through our intuitive mobile app. Such features are vital in minimizing distress and expediting reunification if your pet becomes lost or accidentally wanders away. The Devee Tag ensures that anyone who finds your pet can quickly access your contact information and sends GPS notifications to your phone & email - providing peace of mind and enhancing your pet’s safety.

Beyond its practical advantages, the Devee Tag offers a unique platform for self-expression. You can customize your tag with your pet’s image and your name, select from editable name options, or design a distinctive style that truly reflects your pet’s personality. This blend of utility and aesthetic appeal transforms the Devee Tag from a mere identification tool into a stylish accessory that celebrates your pet’s individuality
