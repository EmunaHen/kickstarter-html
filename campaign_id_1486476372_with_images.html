Story

The device made up of a wireless wearable and a wireless tag is intended to alert people who unintentionally forget to take their valuable belonging such as cell phone, laptop, purse, tablet, pets, briefcase, portfolio, etc., before leaving a public place. This alert allows the person to go back and gets his/her belonging. The system is a standalone as it does not use any communication service, smart phone, etc.

About this item

·         [Never leave your loved ones or valuables behind again] Forget-Me-Not is a sleek electronic device made up of a wearable alert system and a tag to be attached to your belonging. It is designed to alert you whenever you leave your valuable belongings, like your purse, smartphone, wallet, notepad, tablet, laptop, briefcase, keys, etc., behind. Compact and stylish, this wearable device vibrates when you walk away from your belonging to alert you to go back and retrieve it before it is stolen. Ideal for anyone who may forget valuable belongings in a public place.

·         [Standalone Device] Does not use a cell phone to alert you, does not need any app, does not need internet to operate, and does not need a monthly subscription fee.

·         [No setup required] Ready to work out of the box.

·         [USB Rechargeable lithium battery] for the wearable. The wearable is rechargeable using any micro USB battery charger. USB cable not included.

·         [8-Month battery life] for the tag.

·         [Privacy] Forget-me-not doesn’t connect to the internet or any public network and doesn’t collect any personal data thus 100% privacy.

·         [Wearable Choices] The Wearable can be worn as a watch, key fob, or lanyard/necklace, choose the style you want whenever you want as it comes with the band for wearing it as a watch and the silicon ring to wear it as key fob or a lanyard/necklace.

·         [Easy Maintenance] Just change the tag battery approximately once every eight months.

·         [Expandable] Currently only one belonging is monitored. If you want to monitor up to 10 belonging using one wearable, contact us for a customized solution.
