Story

Duovora redefines multilingual dialogue with its dual-screen display—each person sees their own real-time translation, full-screen, with no need to share, flip, or wait. It’s a face-to-face experience that feels natural, intuitive, and uninterrupted, whether you’re welcoming international guests at reception, closing deals in a boardroom, or bridging cultures in high-stakes diplomacy.

Duovora doesn’t just translate—it understands. Its built-in AI captures the full conversation, highlights key takeaways, and generates instant summaries you can scroll or export anytime. Every session is saved, timestamped, and easy to revisit—perfect for follow-ups, compliance, or just keeping things crystal clear.

From bustling airports to quiet clinics and high-stakes conferences, Duovora handles 50+ languages with speed and clarity. Its advanced microphones and smart noise filtering capture every word naturally—no shouting, no repeats. Lightweight, professional, and built to fit right into wherever global conversations happen.

Duovora transcribes multilingual meetings in real time and captures both sides with dual 1080P cameras. No missed details, no manual notes. When the meeting ends, built-in AI generates smart summaries, highlights key takeaways, and exports everything—turning every conversation into clear, actionable insight.

Duovora can do more than translate—it can inform, guide, and assist around the clock. Build a tailored Q&A knowledge base and let Duovora answer routine visitor questions, offer directions, or deliver key info in any language, any time, no staff required. It’s like having a multilingual concierge that never takes a day off and always knows the answer.

Getting started with Duovora is as effortless as using it. There are no apps to install, no accounts to create, and no IT support required. Just plug it in, pick your screen size—7” for compact spaces or 10” for immersive visibility—customize your greeting, and start translating. It’s made for businesses that move fast and people who don’t have time to troubleshoot.

Duovora was born from a simple yet powerful realization: in a world more connected than ever, language remains one of the last great barriers. After a personal experience with a failed translation at an international summit, our team knew something had to change—not just the tools we use, but the way we connect.

We envisioned a device that didn’t interrupt eye contact or break the flow of conversation. Something intuitive, present, and designed not for tourists or travelers, but for professionals, educators, service staff, and global teams. Duovora took shape not as a gadget, but as a communication experience—clear, intelligent, and human.

What sets Duovora apart is not just its dual-screen design or AI-powered summaries, but the belief behind it: that everyone deserves to be understood. Whether across a front desk or across the world, Duovora bridges meaning—not just words. It empowers people to listen better, respond faster, and connect deeper.

We didn’t build a translator. We built a tool for trust. A tool for diplomacy, hospitality, and service. A quiet revolution in how we meet, learn, and work together—without language getting in the way.

This is our story. But more importantly, it’s the start of millions more—told in every language, on every screen, across every border. That’s Duovora.

Risks and challenges

We’ve worked hard to bring Duovora to its current stage, with working prototypes, finalized core features, and a clear production roadmap. However, as with any hardware project, there are potential challenges. Manufacturing delays could arise due to supply chain fluctuations or component shortages, especially for display panels and camera modules. We’ve partnered with a reliable OEM with consumer electronics experience and have built in time buffers to minimize disruption. Software optimization—including AI translation accuracy and UI stability—will continue through final testing. Our team will push regular firmware updates to ensure the best user experience at launch. Shipping and logistics may vary based on region, regulations, or customs clearance. We’re working with established fulfillment partners and plan to prioritize transparent communication for all deliveries. We’re fully committed to transparency, timely updates, and proactive support. No matter what happens, we’ll keep our backers informed and involved every step of the way.
