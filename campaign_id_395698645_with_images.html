Risks and challenges

We’ve been developing the Dipal D1 3D AI Character Pod since 2022, investing nearly 3 years to perfect our technology and production processes. We’re confident in our ability to deliver a high-quality product on time, as our technical foundation is solid and our manufacturing partners are reliable. The only potential challenge we foresee is the uncertain trade situation between the U.S. and China, as our products are manufactured in mainland China. Shifting tariffs or changes in trade policies could lead to unpredictable customs costs, which might affect our final delivery costs. That said, we’re committed to working closely with our logistics partners to minimize these impacts and ensure a smooth delivery process. Beyond this, we believe we’re well-prepared to bring this project to life without significant risks.
