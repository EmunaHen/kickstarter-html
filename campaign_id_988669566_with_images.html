Now I'm raising funds to manufacture our latest design with high quality materials, powerful and silent motor to be used by the public. This will allow travelers and anyone on the go to easily style their hair without sacrificing precious space or time.

Why do I care? In a modern world I want to be free from bulky devices when on the go. Either going to the gym, spa, traveling. I want to create a solution that allows people to look and feel their best without compromising on style or convenience. See more at https://www.youtube.com/@TravelSizeBeauty and https://medium.com/@4olgak

How will I make it happen? I've already developed a working prototype that is significantly smaller than existing hot air brushes. Patent is pending. The next steps are:

  • Electrical and mechanical engineering: Tune artifacts into production ready. Electrical design and documentation for factory use.
  • Manufacturing: Find the right factories and produce travel size hot air brushes in scale, assembly and pack.
  • Shipping: Email to the paid customers confirming their shipping address before sending it.

Who am I? I'm an engineer with experience in both large corporations and small startups, including a hardware one. My background in applied mathematics and computer science, coupled with a passion for solving real-world problems, has led me to develop a groundbreaking new product. Identifying a need for a travel-friendly hair styling solution, I leveraged my technical expertise to create a compact, foldable, and premium-quality styler. This device addresses the challenges faced by women like myself who value both style and efficiency while traveling.

Dyson. Many people love Dyson and carry it in their handbags. I also have Dyson at home, so I understand the quality of the professional care. Goldie is on the go addition to that.

Risks and challenges

The potential risk for this product is possible delays in manufacturing. We are committed to making sure our supporters get a product that delivers on its promises. We will continually update you on any challenges during the final manufacturing phase and will do our best to launch a quality hair styling tool in a timely manner. We have a functional prototype and patent pending. Additionally, there are some general risks and challenges that pledgers should be aware of. · Please understand that the appearance and function of the product may be adjusted due to the progress of R&D and production. · General production and product certification may take longer than expected. · Packaging and transport problems. · Shipping delays because some parts are manufactured in China. · Managing Volume Expectations
