Auto Ears are rugged and reliable, surviving all of these tests with ZERO failures:

Questions and Answers:

What are electronic hearing protectors?

Auto Ears are electronic hearing protectors.  Auto Ears protect your hearing from loud sounds, while still allowing you to clearly hear the world around you!  You can hear a whisper from across the room, but your ears gain protection from loud sounds over 83 decibels.

Auto Ears are not "noise cancelling headphones."  Noise cancelling headphones do not protect your hearing, and they block outside sound and conversations.  Noise cancelling headphones are for gyms and airplanes.  Auto Ears are for shooting ranges, factories, work sites, and anywhere you need to protect your hearing while maintaining the ability to hear clearly.

How does music sound through them?

Because Auto Ears are designed to protect your hearing, they are full of foam with a sound-blocking structure.  This foam reduces audio quality when listening to music via Bluetooth.  

We want to be honest and set realistic expectations for music performance.  Music quality would be best described as "surprisingly adequate."  Highs and mids are plenty clear, but there is very little bass due to the sound-damping foam.  We have listened to a variety of genres of music and podcasts through them for many hours with no complaints - they provide entertainment while protecting your hearing!  Please do not order these expecting a premium audio experience out of a device that is primarily designed to protect your hearing.  Science hasn't figured out how to do that yet!
