Story

Riding a motorcycle is the ultimate freedom. However, the strain on your back can sometimes hinder that freedom. We have poured over 15 years of development experience and more than 40 years of passion as riders into creating an entirely new assist suit specifically for motorcyclists. This suit significantly reduces the load on the erector spinae muscles experienced during a forward-leaning riding posture, liberating you from fatigue. We aim to deliver this innovative assist suit to riders worldwide and dramatically change their riding lives. To make this a reality, we ask for your support to cover the costs of molds and initial investments for mass production.

Long hours of touring, aggressive riding on winding roads, maintaining posture at traffic lights... the more you love riding, the more you've had to confront back strain. Maintaining a forward-leaning posture, in particular, puts an immense load on the erector spinae muscles, often leading to fatigue and pain in the latter half of a ride. This physical burden can decrease concentration and diminish the overall riding experience.

Our Motorcycle Assist Suit was born precisely to solve this problem. This innovative suit supports your erector spinae muscles, dramatically reducing the strain caused by a forward-leaning riding posture. It fits like a second skin, providing powerful assistance without impeding your natural movements. Now you can ride endlessly, enjoying the open road without worrying about fatigue.

We have undergone rigorous development and testing to deliver the best possible product to riders.
