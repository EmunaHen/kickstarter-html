Introducing SpeeDisk

Dual-Slot Reading Design, Twice the Efficiency — Say Goodbye to Storage Anxiety

Tired of constantly swapping memory cards and losing precious time during projects? With our thoughtfully engineered dual-slot design, you can read both SD and microSD (TF) cards at the same time. No adapters, no interruptions — just seamless, simultaneous access to your footage and files.

Whether you’re transferring high-res images from your DSLR, 4K video clips from your drone, or project files from multiple devices, this efficient setup dramatically simplifies your workflow. Instantly move content on location, streamline your post-production process, and keep your creative momentum going without missing a beat. Say goodbye to storage anxiety and hello to a faster, smoother, and smarter way to manage your media.

Magnetic Portability, Secure & Effortless Access

Designed with a powerful and dependable magnetic connection, this compact SD/TF reader effortlessly attaches to your laptop, smartphone, or any metal surface — whether it’s a camera bag, desk edge, or creative workspace. Even when you’re on the move, commuting, or out capturing moments in the wild, it stays firmly in place, ready for instant use whenever you need it.

Tailored for mobile professionals, digital nomads, and outdoor creators, this sleek accessory ensures your essential data tool is always within arm’s reach. Say goodbye to fumbling through bags or tangled accessories — enjoy seamless access to your storage and connectivity companion, no matter where inspiration takes you.

Enhance Your Experience
Enjoy a High-Definition World Like Never Before.

About the Team

We are a focused team of engineers and designers with years of hands-on experience in developing reliable connectivity and storage products. Backed by a solid foundation in manufacturing and product development, we've brought to market a variety of USB accessories, docking stations, and mobile workflow tools — all created with a clear emphasis on practicality, performance, and user-centered design.

The idea for SpeeDisk came from ongoing conversations with mobile creators, photographers, and professionals who often work in fast-paced or outdoor environments. Many shared the same challenges: oversized card readers, limited device compatibility, and tangled setups that slowed down their workflow.

Motivated to solve these issues, we developed SpeeDisk — combining a compact magnetic form, dual card slot access, and USB 3.2 Gen2 speed in a single, efficient tool. From the streamlined pebble-shaped body to its versatile mounting options, every detail was crafted to help users stay focused and efficient wherever they work.

With your support, we’re excited to bring SpeeDisk into the hands of creators worldwide, as a dependable tool built to keep up with your vision.

Risks and challenges

At SpeeDisk, we are committed to delivering a high-quality, reliable product. However, as with any hardware project, there are inherent risks and challenges we continuously work to mitigate: Supply Chain Delays: Component shortages and international logistics may impact our production schedule. To minimize this risk, we’ve partnered with experienced manufacturers and secured backup suppliers for key parts. Compatibility Testing: We aim for broad compatibility with various USB-C devices, including MacBooks, iPads, and Android phones. We conduct rigorous testing across major platforms, but rare compatibility issues could arise depending on user configurations. Manufacturing & Quality Control: Mass production often reveals unforeseen technical issues. Our team has extensive experience managing production lines and will implement thorough quality control checks to ensure each unit meets our standards. Shipping & Fulfillment: While we strive for on-time delivery, customs clearance, global logistics constraints, or local regulations may cause delays in certain regions. We will keep all backers informed with regular updates. We are confident in our ability to deliver SpeeDisk as promised, and we are fully transparent about the process. With your support, we’ll bring this thoughtfully-designed tool to creators and professionals around the world.
