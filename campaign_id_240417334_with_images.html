Story

Drive Stories is an audio app that intelligently plays you stories about the area around you.

Drive Stories is a passionate return to the open road and the driving experience, helping unlock location stories from all around the world - allowing us to create enjoyable moments of open-road freedom and a leading example of how technology can help unlock knowledge and information in an exciting new way.

The idea for the app was inspired by traveling in various locations around the world and wondering about the story of the places we were driving through. Maybe we saw old historical towns and wondered about it's past and the people that came from it, or saw deserted factories and wondered, 'what type of industry was here in the past?', or drove through beautiful natural landscapes and thought 'what types of wildlife are in those hills?', or saw amazing buildings in a new city and asked 'I wonder about the architectural history and influence here?'. The list goes on.

What we found is that information on locations (specifically information about cities, villages, towns, etc) around the world IS available, but the way to tap into it is cumbersome, complicated, un-curated and requires sifting through hundreds of confusing Google search results. It's simply not easily accessible while driving, as a passenger, or even walking around a city.

We're done with the head-down-in-phone thing while the world passes us by. We want to be present, windows down, music on, enjoying (and learning) about what's around us. The world is outside our window, and we want to know about it.

What we've created with Drive Stories is a hyper-easy, on-demand and intuitive interface to access and enjoy all of this phenomenal data in an incredibly easy way : just hit 'play' and drive and listen. It's that easy.

The result: one of the world’s most robust and comprehensive location-information platforms available on the market

Audio stories, on demand, for any location, anywhere in the world. 

The app taps into over 4 million cities, towns and villages from all around the world, in over 50 languages - dynamically playing you audio stories from 15 categories, whenever and wherever you want.

  • Audio stories, on demand, for any location, anywhere in the world.
  • Over 4 million cities, towns & villages from around the world
  • Available in over 50 languages
  • 15 story categories to choose from
  • Available on Apple CarPlay & Android Auto, as well as Apple iOS and Android Phones Apps

Integrated directly on your vehicle's Apple CarPlay & Android Auto system for ease of use and simplicity. Just tap the icon, select a location and hit play. 

You can also use the native iOS and Android app directly on your phone. The interface features large visual category cards and an easy location list - just select the category you are interested in for your location and hit play. The audio will play over your car's speakers and you're good to go! 

Your Ultimate Road Trip Companion

As we've prototyped and built the app, our excitement has grown over using Drive Stories as a frequent resource and companion on drives (we're even using it while walking around cities). It becomes a quick audio reference and storytelling resource when you're curious about the area you're in. We're excited to hear how you use it and all the ways we can continue to grow and refine the experience. 

Anywhere in the world, Drive Stories works

The app will automatically find your GPS coordinates and based on your geolocation, will identify 10 of the nearest cities, towns and villages near you. This gives you control and granularity if you are curious or have a specific location in mind. The possibilities are pretty staggering: there are roughly 4 million cities, towns and villages in the world. Multiply that by 15 story categories for each location, and you can see Drive Stories has the potential of delivering well over a potential of 60 million unique stories.

The Drive Stories platform is delivered on both iPhone and Android and natively integrated on Apple CarPlay and Android Auto. We have built a bespoke platform that leverages a phenomenal amount of location data and through clever use of technology and robust data sources, delivers audio stories in a way that emphasizes the enjoyment, romance and experience of a road trip.

Bringing History, Wildlife & Hidden Tales (and more) To Life As You Drive

Within the app, you simply tap ‘play’ or choose from one of 15 categories, and the app plays you audio stories - from History, Arts & Culture, Literature, Architecture, Wildlife & Nature, Important People and more. You can choose from one of around 10 voices that you prefer, as well as from 50 languages - thereby making all of our stories accessible to pretty much anyone, anywhere, in the world.

15 Categories to Choose From

We have built the capability to create compelling stories from across 15 categories, enabling a wide variety of information and knowledge to be played while exploring different locations. Just swipe through the category list, tap one and it will play you a story immediately!

Making Every Road Trip an Adventure

The Drive Stories platform values experience over technology: imagine putting your finger anywhere on the map, anywhere in the world, and hearing stories from 15 categories, in over 50 languages, of that location. This literally unlocks 10’s of millions of factual stories around the world.

Unlocking the Stories of the Location Around You

The experience becomes something of an expert companion with you along your driving adventures. You may have Maps up for directions, or Spotify playing music. But with Drive Stories, it's always there - ready to play you a story on-demand, in any location. Simply swipe over to the app on CarPlay or Android Auto (or on your phone), select your location and category, and hit play. 

It's like an expert location guide sitting next to you in the passenger seat, ready to tell you stories about the location you are driving through!

Available Globally, in Over 50 Languages

We are incredibly excited to build in language support for over 50 languages across the world. This allows phenomenal global access to our app and an incredibly robust library of content at your fingertips regardless of where you are in the world. This not only allows our app to be enjoyed by people all over the world, but also unlocks exciting potential for travellers in foreign-speaking countries, unlocking story content in your native language in any location in the world.
 

Easy, Intuitive & Integrated

Our belief is that technology should power experiences and be invisible and seamlessly integrated into ‘moments’ that can enhance and add value. Drive Stories wants to make every drive a memorable one, no matter where the road takes you.
