Story

As social networking grows globally, people increasingly seek to share life's moments. Yet most cameras are too bulky, lack instant connectivity, or suffer from poor quality.  This is especially true for Vloggers and outdoor adventurers who need an ultra-light, unobtrusive camera that offers precise framing and unwavering stability. We created the S111 to solve this: a truly portable solution designed to effortlessly record your daily highlights.

S111-The Lightest Hands-free Head-mounted Mini Camera

S111 weighs only 24g – significantly lighter than most competitors and lighter than many wireless earbuds – the S111 is the world's lightest head-mounted mini camera. Its comfortable, secure head-mount design ensures it stays perfectly stable during any activity, from cycling and skiing to intense movement, without shaking or slipping. S111 weighs only 24g, the ultra-light body pairs with the head mount for a secure fit that won't shift. Positioned perfectly at eye level, the lens is ideally designed for immersive first-person perspective shooting – letting you focus entirely on the experience, free from distractions.

First-Person Masterclass Capture: Document hands-on tutorials (cooking/crafts) with uninterrupted over-the-shoulder perspective.

Seamless Life Logging: Capture street scenes, museums, and nature hands-free while moving.

Multi-Terrain Adventure Logging: Auto-record trails, caves, and environmental details during cycling/hiking/exploration.

Pro Meeting Mode: Crystal-clear conference calls with voice isolation.

The S111 features a 0.96-inch IPS display for real-time preview– solving a major pain point of traditional head cams where you couldn't confirm your shot instantly. This revolutionizes your filming experience, ensuring you never miss a moment. Enjoy instant playback and make on-the-spot adjustments Instantly.

The S111 capture stunning 2.7K Ultra HD video, with 1080p and 720p options. Experience incredibly detailed visuals and true-to-life colors, delivering a truly immersive first-person perspective that lets you relive every action-packed moment.

Its 110° wide-angle lens grabs a broader field of view. Shoot effortlessly in landscape or portrait orientation to frame the entire scene, making it perfect for capturing fast-paced action without missing a beat.

Start or stop recordings, change settings, preview footage, or download files—all from your smartphone.

The S111 delivers approximately 1 hour of continuous recording time at all resolutions—whether 2.7K, 1080p, or 720p. Featuring USB Type-C Quick Charge technology, it fully recharges in just 30 minutes, eliminating battery anxiety and letting you capture life freely. The battery maintains up to 500 charge cycles with only 20% capacity loss.

S111 support 128GB TF card . Effortlessly record every moment of your journey.
