Traveling light shouldn’t mean carrying dirty clothes. Hand-washing in sinks is messy and time-consuming, laundry services are pricey, and most portable wash bags are bulky or wasteful. Treklean changes that with a compact, eco-friendly solution that keeps you fresh on any adventure.

That’s where Treklean comes in—designed to make cleaning your clothes effortless, compact, and eco-friendly, no matter where the journey takes you.

  • Say goodbye to bulky laundry gear and hello to Treklean—the 3-in-1 adventure hub that functions as a waterproof backpack, compact washing machine, and compression storage. Designed for campers, hikers, travelers, and gym-goers, it will give you a warm, effective wash—anywhere.

  • Despite folding to palm size, it expands to 22L—enough to clean undergarments for three people at once.

  • Lightweight, durable, and eco-conscious, Treklean transforms the way you do laundry on the move.

  • Reduced Detergent Use: Treklean's Hexatech Scrub system enhances mechanical cleaning, minimizing the need for detergent.
  • Sustainable Materials: Constructed with Elite Polyether TPU, offering durability and resistance to microbial growth and hydrolysis.
