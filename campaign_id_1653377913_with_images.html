Story

You're not alone. Whether you're caring for an elderly parent, juggling your own medications, or simply managing a daily routine—forgetting (or double-dosing) is an all-too-common stressor.

memoButton is here to change that.

What is memoButton?

How does it work?

The idea is simple.  You wake up in the morning and memoBox is red.  You take your medicine, press the memoButton, and the memoBox light will turn green.  It stays green until the next morning giving you visual confirmation that you've taken your medication.

memoButton is composed of two parts: The memoButton itself, and the memoBox with the red and green lights. You place the memoBox above your door, on your nightstand, or wherever you need it... and you press the memoButton when you've taken your medicine.  memoBox will automatically return to red before you wake up the next morning so that you know that you haven't yet taken your medicine.

Communication between memoButton and memoBox is wireless. If you're using the memoButton Pro app (coming early 2026) then it will even save the time and date for each press of the memoButton.  So that even if you're out and about you can be aware of whether or not you've taken your medication.

What's in the Box?

Each memoButton comes with the button itself, the box with the red and green lights, a USB cable, and an instruction manual. (Colors may vary due to the manufacturing process.)

Designed for Real Life

In today's world mornings are more complex than ever. You've got to make sure that your phone's charged, you've checked your email, packed your charger, and that you have a thousand things ready that no one else has ever  had to think about in the history of the world.

It's easy to just walk out the door and forget to take your medicine. And that can be dangerous. At the same time, you don't want to be fondling through your pockets or purse for your phone, unlocking it, and then looking for some app when you're in a hurry.

memoButton eliminates that stress by giving you an instant visual confirmation.

Just one glance tells you what you need to know. And the time that you save with memoButton gives you enough time to take your medication if you see that you haven't taken it. The peace of mind that you get from knowing you have will last all day.

Why We Made It

Something you could count on with one press.  So we went to the workshop.  We created a design.  Something that was small and light enough that anyone older than a baby and younger than 900 could use it.  We had three criteria: It had to be wireless (at least the button itself), it had to be simple, and it had to be light.

Long-lasting design

Each memoButton is built to last. Components such as the USB cable that powers the memoBox and the battery that powers the memoButton are of a common variety that can be easily replaced with off-the-shelf items.

Reusability and recyclability

Not only are the plastics used to create each memoButton and memoBox 100% recyclable... but each one is actually MADE using recycled plastic. Should, for whatever reason, you decide to no longer use memoButton... please recycle responsibly.
