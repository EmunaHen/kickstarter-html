Hi Makers

The story behind Multi Shelf - from the idea to the 3D-printed shelving system

Hello!

I'm Maik, 50 years old, from Germany - tinkerer, developer and creative mind behind this project.

About a year ago, I discovered my passion for constructive 3D printing solutions. What started as an experiment in the workshop quickly developed into a real mission: to design things that are practical, functional and stylish at the same time - and that you can't buy off the shelf.

One of my biggest goals was to develop a shelving system that is as flexible as everyday life itself. A system that can be adapted, extended and changed at any time - without tools, without frustration, simply well thought out.

Multi Shelf was born:
A minimalist shelving system that is made to measure using 3D printing and integrates perfectly into any home, office or workshop. Whether a small corner or a large wall - Multi Shelf grows with your ideas!

Why Kickstarter?
With your support, I want to take the next step: realize even more creative, everyday 3D projects. Because I already have more ideas in my head - and on my sketches - that are just waiting to become reality.

Any support will not only go towards the further development of this shelving system, but also towards the development of new solutions that make everyday life smarter, more flexible and a little easier.

Become part of the idea - support Multi Shelf and join me on my journey from tinkerer to problem solver for your home!

You will only receive the STL files, not a physical product.
