Story

Koi x1: When design transforms our relationship with technology

In our design studio, an obsession was born: to rethink, from the ground up, how we interact with the technology that has already become part of us. Not as users, but as human beings who integrate it, almost without noticing, into their daily lives, their bodies, their most intimate routines.

We didn’t want to design just an accessory. We wanted to redraw the boundaries of a relationship: the one we have with one of the most personal, most visible, and most tactile devices of this digital age: the Apple Watch.

The starting point wasn’t a market need, but an essentially human question:
What if technology could feel more natural? More accessible? More intuitive, even more emotional?

That’s how Koi x1 was born

Koi x1 is not just a case.
It is a functional and aesthetic reinterpretation of how to wear, use, and feel the Apple Watch.
A 3D-printed design that transforms the watch into a freer, more ergonomic tool, better aligned with the body and mind.

The digital crown is operated from above, freeing the thumb and allowing smooth navigation with a single motion.
You only need one hand to access its functions. 
No screws. No adhesives.
Every part is precisely designed to fit harmoniously, as if the watch had been conceived within this new body from the start.

Every angle, every curve, responds to an intention: not only to make use easier but to evoke a sense of functional beauty.
Because usefulness can also be elegant. Because form can also inspire emotion.

Koi x1 is just the beginning.

This is not an isolated product. It is the first step of a broader vision:
to create hardware and software guided by a clear principle, to make technology feel more human.

With your support, you’re not just protecting your watch. You’re unlocking the potential of a new way to connect with it. You’re betting on a different way of understanding technology: not as an imposition, but as a sensitive extension of your body and lifestyle.

Your contribution makes possible:

Improve the current design by optimizing shapes, finishes, and compatibility with more Apple Watch models.
Explore new materials that are more durable, sustainable, and sophisticated.
Develop exclusive software designed for this new way of interacting: applications that leverage the ergonomics of the KOI X1 to offer smoother, more accessible, and deeply personal experiences.

Koi x1 is not just an accessory. It is a statement of principles.

This Kickstarter is not about mass scaling. It’s about connecting with people who understand the value of purposeful design. People who feel, like us, that technology can be kinder. More honest. More ours.

Supporting Koi x1 means being part of that change.
A change where hardware and software not only work well,
but also feel right.
