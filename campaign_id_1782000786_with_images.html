Story

We're building DeliverCore, a unified email deliverability platform that gives businesses, agencies, and MSPs complete control over their email infrastructure through one dashboard. Instead of juggling multiple expensive tools, you get DNS monitoring, DMARC compliance, inbox placement testing, and reputation management in a single subscription.

Why you care about it: Here's what this actually costs businesses today: Proofpoint charges $500+ monthly per domain. DMARC analyzers run $200+ monthly. Warm-up tools cost $50+ per email account. A mid-sized agency monitoring 20 client domains pays $15,000+ annually for email deliverability tools that don't even talk to each other. We're building the same functionality for under $100 monthly.

How you plan to make it happen: The core functionality already works. I've got DNS monitoring running, DMARC parsing working, and basic alerts firing. Your funding lets me hire an experienced developer to build out the full platform. We're talking AI-powered warm-up campaigns, inbox placement testing across major ISPs, and multi-client management for agencies. After that, we launch as a SaaS platform.

Who you are: I'm the guy agencies call when their client's emails mysteriously end up in spam folders. I've troubleshot everything from Fortune 500 companies to small e-commerce shops. I built this because I got tired of explaining to clients why they needed to pay for six different tools to solve one problem.

DeliverCore is already in early stages of development, built on proven technology that our team has extensive experience with. Our focus is building a robust email deliverability platform that solves real problems for businesses, agencies, and MSPs who depend on reliable email performance.

 

Risks and challenges

Email deliverability isn't simple stuff. DNS records, SMTP protocols, and ISP filtering systems all interact in ways that can break unexpectedly, which is why I'm bringing in a developer who's actually built email infrastructure before. We've got a realistic timeline because I've already worked through the major technical hurdles. B2B tools also take time to catch on, but I've got a network of agencies and consultants who've been asking for exactly this solution since they're tired of cobbling together expensive enterprise tools or explaining to clients why email monitoring costs more than their entire marketing budget. Sure, companies like Proofpoint and Valimail have deep pockets and enterprise sales teams, but they're completely ignoring the small business market where most agencies can't afford $500 per month per client for email monitoring. That's our opening. The other challenge is keeping up with constant changes since email standards and ISP requirements evolve all the time. Gmail updates their filtering, Microsoft tweaks their authentication requirements, but I stay on top of this stuff professionally and the subscription model means we can keep updating the platform as things change.
