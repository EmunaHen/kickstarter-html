💎 Campaign Intro

ShadowBand | A Smart Ring Designed for Wellness + Peace of Mind
Silent alerts. Emotional check-ins. Wellness tools - all from your fingertip. Hi, I’m Lakeisha Robinson - founder, mom, and passionate innovator. I created ShadowBand after the devastating loss of my sister. That pain made one thing clear: the trauma of not knowing can be as damaging as the event itself. What if she had been able to silently alert us? What if we had just a moment’s notice? From that vision, ShadowBand was born - a smart ring that empowers quiet connection, emotional safety, and peace of mind without sacrificing elegance or comfort. With just a discreet tap on the gemstone, ShadowBand connects wearers to trusted contacts, sends real-time GPS updates, and opens a mobile wellness toolkit with resources like:

Breathing guides

Mood journaling

Stress-reducing techniques

Insights to help manage cortisol - a key factor in trauma-induced inflammation and chronic health issues

This campaign supports the build of our first working prototype and companion app - the foundation for what we hope will become a powerful tool for families, caregivers, students, and travelers alike.

✨ Whether you’re navigating life’s unknowns or simply seeking peace of mind, ShadowBand is for you. Join our movement to stay grounded, connected, and cared for - all from your fingertip.

✅ The Problem

We live in a fast-moving world full of unpredictability - and far too often, emotional and physical safety becomes an afterthought.

Loved ones can’t always reach us when it matters most

Stress, anxiety, and trauma often go unspoken

Cortisol overload from chronic stress leads to inflammation, fatigue, and long-term health issues

Traditional safety devices are bulky, obvious, and often left behind - especially by women, teens, and vulnerable groups

Whether you're walking home alone, navigating a difficult moment, or simply need a quick emotional check-in, there hasn’t been a solution that’s both elegant and empowering.

Until now.

ShadowBand is more than a smart ring - it’s an emotional wellness tool disguised as fine jewelry.

With a simple tap, wearers can:

Instantly notify their trusted circle

Send their live location

Access a discreet in-app toolkit for stress relief and emotional self-care

And feel grounded, not overwhelmed - knowing they’re never truly alone

ShadowBand blends technology, emotional wellness, and minimalist design to help people feel safe, seen, and supported - without ever having to say a word.

Risks and challenges

⚠️ Risks & Challenges As with any hardware startup, developing a new product from scratch involves some uncertainty. While we’ve done significant groundwork — including design mockups, early app visuals, and a provisional patent filing — here are the key areas we’re navigating: 🔧 Hardware Integration Building a miniaturized smart ring with GPS, tap detection, and contact-based charging in a discreet, fashionable form factor is complex. We’re working with potential engineering partners and 3D printing firms to ensure the first prototype is both beautiful and functional. Delays in sourcing or unexpected technical hurdles could impact timelines. 📱 App Development Our companion app will launch with basic features like alert sending, location sharing, and a wellness toolkit. More advanced functions will be rolled out in phases. App development often involves testing and debugging, which may affect our estimated delivery. 💰 Solo Creator — But Not Alone This campaign is currently being led by a solo founder with support from advisors, wellness experts, and consultants. As funding becomes available, we will bring on developers, engineers, and operations professionals to move things forward more efficiently. Backer support directly helps expand this team. 📦 Fulfillment & Shipping Manufacturing and shipping logistics — especially for wearable tech — can be unpredictable. We’re taking a lean, phase-based approach to reduce risk, starting with small batch production and controlled fulfillment. 🛑 Product Classification ShadowBand is not a medical or emergency device and is not intended to replace certified safety products or professional care. Its features are wellness and connection-focused and should be used with personal discretion. 💬 Our Promise We are committed to full transparency and open communication. Backers will receive regular updates, honest progress reports, and opportunities to give feedback throughout development. ShadowBand is built on trust — and you’re part of that journey.
