Story

 Ostation is proudly developed by Olight Group, a brand trusted by millions since 2007 for high-performance flashlights and portable lighting gear. After more than a decade of lighting the way, we're taking the next step: helping you power your everyday life smarter and more sustainably.
 Ostation is the first step in that journey. Designed based on real user feedback and everyday needs, it reimagines how people store, charge, and manage batteries at home.
In 2024, we launched the original Ostation X on Kickstarter — a bold all-in-one battery hub that redefined what a charger could be. Backed by 5,845 supporters and raising $1.14 million, that campaign laid the foundation for what's next: a smarter, simpler battery future.
This campaign is fully managed and promoted by Vinyl,an official Kickstarter Expert Partner.
Since 2017, Vinyl has raised $150M+ and led million-dollar projects like AnkerMake ($8M+) and LaserPecker ($6M+), providing expert promotion and marketing services for global success.

Risks and challenges

We’ve spent months refining the design and testing the Ostation 2 Series across multiple iterations. Still, as with any hardware project, there may be challenges along the way — from supply chain delays to unforeseen logistics issues. That said, this isn’t our first launch. Backed by Olight’s experienced R&D and production teams, and building on the success of the original Ostation X, we’re confident in our ability to deliver. We promise to keep backers fully informed at every stage — from manufacturing updates to delivery timelines. Your trust means everything to us.
