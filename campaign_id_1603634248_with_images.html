✈️ AirNav to Launch on Kickstarter – A Revolution in Airport Navigation, Powered by People

Los Angeles, CA.
Today, entrepreneur and frequent traveler Ken Crause announces the upcoming Kickstarter launch of AirNav, a groundbreaking mobile app that aims to make navigating airports as simple as walking through your neighborhood.

AirNav is more than just an app — it's a movement, built for travelers, by travelers.

“I’ve spent years frustrated by the stress of navigating major airports,” says Crause. “AirNav is the solution I always wished existed. Now, with the power of the community, we’re finally building it — together.”

🛫 A Travel Tool That Millions Need

Whether you’re flying internationally or just catching a quick domestic connection, airports can be overwhelming. AirNav brings real-time, turn-by-turn navigation, live flight updates, and in-airport search tools straight to your phone — no more getting lost, missing flights, or hunting for signs in foreign languages.

Imagine searching “Gate C19,” “Starbucks,” or “Delta Check-In” and instantly getting step-by-step walking directions — just like Google Maps, but inside the airport.

👥 Backers: The Heroes of This Journey

This project will be brought to life by a community of backers who believe in innovation, accessibility, and making global travel less stressful for everyone. And that includes you.

Every pledge you make is more than just support — it’s a step toward creating something truly transformative.

“Backers aren’t just supporters — they’re co-founders of this movement,” says Ken Crause, founder of AirNav. “AirNav won’t exist without You, and every dollar pledged helps us turn airports into smarter, walkable spaces.”

🎯 What the Kickstarter Campaign Will Fund

Launching soon on Kickstarter, the campaign aims to raise $200,000 to build the MVP version of AirNav and deploy it in 10 of the world’s busiest airports. Future goals include AR navigation, multi-language support, and global expansion to 100+ airports.

The funds will go toward:

·     Cross-platform app development (iOS & Android)

·     Data partnerships with international airports

·     Seamless UX design

·     Real-time flight integration

·     Live walking directions within airport terminals

💎 Perks Designed to Thank & Empower Our Backers

Every supporter will receive meaningful rewards, including:

·     Early Beta Access

·     Lifetime Basic or Premium Memberships

·     Exclusive Discounts from Travel Brands

·     Founding Supporter Recognition

·     Corporate Sponsorship Opportunities for travel-related companies

🌍 Join the Movement. Be Part of the Future of Air Travel.

AirNav is not a big tech company project — it’s a people-powered innovation that will only become reality with community support. If you've ever been lost in an airport, missed a gate change, or just wished your phone could guide you to your terminal like it does through city streets — this is your moment.
