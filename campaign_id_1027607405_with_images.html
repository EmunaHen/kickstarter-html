Story

NANORISM WASH is an innovative laundry accessory that dramatically boosts cleaning power-simply by attaching it to your washing machine. This newly developed model has been specifically created for launch on Kickstarter and is not yet available for general sale. Using a patented ultrafine bubble generator (Patent No. 7042013), it produces microscopic bubbles that penetrate deep into fabric fibers, designed to help break down and reduce stubborn stains and unpleasant odors.
It also helps keep your washing machine clean by suppressing the buildup of mold and grime inside the drum and drain hoses.
No need to worry about changing your detergent-just use the one you already have. 
Many users have reported a fresher feel to their laundry after using similar technology.

It’s an easier, more effective way to do laundry every day!

When NANORISM WASH is attached to your washing machine, it generates a stream of fine bubbles-known as “Tornado Bubbles”-in the center of the hose. These ultrafine bubbles mix with the water and deeply penetrate fabric fibers, breaking down and removing stubborn dirt and odors.

They also enhance the effectiveness of your detergent, allowing you to use less detergent and water while still getting powerful results. With every wash, not only do your clothes come out cleaner, but the drum and drain hose stay cleaner too-improving the overall hygiene of your washing machine.

We’ve spent over 10 years researching fine bubble technology. The fine bubble core used in this product was officially patented by the Japan Patent Office in 2021.

For this project, we focused specifically on two key challenges in laundry: removing deep-set dirt from fabric fibers and eliminating odors caused by damp clothes. Multiple tests were conducted to verify its effectiveness in these areas.


Tap water flows through supply pipes under pressure, and it is believed that air is dissolved in the water in a saturated state.

When the water passes through the center of the fine bubble generator core, it maintains nearly the same pressure and flow rate.

Meanwhile, due to the special internal structure of the core, the water that does not pass through the center is forced to collide with the inner walls, where it is compressed and accelerated.

This creates a pressure difference between the center flow and the surrounding flow, which causes a decompression effect that releases dissolved air into the water, forming fine bubbles.

Our goal with NANORISM WASH was simple: to make laundry easier, cleaner, and more effective—without changing your routine.

The biggest feature of NANORISM WASH is that it helps maximize the power of your regular detergent, allowing you to remove tough stains and odors without any special products or steps. Just attach it to your washing machine, and it works silently in the background—creating ultrafine bubbles that reach deep into fabric fibers to break down dirt, eliminate musty smells, and even clean your washer’s drum and drain hose over time.

The idea for this product came from a common question:

“Isn’t there a better, simpler way to do laundry?”

Many people struggle with laundry challenges like clothes that never feel fully clean, lingering odors after washing, or concern about hidden grime inside the machine. Sweat and body oils can settle deep into fabrics, and most detergents can’t fully reach them. On top of that, the inside of the washer—especially behind the drum or under the pulsator—is prone to mold, bacteria, and residue that builds up out of sight.

To solve this, we turned to ultrafine bubble technology. These microscopic bubbles can penetrate deeper than water alone, helping to break down and lift away dirt and odor at the source. But we didn’t stop there. We designed NANORISM WASH to also help clean your washer itself, reducing the buildup of mold and bacteria in the drum and hoses.

And because American washing machines use higher water pressure than those in Japan, we engineered NANORISM WASH with a durable metal body—strong enough to withstand constant pressure while maintaining stable performance over time.

We didn’t want to create just another cleaning gadget. Our mission was to design a tool that improves your entire laundry environment—from your clothes to your machine.
