Story

Bring classic analog warmth and punch to your music with the AR-110. Created for producers, musicians, and live performers who crave authentic drum sounds with hands-on control. 

The AR-110 is a compact analog drum synthesizer with classic sounds, separate outputs, and volume controls for each instrument. Its ease of use and affordability make it a great addition to both amateur and professional studios. Designed and assembled in Canada for sound producers all over the world. 

If you love analog drum sounds and hands-on control, we’d love your support. Back this project to help us build a solid, great-sounding analog drum synthesizer with individual outputs and MIDI. Help bring Canadian-made analog sound to the world — join the backing now!

  • 100% Authentic transistor based Analog Sound - No digital tricks, just pure synthesis.
  • Individual Outputs & Volume Controls — Mix and shape your drum sounds live.
  • Versatile Kit — Bass drum, snare, open and closed hi-hat,  cymbal, and claps.
  • Built Tough — Rugged and compact for studio and stage. 

AR-110 offers a set of most popular instruments in a compact housing. It is very easy to operate, as it does not require any additional settings, but offers separated volume control for each instrument. In addition to that, it has an individual output for each instrument, which allowing separate FX routing, independent tracking, and greater flexibility for studio and creative live performance.

AR-110 is a perfect inexpensive choice for electronic, techno, hip-hop, rhythm and bass, house and other popular music genres. It is suitable for both professional and amateur music creators. This drum synthesizer is miniature in size, taking up virtually no space on your desk or in a home studio.

This drum synthesizer is made in Canada — a country where devices like this haven’t been produced in a long time. That makes it not only rare, but also something truly special and refreshing.

Analog circuits generate classic drum sounds with six-voice polyphony. Use individual outputs or the master mix to plug into your setup. Control levels live with dedicated knobs.

AR-110 Raw Sounds. Good headphones or speakers will reveal all the sound nuances.

We started by designing each drum voice as a standalone analog circuit, carefully shaping its sound and response. MIDI support and individual outputs were part of the concept from the very beginning. 

After several hardware revisions, we optimized the signal paths and finalized a compact, stage-ready design. This synthesizer is the result of practical engineering and a true passion for analog sound.
