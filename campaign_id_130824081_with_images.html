As Seen In...What is ALPON X5 AI?

Meet the ALPON X5, the edge AI computer built for visionaries. It combines the familiar freedom of Raspberry Pi with the kind of AI performance once found only in data centers. In plain terms: your projects can now see, hear, and make decisions in real time, right where you need them – no cloud required. We designed ALPON X5 to make advanced machine learning accessible to everyone from hobbyists to industry engineers, without the usual complexity.

If you’ve ever tried building a smart device with a Raspberry Pi and found yourself juggling cables, heat issues, or cloud dependencies, you’ll understand the problem we set out to solve.

ALPON X5 is a complete AI computer — combining powerful processing, integrated connectivity, and rugged design — all in a single device that’s ready to use out of the box.

Why we built it

Today, sending every camera image or sensor reading to a distant cloud isn’t just slow – it can be unreliable, expensive, or even a privacy risk. Think about a factory floor or a farm: decisions need to be made in milliseconds, even if the internet goes down. 

ALPON X5 is our answer to that reality. By bringing cloud-level AI power into a small, rugged device, we put intelligence on-site, where it belongs. The result? Faster responses, greater reliability, and total control of your data. It’s not just about tech specs – it’s about trust and independence.

Risks and challenges

Final Certification and Compliance: While ALPON X5 has been engineered for global standards (CE, FCC, carrier certifications), the formal testing phase carries scheduling risks. Our mitigation: Pre-certification tests are already underway, and we’ve partnered with experienced certification labs to minimize any delays. Supply Chain and Scaling Production: Global component sourcing remains somewhat volatile, especially for active semiconductors and modules. Our mitigation: We've secured multiple sourcing options and flexible component pathways to protect against shortages. Final assembly, quality control, and testing will be handled directly at our own ISO 9001 certified Sixfab facility — giving us full oversight of product quality and shipment readiness. Sixfab has over 9 years of experience producing electronics and edge computing devices for global markets, and we’re leveraging that expertise to ensure smooth delivery. Final Thermal Tuning: Real-world deployments in harsh environments require highly reliable thermal management. Our mitigation: Our passive cooling system has passed internal validation, and we are finalizing the production heatsink design through extended field testing. AI Software Stack Expansion: While DEEPX DX-M1 already supports key frameworks (PyTorch, ONNX, TensorFlow), ensuring smooth integration with newer DEEPX Model Zoo releases and SDK updates may require tuning. Our mitigation: We have direct collaboration with DEEPX from the first day of concept and are continuously updating our software stack and OTA platform based on field feedback. Global Fulfillment Logistics: Shipping globally always brings challenges: customs delays, regional regulations, and local carrier variations. Our mitigation: Sixfab already has a proven logistics track record — with over 20,000 orders shipped to more than 100 countries through our USA and EU warehouses. We are experienced in global fulfillment and will continue using our trusted logistics partners and regional hubs to ensure smooth and timely delivery to our Kickstarter backers.

The ALPON X5 is equipped with a powerful AI accelerator (DEEPX DX-M1) and is capable of running a wide range of AI models, including but not limited to computer vision, pose estimation, audio classification, visual language models (VLMs), and other edge AI workloads. While ALPON X5 supports many popular AI frameworks (such as TensorFlow Lite, ONNX, PyTorch, and OpenVINO), the performance of individual models may vary depending on model size, optimization techniques, and deployment conditions. Some advanced models (like LLMs or high-complexity image generators) may require additional optimization or model compression to run efficiently on the device. We will continue to work closely with DEEPX and the open-source community to expand model compatibility and provide updated resources, examples, and deployment guidelines for ALPON X5 users.

Long-lasting design

The ALPON X5 is built with longevity in mind, ensuring long-term usability and sustainability. The hardware is designed with industrial-grade components that support extended operational lifespans, even in harsh environments. Passive cooling mechanisms minimize mechanical wear, while modular and upgradeable storage options allow for extended serviceability. The use of long-term supported software ensures that security updates and optimizations will be available for years to come. Additionally, energy-efficient architecture minimizes power consumption, reducing operational costs while maintaining high performance.

Sustainable materials

We are committed to sustainability and minimizing environmental impact. The ALPON X5's enclosure is designed comply production with recycled aluminum, reducing the need for newly mined materials while maintaining durability and thermal efficiency. Our packaging prioritizes recyclable and biodegradable materials, reducing plastic waste and ensuring responsible disposal. By choosing sustainable materials, we aim to contribute to a circular economy and reduce our carbon footprint while delivering a high-performance AI computing solution.
