Story

THE PROBLEM: 

Full-sized desktop computers clutter your workspace, so a computer with a much smaller footprint will allow more freedom to do whatever you want on your desk. Sadly, most compact mini PCs have fixed components, limiting their upgradeability, and usable lifespan. Other devices may have some level of customizability, but look boring,  just a box with vent holes.

THE SOLUTION:

The XDO Pantera 2 super compact yet capable. Its small footprint does not compromise performance. Unlike the previous generation Pantera, XDO leaves the old plain-box design in favor of having desktop-level power, functional aesthetics, user-replaceable memory, faster and bigger storage capacity, even more upgraded ports, all while keeping the device small enough so it does not take up a lot of space on your desk.

HERE ARE SOME OF THE HIGHLIGHTS OF XDO PANTERA 2

The Pantera 2 uses the AMD Ryzen 7 8840U 8-core 16-threads processor for superior multitasking and processing.

WHAT’S NEW? 

Pantera 1 vs Pantera2

The first iteration of the Pantera uses an Intel J4125. It is a low-power processor designed for entry-level computing tasks. It has a modest clock speed and limited graphics performance, suitable for basic tasks like web browsing, office productivity work, and media playback. It is designed during the pandemic to be the perfect basic setup for those who just started working from home.

The new Pantera 2, however, is a huge upgrade, and now uses the AMD Ryzen 7 7840U with AI capabilities, which is a much more powerful processor, based on AMD’s latest Zen 4 architecture and built with a 4nm process. Far superior to the basic Intel UHD graphics on the J4105, this Ryzen chip is capable of running demanding tasks such as video editing, multitasking, and gaming, thanks to its integrated Radeon 780M GPU.

Why is USB4.0 so big?

We actually had to remove dual ethernet ports in order to allow enough PCIe lanes to be freed up to support USB4.0 module. While it slowed our development down, it ensured we future-proofed the Pantera 2.

WHY CHOOSE A PANTERA 2?

1. SMALL FORM FACTOR

Its compact design frees up desk space, providing more room for other essentials. Unlike traditional desktop towers, the Pantera 2 is compact enough it fits right on the palm your hand, making it easy to slip into a bag or backpack.

2. HOME ENTERTAINMENT HUB

Enjoy your favorite content on a big screen, watch 4K movies, view family photos, and stream music. With a reliable internet connection, the Pantera 2 becomes a versatile media-streaming device, letting you watch Netflix, Amazon Prime, or YouTube. You can expand storage either using the front MicroSD slot, or upgrade the SSD yourself for more capacity. We recommend opting for a higher SSD capacity than you initially think, as storage needs tend to grow rather quickly.

3. ENHANCED CONNECTIVITY OPTIONS

Despite its small size, the Pantera 2 offers a variety of connectivity options. It includes multiple USB ports, HDMI 2.1, Bluetooth 5.3 and WiFi 6e support, allowing seamless connection to various devices. This flexibility lets you quickly connect external peripherals, monitors, and accessories to suit your setup, transforming your workspace with minimal hassle.

4. ENERGY EFFICIENT DESIGN

Designed with energy efficiency in mind, the Pantera 2 consumes significantly less power than traditional desktop computers. This compact computer's lower power draw not only reduces your electricity bills but also makes it an eco-friendly option. You’ll save energy while enjoying a fully functional desktop experience without sacrificing performance.

5. CUSTOMIZABLE RGB LIGHTING AND FRONT OLED STATUS DISPLAY

The Pantera 2 features customizable RGB lighting, letting you personalize the look to match your workspace or mood. Change the lighting to whatever color you wish. The front OLED status display shows you critical system information like time, CPU usage, and system temperature, so you don’t have to switch screens when you’re heavy at work on a project.

6. EASILY UPGRADABLE STORAGE AND MEMORY

The Pantera 2 is built for easy upgrades, allowing users to replace or expand the dual-channel RAM and PCIe SSD without hassle. Its accessible design means you can customize storage or memory yourself as your needs evolve, extending the device’s longevity. Anyone who has replaced RAM or SSD before will find the Pantera 2 a breeze in serviceability. This flexibility makes the Pantera 2 a future-proof investment, adapting with you as your storage and performance needs grow.
