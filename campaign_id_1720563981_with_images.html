Story

Have you ever felt uneasy while traveling alone? 

Perhaps you’ve checked into a hotel late at night and couldn’t shake the feeling that someone might be watching. Have you ever wondered if there could be hidden cameras in the room, or felt nervous walking back alone after dinner in an unfamiliar city?

In moments like these, wouldn’t it be comforting to have a simple yet powerful tool by your side — something compact, discreet, and easy to carry?

The DA01 safety alarm is designed for exactly these situations. With its sleek keychain design, you can easily attach it to your bag, phone, or car keys. If you ever feel threatened, just pull the pin to activate a loud, piercing alarm that draws attention and helps deter danger. It’s peace of mind in your pocket — because no matter where your journey takes you, your safety should always come first.

Environmental commitments

Visit our Environmental Resources Center to learn how Kickstarter encourages sustainable practices.

Long-lasting design

Our product features a durable ABS plastic shell enhanced with an electroplating finish, giving it a sleek metallic look while ensuring long-term resistance to wear and tear. This robust design extends the product’s life cycle, reducing the frequency of replacements.

Reusability and recyclability

Additionally, ABS is a recyclable material, and we encourage responsible disposal and recycling practices to minimize environmental impact.

Sustainable materials

While ABS is a high-quality plastic, we are actively exploring the integration of more sustainable alternatives in our future designs, such as bioplastics or recycled ABS. Our current materials are chosen for their durability, which directly supports sustainability by reducing waste and resource consumption over time.

Environmentally friendly factories

We partner with factories that prioritize environmentally conscious practices, including efficient energy use, waste reduction, and strict compliance with environmental regulations. Our electroplating process is carefully managed to minimize emissions and reduce water usage, aligning with our mission to contribute to a cleaner, greener production ecosystem.

Sustainable Distribution

Our products are sold through both Amazon and our independent online store, allowing us to optimize logistics and reduce our carbon footprint by leveraging regional warehouses and efficient delivery networks. We’re also working towards more eco-friendly packaging solutions, using recyclable or biodegradable materials wherever possible.

Something else

We believe sustainability goes beyond materials—it’s a mindset embedded in our product design, customer support, and long-term goals. We promote user education on responsible product use and disposal, and we’re committed to continuously improving our environmental performance through innovation and collaboration with eco-conscious partners.
