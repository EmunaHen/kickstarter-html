Story

A perfect match for your Mac mini with nostalgic aesthetics that honor computing heritage. Its compact form seamlessly integrates while adding 14 ports of modern connectivity. The ideal blend of vintage charm and contemporary functionality.

Perfectly fit for Mac mini M4/M4 Pro

Wokyis M5 (80Gbps) packs 14 essential connections - from a 5-inch display to an M.2 SSD slot - in one sleek dock. Get pro-grade connectivity without the dongle mess, all designed to match your Mac mini M4/M4 Pro perfectly.

All essential ports include 10Gbps USB 3.2 ports and an SD/microSD 4.0 card reader. 3.5mm earphone jack delivers ultra-wideband audio input and output at 32bit 8-384kHz spectrum.

Easily add up to 8TB of fast NVMe storage via M.2 slot, while M5 10Gbps delivers 1000MB/s transfer speed and M5 80Gbps delivers 7000MB/s transfer speed for instant 8K video editing. Plus, Wokyis M5 80Gbps version will include a fan to prevent overheating and performance throttling.A perfect combo for creators needing massive, organized storage without desktop clutter.

This vibrant display keeps your workflow smooth: monitor system stats, control music, or showcase pixel art. It's equal parts nostalgic charm and modern functionality, transforming your dock into an interactive workstation.

This thoughtfully crafted dock enhances your workspace with intuitive features that simplify your workflow, while clever cable management maintains your desk's clean aesthetic. Every detail is designed to elevate your daily productivity with effortless elegance.
