Story

Ever feel like your camera just can’t keep up?At present, zoom cameras are relatively bulky, but our zoom cameras have wide-angle and zoom functions, and can also be compact and lightweight. 

Missing the moment because of slow focus, limited zoom, or blurry distant shots?Fast-moving subjects and complex scenes shouldn’t mean compromised. 

Meet the Amkov F3B — a revolutionary Triple-Lens Photo/Video Hybrid Zoom Digital Camera designed for creators, travelers, and photography enthusiasts.

It’s built to transform your shooting experience across the board.
Capture everyday moments with ease or shoot high-quality telephoto videos with precision — it offers unmatched versatility across a wide range of shooting scenarios, turning every moment into a masterpiece.

The Amkov F3B digital camera revolutionizes imaging with its cutting-edge triple-lens collaborative system. Seamlessly integrating wide-angle, mid-focus, and telephoto lenses, it offers extraordinary versatility for capturing every shot, whether in dynamic urban environments or expansive outdoor vistas. Each lens is meticulously engineered for optimal performance, ensuring your shots are rich in clarity, depth, and detail—no matter the setting. With a stackable lens design, ultra-thin body, and ultimate portability, it’s easy to take anywhere and use effortlessly.

The wide-angle lens, equipped with a Sony 1/2.8" CMOS sensor and 12MP resolution, delivers exceptional image quality. Enhanced by a 64MP oversampling process, it combines low-resolution images into one high-resolution shot, improving sharpness and detail. With a 76.4° field of view, 2X digital zoom, and 5cm autofocus, it captures wide scenes and close-ups with remarkable clarity and precision

Optimized for portraiture, the mid-focus lens features a 41° field of view, ideal for subjects 3 meters away. Its design brings subjects into sharp relief while subtly blurring the background, offering a natural bokeh effect that enhances facial details. Whether capturing fleeting expressions or dynamic poses, this lens excels in delivering razor-sharp clarity and exquisite tonal richness.

The telephoto lens, with its 19° narrow field of view, is engineered for precision in long-range photography. Perfect for subjects over 7 meters away, it brings distant scenes into sharp focus, maintaining exceptional clarity even at a distance. The 3X digital zoom further enhances flexibility, allowing you to magnify distant subjects effortlessly. Ideal for landscapes, architecture, or action shots, this lens ensures every frame is rich in detail, from the furthest points of the frame to the finest nuances.

From close-ups to distant views, the Amkov F3B’s triple-lens system ensures effortless mastery across any subject, capturing every moment with exceptional precision.

The Amkov F3B’s 12X hybrid zoom combines 4x optical and 3x digital zoom, delivering seamless flexibility without lens swaps. Effortlessly transition from wide shots to close-ups or bring distant subjects into focus with speed and precision.
4x Optical Zoom: Crisp, high-quality images with zero loss of detail.
3x Digital Zoom: Enhanced flexibility for capturing distant subjects with ease.

Support for 5K at 30FPS video recording delivers sharp, detailed footage, perfect for Vlogs, short films, and professional video production. The built-in high-quality microphone and speakers ensure precise audio-visual synchronization, capturing every subtle sound detail.

Capture every intricate detail with 64MP resolution, delivering natural, true-to-life color and light performance for breathtaking still images.

The F3B is equipped with advanced manual controls to adapt seamlessly to any shooting environment. Adjustable ISO lets you fine-tune exposure for varying lighting conditions, while ±3 EV exposure compensation ensures balanced brightness in complex scenes. Multiple white balance modes provide true-to-life color reproduction, accurately capturing the mood of every moment. Anti-flicker technology further enhances image stability—ideal for fast-paced shooting and professional-grade video production.

The Amkov F3B camera features a 2.8" IPS screen (640×480 resolution) for clear, detailed viewing. With responsive buttons for smooth navigation, every interaction is intuitive and effortless.

The Amkov F3B is equipped with a high-performance NP-40 professional battery (1300mAh), combined with advanced Type-C fast charging, reaching a full charge in just 2–3 hours. Enjoy up to 2 hours of continuous snap shooting and up to 1.5 hours of video recording on a single charge. 

This perfect balance of long-lasting battery life and rapid recharging ensures uninterrupted power for extended shooting sessions. Whether on location or during intensive projects, the F3B keeps creators focused on their vision — not their battery.

Perfect for long shoots and managing high-resolution content, it meets the rigorous demands of professional photographers and videographers, ensuring smooth, uninterrupted work.
