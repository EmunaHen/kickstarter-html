FAQ

How long does the filter last?

The Salus Air Filter is designed to last approximately 1-2 months under normal conditions. Factors like air quality, usage frequency, and environmental conditions can affect this timeframe.

Will the filter reduce my AC unit's efficiency?

Like any filter, the Salus Air Filter will cause some reduction in airflow. However, our filter is specifically engineered for window AC units with a design that balances filtration performance with proper airflow. Unlike standard filters that could cause your machine to overwork, Salus is optimized to protect your AC unit while still providing excellent air purification.

How do I know when it's time to replace the filter?

We recommend replacing your filter every 1-2 months for optimal performance. However, if you notice visible dirt accumulation or reduced airflow, it might be time to replace it sooner.

Can I wash and reuse the filter?

No, the Salus Air Filter is not washable as this would damage the specialized nanofiber material. It's designed to be replaced periodically for optimal performance.

How does this compare to a standalone air purifier?

The Salus Air Filter leverages your window AC's powerful airflow, which circulates air 120-300 times per hour versus just 2-5 times per hour for typical standalone air purifiers. This means significantly more air passes through our filter, cleaning your air far more efficiently.

Is my AC unit compatible with the Salus Filter?

We currently support models from Midea, Keystone, and Frigidaire. You can check our compatibility section to see if your specific model is supported. If you don't see your model listed, please contact us.
