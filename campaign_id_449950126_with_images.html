Story

“We asked Science to Translate Love” 

In 2018, a team of animal behavior scientists and AI engineers at the University of Cambridge made a breakthrough: they proved that pets’ emotional responses are deeply tied to how they physically perceive the world. A dog’s wagging tail isn’t just excitement—it’s a reaction to the specific shade of blue in your jeans (since they can’t see red). A cat’s midnight zoomies? Triggered by dust particles moving at 60fps in moonlight, invisible to human eyes. Therefore one question haunted our team: 

"If pets see the world so differently, how can we ever truly understand them?"

A PetLens is here to change that


Our teams saw it and merge cutting-edge research with AI-driven empathy: 

• Science Core: Partnered with zoos and labs to map the visual systems of 50+ species (from cats to chameleons). 

• Tech Leap: Developed real-time filters that don’t just "simulate" animal vision— they reconstruct it using device sensors (e.g., adjusting light wavelengths, frame rates, and FOV). 

• Emotional AI: Trained models on 10,000+ hours of pet behavior videos to decode subtle cues (a rabbit’s ear twitch = curiosity, not fear).Record pet’s reaction to their reflection (with vision filter). AI analyzes self-awareness cues. 

Early testers cried during trials. One cat owner finally understood why her pet ignored red toys (they appeared gray) but went wild for a blue blanket. A parrot owner realized her bird wasn’t "biting"—it was trying to preen her hair under UV light invisible to humans. 

A PetLens wasn’t just an app anymore. It became a bridge between species.

Memory Suite: Science taught us their vision. Love taught us their heart.

L – Love through Their Eyes: A PetLens creates daily AI-generated diary entries and milestones by capturing moments through pet-specific vision, helping owners understand their pets’ unique worlds and deepen emotional bonds. 

E – Eternal Memories & Tributes: The app’s Legacy Tools translate pet vision and hearing data into personalized sensory memorials, preserving beloved companions’ memories even after they’re gone. 

N – Network of Empathy & Connection: A PetLens fosters a global pet-centric community, enabling joyful sharing of unique pet perspectives, arranging pet playdates through AI-driven matching, and boosting lost pet recovery and adoption through crowdsourced vision data. 

S – Saving Wildlife through Vision: “Wild Lens” empowers users to protect wildlife by capturing and identifying rare species through specialized vision filters and AI alerts, collaborating seamlessly with conservation and rehabilitation efforts worldwide.

Risks and challenges

A PetLens, like any innovative tech project, faces a few challenges: 1. AI Accuracy & Bias: Animal vision models may misrepresent certain species due to incomplete scientific data. We’ve partnered with universities and animal protection charity to expand datasets. 2. Real-Time Processing Limits: High-fidelity filters (e.g., UV/thermal simulations) could lag on older devices. Our team is prioritizing core features and refining them through constant updates. 3. Privacy & Exploitation: Location data from rescue posts could be abused. We will ensure GPS auto-off unless "Lost Pet Alert" activated or permission granted. We're prepared to tackle these challenges head-on and will keep our backers fully informed throughout the journey.

Use of AI

A PetLens generates AI content and harvests user data to train AI. It relies on the kind of generative AI tools that have become prevalent in recent years. Our intelligent matching algorithm is built on tried-and-true machine learning techniques—the same kind that have been around for decades—to build the Vision Reconstruction Engine and Behavior-Decoding system. We leverage sophisticated AI and machine learning algorithms to analyze animal behavior, calculate bonding, and continually refine behavior-generation methods based on learning outcomes. To keep A PetLens a safe and welcoming space, we implement privacy and data protection measures, together with end-to-end content encryption. We may leverage AI-powered models to help detect harmful behavior and protect users—but we always have the final say.
