Our Mission: Sustainability Meets Convenience

Every day, millions of people enjoy their coffee-to-go – but convenience often falls short: burning fingers, unstable cups, and excessive waste. That’s where the Cup Clip comes in! The Idea Behind the Cup Clip

The Cup Clip was designed to offer a simple yet effective solution to an everyday problem. Our innovative Cup Clip provides:

  • Comfort: No more direct contact with hot cups.
  • Flexibility: Adapts to different cup sizes.
  • Sustainability: A reusable alternative to disposable solutions.
  • Style: A sleek design that enhances the coffee-to-go moment.

These upgrades make the Cup Clip more versatile, sustainable, and stylish than ever before.

Long-lasting design

The Cup Clip is engineered for everyday use with a sturdy, high-quality build that withstands wear and tear. Whether you’re commuting, traveling, or grabbing coffee on the go, this reliable clip will be with you every step of the way. Its smart design ensures a secure hold for your cup – day after day, year after year.

Reusability and recyclability

Say goodbye to single-use solutions. The Cup Clip is fully reusable and designed to replace disposable cup sleeves and holders. When it eventually reaches the end of its life, the materials can be recycled – making it a responsible choice for anyone who values sustainability and circular design.

Sustainable materials

The Cup Clip is made with Sulapac – a biodegradable, bio-based material that combines the natural look and feel of wood with the performance of traditional plastics. It’s free from microplastics, industrially compostable, and sourced from renewable raw materials. Choosing Sulapac means choosing a cleaner planet, without sacrificing quality or durability.
