✨ Meet LORA — The AI Vision Assistant That Sees, Listens, and Understands

Imagine a voice assistant that doesn’t just follow commands — it truly understands you: your words, your face, your gestures and your habits.

Introducing LORA, the world's first AI Vision Assistant that works both online and offline. It taps into powerful cloud AI when you’re connected and switches seamlessly to its built-in GPT when you’re offline — so it’s always ready to help.

What sets LORA apart? 

Unlike traditional assistants, LORA doesn’t just listen — it sees, understands gestures, and adapts to your habits. By combining real-time computer vision, voice verification, and gesture control with its advanced GPT-powered brain, LORA delivers deeply personalised, context-aware assistance that feels genuinely human.

With its intelligent computer vision, LORA brings to life: 

  • Face Recognition — Personalised interactions tailored just for you.
  • Gesture Control — Seamlessly control your assistant with intuitive hand movements.
  • Infinity Vision — Understands visual context in real time, making interactions smooth, natural and truly human-like.

LORA recognises faces in just 300ms ⚡️ —  supporting multiple users with personalised responses for each one. It adapts instantly to who it sees, making every interaction unique. And with all face data processed locally 🔐, your privacy is never comprised.

The Assistant understands your hand movements — no need to speak or touch anything. With support for 4 distinct gestures, you can control music, trigger actions. Detection happens in real-time and offline, ensuring privacy and responsiveness even without an internet connection. Whether you're relaxing or multitasking, a simple gesture is all it takes.

Infinity Vision Mode is LORA’s advanced real-time perception system, designed to continuously monitor and interpret activities within its environment using AI-powered computer vision.

Infinity Vision Mode automatically captures snapshots when it detects new or unusual activity in its surroundings. This snapshot is sent to Vision GPT, which analyses the scene and determines the context. Based on the analysis, LORA performs the appropriate action.

Capable of understanding and responding to any question — from generating a creative recipe to explaining random facts or complex ideas. Whether casual or curious, every query is met with clear, thoughtful answers.

Powered by an advanced, custom-built cognitive engine, LORA remembers, adapts, and grows smarter over time.

  • Personalisation: Learns your routines, preferences, and conversational style.
  • Memory Architecture: A unique 10-thread memory system efficiently categorises and retains important interactions, habits, and meaningful data while discarding temporary or irrelevant information.

To support this, LORA comes with a built-in classification model that analyses every incoming request. If the message is personal and meaningful, it's remembered. If it's general or temporary, it's simply answered and forgotten — keeping LORA fast, efficient and privacy-first.

Your assistant knows you — your name, routines, and story. Remembers who you are and adapts just for you.

LORA recognises more than words — it identifies who's speaking. Its advanced voice verification adapts actions and responses based on recognised user, ensuring tailored reminders, music and preferences.

Enjoy precise, clean speech interactions:

  • Two-Stage Recording System: Combines Voice Activity Detection with specialised sound classification, minimising false activations.
  • Real-Time Noise Suppression: Reduces background noise by up to 95%, ensuring your voice is clear and commands accurate.

Simplify your daily routine with intuitive voice and gesture commands. From setting alarms and timers to managing personalised reminders, LORA ensures seamless organisation tailored specifically to your needs.

It gives you real-time, voice-powered access to the internet.
Ask anything — from breaking news and live weather updates to deep research or quick facts. LORA searches the web for you and delivers answers in a natural, spoken conversation. No screens, no typing — just instant, meaningful responses from the world’s information, right when you need it.

Even without an internet connection, LORA stays fully functional. Whether you're deep in the forest or completely off the grid, LORA continues to support you with fast, private, and reliable assistance. It's built to perform wherever you are, anytime.

Why LORA is Truly Unique?

LORA isn't just another voice assistant — it's totally new category of intelligent companion. Unlike typical assistants locked in your phone or the cloud, LORA is designed for real-world interaction — combining voice, vision, and gesture to understand and help you naturally.

How LORA Compares to Other Assistants?

In normal use, LORA sends requests to secure cloud server, where powerful GPT-based models generate intelligent responses in real time. This allows LORA to provide fast, accurate, and highly capable conversational interaction — always up-to-date.

When no internet connection is available, or if the user prefers offline mode, LORA can still operate using its built-in local language model. In this mode, features like basic conversation and object detection continue to work — without sending data to the cloud. While offline functionality is more limited, it ensures LORA remains helpful even without a connection.

Access to cloud features requires subscription to support server costs. We estimate it at around 5-10 € per month, depending on subscription type.

Our assistant is powered by the Raspberry Pi 5 with 4GB RAM. It features speakers (120–20,000 Hz), a 720p camera for vision tasks, and a sensitive mono microphone to capture your commands. Everything you need for a truly smart assistant, in one compact device.

The Assistant is designed to be versatile, intuitive, and helpful across different environments. Whether you're managing your home, supporting a loved one, studying, or working — LORA adapts to your needs, offering smart assistance without screens, complexity, or noise.

In the future, LORA can be trained for specific use cases — with potential versions tailored for offices, hospitals, and universities.

If there is strong demand, we plan to transition from 3D printing to injection molding for more efficient large-scale production and a polished finish.

Risks and challenges

1. On-device Resource Constraints: Running LLMs or multimodal models on embedded devices available on-site like Raspberry Pi faces challenges owing to restricted CPU/GPU power and memory bandwidth. Solution: Apply model quantization, pruning and distillation to reduce computational load. Use lightweight models, run inference via ONNX Runtime, TFLite. 2. Reliability: False positives from wake word or gesture detection due to environmental variability. Solution: Use multi-modal verification, introduce calibration modes for different environments. 3. Privacy & Security: Secure, encrypted in-device storage and well-defined retention policies are needed to protect conversation history, face data, and user preferences. Solution: Use encrypted local storage, enable secure boot, provide users with data control options. 4. Maintenance & Costs: Keeping ML models current requires an ever-running integration pipeline with the ability to test for performance both against hardware versions and edge cases. Updating fresh new features or patches into distant devices needs efficient update methods. Also, relying on external APIs like Bing Search, OpenAI, or weather streams introduces cost scalability concerns; batched requests, request caching, and utilization caps are needed to limit erratic increases in billing. Solution: Build an automated pipeline that is adjustable to new updated models.
