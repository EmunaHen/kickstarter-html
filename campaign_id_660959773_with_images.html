Story

We are fidgeters by nature.  We chew on our eye glasses, click our nails, repeat words, etc.:  We toy with objects, thoughts, ideas and actions.  We turn them over and over until we find what we're looking for.  What if there was a gadget that allowed us to fidget with it in a multitude of ways; one that not only had function, but style?    

 

The Magnecron bracelet was designed with both form and function in mind.  It took months of research and development to nail down the final concept and find the right materials to make the final prototype.  It had to feel right in the hands when fidgeting with and had to look good.

The First Prototype

The initial idea was for the Magnecron to be a bangle, having a solid ring of magnets.  This not only was limiting in function, but impractical in terms of manufacturing.  Welding quote inquiries were rejected, and it didn't fit well when wearing it.  Its function was stuck in 2D.

The Final Prototype

The idea for the final prototype came soon after constructing the initial one.  Soon, I would make bracelets out of countless types of rope, bungee cord and paracord.  When  I finally arrived at the perfect combination of cord and magnet, everything fit together and operated as smooth as butter.   

This bracelet is constructed with only the finest materials and components on the market.  Only the strongest magnets, the most durable steel clasps, the best adhesive, and the toughest paracord are used.     

I lucked out and found a company that not only provided the perfect paracord to construct the bracelet, but one that also had a collection of vibrant colors for the bracelet's customization.  When I say this bracelet will be custom, I'm not just talking it'll fit great on your wrist.  You'll have several different colors to choose from.  Check them out below:  

It took a long time to design the gold-plated edition of the bracelet.  I not only had to sift the entire internet for magnets that were plated in real gold and not fake gold color, but I had to search for durable bayonet clasps that were also plated in real gold too.  At the end of the day, I'm proud to include this special edition within the collection.  

The Magnecron bracelet is not just for show or for fidgeting with.  Sometimes the mind needs something to focus on while meditating or needs a mode of distraction in situations where you want to switch gears or where stress management is needed.  

The Magnecron bracelet is modular in nature.  You can connect multiple bracelets together either by clasp or by magnet.  Your imagination is the limit.    

Shipping was a challenge due to international package costs being much more expensive than domestic, so I was not able to have one cost for everyone across the board.  However, with the light weight of the products, I was able to work out the costs to be the same no matter what reward package you order.  All the reward categories fell within the same package weight range.  

Magnets aren't for everyone.  Some have sensitive medical devices or small children to be mindful of.  Please read the medical bulletin below for more information.    

Risks and challenges

Designing the bracelet with the right materials was a big challenge. You can't construct this bracelet out of just any paracord or set of magnets. Everything was carefully selected to fit like magic and be as smooth as butter when fidgeting with. As for risks, I don't foresee many as I am quite familiar with product manufacturing and shipping. I care about this project a lot so I will personally oversee the entire process of construction, quality control and fulfillment to ensure minimal issues.
