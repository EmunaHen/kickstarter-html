Risks and challenges

Technology R&D risks: There is still room for optimization in the deep integration technology of color e-ink screen and mobile power bank. Compatibility issues between software and hardware may lead to unstable functions, such as occasional interruptions of Bluetooth and NFC connections, or delayed responses of AI-generated wallpaper functions. Supply chain risks: The supply of raw materials may be affected by global market fluctuations, natural disasters, or supplier production problems, resulting in delayed delivery of key components such as battery packs and e-ink screens, which in turn affects the overall production schedule. Manufacturing risks: Production process problems may arise during mass production, such as insufficient yield of precision patch processes, or production stagnation caused by equipment failure on the assembly line, affecting the delivery cycle and quality of the product. Logistics and transportation risks: Uncertainties in international logistics, such as customs clearance delays, damage or loss of goods during transportation, may result in the product not being delivered to supporters on time and in good condition.
