Story

Starlink’s high power consumption creates several challenges for outdoor use. Conventional power supplies often require large, bulky batteries that are heavy and take up too much space. Getting reliable power outdoors is inconvenient, and in bad weather, solar panels may not provide enough energy. On top of that, using a DC converter can be risky due to compatibility concerns, and DIY setups for a Starlink DC power supply are often too complex and time-consuming.

Introducing the EL8 Pro—a compact, efficient, and intelligent outdoor power solution designed specifically for Starlink. Say goodbye to bulky, inefficient power stations, energy-draining AC supplies, and complicated DIY setups. With just a simple plug-and-play connection, you’ll enjoy endless power and seamless connectivity, wherever your adventure takes you.

Designed Specifically for Starlink
3-in-1 Compatibility: Whether you have a Starlink Standard, Standard Actuated, or Mini dish, the EL8 Pro has you covered. It offers two DC output voltages, so you can easily switch between Starlink models without hassle.

30% More Power-Efficient: Unlike traditional power stations that suffer from AC inverter losses, the EL8 Pro delivers direct DC PoE power to your Starlink dish, eliminating energy waste and maximizing your battery life. Plus, with an additional DC 12V output to power your router, you’ll save even more energy. 

Less power consumption = Longer runtime, giving you more of what matters—uninterrupted connectivity on your adventures.        

Reviews

LAN Port for the 3rd Party Router Setup: The EL8 Pro includes a LAN port that lets you connect your preferred router for a personalized network setup. Choose your speed, configure settings, and get the most out of your Starlink connection.

No complicated wiring, no DIY setup. Simply plug in your Starlink system, and the EL8 Pro will handle the rest. You’re up and running in just three easy steps—easy setup, guaranteed.

1. 648Wh Detachable Battery: The EL8 Pro comes with a 648Wh internal battery that’s easy to replace, giving you the ability to extend your outdoor adventures without worrying about running out of power.
2. Optional 298Wh Battery: For those on a tighter budget or lighter trips, we also offer a 298Wh battery pack, allowing you to tailor your power solution to your needs.

Built to withstand the elements, the EL8 Pro uses safe DC voltage to avoid high-voltage hazards and eliminates the need for heat vents, making it both dustproof and bug-resistant. It’s also rated IP54 for water resistance, making it suitable for all outdoor conditions.

 Solar-Powered Efficiency: The EL8 Pro supports both AC charging at home and solar panel charging in the wild. Equipped with an MPPT charge controller, it maximizes solar efficiency, so you can keep your gear powered up even off the grid.

Charge & Power Simultaneously: While charging, EL8 Pro powers your Starlink—automatically prioritizing Starlink when charging power is high, and using the battery when it's low.

The EL8 Pro features a user-friendly display that shows the POE output, dish voltage, power status, and battery levels in real time. This allows you to monitor your Starlink’s operation and power usage easily, ensuring optimal performance wherever you are.

The EL8 Pro doesn’t just power your Starlink—it also features two QC 3.0 18W USB ports and one PD 60W USB-C port, delivering fast charging for your phones, laptops, cameras, and more. Perfect for outdoor tech lovers who need to keep multiple devices powered up.
