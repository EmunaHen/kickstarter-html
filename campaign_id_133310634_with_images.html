Story

Bacbudy has been pushing the boundaries of exoskeleton technology since 2023. Partnering with top-tier universities and construction companies in Hong Kong (Hong Kong Polytechnic University, QS ranking 57) and the United Kingdom (University of Cambridge, QS ranking 5), we have created a revolutionary exosuit that feels like everyday clothing. It dramatically reduces the effort needed to lift heavy objects and transforms the way you work! 

Risks and challenges

We’ve crafted multiple cutting-edge prototypes of the Bacbudy Exosuit and mapped out a killer plan to launch production. With a detailed bill of materials, we know exactly what it takes to bring this innovation to life and meet our delivery promises. But that’s not all! We’re anticipating an overwhelming wave of excitement, and we’re fully prepared to ride the tide. While we can’t predict every twist and turn—like supplier hiccups or customs surprises—we’re set to tackle any challenge head-on. If demand skyrockets, we’ll be working tirelessly, 24/7, to ramp up production and deliver your Bacbudy Exosuit without missing a beat. Every adventure has its risks, but we’re here to assure you: we’ve tried our best to got it all under control. Our commitment to our backers is unwavering, and we can’t wait to bring this thrilling experience to life for you!
