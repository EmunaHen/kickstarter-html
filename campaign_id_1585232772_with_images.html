Story

In today's fast-paced life, traditional coffee machines are falling behind. They're complicated, limited in function, lack personalization, and can't meet modern users' need for quick and convenient coffee. Now, the KAXFREE SF1 Max arrives as a new smart coffee machine. It offers a wide range of hot and cold drinks—from bold espresso to smooth lattes, classic cappuccinos to refreshing cold brews.

It features a sliding LED screen with dual-order modes and customizable settings. Connecting to the app enables remote ordering, timed startup, and AI bean recognition. The SF1 Max overcomes traditional limitations, making coffee-making more convenient, efficient, and personalized.

Using the Venturi principle, it creates a low-pressure zone with high-speed steam in a narrow area, blending milk or alternative with air to produce smooth and creamy froth. The dual-channel design ensures that the making of cold and hot froth doesn't interfere with each other. You can switch seamlessly between cold and hot froth without changing the milk tank. Whether you're enjoying a velvety latte, a rich cappuccino, or a refreshing iced macchiato, you'll have a convenient and efficient experience.

The large 6.86“ display breaks through the traditional limitation, providing tablet-level visual enjoyment. Quickly responded touch control ensures coffee recipes switch and coffee parameter adjustments are smooth without delay.

Choose from 28+ hot and cold recipes, crafted by Q-Grader baristas. From espresso shots to iced lattes, every cup is a barista’s masterpiece, made easy with one-touch brewing.

While exceptional coffee is just a touch away, KAXFREE also invites you to unleash your creativity. With adjustable parameters, you can experiment with key features, crafting your own unique flavors and aromas in the machine and an innovative APP. More options, more flavors

With OTA upgrade, the SF1 series will have more possibilities, new coffee recipe updates, more recognition of coffee bean brands, and cleaning visualization.

Cold brew extraction is a craft, and achieving fast, rich flavor requires the right technology. KAXFREE’s 20-bar high-pressure pulsed extraction system ensures instant cold brew in just 1 minute, delivering smooth, full-bodied coffee. With remarkable speed and efficiency, it eliminates the long wait without sacrificing taste or quality.

Enjoy a perfect cup every time with KAXFREE’s self-developed precision grinding system in just 20 seconds at its fastest, which is suitable for different roasting coffee beans. Whether you’re brewing bold espresso or smooth cold brew, KAXFREE gives you full control for the perfect grind.

The Anti-Gravity Extraction, Smart Coffee Powder Pre-Infusion, and High-precision Secondary Filtration work together with an advanced micron-grade filtration system, ensuring a smooth, grit-free finish. Enjoy café-quality coffee made perfectly, every time.

Empower you to enjoy your perfect cup of coffee at your fingertips, anytime, anywhere.

Pre-order your coffee before bedtime, wake up to a fresh brew, and save an extra 10 minutes of sweet dreams

Every customization represents a mood with its unique characteristics. SF1 Max supports adding a customized recipe to My Favorites in the machine and the app, and has no user limits. This means every family member can have their own user profiles with signature recipes. Flavor record, record every mood.

KAXFREE’s one-click automatic cleaning and maintenance programs keep your machine in top condition, avoiding the complicated steps of manual disassembly and flushing, ensuring hassle-free use and the high level hygiene with every cup.

The coffee machine features a fridge-friendly milk tank with a retractable spout that fits various cup sizes. The spout height is adjustable between 79mm and 134mm, and the milk tank spout can tilt up to 30 degrees for better flexibility. Additionally, the milk tank spout tube can be extended by 23mm for added convenience.

Risks and challenges

As KAXFREE continues to expand in the smart coffee machine market, several risks and challenges must be addressed. Moreover, as KAXFREE expands globally, compliance with diverse international safety and food-grade certifications becomes essential to gaining consumer trust. Overcoming these challenges will be key to KAXFREE’s long-term success.
