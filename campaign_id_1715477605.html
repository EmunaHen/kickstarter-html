Story

Hi! We’re the co-founders of Vobble, and more importantly, parents.

Did you know? 71% of the US parents today are concerned about their kids' digital use. When the pandemic hit, we saw our kids (like many others) slowly drifting and getting lost in the world of videos and social media. We knew something had to be done.

After 30+ years of building products and brands globally, we set out to create an immersive, interactive listening experience. Something kids choose and parents can trust without guilt.

Storytelling has been around for generations. Remember these audio players? It's time for an audio player designed to engage today's children.

Vobble is an interactive audio player that lets kids do more than just listen to plain audiobooks. It helps kids step into the world of their favorite characters where they can:

  • Listen to highly dramatized audio stories, music and podcasts
  • Create their own audio stories 
  • Play interactive audio games
  • Explore their favorite topics through chats with fun and engaging characters

Vobble comes with a tiny screen that's only used for navigation. It’s just there to help kids pick what they want to listen to, kind of like flipping through a playlist or tapping on a favorite character.

No videos. No ads. No sneaky apps.

And it’s not just a screen. Vobble also packs a mic, an 8MP camera, in-built 4G with data, Bluetooth, WiFi, and powerful speakers.

Kids can stream content seamlessly at home or download their favorites directly onto the device for offline adventures. Perfect for car rides, plane trips, or cozy corners without internet. 

No data sharing, no ads, no worries. 

You call the shots with full parental control, while Vobble delivers handpicked content made just for kids. 

And with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) compliance in progress, we’re making sure safety isn’t just a feature. It’s the foundation.

Our AI? Smart enough to chat, safe enough to trust. 

Vobble’s AI is designed by parents, for kids and comes with 4 layers of protection, each thoughtfully crafted to keep your child safe. It includes profanity filters, content moderation, and real-time verification. Any questionable input from kids isn’t just blocked, it’s also instantly flagged to parents.

Risks and challenges

After years of building brands at PepsiCo and Unilever (and even landing a deal on Shark Tank), we set out to create something kids truly need: a screen-free, imagination-first audio experience. That’s how Vobble was born. We’ve led global projects across design, testing, manufacturing, logistics, and retail. We’ve brought all that experience into building Vobble. Every part of the supply chain has been carefully planned to deliver a high-quality product, on time. Our manufacturing partner is a leader in high-quality audio devices with years of experience producing durable, safety-tested products for global markets. We’ve already invested in tooling, created production-ready prototypes, and completed third-party lab testing. Vobble meets the highest global toy safety standards. Bringing Vobble to life comes with challenges, but we’re prepared to tackle them. While our growing library includes both original and licensed content, expanding it requires ongoing partnerships. We're actively collaborating with top publishers to keep our content fresh and exciting. We know that surprises can happen, and we’re ready for them. Most importantly, we’re committed to being transparent, proactive, and keeping our backers informed at every step. Vobble is built with care. For your kids, and for your peace of mind.

Use of AI

Vobble leverages AI tools to help generate personalized audio experiences, allowing children to create their own AI-assisted stories using prompts, photos, or voice recordings. Additionally, AI powers interactive character chats making learning and play more immersive. While AI plays a supporting role in content generation and engagement, all creative direction, curation, and development are led by our team of writers, producers and sound designers. Our goal is to use AI responsibly, to enhance human creativity, not replace it.
