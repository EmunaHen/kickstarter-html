Story

Experience powerful, efficient dehumidification with Venowa, our Dual-Semiconductor Dehumidifier. Featuring two semiconductor modules, it delivers up to 800ml/day of moisture removal with a spacious 2.4L tank—meaning fewer water changes. Enjoy smart features like auto shut-off when full, manual defrosting, customizable humidity settings (30%-90%), sleep mode, and a built-in negative ion purifier for fresher air. 

The front water-level window, optional ambient lighting, timer function, and included drainage hose make it perfect for any space. Breathe easier and live more comfortably with this next-level home essential!

Equipped with dual semiconductor modules, this dehumidifier delivers faster and more efficient moisture removal, reaching up to 800ml/day—ideal for larger spaces and humid conditions.

The generous 2.4-liter tank can hold up to 3 days’ worth of water (800ml/day), reducing the hassle of frequent emptying and providing uninterrupted dehumidification.

When the water tank is full or not properly installed, the device automatically shuts off and alerts you with a sound and red indicator light for added safety.

Personalize your space with colorful ambient lights. The device cycles through colors automatically—tap to freeze your favorite hue or turn off the lights anytime.

Monitor water levels at a glance with the front-facing transparent tank window—no need to guess when it’s time to empty.

Set your desired humidity level from 30% to 90%. The device automatically stops once the target humidity is reached, helping you maintain ideal indoor comfort.

You can also set a timer to automatically power off the dehumidifier after your chosen duration—ideal for energy savings and hands-free control.

Refresh your air with built-in anion technology. Just long-press the "+" button to activate and enjoy cleaner, fresher indoor air.

Activate sleep mode for quieter operation, dimmed lights, and lower power consumption—perfect for bedrooms or nighttime use.

Equipped with a pre-filter containing activated carbon, it helps trap dust and odors for cleaner and healthier indoor air.

Risks and challenges

While the Venowa dehumidifier design is finalized and our prototype has passed extensive internal testing, there are still a few challenges that may affect production and delivery: Component Supply Delays: The dehumidifier uses dual semiconductor modules and custom PCBs. Global shortages or delays in sourcing key components may impact our production timeline. Manufacturing & Scaling Risks: While we work with an experienced factory, scaling from prototype to mass production may introduce unforeseen issues, such as yield rate fluctuations or assembly inconsistencies. Quality Control: We have implemented strict QC procedures at multiple stages, but early production batches may still require adjustment to meet our quality standards. Shipping & Logistics: Global shipping remains unpredictable. Delays due to customs clearance, port congestion, or geopolitical events may impact the estimated delivery window. Regulatory Certifications: Compliance testing (e.g., CE, FCC, etc.) is underway. In rare cases, certification delays could impact shipping to certain regions. Despite these risks, the Venowa team is well-prepared. We have alternative suppliers, a responsive production partner, and clear contingency plans. We promise transparent, frequent updates to keep all backers informed throughout the process.
