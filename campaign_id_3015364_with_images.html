Story

Ever Seen A Smokeless Fire Pit That Fits In Its Backpack?

Us neither. So, we built it! 

Arriving in early fall, you can enjoy the full heat of our packable, full-size smokeless fire pit while camping, beaching it, tailgating, and at every other location where you might say, “I wish we had a bonfire right now.”

What Makes Us Different?

Tired of fire pits that are too bulky for spontaneous adventures, too small to actually keep you warm, and too smokey to roast marshmallows without burning your eyes?Enter PACKFIRE, a round fire pit that packs flat into its backpack!

PACKFIRE is built for adventurers who want a full-sized portable fire pit without the bulk—offering Clearburn™ smokeless technology, simple setup, and effortless packability.

With a 17” diameter and a greater burn volume than top competitors, it radiates 360° heat for top-to-toe warmth. Plus, no one has to spend the night chopping away, as PACKFIRE fits full-size 16” logs.

Sets Up In Seconds. Packs Flat. Goes Anywhere.

After a surprising “eureka” moment (and bringing one of our founders out of retirement), we’ve put our years of outdoor industry experience into designing the ultimate portable fire pit. Why? So you can make lifelong memories around a bonfire—wherever and whenever the moment strikes.

Take PACKFIRE Anywhere

PACKFIRE’s patent-pending Fold-Flat™ design makes it the most travel-friendly full-size fire pit around.

Simply slide it into its highly durable, easy-to-carry backpack and keep moving—no more wrestling with awkward, bulky fire pits.

From Backpack to Bonfire In Seconds

Setup is effortless, and intuitive with no tools needed. Just pop out the QuickDraw™ legs and interlock the parts.

Enjoy A Smokeless Experience

Our innovative Clearburn™ technology re-burns heat and smoke before it escapes. This creates hotter, cleaner fires with a wide feel-the-heat zone that warms the whole crew.

Risks and challenges

This isn’t our first fire. The PACKFIRE team includes seasoned product developers, outdoor gear veterans, and former leaders from brands like GCI Outdoor. We’ve taken this fire pit through dozens of design iterations, rigorous testing, and real-world adventures to make sure it’s ready for yours. Our patent-pending Fold-Flat™ design, Clearburn™ smokeless system, and QuickDraw™ leg setup have been tested and refined—so now we’re ready to go to production. Our manufacturing partner is lined up, our materials are secured, and our backpack-ready design is locked in. That said, like with any outdoor product launch, scaling production comes with potential challenges—supply chain delays, shipping slowdowns, or quality control hiccups. But we’ve built a rock-solid plan, and we’re committed to keeping you in the loop every step of the way. We’ll inspect every batch, double-check every shipment, and make sure nothing leaves the line unless we’d be proud to use it ourselves. Our goal is to start delivering PACKFIRE to backers in early Fall 2025. If anything changes, you’ll be the first to know.
