Story

We’re Hervè & Giacomo, founders of AGO and creators of GRANIO, a portal into an uncharted universe of sonic possibilities, designed with a sci-fi aesthetic at its core. Navigate effortlessly with a smooth, ultra responsive joystick, capture and manipulate sounds in real time with the built-in microphone, and unleash your creativity with powerful instruments, versatile sequencers, and onboard speaker. Crafted by producers, musicians, and engineers, GRANIO is for everyone, and much more than just a sampler. It’s a journey that blends intuitiveness, fun, and innovation. We want everyone who uses GRANIO to feel like a kid, with the capabilities of a professional-grade device; because, in the end, rediscovering the child within is the true key to unlocking creativity.

Our mission is to design intuitive musical instruments that empower creators to express themselves freely, anywhere, and without compromise. Every detail in our products is a result of careful engineering and thoughtful design, combining performance, and a user-friendly workflow. Whether you're a seasoned professional or exploring music for the first time, we invite you to join us in building a future where technology serves expression and creativity flows naturally.

TRK mode:
This function manages tracks, instruments, samples, and everything track-related.
Here you can unleash your creativity: mangling, chopping, automating, and much more with your favorite samples. We designed it to be much more than just a sampler

SEQ mode:
This function handles the sequencer, patterns, chains, and snapshots.
It's the roaring engine that powers the mothership, allowing you to harness the full potential of randomization, arpeggios, matrix, and precise piano-roll editing

OTHER mode:
This function acts as a control panel for monitoring and managing the master, speaker, microphone, and recording samples. It also allows you to edit samples and configure settings to suit your workflow

MOD mode:
This function transforms your device into a dynamic playground of expression.
There, you can bend sounds and reshape your performance — all without missing a beat. It’s the bridge between your intent and execution

HOW WE GOT THIS POINT?
Early in 2022, we were elbow-deep in our studio lab, surrounded by solder fumes and half-wired prototypes, when we asked ourselves: 'How can everyone feel the magic of those childhood moments again?' That question sparked our journey to build an audio device that brings those memories back to life. Some iterations later, we found the perfect balance of fun, innovation, and musical exploration, crafted to invite both beginners and seasoned musicians to discover a whole new way to play with sound.

Risks and challenges

We’re developing and producing GRANIO by leveraging the engineering expertise and manufacturing processes we've refined through the creation of SUMMINGFACILITY and DYNAMICENGINE in Eurorack format. Over the years, AGO has built strong, reliable relationships with trusted manufacturing partners. We’re launching GRANIO on Kickstarter because your support is essential to begin production. The upfront costs, such as mold creation and initial manufacturing, are significant. The design phase is nearly complete: we’ve developed and tested circuit boards and mockup units, and firmware development is progressing smoothly. The sounds featured in all GRANIO videos were made using working prototypes. We’re currently fine-tuning the final PCB design, and plan to begin manufacturing once we secure FCC, CE, and other relevant certifications. Throughout GRANIO’s development, we’ve faced and overcome many challenges, applying valuable lessons from our previous products to streamline this journey. That said, innovation often brings unforeseen obstacles. By involving you in this process from the very beginning, we want to share not just the progress, but also the highs and lows that come with building something truly new. We believe that when a project is approached with passion and care, every step, no matter how challenging, is part of a “right and beautiful” path. Some aspects of the design may change, but we’ll keep you informed about every refinement along the way. The more support we receive, the more powerful GRANIO will become, with the possibility to implement additional creative features beyond the essentials.
