Story

RISC-V: The Open Standard Redefining Computing

RISC-V, the open standard instruction set architecture (ISA), enables unprecedented innovation across computing domains—from edge AI to hyperscale data centers. Driven by RISC-V International, the non-profit foundation with 4,500+ global members, this ecosystem accelerates open specification development through technical collaboration across 70 countries. Industry analysts project that RISC-V will capture 30% of core markets by 2028, powering advancements in automotive, HPC, and industrial automation.

This year marks the 15th anniversary of RISC-V, as well as the third anniversary of the VisionFive 2 launch. Motivated by the support of our first campaign backers, we have made significant strides in software porting, development, and optimization.  

As of today, RVspace has achieved over 150,000 monthly visits and a total of more than 4 million visits. VisionFive 2 has fostered a wealth of tutorials and code resources, covering applications in education, AIoT, smart home, and IIoT.

VF2 Family Expands: Meet VisionFive 2 Lite

As RISC-V marks its 15-year milestone, we're excited to introduce the VisionFive 2 family products - VisionFive 2 Lite, your simplest entry point to RISC-V development, starting at $19.9.

Compared to the previous-generation, the VisionFive 2 Lite adds integrated eMMC storage and Wi-Fi, offering a more budget-friendly solution for commercial scaling. 

VisionFive 2 Lite is a budget-friendly and feature-rich RISC-V single board computer tailored for education, AIoT, smart home, and IIoT applications. Powered by StarFive’s JH-7110S quad-core processor, it features robust image and video processing capabilities along with versatile expansion interfaces including PCIe, HDMI, USB 3.0, and Gigabit Ethernet. With full support for mainstream Linux distributions and open-source toolchains, it enables fast development and smooth integration. Whether for learning, prototyping, or embedded deployment, VisionFive 2 Lite delivers an exceptional balance of performance and affordability.

How Can We Use VisionFive 2 Lite?

Education & Development

  • Lightweight open-source RISC-V educational platform supporting toolchains like Python/OpenCV, enabling efficient development of AI vision algorithms and robotic control applications. 

IIoT / Industrial Control Systems

  • Edge Connectivity: Features Gigabit Ethernet, Wi-Fi 6, and PCIe-based M.2 expansion for industrial sensor/device integration.
  • Control Systems: Supports OpenPLC with IgH EtherCAT Master Stack.

Smart Home & Security Solutions

  • Peripheral Control: Directly drives home automation devices (sensors/access systems) via GPIO/USB interfaces  
  • AIoT: Implements person detection using USB-connected Hailo-8 AI accelerator modules, with alerts pushed to Home Assistant via RTSP streams

Lightweight Home Server

  • Native support for OpenWrt (router OS)/CasaOS (container management)/Buildroot (embedded Linux) frameworks.
  • Deploys software-defined routing and SMB/NFS-based NAS services with 24/7 low-power operation.

Software Ecosystem

About Us

Founded in 2018, StarFive is a Chinese local high-tech company with independent intellectual properties. As the leader of the RISC-V software and hardware ecosystem in China, StarFive provides world-leading products and solutions on RISC-V covering CPU IP, SoC, development boards, and etc. Since its establishment, StarFive has delivered a series of RISC-V products, covering smart terminals (laptops, tablets, AI voice devices, etc.); industrial automation (PLC controllers, security gateways, EtherCAT masters, multi-axis servo drives, etc.); energy management (smart gas meters, energy routers, power IoT terminals, etc.); and machine vision (facial recognition, edge image detection, general object recognition, QR code detection and decoding, etc.).
