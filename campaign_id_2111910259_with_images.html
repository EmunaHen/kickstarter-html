Story

This started with an Idea for a simple way of accessing the side gate from either side, As a general contractor having done many repairs for customers One prominent thing ive heard was "hold on i need to unlock it from the back". So the customer would travel around to the back to unlock the pad lock.

This simple design is basically a lock box that holds a double cylinder deadbolt incorporated with a sliding latch that can be accessed from both sides of a hinged gate! 

 This allows the user to install a double cylinder deadbolt to the hinged gate.  

                    Store purchased Deadbolt locks are not typically used on hinged gates in that the deadbolt throw is typically 7/8"-1"; this distance isn't sufficient to pass a gates frames and also you would need a bore hole to install one

  • But with the gate lock-box latch's has a three inch throw and a simple design, a lock-box incorporated with a latch 
  • The option of installing and choosing your own better quality double cylinder deadbolt on the north American market
  • Another point is that other brands proprietary locking mechanisms which can be a hassle If broken if they are incorporated with the activation mechanical aspects of the gate lock or latch. forcing the user to order parts through the manufacturer or replace the whole latch itself. This gate lockbox latch has only one moving part.
  •  The handles both act as the lever and gate handles, This model sliding bolt latch extends 3 inches, extending well enough through the hinged gate frame into the receiver end. In the future i may increase the throw on a different model.  

The best point of this would be security, its robust weighing over 3 pounds of steel, the sliding bolt latch is made from 3/8" x 1-1/4" rolled steel bar. The steel casing of the gate lockbox latch thickness can withstand impacts from a hammer. And the gate lockbox latch system would be secured by 2 bolts Through the outer gate; limiting access while the gate is in a closed locked position.

Its has a clean Modern look, simple and intuitive to use
