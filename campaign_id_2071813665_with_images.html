Constraints of the Old World and the Pain of the Modern Traveler
Imagine a world, until recently, where travel was a journey fraught with complexity, exhaustive searches, and hidden costs. A world where every step—from booking a flight to finding a place to eat—required navigating dozens of disparate apps and platforms, leading to choice overload and constant anxiety about securing the “best deal”. The traveler was exploited, bound by the limitations of traditional apps, lacking the ultimate freedom in their global and personal journeys.

Excess Baggage Marketplace: Innovative Solution and New Revenue

Problem: Exorbitant excess baggage fees are a traveler’s nightmare.

Revolutionary Solution: Anqa360 introduces a unique in-app marketplace for buying and selling excess baggage weight among users. If you have extra weight (e.g., 8 kg from Hamburg to Istanbul), you can offer it for a fee.

Seamless Process: Another traveler needing extra space pays through the app. Upon luggage arrival and delivery, the recipient confirms receipt in the app, Anqa360 notifies the original traveler, and the payment is automatically transferred. This professional feature creates an excellent revenue stream for users and provides a practical solution to a global problem.

Long-lasting design

At Anqa360, we believe that smart travel must also be sustainable. Our commitment to the environment isn't just a feature; it's a core part of who we are. We strive to minimize our ecological footprint in every aspect of our operations, contributing to a cleaner planet and a better future.

Reusability and recyclability

For a digital application like Anqa360, durable design means developing a robust and highly efficient software architecture. We focus on writing clean, optimized code that consumes minimal energy resources on servers and user devices. This ensures the app operates smoothly and efficiently long-term, reducing strain on digital infrastructure and contributing to a sustainable computing environment.

Sustainable materials

We contribute to the principle of reuse by offering digital solutions that significantly reduce the need for physical resources. Anqa360 encourages moving away from printed materials and paper-based processes in the travel sector, thereby minimizing waste. Our efficient software updates and effective design also help extend the lifespan of users' smart devices by reducing resource demands, thus contributing to a decrease in electronic waste.

Environmentally friendly factories

As a purely digital product, Anqa360 does not utilize physical materials at its core. However, our commitment extends to all aspects of our operations. We meticulously choose suppliers and partners who adhere to responsible environmental practices and share our sustainability values. We consistently aim to minimize the use of non-sustainable materials in any essential office supplies or printed marketing materials, always preferring recycled or recyclable options.

Sustainable Distribution

Our digital operations rely entirely on cloud computing services. Therefore, we are committed to collaborating with leading global cloud providers (such as AWS, Google Cloud, Microsoft Azure) who have clear, stated policies regarding the use of renewable energy. We prioritize facilities that aim to reduce carbon emissions and operate highly efficient, environmentally friendly data centers, ensuring our carbon footprint remains as minimal as possible.

Something else

This completely eliminates the need for traditional transportation methods that contribute to carbon emissions and environmental pollutants. The digital nature of our product significantly reduces our overall environmental footprint compared to physical products requiring extensive shipping logistics. Something Else Beyond our core product and operations, Anqa360 strives to promote sustainability through additional initiatives. We encourage a remote work culture within our team, reducing commutes and associated carbon emissions.
