Story

scripted. | Welcome to the future of journaling

Have you ever wanted to journal but struggle to find time in the day?

scripted. is the voice journaling app for people who want to organise their thoughts, keep track of their mental well-being and boost productivity. Each journal entry will then be paired with valuable insights that are displayed in a simple, digestible way, allowing you to truly understand your thoughts.

We’ve specifically created scripted. for those that are new to journaling or have been unsuccessful in integrating journaling into their routine. With scripted. all you need is your voice. We’ll do the rest including daily reminders to journal, prompting you to keep up that streak 🔥

Simply start a journal entry and begin speaking for the app to begin transcribing your voice into text. Once you've finished simply end your recording and scripted. will quickly convert your speech to text. Once complete, our technology will begin to analyse your entry for mood, sentiment, and tone, giving you deep analysis of your entry. A summary will also be generated to accompany each analysis. Furthermore, your original recording and transcript will be available at any time. 

As you journal, monthly highlights will also give you valuable insights at a macro level. Bespoke goals will also be created for you to take into the next month, tailored to meet your needs. 

scripted. is your new tool to unleash your potential, work through emotional struggles, record thoughts in the moment as they happen, as well as much more… 

To summarise, your scripted. subscription will enable you to:

  • 💭 journal using only your voice
  • 📈 easily build your journaling habit
  • 🧠 organise your thoughts
  • 🔍 keep track of your mental well-being
  • 🚀 boost productivity

🌎 Why I Created scripted.?

Hi there! My name is Zack, I’m 26, and I founded scripted. to create the ultimate journaling tool, powered by your voice. 

The idea for scripted. was curated using my personal struggles with mental health. Where my inability to address trauma and speak about emotions negativity affected my life experience in my early twenties. 

When I found journaling, I discovered a new best friend. Someone who I could reveal all my inner thoughts to without feeling judged. No matter how unrefined they were, pure elation or sorrow, dreams for a better future, regrets I pushed down for years. 

Experimenting with voice notes and summarising entries to return to later, led me to develop the scripted. concept into what it is today. scripted. is in my opinion the purest form of journaling, using the voice to generate entries and insights automatically. I found speaking my thoughts even more freeing than hand writing notes, and gained confidence which I took forward when speaking in public at work. 

This is something that I want to share with you as a refined product, and I truly believe that scripted. can be used as a powerful tool for all those that are willing to give it a try!

🏎️ Journal Automatically As You Talk

Journal faster using only your voice and your journal will automatically convert your entry to text in a matter of seconds. Remove the excuse of not having time to journal, and get insights delivered straight to your app.

As soon as your finish your journaling entry our powerful technology will get to work:

  • Converting your speech into text.
  • Summarising your transcript making it easier to digest when you come back to it.
  • Analysing your entry for mood, tone, and sentiment. 

🃏New Prompt Card Every Day

For those days when you’re not sure what to journal about, use our prompt card of the day feature to give you inspiration. Your prompt card will be generated and you can start journaling immediately.

This can be really helpful for those that are new to journaling. This feature will provide hard hitting questions to make you think. Providing an amazing introduction into the journaling process.

📈 Insights Generated For Each Journal

Whatever your reason to journal, get a summary of each entry to dive deeper into what you are feeling. In addition get subjective analysis of mood, tone, and sentiment to dive deep into your thoughts.

Getting insights from your journal entries will help you understand how you were feeling in that moment. You will then be able to deep dive into your journals contents to decide what next steps you need to take to:

  • Overcome a challenge in life
  • Reach out for further help
  • Set goals from the future
  • Understand what your needs are

🗓️ Summary For Each Month

scripted. generates insights for each month allowing you to track the sentiment of your journals across time, give a mood breakdown, and recommend goals to boost productivity.

This will really help you focus your thoughts on what you need, and how you what you can do to springboard yourself into a better position next month. Each individual is unique so these insights will be bespoke to each scripted. user, and everyones experience will be unique!

📔 All Your Entries Securely Stored In One Place

All your journal recordings and transcriptions are encrypted and stored on the cloud. So you can access them at anytime to re-live a journal entry again!

It is powerful to return to a point in time where we were struggling when we are now in a better place. These moments are great fuel to continue moving forward, the human spirit is more powerful than we sometimes give it credit for!
