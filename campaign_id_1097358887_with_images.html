Story

Javapresso combines grinding, brewing, and steaming in one elegant machine. Its soft retro tones add warmth and personality—bringing style and joy to your kitchen. Best of all, it’s beginner-friendly. From your first cup, you can brew with ease and confidence.
Javapresso grinds your beans fresh for every brew—preserving the delicate aromatics that pre-ground coffee often loses. Unlike pre-packed grounds that go stale quickly and develop bitter or sour notes, freshly ground coffee delivers a natural aroma with every cup.
Whether you need bold espresso or smooth Americano, flavor starts with the perfect grind. Javapresso’s 30-level adjustable grinder lets you fine-tune your coffee grounds to match your taste and bean variety. And with a spacious 250g bean hopper, it gives you days of brewing without constant refills.
Hot water flows evenly through the finely ground coffee bed, ensuring every layer is fully soaked and extracted. The result? A rich espresso with deep flavor and a smooth crema — bringing out the best of your beans, every time.
Javapresso’s steam wand delivers consistent, even steam pressure to create smooth microfoam. It’s ideal for making rich, creamy lattes with a perfectly balanced blend of espresso and milk.
Need hot water for your Americano or tea? Javapresso delivers it instantly at the turn of a knob—saving you time and keeping your workflow smooth.
Javapresso’s integrated smart scale measures both weight and extraction time in real-time—helping you hit the ideal espresso parameters every time. Know exactly how many grams are in your cup and how long the shot takes, so you can avoid bitterness from over-extraction or sourness from under-extraction. Get consistent, barista-level results, even at home.
With a massive 3.5L removable water tank, Javapresso lets you make cup after cup without constant refills—perfect for busy mornings or entertaining guests.
Making a great cup is easier than you think. Clear, intuitive buttons for grinding, single/double shot extraction, steam, and hot water—everything is designed for effortless operation, even if it’s your first time.
