Story

Background

The Digital Forensics Discord Group features technical professionals from around the world who on a daily basis work hard in the background of many agencies and businesses to protect and serve local communities. 

The discord group features almost 9000 professionals. Show your appreciation and support with this Challenge Coin. 

Discord have been contacted to use their logo as per the branding page information https://discord.com/branding 

Description

This beautiful, 1.75-inch coin is made from high-quality nickel and features a stunning dual-side design. One side boasts a binary code pattern, paying homage to the language that drives the digital world. The other side showcases a unique and exclusive design, making each coin a true collector's item. Plus, each coin is sequentially numbered, ensuring that your Digital Forensics Discord Challenge Coin is one-of-a-kind.

The coin its self-features at it center the iconic Discord logo with binary around the other edge spelling "Digital Forensics" and the inner intricate circuitry representing the complex ways in which investigation can go. 

On its back a decision tree which depicts the thinking and rapid direction a case can go as you delve into the Gigabyte of data ... Around its edge the Latin words "Unis De Multis" which means "one of many" and is a symbol of the thousands or people working hard daily to provide rapid data results in the Digital Forensics industry. At the bottom the words "It Depends" as it's the most common answer to many questions asked due to the complexity of many factors or the way things work in the technological world of Digital Forensics. 

The Significance

This coin is more than just a trinket - it's a symbol of your expertise and dedication to the digital forensics field. Whether you're a seasoned practitioner or just starting out, the "It Depends " DFIR Discord Challenge coin is a reminder of the hard work and attention to detail that separates you from the rest.

The Plan

Providing we can achieve the Kickstarter goals then we will continue to have all of the 1st edition coins minted in one go.

All the coins will be professionally produced by a UK manufacturer who has the design and ready to go. The manufacturer is highly experienced in the industry of manufacturing these challenge coins. The minting takes around 6-8 weeks to complete depending on the order size.

Upon receipt of the coins, they will all be individually checked and securely packaged in to protective coin sleeves. Estimated 1 week.

Delivery labels and packaging will be produced and coins packaged prepared and ready to go. Estimated up to 2 weeks. 

Delivery will commence where all items will be tracked from source to destination where possible. Any countries not supporting this then we cannot guarantee delivery in the event of any postal miss handling.

Tax/Duty shouldnt apply as the item is low value. If it does occour upon recipe it is the responibility of the buyer to fullfil this payment.

Risks and challenges

The foreseeable risks are very minimal and includes: The project not getting fully funded which would prevent the minting of the coin. Delivery to countries which do not provide tracking facilities as we can not guarantee a replacement in the event of a postal handling misshap. Coins which can be tracked but go missing in the Postal system will be able to obtain a replacement or refund. The design of the coin is complete but colours may differ slightly when printed. We are doing every we can possible to keep it to the depicted design.
