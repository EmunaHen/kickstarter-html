Risks and challenges

1. Technical Roadblocks While the core platform is already live in beta, building a stable and scalable multi-profile social app across desktop and mobile is complex. Unexpected bugs or delays in feature rollout are always possible. How we address it: Our in-house dev team, led by our CTO, is already battle-tested. We're using proven technologies 2. User Adoption & Community Growth A new social platform needs users and creators to thrive. There's always a risk that early adoption might take longer than expected. How we address it: We're launching with a strong influencer and creator-first strategy, including migration tools and early-mover perks to boost engagement. 3. Privacy and Security Expectations We promise zero ads, no tracking, and true privacy. Delivering on that promise requires constant vigilance and infrastructure investment. How we address it: We've baked privacy into the core of Jusap's architecture. Our compliance is GDPR-ready, and we use encrypted storage and TLS 1.2/1.3 by default. 4. Feature Creep It’s tempting to keep adding features based on user requests, but that can delay stable releases. How we address it: We’re sticking to a clear roadmap and engaging our backer community to vote on priorities through our Kickstarter-only feedback channels.
