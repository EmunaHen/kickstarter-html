Story

Take care of your healthy posture with a 3D stand that easily adapts to your parameters!

Introducing the unique EasyLap 3D model - an adaptable stand designed to fit absolutely any laptop model! Versatile, durable, easy to assemble and easy to transport, it is ideal for creating a comfortable workspace anywhere. 

Inspired by the best solutions in laptop stand, EasyLap 3D combines quality and reliable support-free printing process, taking care of both your comfort and the environment.

Why it will make your life easier?

Whether you work or study at home or anywhere else, the EasyLap 3D stand gives you the optimal environment for comfort and productivity in any situation.

  • Adjust the angle and height to work or play in the best position possible (avoid hand and wrist soreness)
  • No wobbling or creaking - Work/study in silence and comfort
  • Everywhere with you! The lightweight design will allow you to assemble it anywhere you want.
  • Better ventilation - Most of the laptop ventilation in the market come from the bottom part of your machine. EasyLap lets your computer breath!

Universality 

  • Suitable for all types of notebooks and laptops with the 2 different size of connectors and a no connector option aswell.
  • Avoids case overheating thanks to airflow design

Why 3D printing is your best option?

  • Avoid overpaying for unnecessary details - Your can have your stand for less than 5 bucks of material.
  • Choose your personal and unique design (from color to texture and material), create your own “limited edition” collection!
  • 3D printing materials will ensure the longevity of the product. (PETG or ABS recommended for gaming)
  • Support the environment by avoiding mass production and emissions.

Why is this stand right for you?

It is your personal assistant in comfortable workspace, design uniqueness, easy assembly with adjustment to your parameters, durability of your laptop thanks to the space to avoid overheating. Reduce your body discomfort when working or studying! 

Try once - and you won’t be able to go on without it!

Licenses

You have 3 options to get a license with this project.

Printer License - You can print and sell as many EasyLap 3D as you want on the medium you prefer.

Master License - You're allowed to print and sell, modify and even sell the digital STL everywhere except on Kickstarter. You're basically a co-owner of the files (but you don't have the right to sell anything else than a personal license if you want to sell the digital files aswell.)

Environmental commitments

Visit our Environmental Resources Center to learn how Kickstarter encourages sustainable practices.

Long-lasting design

I designed the EasyLap 3D with durability and reliability in mind, optimizing the design for longevity and frequent use without the need for replacement, reducing waste and eliminating unnecessary reprints.

Reusability and recyclability

To minimize waste, I will provide recommendations for recycling or reusing any failed prints or excess plastic. The design of the stand is optimized for printing with minimal supports, reducing material waste and making the process more environmentally friendly.

Sustainable materials

For a combination of strength and sustainability, I recommend using materials like ASA, known for its high strength and UV resistance and durability, making it ideal for both indoors and outdoors. PETG is also a great option that is recyclable, easy to print and has good mechanical properties like flexibility and strength.

Environmentally friendly factories

Since the EasyLap 3D is printed in the sight of an STL file, users can print it locally without the need for mass production and long shipping times. I recommend using energy efficient 3D printers or personal printers, using eco-friendly or recycled materials to reduce your environmental impact.

Sustainable Distribution

By providing designs digitally, we will significantly reduce the carbon footprint associated with mass production and logistics. In addition, I'll also share tips on how to optimize your print setup to help reduce plastic consumption and power consumption.

Something else

Together, we can make the EasyLap 3D not only a convenient accessory, but also a step towards a more sustainable future.
