WHAT IS DOUBLEDECK® SNOWBOARDS ABOUT?

DOUBLEDECK® Snowboards presents a completely new and patented snowboard technology – a construction so unique, it fundamentally changes how a snowboard reacts, carves, and feels under your feet. Made in Austria at one of the best snowboard factories in the world, with experience in snowboard manufacturing since the 1980s and using the best materials available in the industry.

At the heart of it lies our patented DOUBLEDECK® technology:

A two-layer design, connecting the motherboard (the base) with a floating arch construction on top – the so-called Bridging Bow.

It’s a game-changer.

Here’s what makes DOUBLEDECK® different:

➤ More forgiveness, less frustration

Our Bridging Bow absorbs shocks and smoothes out errors. Less catching edges, more flow – especially for beginners.

➤ Self-initiating turns

Thanks to our Convex Pressure System (CPS), the board almost turns by itself. It responds intuitively to your body, not against it.

➤ More pop with less effort

The Superslide Rail System (SRS) stores and releases energy in every edge change. You get more rebound with every turn – for playful, surf-like riding.

➤ Gentle on your joints

Our torsion-flex construction (ICS) makes carving smoother and softer – so you can ride longer, with less impact on knees and hips.

After 6 years of development, hundreds of prototypes and real-world testing in all conditions, we can say with confidence:

This is not just an evolution. It’s a new category.

Risks and challenges

We’ve spent the past six years developing, testing, and refining DOUBLEDECK®. However, launching internationally via Kickstarter brings some challenges we want to share openly: 1. Scaled Production Logistics While our boards are produced in Austria at a highly experienced snowboard facility, moving from small-batch to larger international production may require adjustments in timelines. We’ve planned buffers, but delays in material supply or shipping can never be fully ruled out. 2. Global Fulfillment We ship worldwide – and some destinations have longer delivery routes, higher customs complexity, or variable costs. We’re working with experienced logistics partners to keep things smooth, but delays or customs-related fees may occur in some countries. 3. Demand Forecasting Depending on campaign volume, certain sizes or bundles may temporarily sell out or require staggered delivery waves. We will communicate this transparently during the campaign. 4. Communication & Community Expectations We aim to keep every backer in the loop – but with global scale, that also means higher support volume. We’re prepared with dedicated staff to keep you updated at every step. We believe in transparency and accountability. If anything unexpected arises, you’ll hear it from us first – with clear updates and solutions.

Environmental commitments

Visit our Environmental Resources Center to learn how Kickstarter encourages sustainable practices.

Long-lasting design

We have developed a sustainable way to extend the lifespan of our boards. Through our refurbish program called “Let to Rent,” we take back rental boards from retailers at the end of each season. Each board is then refurbished and put back into rental use the following season.

Reusability and recyclability

Thanks to the special materials used in our manufacturing process, we can recycle up to 75% of each DOUBLEDECK board. These recycled materials are reused to produce new boards. This eco-friendly approach helps reduce waste and keeps materials in circulation – because there is no Planet B.

Sustainable materials

Our boards are made with materials that allow up to 75% recyclability at end of life. These materials can be recovered and reused in the production of new DOUBLEDECK boards. This circular approach reduces environmental impact and extends the lifecycle of every board.
