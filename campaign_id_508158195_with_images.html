Story

ZEN 65 only takes up 65% the space of a 108-key keyboard. The 65% layout keeps all essential keys. Enjoy more desk space, less hand movement, and a cleaner setup. Compact enough to carry, efficient enough for any task—free your mind, enhance your flow.
ZEN 65’s brass cavity delivers a rich, fulfilling typing feel. Ultra-low 19mm front height boosts comfort, while gasket mounts and flex cuts soften every stroke. The switches make each press crisp and balanced.
We've placed a 236-gram brass weight between the PCB and battery compartment. Here's why this single piece of brass significantly enhances your typing experience:
  1. High Density, Brass absorbs and smooths vibrations, creating a stable, satisfying bottom-out.
  2. Lower Frequency, Richer Sound. Brass resonates at a lower frequency, offering a deep, premium acoustic.
  3. Controlled Resonance, Consistent Response. Brass evenly distributes vibrations, ensuring every keystroke feels smooth, uniform, and precise.
The 19mm ultra-low front height of the ZEN 65 reduces wrist strain, ensuring a natural, comfortable, and precise typing experience.
  • Reduced Wrist Strain - Keeps wrists straight, reducing fatigue.
  • Natural Typing Posture - Supports relaxed, neutral wrist angle.
  • Increased Accuracy & Comfort - Less movement, smoother typing.
Enjoy a satisfying, cushioned feel right out of the box. Gasket structure and strategic flex cuts provide softness, stability, and refined comfort—no tuning required.
Stock switches, elevated. Premium quality isn't limited to customization—these factory-crafted switches deliver high-end smoothness and consistency right from the start.
