Story

Driflyco Zero is an all-in-one intelligent audio-visual device designed to simplify modern communication and content capture. With 4 non-fisheye cameras, it delivers a 360° seamlessly panoramic view without distortion. Built-in omnidirectional microphone, speaker, and AI-powered voice & face tracking ensure clear audio and focused visuals. 

Its modular magnetic battery offers extended use and doubles as a power bank. With 2 display modes, video recording, Bluetooth speaker and home monitoring functionality, it's perfect for meetings, livestreaming, and remote monitoring. Compact yet powerful, it’s the smart solution for effortless, all-around visual communication.


No more buying multiple tools—this is your complete solution.
Why purchase separate microphones, webcams, or speakers when one device does it all? With integrated audio-visual components, the unit replaces a shelf’s worth of gear in one compact form. Our all-in-one unit integrates panoramic cameras, omnidirectional microphones, and powerful speakers.Ideal for professionals, content creators, and anyone tired of clutter.

Experience meetings like never before with four non-fisheye cameras seamlessly stitched by an onboard chip.
Get a panoramic 360° view without the typical fisheye distortion—what you see is what a natural eye would see. Whether it's a team collaboration or a group event, every detail is captured clearly and naturally. No blind spots. No warping. Just full, immersive clarity. Perfect for professionals who need reliable visual coverage from all directions.

Let the spotlight follow the conversation—automatically.
Equipped with advanced voice-shape AI algorithms, the system detects and tracks the active speaker in real time, ensuring they're always in focus. No manual controls needed. It’s like having an intelligent director guiding your recording or conference—giving your communication a professional edge.

In dynamic group settings, the camera can be set to lock onto one person—ensuring the focus stays where it matters most. No distracting shifts or unwanted auto-tracking. Perfect for interviews, lectures, or when a consistent frame is key.

Switch between full panoramic views and focused close-ups—instantly.
Choose the best way to view your scene: full 360° with dynamic speaker focus or a 180° split-screen format for simultaneous panorama and detail. Great for meetings, livestream, or classroom setups, you get full control over how your audience sees the moment.

Take it anywhere—without the bulk.
Weighing just 230g (plus 220g per battery), Driflyco Zero is travel-ready and easy to carry. Slip it into your bag and head out—it’s ideal for hybrid work setups, field content capture, or on-the-go security.

Stay powered up anywhere with swappable, stackable battery packs.
Forget messy cables and power interruptions. Driflyco Zero features a modular 10,000mAh magnetic battery that snaps securely into place—no tools or wires needed. Each module delivers up to 6 hours of continuous use, and you can swap or stack more in seconds. Power is always within reach.

Need more power? Just stack another battery. Driflyco Zero supports up to five magnetic modules, giving you up to 30 hours of continuous runtime. Whether you're streaming all day, recording events, or traveling light, you’ll never run out of energy.

More than a power source—it’s your backup power bank.
Each detachable battery module doesn’t just run the device—it also doubles as a high-speed charging power bank with Type-C output, digital display, and built-in cable. Perfect for powering phones, tablets, or other gear on the go. 

When you are not using it to charge a device, the cable can also become a handle, making it easy for you to take it anywhere.

Turn it into a Bluetooth speaker at any time.
Connect your phone or laptop via Bluetooth to enjoy high-quality music or conference calls. With physical volume buttons and impressive sound clarity, it’s ready to be your entertainment hub or meeting room companion—wherever you take it.

Choose how you want to store your memories.
Supports TF cards (16–128GB), app-based mobile recording, and even cloud backups. Whether you're capturing moments locally or prefer secure cloud access, the choice is yours. Never lose a key moment again—everything’s backed up your way.

Safeguard your lens—and your privacy—with a sliding shield.
The built-in protective cover not only keeps dust and scratches away but also offers peace of mind when the device is not in use. Whether you’re in a private meeting or taking a break, simply slide it over to protect the camera and ensure your space stays yours.

One device, countless uses.
From professional to personal, Driflyco Zero fits every setting. Use it for remote meetings, live lectures, or as a home monitoring hub. Need music? Switch to Bluetooth speaker mode in seconds. Its all-in-one design means fewer devices—and more freedom.

Designed for flexibility, Driflyco Zero works smoothly with a wide range of video conferencing platforms. Whether for business meetings or online presentations, it adapts to your workflow with ease—so you can focus on what matters, not what device you're using.

We’re not selling gadgets. We’re delivering confidence: the freedom to create, secure, and connect without second-guessing your tools. Because when vision flows effortlessly, potential follows. For industries where seeing clearly is merely the first step, Driflyco builds the lens that focuses possibility.
