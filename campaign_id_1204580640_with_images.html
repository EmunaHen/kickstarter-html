Story

Hi, I’m Don from Los Angeles, CA.
I’m a recording artist, producer, and electrical engineer, and the founder of Doheny Audio—a boutique effects company born from my deep love of sound and circuit design.

A few years back, I made the leap to focus full-time on crafting custom effects for friends, session musicians, and studios across L.A. What began as a passion project quickly evolved into a trusted name among touring artists. I’ve had the privilege of designing treble boosters and other bespoke electronics for legends like Billy Gibbons, George Keer, Barry Johnson, Tyler Bryant, and many more.

Now, after years of hands-on experience, I’m excited to introduce four new pedals—each road-tested by the bands I’ve collaborated with. These aren’t just vintage replicas; they’re original circuits inspired by classic tones and built with a mix of military-grade transistors and carefully sourced NOS components to deliver rich harmonics and exceptional character.

A delay and reverb pedal with a built-in LFO and noise module that replicates the lush, modulated tones of vintage tape echo units.

A versatile overdrive with built-in reverb and delay. With the flick of a switch, you can shift from gritty classic tones to soaring '80s lead tones—perfect for cutting through in solos.

A tone-shaping box inspired by two iconic ‘60s amp EQ sections. Use it as a passive tone sculptor or flip it into buffered mode for maintaining tone over long cable runs.

'60s-style Treble Booster with a switchable frequency selector. The new version will be available in both through-hole and SMD formats, featuring both silicon and germanium transistors—all housed in an upgraded printed enclosure.

Every pedal is handcrafted with attention to tone, durability, and vibe—because I’m not just building gear, I’m building tools that inspire creativity on stage and in the studio.

I’d love your support in bringing these next-level tools to more players around the world. Let’s make some noise together.

Risks and challenges

Doheny Audio is nearly production-ready. Our prototypes have been fully developed and rigorously tested by professional touring musicians to ensure durability, tone quality, and reliability on stage and in the studio. The core circuits are finalized, and the pedals are performing exactly as intended. The only area still being refined is the packaging design, which is currently in progress to ensure every unit looks as good as it sounds. As with any electronics manufacturing, the main risk lies in potential PCB (printed circuit board) issues. However, we’ve minimized this significantly through extensive prototyping, stress testing, and working closely with trusted PCB fabricators. With today’s production standards and our experience in electronics, the chance of failure is extremely low. To be fully transparent, here’s how we’re addressing any potential challenges: - Multiple manufacturing partners: We’re working with more than one supplier to avoid delays in PCB production or component sourcing. - Pre-tested BOM (Bill of Materials): All components have been tested in various conditions to ensure consistency and tone. - Final-stage QA: Every pedal will be hand-tested before shipping to ensure performance and build quality. With your support, we’re confident that we can deliver on schedule and with the level of quality Doheny Audio has come to represent.
