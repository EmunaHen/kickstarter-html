Risks and challenges

Risk: Securing specialized components like the ESP32-C3 chip and full-color LEDs may face supply chain bottlenecks. Precision assembly of the triple touch sensors and LED matrix increases production complexity, risking inconsistent quality if process controls lapse. How we deal with it: Partner with tier-1 electronic suppliers for priority access to critical parts. Implement strict factory audits and module pre-testing (e.g., 24-hour burn-in for each unit) to ensure reliability. Diversify assembly lines to mitigate single-factory risks. Challenge: Prolonged max-brightness LED animations generate heat accumulation around ESP32-C3 chip area. Left unchecked, thermal stress may degrade touch sensor responsiveness and NOR Flash write cycles. How we deal with it: Optimize thermal management for sustained LED performance. Refine adaptive dimming strategy leveraging motion-sensing triggers to dynamically balance visibility and energy efficiency.
