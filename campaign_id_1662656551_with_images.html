What We’re Raising Funds To Do

We’re building BusyBee Errands, a platform that connects busy people with trusted local gig workers who can handle life’s everyday tasks-like grocery shopping, package returns, and more. With your support, we’ll create a seamless, user-friendly app suite (for customers, runners, and admins) that makes getting help with errands as easy as ordering a ride or a meal. Your backing will directly fund the development of our three apps, launch marketing, and the onboarding of our first wave of gig worker “Bees.”

Why We Care

We know how overwhelming modern life can be. Between work, family, and personal commitments, there just aren’t enough hours in the day. We’ve experienced firsthand the stress of endless to-do lists and the frustration of not being able to get help when we need it most. BusyBee Errands was born from a simple belief: everyone deserves a little help to make life easier-and local communities thrive when neighbors support each other.

Our mission is to empower people to reclaim their time, while providing flexible earning opportunities for gig workers in our community. We’re passionate about building a service that’s safe, reliable, and truly makes a difference in people’s daily lives.

How We Stand Out From The Competition

Many gig economy platforms face recurring problems that affect both customers and workers, including unreliable service, lack of trust, unpredictable pricing, poor customer support, and limited control over who performs your tasks. Customers often experience last-minute cancellations, inconsistent quality, and frustration with impersonal rating systems that don’t always reflect true performance or allow for meaningful feedback. Workers, on the other hand, can struggle with unfair ratings, lack of recourse for disputes, and unpredictable earnings.

BusyBee Errands is designed to resolve these issues in the following ways:

  • Building Trusted Relationships: Unlike other gig platforms, where users select from available taskers for each job, customers can reconnect with their favorite helpers for future tasks, allowing them to build a personal, reliable network for sensitive or recurring needs. BusyBee Errands allows customers to reconnect with their favorite Bees for future errands- if both parties are available. This feature helps users build a trusted network of helpers for more personal or recurring tasks- such as house cleaning- fostering long-term relationships and comfort.
  • Comprehensive Feedback and Control: Instead of relying solely on impersonal star ratings, users can write comprehensive reviews about their Bees and, if dissatisfied, can block a Bee to ensure they are never matched again. This gives customers more control over their experience and helps maintain a high standard of service.
  • Vetted and Trained Helpers: All Bees are thoroughly vetted and trained before joining, which raises the standard of reliability and professionalism compared to platforms where anyone can sign up with minimal screening.
  • User Empowerment: By focusing on repeat connections, detailed feedback, and user-driven preferences, BusyBee Errands is tailored for those who value reliability, safety, and the ability to build ongoing relationships with their service providers— something other platforms do not emphasize.

By addressing these common industry pain points, BusyBee Errands offers a safer, more reliable, and more user-centered alternative to existing gig platforms.

How We’ll Make It Happen

We have a clear plan:

  • Build the Platform: Partner with a leading app development team to create three robust apps-for customers, runners, and admins-with features like real-time tracking, secure payments, and easy communication.
  • Recruit & Train Runners: Onboard our first group of trusted gig workers, providing them with branded kits and training.
  • Launch Locally: Start in our home base of Farmers Branch, Texas, and surrounding areas, ensuring quality and reliability before expanding.
  • Grow the Community: Use targeted marketing and community outreach to attract both customers and runners, and continuously improve based on your feedback.

Your support will help us cover the costs of development, marketing, runner onboarding, and the essential tools needed to launch BusyBee Errands the right way.

Risks and challenges

Like any new tech startup, BusyBee Errands faces potential risks such as unexpected app development delays, challenges in recruiting reliable gig workers, and the need to build customer trust in a new service. There’s also the possibility of unforeseen costs or technical hurdles as we launch and scale. To overcome these challenges, we’re partnering with experienced developers, setting clear project milestones, and allocating a contingency budget for surprises. We’ll focus first on a local launch to ensure quality and smooth operations, gather feedback, and make improvements before expanding. Our commitment to transparency, responsive support, and continuous improvement will help us navigate obstacles and complete the project successfully.
