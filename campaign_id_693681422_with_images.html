About Unihertz

Unihertz team comprises passionate designers who believe that technological advancement can create unlimited paths to shape the future of mobile devices. From compact designs, rugged builds to dual-screen setups, our innovations explore new technological frontiers—transforming bold ideas into reality and providing users worldwide with more diverse choices.

Since 2017, we have united a global community of tech enthusiasts, creators, and niche seekers through Kickstarter.

Titan, the first QWERTY smartphone, came out in 2019. We're so grateful for the tremendous support from our amazing user communities and dedicated partners.

Now, we are back, and we will go one step further.

Titan 2 isn’t simply a retro tribute—it’s a new keyboard phone reimagined for the 5G era, sleeker and lighter than its predecessor, yet more powerful than ever.

Titan 2 integrates a fully redesigned physical QWERTY keyboard, delivering a more comfortable, reliable, and versatile tactile typing experience.

Here are the cool features you can experience with the new keyboard:

Titan 2 supports major frequencies and bands globally. This means it'll work with most of the network carriers, so you don’t have to worry about living off the grid.

Good news for AT&T users! we are in the process of acquiring AT&T’s approval. Titan 2 will be authorized to operate on AT&T’s and Cricket’s network!

The latest Android 15 OS improves your productivity while safeguarding your sensitive data, all while providing greater usability and new feature enhancements.

Titan 2 features a refined physical keyboard, a better chipset, high-speed memory and enhanced cameras, plus other key improvements.

Titan 2 features a 4.5-inch square primary display with a resolution of 1,440 × 1,440 pixels, alongside a secondary rear display for an even more imaginative dual-screen experience. Its flat-edge design adds a modern, sleek touch to the device.

Titan 2 comes with a 5050mAh battery with 33W fast charging. Whether you're a multitasker, traveler, or power user, it consistently delivers long-lasting performance to match your pace of life.

Titan 2 is equipped with a dual-lens module for capturing stunning landscapes and detailed close-ups. Its high-quality 50MP main camera and 8MP telephoto lens ensure you'll get the best possible image quality. The 32MP front camera snaps great selfies.

Titan 2 retains the classic red programmable buttons on the side for quick access to your most frequently used apps and tools.

Titan 2 supports dual nano-SIM cards for work-life balance and global roaming.

Titan 2 keeps you connected with advanced wireless capabilities. Enjoy Bluetooth 5.4, NFC, an infrared port, dual-band GPS, and more, all designed for connectivity on the go.

Titan 2 is in the process of obtaining Google Certification, FCC, CE, UKCA, JATE, and others, guaranteeing compliance with trusted, high-quality standards.

With features like the Google Pay, Bluetooth 5.4, WiFi display, fingerprint and facial recognition, USB OTG, FM radio and useful tool box, Titan 2 offers the utmost functional performance.

WE HIT THE STRETCH GOAL! Thank you so much for being part of the Unihertz community. Now every Titan 2 will ship with a designated TPU case—durable, sleek, and protective.

We’ve designed a leather holster for Titan 2 that lets you carry it on your waistband with gentle protection. Combining timeless style and modern aesthetics, it’s a chic and functional companion.
