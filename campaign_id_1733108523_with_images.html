Story

MAMAY

Hi! We are a team from Lviv, Ukraine, and we build buggies for the offroad use. We call them MAMAY after iconic Ukrainian folkloric cossack hero whose fundamental traits were desire for freedom and resistance to oppression. 

In fact, we build not only buggies - our team has experience in delivering more than 20 projects in automotive domain. 

These were busses, e-cars, light trucks, trailers, bodies for sport cars and many others.

At the beginning it was just a dream...

...but now, we develop our own independent project - we are building our own vehicle, and this are just the first steps our our jorney. 

This is our first buggy we built.

It looks similar to current model, but the differences are significant.

It`s easy to work for everybody at the plant, because of 3D design made before.

Also we can make fast changes.

At the plant we build Mamay in small series, but there are more possibilities..

MAMAY is a lightweight four-wheeled high-terrain vehicle, designed and manufactured in the first place for use in sport and entertainment. 

It can be used in agriculture and forestry, too.

It is a joy and fun to drive.

Now, why are our vehicles of any importance these days? 

The answer is simple - they are used by the Armed Forces of Ukraine to evacuate the wounded directly from the battlefield. 

Our vehicles save lives - and this is what matters the most! 

We work continuously on improving the design and manufacture different vehicle modifications depending on the needs.

Efficiency - frankly speaking, one person is enough to evacuate two wounded from the battlefield! And this is sinificant and impressive. 

Our utility interior is easy to clean and maintain.

Nothing extraordinary, simplicity from the day one.

Our vehicles are tested under extreme conditions and circumstances.

Design solutions, that bring you to success!

Let's make a history together!

Long-lasting design

The solid car body is made from rectangular tubes and fiberglass panels. It is durable, but also very easy to repair if necessary. Thanks to the large ground clearance (325 - 520 mm), long suspension travel (260 - 320 mm), protection of systems under the body bottom and the absence of front and rear overhangs, the car overcomes significant obstacles and can do more, than most of the serial offroad vehicles. The capacity is up to 5 people plus a trunk above the engine, an additional trunk on the roof is also possible. The car body provides protection from wind and rain, and an easy access due to the wide armhole. Seatings are made of fiberglass, they are adjustable in length. Installed external lighting allows you to move in the dark and in a convoy.

Reusability and recyclability

We give a second life chance to components such as the engines, gearboxes, wheel hubs and others - this class of cars is not used for long range of service. In this way, we minimize the impact on the environment.
