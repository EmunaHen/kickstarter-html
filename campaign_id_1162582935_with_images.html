Tournament Notifications

Imagine not having to go through social media or asking friends what tournament is coming soon.

Get notified through the location you put (doesn't need to be yours) and at the scheduled time you choose, in a daily basis!

Just create a profile in each game you want to get tournament notifications, then go to your profile tab and set the tournament notifications. If you don't want more games, you won't have to do anything else.

Filter the tournaments, look for the maps for the easy route to go to the event. Add them to your FightersTech's calendar if you want them to be more easily-accessible later on.

Set local reminders (like alarms) for the tournaments you put in your calendar.¬†

This works without internet, it's like an alarm, as said above. This way you won't forget any tournament again!

Check your calendar for added events.

From there, once you click in a day and in a calendar event, you can even access to the maps location if needed!
