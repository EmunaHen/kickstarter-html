365nm UV Light Features:
  • Higher Sensitivity: Enables precise detection of fluorescent reactions from injected adhesives, glues, and other substances.
  • Minimal Interference: Emits near-pure ultraviolet light, reducing interference from purple visible light during fluorescent observation.

Specialized 365nm UV light reveals invisible microstructures and fluorescent markers—ideal for counterfeit detection, mineral analysis, and biological pigment identification. This professional wavelength cuts through ambient light to expose naked-eye invisible details.

CPL (Circular Polarizing Lens) works remarkably for viewing reflective objects like porcelain or gemstones, making details more vivid and clearly visible.

The MS300X equipped with ring light, which has 7 light modes. Turn on the power and the LED lighting automatically light up, day and night can be observed!
