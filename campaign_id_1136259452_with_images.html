Long-lasting design

E700 is cooled using a clean, efficient wind-cooling system, eliminating the complexity, noise, and maintenance issues of water-cooling systems — and making it far more reliable over time. Inside, we use a 1000W-rated fiber laser core — but we’ve intentionally capped the system output at 700W. Why? Because running a laser at 100% of its rated power, day after day, shortens its lifespan and increases the risk of thermal failure. By operating at just 70% of its full capacity, EC700 leaves a 30% power buffer, effectively minimizing stress, allowing for safe power attenuation over time, and significantly improving long-term durability.
