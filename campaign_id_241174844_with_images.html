Story

Story:

Students have a lot going on and making sure you are prepared for the next test that’s just around the corner can be stressful and challenging for many reasons.


• Taking notes to use as test prep.
• Creating note cards that are hard to store after use.
• Having to come up with questionnaires to practice.

These are all things that many students would call tedious and not all that helpful in actually studying for a subject, but more of the steps needed to be able to study the project.

That’s why we are creating a Study Buddy, to tackle these issues head-on and create an app that lets you get all your test prep done with the simple snap of a camera.

How it works:

Let’s say you have a 20-page chapter that you need to review for a test tomorrow. Simply open up our app and, using the Scan Pages feature, you can take a picture of these pages, which will be magically converted into a questionnaire and vocab cards that can be used to study for your exam.

Once completed, you will be able to name your new study packet and have it saved into the Library section of the app. Here, you can organize your packets and add more information to any of them simply by taking more pictures.

To study your new material, simply click on the desired study packet you wish to use. From there, you can select either Quizzes or Vocabulary. This will lead you to short quiz-like mini-games that will help you improve your knowledge of the content.

Your Study Buddy:

Now, let’s face it, studying can get boring for many, mostly because it’s hard for students to feel engaged with a topic for a long period of time, especially when working alone. Luckily, with your new Study Buddy, you are no longer alone. This virtual pet will be there with you every step of the way, helping to motivate you in your academic endeavors.

How it works:


• Each user will be able to select a buddy they wish to work with.
• This buddy will hold the knowledge that you’ve learned while using the app, keeping track of studying streaks.
• Customization options will allow you to earn and spend currency to dress up your pet.

Now, every time you improve, so does your buddy, symbolizing your academic growth.

Who Is This For:


We created this app as college students, for students of any age group or academic level. We also believe it can be used to learn about topics you might find interesting.

What Makes Us Different:


Most educational apps you’ll find are focused on one subject, such as learning a new language. Study Buddy stands out because it allows users to take control over what they want to study, whether that be history, science, English, or philosophy.

Our virtual pet helps make users more engaged in the learning process by gamifying it. We hope this will encourage many users to take one more practice quiz or complete a few more vocab questions to help their pet grow, leading them to grow as well.

AI usage 

AI will play a crucial part in certain areas of our app such as to help interpret the user's scanned book images and engineer questions based of said images. However, it's important to emphasize that no AI will be utilized for the user experience; this includes all UI components and buddy character design will all be done by real artists.

Stretch Goals:

If we go beyond the $10,000 mark, we plan to bring in an artist to work on the project. Currently, the buddies are placeholders, and the team is adamantly against using any AI-generated art. If we receive more than we expect, we will use that extra funding to hire up-and-coming artists to help create unique buddy designs.

Full discloser this Kickstarter will be for creating the Demo of our app which plan to host on app stores. From Here we will be pitching the idea in hopes of receiving more funding leading to the creation of the Full fledge app.

Risks and challenges

Unlike some other Kickstarters, this app is already in active development. We truly believe in Study Buddy and plan to finish the demo no matter the outcome. The demo is 80% complete, with just a few bugs left to fix. So, why Kickstarter? While our work doesn’t cost any servers, releasing the app on mobile storefronts and using AI features does come with expenses. That’s why we’re using Kickstarter, to help cover these costs.
