Product Review

Screwdriver：

Engineered for precision: These micro bits deliver perfect-fit performance for eyeglasses, watches & electronics. Anti-slip grooves and wear-resistant coating ensure effortless tiny screw control.

Tired of stripping tiny screws in your phone or watch? Our S2 alloy steel bits are harder (HRC 58-62) and far more durable than standard chrome-vanadium steel, giving you superior grip and zero slippage. Whether you're a pro or a DIY enthusiast, these bits deliver ultimate precision—so you can tackle delicate electronics with confidence.

Craft knife：

CUTS THROUGH MANY MATERIALS — This exacto knife is able to cut through many lightweight and heavyweight materials. This includes all grades of paper, plastic, cloth, mat board, foam board, delicate fabrics, thin sheet metal. Can also act as a leather knife.

Equipped with 30 additional SK-5 high-carbon steel blades, our craft knife guarantees durability and sharpness. These blades are designed to withstand wear and corrosion, ensuring they remain sharp for longer periods. The Sharp tip allows for precise and accurate cuts, making it ideal for detailed and intricate work. 

Advanced package

  • 100-IN-1 PACKAGE

To make it easier for everyone to use, we’ve prepared a set of advanced tools. With the Premium Kit, you’ll not only get the original 30 screwdriver bits and 20 blades, but also 40 additional screwdriver bits and 10 extra blades—totaling a complete 100-in-1 set.

With this toolkit, you won’t have to worry about worn-out bits, ensuring longer-lasting performance while giving you even more options to work with.

The JK-8X’s innovative dual-flat chuck enables tool-free, 2-second switching between screwdriver and craft knife modes—delivering unmatched versatility for precision repairs, cutting tasks, and creative workflows.

The JK-8X's knurled surface threading enhances grip ergonomics and operational control, reducing hand fatigue during extended use.

Dual-bearing system + Fluid rotation

Precision-ground spindle maintains ≤0.005mm TIR (Total Indicator Reading) to prevent bit deflection, eliminating cam-out and carving errors.

With a maximum speed of 16,000 RPM, it’s optimized for high-frequency manual rotation, ensuring smooth operation—even when driving precision screws—without stalling.
