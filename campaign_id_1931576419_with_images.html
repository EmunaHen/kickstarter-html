Intro

With a passion for electronics products behind Technology Recreation for You (TRY4): 

I bring to you the TRY4 Digital! 

Bringing to market a never-seen-before digital watch style using transparent OLED technology!

Show off your love for electronics and unique timepieces with this special watch!

This unique timepiece is the result of hours of programming, 3D modeling, and devotion of my time, passion, and money. 

You can check out more videos and progress on the YouTube channel and the Instagram page! @technologyrecreation4you

This digital watch uses the mighty TM4C microcontroller from Texas Instruments. 

It is also completely designed and assembled in the silicon hills of AUSTIN, TEXAS.

Why Kickstarter?

I am running a crowdfunding campaign to ensure there is enough interest in the product that it can be produced at scale in order to keep the cost per unit lower. 

There are no risks in prototyping, developing, or manufacturing. I have completed and sorted all of these out: I just need to scale to bring the prices down, for you!

Features

  • Accurate timekeeping, with display customizations.
  • 46 x 36 x 10mm (L x W x H) with interchangeable 22mm strap.
  • Rain and splash resistant (not waterproof).
  • Indium tin oxide (ITO) transparent OLED display provides a never-seen-before style.
  • USB-C rechargeable battery, with 2-week or more life, charges in 1.5 hours.
  • Extra features like step counter, raise-to-wake, coin flip, stopwatch/visual alarm, and a variety of themes.
  • Simple 3 button user interface and physical user manual included.
  • User programmable in low-level C for advanced developers.
