Story

The POOT Story: A Movement About Your Movements

Every person on Earth has one thing in common: when nature calls, it doesn't wait.
But if you’ve ever wandered into a mystery-smelling gas station bathroom or awkwardly searched for a safe stall in public, you know the world has a bathroom problem.

POOT is the unapologetically funny, community-driven solution to a real-world issue:
a lack of transparency, trust, and dignity in public restroom access.

We’re building POOT as the world’s first socially-powered bathroom finder and rating app — with humor, honesty, and helpfulness at the core.

This isn’t just an app. It’s a movement.

🚽 What POOT Solves

🧻 Lack of clean, accessible restrooms while traveling, working, or in cities

📱 No centralized platform to rate, recommend, and warn others about bathroom quality

😂 Taboo topic that actually unites people — everyone has a bathroom story

💡 Opportunity for user-driven gamification, brand partnerships, and certified locations

Risks and challenges

Risks and Challenges We're building something delightfully ridiculous but logistically real — an app that helps people find, rate, and respect public restrooms. It's fun but also a complex software project with moving parts (like toilet seats, hopefully). Development Risks We estimate needing around $65,000 to cover: * Full-stack app development for iOS & Android * Backend infrastructure and location-based features * UI/UX design, testing, and launch readiness * Branding, marketing, and legal costs We've built our budget with contingency, but like any tech project, timelines can shift based on feature changes, App Store approvals, or unexpected bugs. We're working with a trusted developer team and a flexible release roadmap to minimize delays. Content & Community Risks POOT relies on real users rating real bathrooms. Early traction is crucial. That's why we've already begun building a community and social presence before launch — to ensure that by the time POOT hits the app stores, there's an army of bathroom truth-tellers ready to flush the system clean. Moderation & Reliability Bathroom humor is fun — but we're also committed to making POOT helpful, honest, and not gross for the sake of gross. We'll have tools for reporting fake reviews, flagging abuse, and upholding a basic "dignity in the stalls" standard. With your support, we're ready to turn the bathroom break into a movement — and make public restrooms a little less terrifying for everyone. Let's build POOT together. Because when you gotta go, you deserve to know.
