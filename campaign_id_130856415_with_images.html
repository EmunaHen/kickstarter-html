🌟 Our Design Journey: From Idea to Prototype

Creating our product has been a long but exciting journey of iteration, testing, and decision-making.

🟠 The Form Factor
We explored many different shape concepts for our lamp. At one point, we considered a variety of forms—from sharp-edged minimalist designs to playful geometric styles. But in the end, we chose a rounded rectangular shape with orange accents. Why orange? We found it energizing and warm—exactly the feeling we want our users to experience.
We also tried blue accents early on, but during testing, blue gave off a colder and somewhat gloomy feeling, which conflicted with our goal of creating a light that lifts people’s mood, especially during darker months.

🎛️ Control Interface
We started with physical knobs for tactile control but soon realized flexibility was key. After testing a screen-based remote with high defect rates, we landed on a clean, intuitive wireless design inspired by classic TV remotes—familiar, functional, and reliable.

🪵 Materials & Structure
Wood looked great but cracked under pressure—literally. We switched to aluminum alloy for its clean aesthetic, structural strength, and long-term durability.
