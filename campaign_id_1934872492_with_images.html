How It Works!

MEET - PLAY - DATE

The datersGame process contrasts with traditional dating apps. In our approach, two members answer lifestyle questions simultaneously to self-determine compatibility via a fun, non-threatening game. No more putting your faith in a dating app’s algorithm just to get ‘matched’ based on similarities without understanding what those similarities might be.

No more wasting time on a relationship where you find red flags after it’s too late!

Since you’ll be planning a life together with your person, why not base the foundation of your future relationship on shared morals, ideologies, hobbies and everything else in between?

Benefits Of Matchmaking Via Games

The science behind playing games to build human connections is rooted in psychology, neuroscience, and social dynamics. Games, whether video games, board games, or cooperative challenges, create an environment that fosters trust, collaboration, and bonding in several key ways.

Learning about your potential future partner on datersGame offers several benefits:

Encourages Open Communication: A structured game makes it easier to discuss topics that might otherwise feel awkward.

Reveals Values & Compatibility: The questions help uncover shared beliefs, life goals, and relationship expectations.

Makes Learning Fun & Engaging: Instead of a serious interrogation, it keeps things lighthearted and enjoyable.

Reduces Pressure: A game format makes deep conversations feel more natural and less like an interview.

Enhances Emotional Connection: Thoughtful questions can help build intimacy and understanding between partners.

Encourages Vulnerability: People may feel more comfortable opening up in a playful, non-judgmental setting.

Uncovers Red Flags Early: Insightful questions can reveal potential deal-breakers before a relationship gets too serious.

Strengthens Problem-Solving Skills: Some games include hypothetical scenarios that test how well you work together.

Encourages Active Listening: The back-and-forth nature of a game fosters deeper listening and engagement.

Boosts Attraction: Learning new things about each other can increase curiosity and attraction.

The Problems With Traditional Dating Apps
 

Traditional dating apps have revolutionized modern dating, but they come with a variety of issues and problems, including:

Superficiality & Swipe Culture: Many dating apps prioritize appearance over personality, leading to a shallow, looks-based dating experience – Users often make split-second decisions based on photos rather than meaningful compatibility.

Ghosting & Flaky Behavior: The ease of matching makes it just as easy to ignore or unmatch someone, leading to high rates of ghosting – Many users treat conversations casually and may never follow through with meeting in person.

Mismatched Intentions (Hookups vs. Relationships): Many people turn to apps for casual hookups, but others are genuinely looking for serious relationships. This difference in intentions often leads to mismatched expectations and disappointment.– Apps like Tinder often attract a mix of users, making it hard to determine who is looking for what.

Fake Profiles, Bots, & Scammers: Many platforms suffer from fake accounts, catfishing, and bots that spam users with scam messages – Scammers prey on lonely individuals, sometimes leading to financial fraud.

Algorithm & Paywalls: Many apps have opaque algorithms that prioritize users who pay for premium features – Free users often get limited visibility, encouraging them to buy boosts or super-likes to increase matches.

Burnout & Dating Fatigue: Constant swiping, messaging, and rejection can lead to emotional exhaustion and a sense of dating as a chore – Users may become desensitized to matches and lose interest in putting in effort.

Gender Dynamics & Harassment: Women often face unwanted messages, explicit photos, or harassment, making the experience unsafe or uncomfortable – Men may struggle with getting matches due to the imbalance in gender ratios on some apps.

Lack of Authenticity: People often misrepresent themselves in their profiles, leading to disappointment when meeting in person – Filters, curated photos, and exaggerated bios create unrealistic expectations.

Overwhelming Choice & Paradox of Choice: The abundance of options can make people less likely to commit, as they always feel there might be someone better – Endless swiping fosters a mindset of always searching rather than focusing on meaningful connections.

Safety & Privacy Concerns: Apps require personal information, which can be misused if data leaks occur – Users risk stalking, harassment, or even physical harm if they meet someone dangerous.

About the Creator

Hi! My name is Deidre and I am the creator of datersGame.

I love love! As a child, I always had hope that one day I would be married. As I got older, shyness kept me at bay from dating lots of boys; but nevertheless, my dream came true and I was married at the age of 22. Unfortunately, 23 years later I was divorced. After taking time for myself and reflecting within, I realized incompatibility was the main cause of my failed marriage. Love was an important factor in maintaining our relationship, but my heart was deceived because I believed the feelings I had meant we were compatible, but that was not true.

Moving forward with my life, I didn’t allow a failed marriage to douse my desire to be in a successful marital relationship. So, I stepped outside of my “shy box” and signed up on a dating App. My hope in meeting someone compatible was ignited as there were lots of good guys pinging me daily on the App. However, disappointment soon settled in because of the App’s inability to assist me in finding out why I was compatible with the guys the algorithm matched me with. I attempted to figure this out on my own by chatting with a guy, but that didn’t go well because he ghosted me after I answered his question about my favorite food. I was bewildered and shocked but later had an “aha” moment and the idea for datersGame was born!
