Story

We take videos to remember the moments that matter. The people we love. The things we never want to forget. But most of them get buried. In chats. Between random Screenshots. In camera rolls.

Some memories shouldn’t fade like that.

That’s why I made Memyo stickers. Save your video-memories where they belong: on a photo, a page, a gift.

No app. No login. No subscription. Just the memory, where you want it.

Risks and challenges

Making Memyo a reality involves hands-on craftsmanship and reliable logistics. While I’ve planned carefully, some things can still take unexpected turns. Here's what you should know: 1. Shipping Delays If suppliers take longer than expected or shipping lanes are disrupted, delivery timelines could shift. We’ll keep you updated every step of the way. 2. Unexpected High Demand = Slower Fulfillment Each Memyo package is crafted and packed by hand. If demand exceeds expectations, fulfillment might take longer than planned. 2.Sticker Compatibility Memyo works with nearly all modern smartphones, but a few very old phones (made before 2017) may not work. 3. Packaging & Print Quality I'm aiming for great design and a polished packaging. Small variations in print or material quality might occur, especially in early batches. 4. Customs and Import Fees Depending on your location, local taxes or duties may apply. These are out of our control and not included in the pledge price.
