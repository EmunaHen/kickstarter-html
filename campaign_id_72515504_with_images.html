Story

A must-have for frozen treat lovers, HiCOZY F3 is a versatile frozen treat maker that lets you enjoy slushies, soft serve, spiked slushies, milkshakes, and cold drinks at home—all from one compact machine. No ice cubes, no prep work—just pour, choose and press. In as little as 15 minutes, you’ll be sipping or scooping perfectly chilled treats with ease. Make every sip a moment to savor with HiCOZY F3.

Make fluffy frozen treats without ice cubes. HiCOZY F3’s exclusive Quicool™ technology chills your drink in 15–60 minutes—no prep, no dilution, just pure, smooth slush and a quiet operation. Whether it’s a spontaneous craving or a planned party, HiCOZY F3 is always ready.

With our exclusive iWhirl™ system, the machine uses a variable-speed DC motor and adaptive algorithms to automatically and dynamically adjust the paddle’s speed based on the selected mode. This enables a dedicated soft serve function and delivers a smoother, creamier texture in every drink.

Slush, Soft Ice Cream, Spiked Slush, Cold Drink, Milkshake—HiCOZY F3 covers all your frozen favorites and more. With 5 smart presets, it’s easy to get started. Mix your own ingredients and experiment with endless flavor combos—DIY frozen fun starts here!

HiCOZY F3 is optimized to deliver smooth, consistent slushies and soft serve every time. 

Just make sure your ingredients meet a few simple guidelines: Use drinks with at least 6% sugar. Keep alcohol between 3%-16% for smooth freezing.

HiCOZY F3 features a unique dual-layer vessel that prevents condensation from forming and dripping onto your counter. Once your treat is ready, the insulated design keeps it cold for up to 2 hours, so you don’t have to rush to enjoy it. Every sip stays smooth, every scoop stays frosty.

HiCOZY F3 comes with a self-cleaning mode that takes the hassle out of cleanup. Just pour in hot water and press the button—sticky residue gets rinsed away without scrubbing, soaking, or disassembly. Let the machine handle the hard part.

HiCOZY F3 is made for the whole family—kids, grandparents, and everyone in between. Just three steps: pour in your favorite drink, choose a preset, and pull down the handle. With a colorful display and intuitive controls, there’s no learning curve. It’s easy to use and fun to explore.

HiCOZY F3 operates at just 55 dB, equivalent to the sound of a normal conversation or a quiet office environment. Enjoy powerful performance without the noise.

The HiCOZY F3 is designed with your kitchen and lifestyle in mind. A built-in side holder keeps cones and cups within reach. Removable parts go straight into the dishwasher. Its compact design means less clutter and more room to create. From first pour to final cleanup, it’s effortless.
