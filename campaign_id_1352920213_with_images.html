Let’s talk about Elfin Fountain D1 Pro.

Dogs aren’t like cats, their sizes vary widely. From Shih Tzus to Golden Retrievers, their hydration needs vary just as much as their personalities.
Most of us still use basic water bowls. Maybe two if we’re being cautious. But they run out fast. They get messy even faster. So we end up refilling them constantly just to keep up with their hydration habits.
And if we forget to clean or refill? That’s not just a hassle. It’s a real risk.

Sure, fountains solve the volume problem. They’re great for keeping more water on hand throughout the day. But traditional designs bring their own issues because of one thing mostly: the submerged pump.

Traditional pet fountains rely on a submerged pump. Water gets pulled through a screen, pushed by an internal impeller, and sent back up through a nozzle. It works, but when it comes to cleaning, things get messy. The pump sits in water, hidden inside a structure that’s hard to reach and fully clean. Over time, that moisture and buildup can lead to electrical risks most people never think about.

Elfin Fountain D1 Pro takes a different approach. No pump in the tank means no blind spots and no risks. Just a cleaner, simpler system made for dogs and everyday life.
Let’s take a closer look.

Compared to traditional fountains with submerged pumps, Elfin Fountain D1 Pro keeps it simple with just two parts in the water tank. Nothing gets buried, nothing builds up, and cleaning stays quick and easy.

Easy to disassemble in just 2 seconds. No hidden parts. What we see is exactly what we have.

By cutting out the pump, we also cut down the clutter. The simplified structure means less scrubbing and fewer steps, saving much of your time from the usual cleaning routine. Every clean feels complete, and every sip stays fresh.

Every clean is thoroughly clean

Elfin Fountain D1 Pro swaps the traditional submerged pump for MagDrive™ technology. It draws water using magnetic force from outside the tank, so nothing electrical ever touches the water. No exposure, no aging wires, and no hidden risks.

All electronics stay outside the tank, where they last longer and stay safely away from our pet’s water.

MagDrive™ technology turns electrical power into magnetic motion, spinning the impeller to pull and push water. No contact, no compromise.

The Care Kit offers an affordable and eco-friendly way to keep things fresh. We can replace the impeller and water conduit with the Care Kit set. For routine care, just simply rinse it during each fountain cleaning.

All dogs drink with their tongues, but the way they do it can be different. Flat-nosed breeds like pugs, long-eared spaniels, and bigger dogs each bring their own habits.

After almost two years of testing across different breeds and sizes (and species), we landed on a design that works for most. It helps prevent messy drinking, wet fur, and even choking for some breeds.

The water tray is just 0.51 inches deep, carefully engineered to reduce choking risk and keep our pup’s jaw clean and dry while drinking.

The 0.73 ft² tray offers plenty of space for drinking. Its low-spill design keeps water steady and clean, even when our best friend gets a little too excited.

Dogs need anywhere from 0.6 to 4.5 liters of water a day, depending on their size and activity. With a 4 liter (1.06 gal) capacity, Elfin Fountain D1 Pro keeps most dogs hydrated without constant refills. It works well for busy days, bigger breeds, or homes with more than one pup.

Its 5.4" height provides a steady flow of fresh water for most dogs.

Long-lasting design

The Elfin Fountain D1 Pro introduces a new way of thinking about hydration, one that ditches the traditional submerged pump in favor of our MagDrive™ technology. This pumpless approach isn’t just about innovation for innovation’s sake. It’s built around longevity. Fewer mechanical failures. Fewer internal parts that degrade over time. Easier cleaning and maintenance that actually extends the life of the product. We know reliability matters, which is why every Elfin Fountain D1 Pro is backed by an 18-month warranty.
