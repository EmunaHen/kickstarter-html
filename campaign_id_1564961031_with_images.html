Auro Wand uses patented spectral technology to measure deep soil moisture directly and in real time. No more relying on generic weather forecasts or unreliable sensors. This delivers precise data with strong anti-interference capabilities.

Our smart algorithm, built on extensive research, automatically balances evaporation with watering needs. It supports multiple plant types and ensures precise irrigation through a refined watering model.

Auro Wand delivers the right amount of water exactly when needed, adapting to your garden's specific requirements. Once calibrated, it will automatically trigger the electric valve to release water when moisture levels drop below a certain threshold, keeping your lawn or garden hydrated. Over time, the system learns and improves its accuracy with each irrigation cycle, adapting to changing weather patterns and soil conditions.

No complex system planning or wiring! Simply connect Auro Wand to your water faucet, select your plant type, and you're ready to go. Even gardening beginners can master professional irrigation in just five minutes.

Designed for ultimate simplicity and convenience, Auro Wand is the world's first app-free and solar-powered smart irrigation system, providing intelligent watering without extra hassle or energy costs.

Whether you have a lush lawn, vibrant flower beds, or a thriving home vegetable garden, Auro Wand optimizes watering for all your planting needs.
