Introduction

Social media today is broken and lost its initial purpose. It’s dominated by intrusive ads, manipulative algorithms, and platforms that makes profit from selling your data. FindLocall is here to change that. We’re building a privacy-first, Tracking-free social media platform where users can connect, share, and engage without being treated as the product. In simple terms we are "wikipedia" of social Networks. 

With your support, we can launch a new era of ethical social networking—where your data stays yours, and real communities thrive. This project introduces a completely original concept that has never been implemented anywhere in the world — one that is built from the ground up to fully respect your genuine right to privacy.

In a digital landscape dominated by invasive algorithms and opaque data harvesting, we've chosen a different path. This platform does not treat your attention or identity as a product. Instead, we believe your trust is worth more than your data.

Years of investigations and trials across the EU and California have revealed what many suspected all along: major tech and social media platforms routinely collect, analyze, and monetize your personal data without meaningful consent. From dark patterns to shadow profiles, the reality is clear — privacy is under attack.

That’s why this project matters.

We’re creating a system that doesn’t just “comply” with regulations — it prioritizes your privacy as a core design principle. No hidden trackers. No third-party profiling. Just tools and experiences that empower you, not exploit you.

If you're ready for a platform that values people over profiling, you're in the right place.

Core Principles of FindLocall

Unlike traditional social media, FindLocall is built on 3 core principles:

  •  Privacy & Security

-No tracking, no data selling
 -End-to-end encrypted messaging
-Users have full control over their data and what they want to see where and whom they want to post or connect. 

  • Community-Driven, Not Algorithm-Controlled

-No engagement-based algorithms—"what you see is what’s shared".
- Interest-based communities for real connections
- No manipulation, no shadowbanning, no "viral" gimmicks.

  •  Ethical & Sustainable

- Free from Corporate or any 3rd party supervisions.
-User-funded, not advertiser-driven
-Future-ready with decentralization plans

Risks and challenges

Risks & Challenges FindLocall faces challenges like competing with Big Tech, scaling without conventional ad- revenue, and ensuring secure, independent infrastructure. We tackle this through community-driven growth, ethical monetization such as: freemium, Marketplace-subscriptions,& donations. User adoptions is addressed via invite-based growth, login or sharing on multiple social platforms and word of mouth, while security risks are managed with encryption, regular logs & audits, and regular security updates. By prioritizing privacy, transparency, and sustainability, we can build a truly ethical social media platform—with your support!
