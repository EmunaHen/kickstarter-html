Story

For too many artists, the dream is a trap.

My name is Max, and for years, my creative life has been on hold while I've worked gig-economy jobs just to survive. I'm not alone. Millions of talented musicians, filmmakers, and creators across the USA are caught on an algorithmic treadmill, burning out to create content for platforms that profit from their work but leave them with pennies.

We have the talent. We have the drive. But we lack the tools, the community, and the support to turn our passion into a sustainable career.

It's time to build our own system. That system is MyUVI.

MyUVI is more than just an app—it's a digital ecosystem designed from the ground up to put power back into the hands of independent artists. We've already invested thousands of hours and dollars into developing the core platform, and now we need your help to launch it to the world.

What is MyUVI? Your All-in-One Creative Hub

MyUVI is the command center for your creative career. A single online platform and mobile app where you can:

  • Distribute & Monetize: Upload your music and videos directly to Spotify, Apple Music, YouTube, and more, all while tracking your analytics and royalties in one place.
  • Build Your Community: Create your own dedicated fan hub, share exclusive content, and connect with your audience without fighting algorithms.
  • Access a Professional Marketplace: Need a beatmaker for your next track? A video editor for your vlog? A graphic designer for cover art? Our marketplace connects you with vetted creative professionals from across the country.
  • Find Gigs & Collaborate: Pitch your music to playlists and labels, find collaborators for your next project, and discover opportunities you'd never find alone.

More Than an App: Our Physical Hub in Seattle

While MyUVI is a nationwide digital platform, every movement needs a home. As a special benefit for our community, our headquarters in Seattle will house United Vision Studios—a professional-grade recording and video production facility. MyUVI members will get exclusive access and discounted rates, providing a physical space to bring their digital ideas to life.

Our $50,000 Goal: The Official Launch Fund

This Kickstarter campaign is focused on one clear goal: to fund the official public launch of the MyUVI platform and its mobile apps.

Your support will be allocated to these key launch activities:

  • Final App Development & Deployment ($15,000): To complete the final coding, rigorous testing, and successfully launch the iOS and Android apps on the App Store and Google Play.
  • Server & Infrastructure Costs ($8,000): To cover the first 6 months of our dedicated server costs, ensuring the platform is fast and reliable for thousands of users.
  • Community & Support Team ($15,000): To hire our first community manager and technical support staff who will onboard new artists and ensure everyone has a great experience.
  • Launch Marketing Campaign ($7,000): To spread the word to creators across the USA and attract the first wave of talent to the platform.
  • Kickstarter Fees & Fulfillment ($5,000): To cover platform fees and deliver your amazing rewards.

Risks and challenges

We believe in transparency, and launching an ambitious project like the MyUVI ecosystem comes with its own set of challenges. We have identified the primary risks and have a clear plan to mitigate them. 1. Software Development & Timelines The Risk: Software development is complex. Unexpected bugs or technical hurdles could potentially delay our projected launch timeline for the apps and platform. Our Mitigation Plan: The core of MyUVI is already in an advanced stage of development. The funds from this campaign are not for starting from scratch, but for the crucial final stages: rigorous testing, user interface polish, and deployment. Our founder, Max, has over 10 years of development experience and has built a clear, prioritized roadmap. We will focus on delivering the core features flawlessly and on time. 2. Building a Community from Day One The Risk: A digital platform is only as valuable as its community. Our challenge is to ensure that from the day we launch, MyUVI is a vibrant and active space, avoiding the "empty nightclub" problem. Our Mitigation Plan: This is a primary focus for our team. Part of this campaign's budget is specifically allocated to a targeted launch marketing campaign aimed at creators. Furthermore, this Kickstarter campaign is our community-building strategy. Every one of you, our backers, becomes a founding member, creating an engaged and passionate user base before the platform even goes public. 3. Technical Scalability & App Store Approval The Risk: A surge in popularity after launch could put a strain on our servers. Additionally, the Apple App Store and Google Play have their own review processes which can be unpredictable. Our Mitigation Plan: We have already sourced and budgeted for a robust, long-term dedicated server solution capable of handling thousands of users. We are building our apps to the highest standards, following all developer guidelines from Apple and Google to ensure a smooth and speedy approval process. Our project timeline includes a buffer specifically for these app store reviews. We are incredibly passionate about this project and fully committed to bringing it to life. We will maintain open and honest communication with you, our backers, at every step of the journey.
