Risks and challenges

Risks & Challenges Every strong project comes with its share of challenges — here’s how we plan to manage them: Production Delays Risk: Supply chain issues or manufacturing bottlenecks Our Solution: We’ve secured alternative suppliers in China and India and established flexible procurement contracts to ensure production reliability. Logistics & Shipping Issues Risk: Shipping delays or customs processing holdups Our Solution: We’re working with experienced logistics partners who specialize in high-volume deliveries and international fulfillment. Rapid Growth Risk: Demand could exceed our current infrastructure Our Solution: We’ll scale in regional phases — starting with DACH, then expanding to the EU and North America — ensuring sustainable, controlled growth. Maintaining Quality at Scale Risk: Ensuring consistent quality as volume increases Our Solution: Our in-house quality control (QC) team performs factory-level inspections and ongoing testing to maintain top-tier product standards. Regulatory Compliance Risk: Varying standards and regulations across markets Our Solution: We collaborate closely with compliance experts and focus on securing CE, GS, and UL certifications for each product.
