Story

Hello World! We are two college founders and problem-solving engineers at heart. As you may have noticed, water consumption rates have increased globally, while sustainable solutions remain either too expensive, overly complex, or inaccessible due to systems that require full irrigation remodeling. That did not sit right with us. So, we created Clydro, a compact, smart, instant-install device that uses real-time data to tell you exactly when your lawn or garden needs water, because no guessing means no waste. 

Whether you're a homeowner, renter, or gardener, Clydro makes water-conscious decisions effortless. In our dorm room, we built the first working prototype and a website to display the data. This website processes soil and weather data to communicate the best time to water. We've tested it, refined it, and now it's time to see: are you interested in a future where water isn't wasted and plant care is optimized? 

That's why we're here on Kickstarter! Not just to raise funds, but to raise a community that believes in smarter, simpler, and more sustainable living. Therefore, if saving water, money, and the environment resonate with you, help us turn this goal of ours into a reality!

Risks and challenges

We believe in total transparency. Therefore, here are all the risks included with this project: - Manufacturing & Supply Chain: As a hardware product, Clydro requires reliable sourcing, tooling, and production. If demand is met we will be working with vetted manufacturers and design everything with mass production in mind, but unexpected supply chain delays can occur. - Connectivity & Compatibility: Clydro connects to WiFi and syncs with a user interface. We’re actively testing across various networks and will continue improving compatibility with different home systems. - Scaling Support: As two founders, we’re hands-on with everything — from engineering to customer support. If this campaign grows quickly, we may need to bring on additional help to maintain quality and responsiveness. - Weather Variability: Because Clydro relies on data and environmental sensing, its performance may vary slightly in extreme or unpredictable climates. We’re continually improving our algorithms to be more adaptive. In case you want to garden in Antarctica.

Long-lasting design

We've designed Clydro to last by taking into account these various factors: - Weather: Clydro is extremely weather resistant and built for the outdoors. Therefore, rainy days or high UV exposure will not cause damage. - Battery Life: By implementing ultra-low power modes and smart sleep cycles, Clydro wakes up only when needed to collect and transmit data. - Customer Support: We stand behind our product, so that if anything goes wrong we're committed to helping you fix it, not force you to buy a new one.

Reusability and recyclability

We plan to implement a take back program, which allows customers to trade in older devices for credits, which we'll reuse and recycle.

Sustainable materials

Clydro is built with sustainably as its core principle, starting with the outer case itself. Currently we use PLA (Polylactic Acid), a plant-based, biodegradable plastic derived from renewable resources like corn starch and sugarcane.
