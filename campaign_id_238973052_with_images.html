Meet Zaire Helmets

Zaire Helmets were born from a simple question:
What if your helmet could do more than protect you?
What if it could communicate, alert, and connect, all without distracting you from the ride?

We didn’t set out to make just another helmet.
We set out to build a co-pilot.

With built-in Bluetooth audio, walkie-talkie communication, blind spot monitoring, and integrated turn signals, Zaire Helmets are designed for riders who demand more awareness, more safety, more control.

Whether you're leading the pack, riding with friends, or navigating solo through busy streets, Zaire keeps you in sync, in command, and in the moment.

This is cycling, upgraded.
This is Zaire.

Behind the Tech: Core tech in Zaire Helmet

Wireless Audio with Smart Sound System.

Ride with your playlist, take calls, and hear directions, all without touching your phone.
Zaire’s near-ear speakers use bone conduction technology to transmit sound through vibrations, keeping your ears open to traffic, voices, and your environment.

No wires. No earbuds. Just smart, safe sound.

Built-in Walkie-Talkie

Zaire’s built-in walkie-talkie lets you talk to other Zaire riders in your group.
No phones. No apps. No signal needed.
Just press a button and speak with crystal-clear voice and real-time connection.
Ride together. Stay in sync.

Smart Lighting System

Zaire Helmets feature adaptive lighting that works with you, not against you.

  • Auto Lights turn on in low lighting.
  • Auto Brake Light alerts when you slow down or stop.
  • Wireless Turn Signals via handlebar remote.
  • High Beam Mode for extra visibility at night.

Stay seen. Stay safe. No switches, no distractions.

Built-In Dashcam

Zaire’s front-facing dashcam records your ride automatically.
Wide-angle lens. Loop recording. No setup needed.
Capture every ride, for safety or the story.

Safety At Its Core

Impact Protection

Zaire Helmets are fully CE and CPSC compliant. Built with a lightweight high-strength shell, and an impact-absorbing liner engineered to meet rigorous safety standards in both the EU and US. Because real protection starts with a solid foundation, and we don’t compromise on the fundamentals.

Blindspot Detection

Ultrasonic sensors continuously monitor what’s behind you, alerting you to approaching vehicles and riders before they enter your blind spot.
Stay ahead of the unexpected with real-time awareness, built right in.

One Remote - Total Control

The handlebar-mounted remote puts full control right where you need it, activate lights, change music, or communicate with your group, all without taking your hands off the bars.
The handlebar remote is glove-friendly, intuitive, and always within reach.
Because staying in control means staying safe.

Turn Signals & Rear Lighting for Visibility and Safety

Zaire’s bright rear LED lights and turn signals make sure you're seen, and most importantly, understood. Signal your intentions clearly with a wireless remote and stay visible in low light or heavy traffic.
Because visibility isn’t just convenience, it’s your first layer of protection.

Open-Ear Audio - Full Awareness

Traditional headphones block out the world. Zaire doesn’t.
Our open-ear (bone conduction) audio keeps your music, calls, and walkie-talkie communication clear, all without covering your ears or isolating you from your surroundings.
You hear your ride. You hear your environment. You stay alert.

Risks and challenges

Like any other new product, we are bound to encounter challenges along the way while bringing this project to life. Here are some challenges we anticipate, and the solutions we’ve put in place to ensure smooth development and delivery: Manufacturing & Production: Scaling from prototypes to full production can reveal unforeseen bottlenecks, from tooling delays to batch defects. Our solution: We've already vetted manufacturers, finalized the design, and built in buffer time to handle any unexpected slowdowns without affecting delivery timelines. Component Availability: Global supply chain issues can affect the availability of certain chips, sensors, or parts. Our solution: We chose widely available components and built flexibility into the hardware design to allow for qualified substitutions without compromising performance. Safety Certifications: To meet international safety standards (like CPSC and EN1078), Zaire Helmets must pass multiple lab tests, and sometimes even minor changes can trigger re-testing. Our solution: We’ve designed Zaire with certification in mind from the start, using compliant materials and working with labs during prototyping to stay on track. Feature Expectations: As a smart helmet, users may expect app support, OTA updates, or expanded connectivity features. Our solution: We're laser-focused on core functionality that works out of the box: Bluetooth audio, walkie-talkie, turn signals, and blind spot detection. No app required. Shipping & Fulfillment: International shipping can bring delays due to customs, regulation differences, or carrier hiccups. Our solution: We’re partnering with experienced logistics teams and fulfillment centers that specialize in hardware Kickstarter projects. We’re committed to transparency and will keep our backers updated every step of the way. Your trust means everything, and we’re building Zaire Helmets to exceed expectations.
