Risks and challenges

As the first desktop hybrid laser engraver, we have conducted extensive testing on the HD1 series to ensure the quality of the final product. If any delays occur due to these unforeseen circumstances, we will keep our backers informed with the latest updates and provide ongoing support throughout the process. We are fully dedicated to ensuring that each product reaches the doorstep of every supporter!
