Struggling with weak outlets and sluggish charging on the go?
When you're hopping airports, hotels, or global routes, keeping phones, laptops, and tablets powered is a constant pain.

Meet the SLID n-CHRG 170W Travel Adapter—a compact, high-power universal charger that powers all your gear anywhere in the world.

n-CHRG utilizes the industry-leading GaN Chip (4th Generation) Power IC to better power and performance. The new Gallium Nitride semiconductor material allows for power devices that can operate at higher voltages and switching frequency which results in smaller and more efficient chargers.

SLID n-CHARG Travel Adapter packs powerful, high-speed charging into a sleek, card-sized design.

Compact yet versatile, it keeps all your devices powered effortlessly—anytime, anywhere in the world.

The SLID n-CHARG 170W Travel Adapter is built for serious charging, packed with high-powered ports to handle all your devices:

🔹 140W USB-C – Charges powerhouse laptops like the 16” MacBook Pro or Dell XPS at full speed
🔹 140W USB-C – Perfect for tablets and compact laptops
🔹 30W USB-C – Ideal for flagship phones and extra devices
🔹 18W USB-A – Wide compatibility for accessories like headphones, speakers, and more
⚡ AC1 Universal Socket – Supports global plug ratings up to 2500W, perfect for high-power appliances like hair dryers, curling irons, kettles, and travel irons
⚡AC2 Socket – Designed for American and Japanese plug types, ensuring seamless compatibility

Industry-Beating Low-Temperature Operation

With so many electronics densely packed in a tiny space, there is a valid concern about heat generation and dissipation in such a small space.

So we measured the temperature across 30 points of the n-CHRG charger while it is charging the 16" MacBook Pro from 0% to 100% battery level. The internal components temperature did not exceed 90ºC while the exterior housing temperature did not exceed 60ºC, beating industry-standards of 130ºC and 65ºC respectively.

We tested n‑CHRG’s 140W port on a single device, delivering full-speed charging with stable performance and zero overheating.
