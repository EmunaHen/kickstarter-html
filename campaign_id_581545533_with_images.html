Story

Other PPFs get bent out of shape and start to peel under high heat. But not SERVOCAR. Our PPF boasts powerful heat resistance to withstand up to 180 °C (356 °F), so even when you’re driving through the scorching desert, your car stays protected.

SERVOCAR protects your car from scratches and other kinds of damage. Just take a look—our results speak for themselves.

And we all know that damage to our cars can happen at any moment, whether it’s from the road, dust, or weather.

SERVOCAR is made at the perfect thickness for easy application and strong proection from rock chips, dirt, debris and other minor impacts. You’ll never have to worry about car deterioration again!

Apply SERVOCAR on any car surface to keep your vehicle in pristine condition. Our invisible fortified barrier keeps your car looking great.

SERVOCAR avoids the two main issues of PPF—orange peels and yellowing. We used a premium quality Argotec TPU and UV absorbent TOP coating to keep your car looking beautiful.

We conducted a QUV accelerated weathering test to test SERVOCAR’s performance in ambient conditions. Our test results speak for themselves—NO discoloration!

SERVOCAR’s top coating was especially created to prevent any orange peel eyesores from showing up on your car. See for yourself how SERVOCAR stands out compared to other PPFs.

SERVOCAR comes at a whopping 70 inches wide. Compare that to competitor films that measure at just 60 inches.

SERVOCAR’s big enough to use on your SUV or pickup truck. You can even cover a boat or plane with our PPF.

For cyclists and motorcycle owners, the 23 inch PPF kit is the best way to protect your ride or provide partial coverage for a car in the places that matter most.

SERVOCAR has the miraculous ability to restore itself to its original, pre-scratch condition. Just apply hot water to the scratched surface area and watch the magic happen!

Swirl mark and other light scratches can even be treated in ambient temperatures in just 10 minutes.

With outstanding hydrophobic properties, water will slide right off the surface of your car to keep your car crystal clear and free of water spots. Your car will continue to look pristine and clear months after you’ve driven it off the lot.

We tested SERVOCAR to see just how much dirt and mud it can handle. Even in absolutely brutal conditions (6 days at 185°F + 85% humidity), SERVOCAR maintained its outstanding hydrophobic properties while other brands could not.

Thanks to its amazing hydrophobic properties, SERVOCAR makes it easier than ever to clean and maintain your car.

We’ve spent 5 years researching the perfect adhesive layer composition to keep your car’s paint looking flawless even after long periods of use.

Compared to other 70 inch PPFs out there, SERVOCAR comes with much better features and a much more affordable price to protect all manner of vehicles.

SERVOCAR is made by Enhance LABS. Our team has been making LCD-TV films and adhesive tapes since the 2000’s to become leaders in our industry. We leveraged our unique coating technologies and variety of film ventures to create SERVOCAR. We’re confident that this brand new, premium product will be the perfect coating for your car.
