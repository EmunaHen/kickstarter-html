Story

Other roasters may leave you guessing, but BeanGo Cube X Pro listens for the first and second crack with auto detection sensors, giving you real-time feedback and confidence in every roast.

While some systems limit you to built-in panels, BeanGo Cube X Pro combines mobile convenience with the customization power of Artisan software, made for both casual and expert users.

Many high-performance machines are bulky and heavy. BeanGo Cube X Pro delivers commercial-level results in a lightweight, desktop-friendly design, making it ideal for kitchens, cafes, or classrooms.

Every roast has a critical turning point, and BeanGo Cube X Pro detects it before you do. With built-in acoustic sensors, it accurately identifies the first and second cracks, ensuring you capture the exact moment when flavor begins to develop. Roast to your taste preferences.

BeanGo Cube X Pro delivers accuracy at every stage of the roast. From PID-stabilized preheating to automatic crack detection and logging, each step is managed by smart sensors and intelligent controls. Whether you're a beginner or following a refined roast profile, you can count on consistent results without manual guesswork.

Precision starts before the beans even drop. BeanGo Cube X Pro preheats to a fixed-target PID value, then dynamically adjusts heat, fan, and drum speed for curve stability throughout the roast.

Once you’ve found your perfect roast, BeanGo Cube X Pro helps you replicate it. By automatically detecting bean temperature, DTR, BT rise, and cracks, it replicates your profile with up to 90 percent accuracy for consistent flavor every time.

No more guesswork. With PID preheating and a door sensor that detects when you load, BeanGo Cube X Pro logs the roast start automatically and precisely. Smart design ensures every roast begins right.

BeanGo Cube X Pro ensures a smooth and safe finish. The Drop Door Sensor marks the end of the roast, while the Drop Tray Sensor locks the tray in place and stops the drum to prevent spills. Precision and peace of mind in every batch.

With built-in, expertly designed roast curves in the BeanGo Cube XPro app, with one tap you can start a full timed and sensor guided roast cycle. Easy for beginners, convenient for busy days, and consistent every time.

Connect BeanGo Cube X Pro to ARTISAN and take full control of all your roasting variables — heat, airflow, timing, and more.

Small enough for weekend experiments, big enough for meaningful results. BeanGo Cube X Pro lets you roast between 100 and 300 grams, ideal for personal use, sample testing, or educational sessions.

Great coffee deserves easy cleanup. The BeanGo Cube X Pro features a removable chaff drawer, anti-clog venting, and modular parts for quick, simple cleaning. Chaff trays under the chamber and at the vent collect all chaff—eliminating mess and extra work.  Less mess, more roasting.

Whether you are brewing at home, refining skills, or sampling beans in a cafe, BeanGo Cube X Pro fits your space and your style. With a compact design, professional control, and a clean workflow, it is built to adapt to your needs.

Wake up to the aroma of beans you roasted yourself. From weekend batches to daily brews, BeanGo Cube X Pro fits your routine, transforming roasting from a tedious task to a treasured experience.
