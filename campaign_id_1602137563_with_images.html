Story

GameMaker Plus: Empowering Young Athletes Through Smart, Interactive Training

Join us in revolutionizing youth sports with mobile-first training tools and Bluetooth-enabled targets. 

Hi, I’m Mike Wales, founder of GameMaker Plus.  After overcoming a spinal cord tumor and relearning how to walk, I turned my passion for sports into a mission: helping kids train smarter, build confidence, and fall in love with the game.  With two apps already live—Basketball and Soccer—we’re ready to expand. And we need your help.

I started with a concept and with help from my family, we built several proto-types and started testing.

Prototype History: From Foam Targets to Smart Training

 Where It All Began

Our journey started with a simple idea: make training more fun, interactive, and effective. The first prototype was built around an impact-sensitive foam target, designed to respond to kicks, hits, or throws. We tested multiple sensor technologies, but the embedded impact sensor stood out — it could handle the force of a soccer ball and delivered reliable feedback.

       

Early Wins & Challenges

We paired the foam target with a custom electronic module powered by a microcontroller. The system worked beautifully in testing — responsive, durable, and engaging. But there was a catch: the cost of production was too high to make it viable for everyday athletes and coaches.

 The Pivot to Mobile

To solve the cost challenge, we shifted gears. Our next phase focused on integrating the experience into a mobile app, reducing hardware needs while keeping the interactive spirit alive. We explored wireless options like 433 MHz controllers and Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE), eventually deciding to start with a pure phone app.

The result? A sleek, affordable app with unique training platforms that still deliver interactivity — no extra hardware required.

Generation 2: Bringing It All Together

Now, we’re ready to take it further. Generation 2 will combine the best of both worlds:

  • Interactive targets
  • Bluetooth connectivity
  • Seamless integration with our mobile app
  • Expansion into new sports like hockey and lacrosse

This next-gen system will be modular, scalable, and sport-specific, opening doors for athletes of all levels to train smarter and play harder.
