Story

Bring Your Drinks to the Adventure!

Welcome to the first Swords & Flowers project: the Fantasy Tankards! A collection of highly detailed, 3D-printable STL files that transform your standard beverage can into an epic mug. Whether you're a tabletop gamer, fantasy fan, or 3D printing enthusiast, these designs bring immersion and fun to your game nights.

Designed for 330ml and 355ml cans, these tankard sleeves slide over your drink and turn it into a piece of gear worthy of a tavern hero. With designs inspired by D&D classes, Norse legends, and fantastic beasts, there's a tankard for every player in the room.

What you will get:

By backing this project, you will receive 12 unique tankard designs in the form of STL printable files. Every design will be delivered in a single piece and separated in multiple parts to be easier to print in any printer.

Backers will receive their rewards via MyMiniFactory. Make sure to have your account!

This campaign only offers digital files. No physical prints will be delivered in this project.
