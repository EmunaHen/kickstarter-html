Story

Meet ZimaBoard 2

Four years ago, we launched ZimaBoard 1 on Kickstarter with the support of 1,880 backers, kicking off our journey into home servers.Since then, CasaOS, ZimaOS, ZimaBlade, and ZimaCube have evolved with the help of our growing community.
Now, we proudly present——ZimaBoard 2

Board. Beyond

The all-new Intel N150 processor unleashes ZimaBoard 2 with explosive hyper performance. Compared to its predecessor, ZimaBoard 2 delivers CPU processing power up to 3 times faster. Armed with next-gen DDR5 memory, soaring up to 16GB, these upgrades supercharge multitasking and deliver lightning-fast response speeds, making ZimaBoard 2 an unstoppable force against any challenge.
ZimaBoard 2 retains the renowned versatile interface design of ZimaBoard 1, effortlessly adapting to various scenarios. The newly upgraded PCIe 3.0 interface and dual 2.5G Ethernet ports unlock greater hardware expansion possibilities and ultra-fast data transfer experiences. The new MiniDP 1.4 interface delivers vibrant 4K visuals for your movies, while the classic dual SATA interfaces allow ZimaBoard 2 to easily expand storage capacity.

Mini NAS

Don't let its size fool you. With just a cable and a drive, ZimaBoard 2 becomes your Mini NAS. Its palm-sized form is perfectly paired with an elegant fanless silent design, freeing you from the bulk and noise of traditional NAS hardware. Coupled with the pre-installed ZimaOS, it delivers an extremely friendly personal cloud experience, far surpassing conventional NAS systems.

- ZimaOS

- Data Centralized

Files, your data all-in-one hub, effortlessly gathers your scattered data into one place. From important files on a USB drive to travel photos in Dropbox or creative assets on a Samba LAN device, you can import everything into Files with a single click. Simple and efficient.

- High-speed Backup

Fast, fast, fast backup. Zima Client makes saving your creative projects and photo libraries blazingly quick. You’re at your desk editing a 10GB 4K asset on your desktop when you’re suddenly called to a meeting in the conference room. Just click to backup, and your work is safely backed up to your laptop at speeds up to 280MB/s in a Samba environment. Trust me, by the time you reach the meeting room, your files are securely backed up across devices.

- Massive Storage

A home for the things you love. With ZimaBoard 2, you not only get a massive 36TB capacity but also ZimaOS-powered RAID creation. So you can build a truly personal digital content library, storing every 4K Star Wars and Marvel movie with ease. Plus, RAID protection means no worries even if a drive fails.

ZimaBoard 2, as a soft router with dual 2.5G ports, lets you master your network with ultra-low latency and rock-solid stability. With such mastery, set up VPNs, configure firewalls, monitor traffic flow, and block ads for a streamlined experience. Enjoy an ultra-fast, ultra-secure, and private network you master.

- Ironclad Digital Fortress

Secure your network with ZimaBoard 2 running soft router systems, fueled by VPN encryption and firewall protection. Running OpenWrt or pfSense, it delivers private connections and robust threat defense. In a café, you adjust your smart home lights via OpenWrt’s encrypted VPN, while pfSense detects a hacker’s connection attempt, instantly blocked by the firewall. It’s midnight, your smart speaker’s LED flashes suspiciously, but ZimaBoard 2’s dual 2.5G ports catch rogue uploads in real time, letting you analyze and block them with ease. Enjoy a secure, transparent network you secure.

Risks and challenges

We’re beyond excited to launch ZimaBoard 2! With the success of our Zima series on Kickstarter, we’re confident that ZimaBoard 2 will be delivered to every backer in its finest form, on time. That said, global tariff uncertainties, challenges in sourcing chips on a large scale, and the use of custom parts could impact our timeline. We’ve proactively increased investments in our supply chain and are working tirelessly to minimize these risks. With regular updates and transparent progress reports, we’ll keep every backer informed every step of the way. We’re fully prepared to tackle any challenges and are committed to delivering a product that lives up to your support and enthusiasm!

Environmentally friendly factories

Our production facilities adhere to stringent ISO and BSCI certifications, committing to robust social and environmental responsibilities. By optimizing the supply chain and minimizing transportation distances between material suppliers and factories, we significantly cut carbon emissions, contributing to a sustainable future.
