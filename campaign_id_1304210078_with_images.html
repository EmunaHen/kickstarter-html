This is not just a cable. It's the freedom to place your devices where they belong, not where cable length limits them.Whether you're playing AR/VR Headset, enjoying home theater, or hold a Video Conference , this cable delivers flawless 80Gbps performance over long distances where regular copper cables fail.

Perfect For 

Active Optical Cable (AOC) - Hybrid optical cable with fiber and copper wire

Yeeteng cable is precision-engineered for the future.Inside, a hybrid core of ultra-fast optical fiber and power-efficient copper works in perfect harmony — delivering lightning-speed data transmission with rock-solid power delivery.This advanced Active Optical Cable isn’t just high-performance — it’s a smart, adaptive architecture built for the next generation of connectivity.Expect unmatched bandwidth, stability, compatibility, and durability — all in one cable.

Photoelectric Conversion Chipset

This advanced chipset converts electrical signals into optical signals and vice versa.
which enables true long-distance, high-bandwidth data transmission with near-zero latency. This chipset transforms Yeeteng cable into a high-performance optical engine — stable, intelligent, and future-proof.

Super Speed Transmission -Step into the next generation of connectivity

Powered by USB4 Rev2.0, our cable delivers unprecedented data transfer speeds of up to 80Gbps — twice the bandwidth of previous USB4 and four times faster than USB 3.2 Gen 2x2.With this kind of speed, you’re not just transferring data — you’re unlocking new creative and professional possibilities.

Incredible Design

Carbon Fiber Shell 

Experience the perfect blend of strength and elegance with our Carbon Fiber Shell. Crafted from advanced aerospace-grade carbon fiber material, this shell is stronger than aluminum but significantly lighter, reducing overall device weight without compromising protection. And protects internal components from drops, bumps, and everyday wear and tear.

Mixed Fiber Braided

Our cable is wrapped in a mixed outer layer made from high-density braided nylon Aramid fiber — which its five times stronger than steel by weight. They provide extreme tensile strength, preventing fraying, stretching, or breaking under pressure. And the mixed weave offers a textured, professional look that matches the cable’s performance.

Risks and challenges

At Yeeteng, we believe in transparency — and while we’ve invested months into engineering, testing, and planning this project, we’d like to share a few potential risks and how we plan to manage them. 🔧 1. Production & Component Supply Risk Challenge: The USB4 Rev 2.0 spec requires high-precision components such as active chipsets, optical modules, and aramid fiber materials — any delay from suppliers could impact production timelines. Mitigation: We’ve already locked in production slots with our manufacturing partners and pre-secured key chipsets in advance. We’ve also built buffer time into our schedule to accommodate minor delays. 🔍 2. Quality Assurance for High-Speed Long Cables Challenge: Producing 10m–30m active cables with stable 80Gbps performance, PD 100W, and multi-display output requires strict testing. Any flaw in photoelectric conversion or shielding can affect performance. Mitigation: We conduct 100% signal integrity testing on every unit before shipping. Our QC process includes: Eye diagram analysis Crosstalk & jitter tests PD load simulations Compatibility tests on Windows/macOS/Thunderbolt setups 🌐 3. Global Logistics Uncertainty Challenge: International shipping delays, customs clearance, or regional disruptions (e.g. strikes, port congestion) may affect delivery schedules. Mitigation: We work with multiple logistics providers (DHL/FedEx/4PX), and will provide real-time tracking to all backers. We’re also preparing alternative shipping channels for key regions. 🔄 4. Standards Compatibility Evolution Challenge: USB4 Rev 2.0 and DisplayPort 2.1 continue to evolve. While our cable is compliant with current specs, future firmware or device-side updates could introduce unforeseen issues. Mitigation: We are actively testing our cables on a wide range of current and upcoming devices, including developer hardware. If required, we’ll publish updated compatibility lists and offer firmware guidance. 🙌 5. Unexpected Backer Demand Surge Challenge: If demand exceeds projections, it may stretch our fulfillment capacity. Mitigation: We’ve built scalable manufacturing and logistics plans, and have a network of backup suppliers. If needed, we’ll ship in waves to ensure fair delivery and keep backers updated throughout the process. 🛠️ We’re Ready This isn’t our first hardware launch — and we’ve learned how to adapt, communicate, and deliver. Your support helps us bring professional-grade cable technology into more hands — and we’re committed to keeping you informed every step of the way.
