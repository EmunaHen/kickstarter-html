Story

Meet Eliza Dolls: The First STEM Learning Doll with Real-World Sensors

Eliza Dolls are the first educational dolls designed to teach kids coding and hands-on science through real-world sensors. Each 18-inch Eliza doll comes with programmable lights, a set of snap-on sensors, and an activity workbook packed with fun STEM projects. Kids (ages 5+) can connect Eliza to a simple app and use kid-friendly, block-style coding to complete challenges like making Eliza’s earrings light up based on sound, measuring a plant’s moisture to help Eliza care for it, or using a heartbeat sensor to explore biology. Eliza Dolls combine classic imaginative play with interactive learning to help kids build problem-solving skills, curiosity, and confidence—all while having fun guiding their doll friend from Planet Codelia as she explores Earth.

Eliza Dolls were created from the PhD research of Dr. Eliza Kosoy, who earned her doctorate at UC Berkeley studying at the intersection of AI and child development. Her research inspired a new kind of toy that empowers kids to explore technology through play.

E-LIZA DOLLS

Eliza isn’t just a doll—she’s an astronaut from Planet Codelia, visiting Earth on a mission of exploration and discovery! Through fun, hands-on scientific coding projects, girls get to teach Eliza about our planet, from its colors and plants to its mysteries and truths. Together, they embark on a journey of curiosity, creativity, and learning.

Each Eliza Doll comes equipped with user-friendly technology, allowing girls to:

  • Connect Hardware: Easily plug in and attach components to their doll.
  • Experience Interactive Responses: Observe how Eliza reacts to their coding creations.
  • Learn Through Play: Enjoy a fun, educational journey that fosters creativity and technical skills.

Why Eliza Dolls Matter

Did you know that women make up only 28% of the STEM workforce in the U.S., with women of color representing less than 10%? Despite their talents, many girls lose confidence in STEM-related subjects by the time they reach middle school. Eliza Dolls are designed to address this gap, inspiring curiosity and confidence in girls from an early age.

Our Mission
We aim to bridge the gender gap in STEM by inspiring confidence and curiosity in young girls, encouraging them to pursue careers in science and engineering. Risks and challenges

While we’re confident in delivering Eliza Dolls, there are a few real challenges we’ve planned for: Manufacturing & fulfillment: Small delays or quality issues can happen when scaling from prototype to production—but we’ve lined up backup suppliers and built in buffer time. Tariffs & import costs: With the U.S. ending its “de minimis” duty-free rule on imports from countries like China, small bundles are now subject to significant tariffs—even on low-value goods To manage this, we’ll use Kickstarter’s new Tariff Manager to transparently pass actual duty costs to backers post-launch Funding shortfall: If we don’t hit our target, it may limit our ability to fund production tooling or full rewards. In that case, we’ll adapt by scaling back add-ons, adjusting tiers, or offering refunds—keeping you informed every step of the way. Bottom line: We’ve built smart buffers, tools, and contingency plans into our roadmap to ensure your support leads to a high-quality, on-time delivery of Eliza Dolls.
