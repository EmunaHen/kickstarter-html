What parts of your project will use AI generated content? Please be as specific as possible.

SkippyFit Community is primarily a human-designed mobile and web application built by our team of developers and fitness experts. AI is not used to generate creative content (e.g., visuals, designs, stories) for the campaign or platform interface. However, the application incorporates a feature called Skipper AI, which provides users with fitness-related assistance such as: answering frequently asked fitness questions, providing basic workout and diet suggestions based on user goals, interpreting user data (e.g. weight, reps, progress trends) to offer tips. All responses from Skipper AI are generated using custom, rule-based prompts and models trained to offer informational assistance, not creative works. All final fitness plans, expert insights, and user content are human-curated or provided by verified professionals.

Yes. Our AI assistance feature does not use copyrighted third-party datasets or creative works.
