Power Management & Efficiency

Laptops demand battery optimization, regulated power distribution, and USB-C PD compliance. We built a custom power management system that works reliably with the Raspberry Pi CM5 modules and any 45W PD Power Supply in the market.

The battery installed is fixed but serviceable. It is a 55Wh Rated Energy or 4800 mAh Rated Capacity Li-polymer Battery.

GPIO Access in a Laptop Form

The heart and soul of Raspberry Pi is its 40-PIN GPIO. We refused to leave it out ‚Äî and engineered a way to bring full 40-pin GPIO access into a compact laptop form, accessible via Argon ONE UP 40 PIN GPIO Module that communicates via a modified 2 USB-C side ports of the Argon ONE UP.¬†

We were able to maintain full USB 2 functionality of these ports when the GPIO Module is not connected.
