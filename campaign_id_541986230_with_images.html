Story

Womier ERA75 draws inspiration from the evolution of time and technology. Its one-piece molded case features precision-crafted patterns that flow seamlessly across all the aluminum body. The design captures the rhythm of changing eras and tech trends. From Ice and Bronze to Cyber and Doomsday, each color kit reflects a distinct chapter from the past, present, or future.

To complete the theme, we’ve also designed exclusive themed keycap sets for each era. These can be purchased separately as add-ons, giving your ERA75 a truly immersive look. 

Durable and Silky Smooth Finish
ERA75 features a high-quality aluminum case that delivers solid structure and long-lasting durability. Its fine powder-coated finish offers a rich, saturated color and a smooth texture that looks as stunning as it feels.
Diamond-Inspired Table Cut Design
Unlike the typical rounded or flat edges, the ERA75 features bold table-cut detailing, which brings geometric elegance and sharp reflections rarely seen in mechanical keyboards. From every angle, it delivers a character that truly stands out.
Personalize your board with small accents or bold touches, it’s your call.

To bring those patterns to life, ERA75 comes with DIY sticker sets designed to add color and personality to the sculpted surfaces. The stickers adhere directly to the textured areas, allowing you to highlight every detail with your choice of hues and graphics.


The grooves on the case can guide the stickers right into place—no bubbles, no stress. We chose a firmer material and included a tweezer in the box that makes applying them super easy, even if it’s your first time. It'll be fun to highlight the details you love!

ERA75 offers reliable connectivity and high-speed performance. You can seamless switch between three connection modes—wired, Bluetooth, and 2.4GHz wireless—making it the ideal keyboard for any setup. Whether you're at your desk or moving between devices, you’ll always stay connected. Each mode is easy to access through simple key combinations.

In wired mode, the ERA75 delivers low-latency performance with response times as fast as 8ms, providing a smooth, lag-free experience suitable for gaming and productivity. The polling rate increases to 1000Hz for ultra-responsive input and precise feedback.

With a powerful 8000mAh battery, the ERA75 delivers up to 350 hours of use on a single charge. Whether you're working, gaming, or creating, you can count on reliable performance without constantly reaching for a charger. More time typing, less time plugged in.

ERA75 can be programmed with VIA, the open-source configurator that lets you remap keys, set macros, and tweak lighting—right from your browser, no software installed needed.
But customization doesn't stop at software. We're committed to empowering your creativity by providing open-source firmware files for the ERA75. This means you have full access to the design blueprints, allowing you to fully modify the keyboard on your own.
The ERA75 features a compact 75% ANSI layout, providing essential keys in a space-saving design that's perfect for clean setups and efficient workflows. For users who prefer the ISO layout, optional add-on plates are also available!

Okay, a volume knob is a must-have for some—but not everyone. That’s why the ERA75 lets you decide. Plug it in when you want quick volume control, or leave it off for a clean, minimalist look. Either way, you get both style and flexibility, built right into your layout!

These double-shot keycaps are crafted from high-quality PBT and are oil-resistant, fade-proof, and built to last. The legends won’t wear off or scratch, even with heavy use. Designed with an ergonomic Cherry profile, they offer a comfortable curve that fits your fingers naturally, making them ideal for long typing sessions.

ERA75 comes with 17 stunning built-in lighting effects. You can also use VIA’s web-based configurator to customize colors, brightness, and modes in real time. Whether you prefer a calm glow or full-spectrum animation, ERA75 adapts to your vibe.

The ERA75 features a gasket-mounted structure designed to soften keystrokes and absorb vibrations. Rather than using rigid screw connections, the case is supported by gaskets, which provide added flexibility and a gentler bottom-out feel. When paired with a multi-layer damping system, pre-lubricated POM switches, and fine-tuned stabilizers, this structure provides a more refined and consistent typing experience.

We paired each keyboard with switches in a matching color scheme to ensure a perfectly coordinated, polished look. The switches are made of full POM material, so they won't wear out easily after prolonged use and provide deeper sound. The switches are pre-lubed for an enhanced typing experience. The LED diffuser design allows the RGB lighting to be distributed more evenly, enhancing the keyboard's lighting effects.
