Story

Introducing MUITAVY

Tired of charging chaos? Tangled cables, incompatible adapters, and the endless hunt for open outlets clutter both your desk and your mind. Slow charging speeds, bulky power bricks, and constant device juggling only add to the frustration. It’s time to simplify your space, speed up your workflow, and cut the cords — without compromise.

Meet MUITAVY Chargebox — the 200W all-in-one power hub that rewires your charging routine. This 4-in-1 charging station integrates multiple high-speed outputs, allowing you to power up smartphones, tablets, headphones, and other portable devices effortlessly from one sleek, organized hub. Designed for both home and office, it eliminates the need for multiple chargers, delivering fast, safe, and reliable charging while keeping your workspace clean and efficient. Say goodbye to clutter and hello to a smarter, faster, and more convenient charging experience.

MUITAVY Chargebox comes equipped with three integrated charging cables, making fast charging as simple as plug and go. It supports versatile USB-C output and includes a Lightning adapter, allowing you to easily switch between different devices without carrying extra cables. Whether you’re charging a smartphone, tablet, or wireless earbuds, Chargebox delivers reliable, high-speed power with a streamlined, hassle-free experience — designed to keep up with your dynamic, on-the-go lifestyle.

Unleash High-Power Fast GaN Charging with MUITAVY Charge Box!

Say goodbye to slow charging and cluttered outlets. MUITAVY Charge Box delivers an impressive 200W total output, making it the ultimate charging solution for all your devices — from smartphones and tablets to power-hungry laptops. A single charging cable supports up to 165W of blazing-fast power, while using multiple cables at once still provides up to 100W per port, ensuring every device gets the speed it deserves.

Even high-demand devices like MacBooks charge quickly and efficiently, minimizing downtime and maximizing productivity. Whether at home, at work, or on the move — keep your entire tech setup fully powered and ready to go with MUITAVY Charge Box, your all-in-one high-speed charging hub.

MUITAVY charger adopted GaN III generation chip technology, built-in multi-protection system and premium microchips. With higher energy efficiency and upgraded heat dissipation, Gallium Nitride (GaN) makes our chargers smaller and safer without sacrificing a drop of power. Built-in overcharge protection, overheating protection, short circuit protection, over-current protection, and over-voltage protection.

4-in-1 Convenience: Fast Charge Multiple Devices Together

The MUITAVY Charging Box is your ultimate all-in-one charging solution. Equipped with 3 USB-C output ports and 1 USB-A port, it powers up to 4 devices simultaneously — from laptops and smartphones to smartwatches and wireless headphones. No more juggling multiple chargers or fighting over outlets.

Whether you're at home, in the office, or on the move, one compact MUITAVY Charging Box effortlessly replaces a drawer full of adapters, delivering fast, reliable power for all your essential devices. Simplify your setup and stay charged anytime, anywhere.

Retractable Cable Design for Effortless Storage and Use

Chargebox comes equipped with 3 x 80cm retractable Type-C cables, each designed with a magnetic tip for effortless organization. This smart design keeps your cables tidy, accessible, and ready when you need them. Simply pull out the cable for plug-and-play use, and when you’re done, slide it back into its slot — the built-in magnetic edge snaps it neatly into place. No more messy cables or tangled cords. It’s a seamless, space-saving charging solution that keeps your workspace clean and efficient.
