Story

🧠 The idea behind S137M started with something simple — care. I designed it as a gift for my wife, so she wouldn't accidentally burn herself with overly hot tea or coffee.
But as soon as we began using it, I realized: this little device could bring comfort and safety to many more people.

The S137M is a Non-Contact beverage thermometer designed to help users easily and safely check the temperature of their drinks. With its sleek and compact design, it features a precise infrared sensor, a bright visual indicator, and sound alerts. A simple way to know when your drink is ready to enjoy — convenient, safe, and fun for both adults and children.
This project aims useful gadget to life.
The device is especially useful for coffee and tea lovers, parents, and cafes. During testing, we also discovered a beautiful cup-illumination effect, which added extra charm to the device.
We invite your feedback and support to help shape its future. Your feedback will help guide further development.

🟦 Project Summary  
The S137M is an elegant, compact device for precise, safe and hygienic measurement of the surface temperature of tea, coffee or other drinks directly in the cup or glass. Thanks to the precise infrared sensor and intuitive indicator lights, it provides instant feedback. Available in size M, it is versatile for both personal and professional use.

Risks and challenges

Risks and challenges We are currently transitioning from working prototypes to a fully developed, production-ready product. The main risks and challenges include: Manufacturing delays: Setting up reliable production (PCBA and enclosures) may take longer than expected, especially when working with new suppliers. We are mitigating this by consulting with experienced manufacturing partners both in the US and China and by planning buffer time in our schedule. Component shortages: Global supply chain issues can affect component availability. To reduce this risk, we are choosing widely available components and working with multiple suppliers. Quality assurance: Moving from prototypes to mass production requires rigorous testing to maintain the same performance and safety standards. We plan to invest in proper quality control and pilot batches before scaling up. Certification and compliance: Depending on feedback and demand, we may need to obtain safety certifications (especially for international markets). We have factored this into our timeline. As the creator, I am fully dedicated to overseeing every step of this project — from technical development to communication with suppliers and backers. This is a personal commitment, and I am prepared to put in the necessary time and resources to ensure successful delivery. We are committed to transparent communication and will provide regular updates as we work through each stage of production. Your support will help us overcome these challenges and bring the S137M to life.
