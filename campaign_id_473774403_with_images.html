📘 AI Disclosure: How Parentul Uses AI

Parentul is powered by advanced AI to provide personalized, supportive parenting guidance that feels human, empathetic, and effective, because parenting doesn’t wait for office hours.

Here’s how AI is used in Parentul:

🔍 What elements are created using AI?

Conversations with the AI assistant are generated using large language models (LLMs) like GPT-4 or similar transformer-based models.

Suggested scripts, calming techniques, co-regulation tools, and personalized parenting advice are generated dynamically in real time.

Mood tracking and behavior insights are learned over time via feedback loops to make the system smarter and more tailored with use.

🧠 What is original and what is AI-generated?

The parenting methodology, known as the 3C+ Framework, is completely original and based on founder Richard Dixson’s trauma-informed coaching experience.

All guidance is rooted in real psychological and parenting science, including Trust-Based Relational Intervention (TBRI®), trauma-informed care, and conscious parenting practices.

Visual assets, illustrations, and narrative content are created by humans. AI is employed solely to assist with text responses and conversational flows.

📊 Data and sources:

Parentul uses no public user data for training. All AI models are used under proper licensing.

Any behavioral data collected is anonymized, encrypted, and never shared or sold.

Consent is requested during onboarding, and users can opt out of data-driven personalization if they choose.

🛠️ AI Tools Used:

Generative AI: for dynamic dialogue generation, personalized advice, and parenting script suggestions

Machine Learning: for pattern recognition, outcome prediction, and user feedback learning

Natural Language Processing (NLP): for interpreting tone, emotional cues, and user intent

Our goal is simple: to offer the most accessible, private, and effective parenting support, without sacrificing humanity.

How we’ll handle it: We’re constantly feeding it real feedback from parents like you—so it gets smarter, more helpful, and more human with time. 🐞 “Tech always bugs out…” Yeah.
