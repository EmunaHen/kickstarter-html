Every BUG device will undergo thorough quality control, programming, and testing prior to shipment to ensure it meets our high standards for reliability and performance. The primary risks relate to logistics—specifically shipping and delivery. While we work closely with our fulfillment partners to ensure safe, trackable, and on-time delivery, there may be occasional issues such as delays, damage, or loss during transit. In such cases, we will provide support and seek the best possible resolution for our backers.
