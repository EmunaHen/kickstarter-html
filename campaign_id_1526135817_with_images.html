Story

💬 Introduce Your Project

What if your voice could be heard when your loved ones need it most — even after you're gone?

One Final Moment is an app that allows people to record and schedule personal video messages to be delivered after their passing. It gives everyone — from parents and partners to mentors and friends — a way to leave heartfelt messages, important wisdom, or lasting goodbyes for the moments that matter most. These messages can be delivered privately to specific loved ones, shared during memorial services through QR codes, or made public for the world to see.

Your story doesn’t have to end. Help us bring this app to life — and give people the chance to speak, connect, and heal across time.

💡 What Are You Planning to Make?

We’re building a mobile app where users can:

Upload personal videos in categorized sections like “One Final Moment,” “Advice,” or “Celebration”

Schedule those videos to be delivered at future dates or life milestones (e.g., birthdays, anniversaries)

Choose whether videos are private, shared at a memorial, or published publicly

Assign trusted contacts to release messages after their passing

Receive guided story prompts and tools to make recording meaningful and easy

Videos can be accessed through a personal notification system, private login, or scannable QR code provided at events.

🌱 Where Did This Project Come From?

The idea for One Final Moment was born after I lost both my grandfather and grandmother at the end of 2024. My grandfather was a guiding force in my life — a source of strength, wisdom, and inspiration. His passing left a silence that felt especially heavy because there were still things I wish I’d heard him say, lessons I wish he’d been able to share.

In that grief, I realized how powerful it would be to give people a way to leave behind their voice, their story, and their love — not just for closure, but for comfort, celebration, and remembrance. That’s when One Final Moment began.

Since then, I’ve incorporated the business as a C-Corp, set up banking and legal protections, secured the trademark, and completed the initial app designs with Goji Labs.

We are now ready to begin full development of the MVP (minimum viable product) — and launch the first version of the One Final Moment app towards the end of 2025.

Risks and challenges

Building an app as meaningful and technically sound as One Final Moment involves both emotional sensitivity and technical precision. As with any digital platform, there are key challenges we’re aware of — and prepared to face: 1. Technical Development Delays Creating a secure, user-friendly app that allows video storage, personalized delivery, and long-term access takes time. We're working closely with a reputable app development team, Goji Labs, who have already completed the design sprint and are currently building the app’s interface. With their expertise, we've set a clear timeline, and we’re prepared to adjust if any unforeseen delays arise without compromising quality. 2. Data Storage and Retention One of the biggest challenges is offering a sustainable way to store legacy videos for years after someone passes. To manage this, we’re designing tiered storage and prepaid options that allow users to choose how long their videos are retained. We’re also exploring partnerships with trusted cloud storage providers to ensure longevity, security, and scalability. 3. Monetization and Ongoing Costs Maintaining video infrastructure, user support, and app updates will require ongoing funding. That’s why we’ve crafted a hybrid monetization model — combining subscriptions, prepaid storage, and a free public legacy library — to generate sustainable revenue without locking anyone out of the experience. 4. Sensitive Nature of the Platform We understand that users will be sharing deeply personal, emotional messages. To ensure this is handled with care, we are prioritizing user privacy, encryption, and respectful design at every step. Our goal is to make users feel safe, seen, and heard — never exploited. 5. Fundraising Limitations While we’ve carefully budgeted the MVP development costs, unexpected expenses could arise. In that case, I (as the founder) am prepared to personally bridge funding gaps or seek additional angel investment to ensure the platform is fully realized. By acknowledging these risks, we also want to reassure backers that we’re serious, prepared, and committed to delivering One Final Moment with thoughtfulness, integrity, and transparency.

� Environmental Commitments At One Final Moment, we are committed to building a business that is not only emotionally meaningful but also environmentally responsible. While we are a digital-first platform with minimal physical output, we are taking intentional steps to reduce our environmental footprint: 1. Minimal Physical Materials Our core service is 100% digital, reducing the need for physical production, packaging, or shipping. We limit tangible rewards to only a few meaningful items, such as the keepsake hourglass, and we prioritize sustainable materials and minimal packaging for those. 2. Cloud Hosting with Green Providers As we build our infrastructure, we are actively seeking cloud hosting and storage partners who invest in renewable energy and maintain carbon-neutral or low-emission data centers. 3. Paperless Operations All company operations, communications, and documentation are conducted digitally, eliminating the use of paper and printed materials. These small but intentional steps help ensure that One Final Moment honors not only the memories of our loved ones but also the future of the planet we all share.
