Risks and challenges

At MistFlow, we’re not new to building fans — in fact, we’ve been doing it for years for major industrial and commercial clients. With a strong background in OEM and ODM manufacturing, we have extensive experience in production, quality control, and logistics. This greatly reduces the common risks many first-time creators face. That said, launching a product under our own brand, especially through Kickstarter, does come with its own set of challenges: Supply Chain Disruptions: While we have long-standing relationships with our component suppliers, unexpected global events (such as shipping delays or material shortages) could impact our timeline. We've built in buffer time and have backup suppliers to minimize this risk. Manufacturing Timeline: Even with our in-house production capabilities, scaling up from prototypes to mass production can encounter minor delays. We are committed to keeping backers informed every step of the way with honest, timely updates. Product Refinement: As we transition from our proven industrial designs to a consumer-focused product, small tweaks may be made to optimize the final user experience. All changes will be made with quality, safety, and performance in mind. International Shipping: We’ve shipped products globally for years, but customs or carrier delays can occasionally occur. We’re working with reliable logistics partners to ensure smooth delivery to as many countries as possible. We’ve built MistFlow on a foundation of experience, precision, and accountability. With your support, we’re confident we can deliver not just a product — but a premium, reliable cooling experience.
