Story

Revolutionary Four-Section Carbon Fiber One-Click Monopod, Reaching Up to 1750mm!
Its height, stability, and portability stem from cutting-edge internal features: the innovative one-click mechanism allows all four sections to extend and retract with a single press, delivering greater height for diverse environments without the hassle of extra accessories like extension poles.
After 5 years of refinement and 3 prototype iterations, we’ve nailed it with the COMAN GO!
A carbon fiber monopod that perfectly blends height, stability, and efficiency in one sleek package!

Four-Section Leg Tube
COMAN engineered an advanced one-button system, transforming the GO! series into a groundbreaking four-section monopod.

Compact Storage
The four-section leg tube design achieves a minimum storage height of 660mm, shrinking to just 512mm when the foot support is removed.

Impressive Height
With a maximum deployment height of 1750mm, it’s built for versatility.

The COMAN GO! delivers a faster, more efficient solution for photographers who value speed, enabling quick adjustments to adapt seamlessly to any shooting environment.
The Easy-Release Module Press unlocks with a single press for effortless installation. It also features a safety lock to boost efficiency and security.
With the COMAN Family Gimbal's Quick-Mount Design, setting up is a breeze—just lightly press the camera's bottom quick-release plate to complete the installation.

Experience the next level of performance with COMAN’s all-new one-click lifting handle—engineered with cutting-edge forged carbon fiber technology, the same advanced material used in high-end sports car spoilers. This breakthrough design combines ultra-high strength with impressive resistance to stretching and warping, all in an incredibly lightweight and sleek package. It’s a first in the world of monopods. Even better, it stays comfortably warm to the touch in cold weather—unlike traditional metal handles. Stronger. Lighter. Smarter.

With the COMAN GO! monopod, you'll enjoy a broader and higher field of view, reaching up to 1750mm in height. Unlike other one-click monopods, it eliminates the need for an extension rod, simplifying your setup.
You can pair the Go! monopod with a DJI to create a rocker arm mode, where the taller monopod provides a wider field of view and greater movement range. Plus, the 1/4-inch top screw at the base ensures it stays secure, so you don’t have to worry about it slipping.
The new "B-2" carbon fiber pattern features a highly extended shape, ensuring exceptional stability under heavy loads and variable extensions. Its carbon fiber structure is designed to be more compact and robust, enhancing durability and performance.
The 19cm foot support, crafted from ultra-high-strength steel, boasts a one-piece design for unparalleled durability and reliability. It’s 300% stronger in compression than aluminum alloy foot supports, effortlessly handling any load. This robust construction enables the foot tube to support up to 20kg.
The V5 gimbal supports horizontal 360 ° and pitch ± 90 ° gimbal adjustment, with smooth hydraulic damping and free mirror movement adjustment.
The V5 gimbal is compatible with 99% of Manfrotto-spec quick-release plates on the market and can be adjusted with limit switches to work seamlessly with DJI RS2-4 stabilizers.
The M1 series comes in a few different versions: the M1s, the M1, and the M1 Pro. Each one can connect to different types of gimbals. The M1s has a 3/8-inch mount, so it works directly with gimbals that have a 3/8-inch threaded hole. The standard M1 comes with a 1/4-inch screw mount and includes a 1/4-inch to 3/8-inch adapter, which means it can connect to gimbals with either a 1/4-inch or a 3/8-inch hole. Finally, the M1 Pro has a self-adapting 1/4-inch & 3/8-inch mount, allowing it to connect to both 1/4-inch and 3/8-inch gimbals without needing any extra adapter.
The V5 gimbal features two-stage, one-key foot support with pan-tilt angle adjustment. Lightly stepping on the pedal releases the foot support’s ball head angle limit, while fully pressing it switches to a free state. Pressing the pedal again returns the foot tube to its vertical position. Discover even more exciting possibilities waiting for you to explore!
The M1 series includes three models. Compared to the M1 V5, the M1S swaps the V5 hydraulic gimbal for a ball head and quick-release clamp, making it ideal for photography enthusiasts. Additionally, it replaces the innovative B-2 carbon fiber texture with the classic flagship 3K carbon fiber texture to lower costs.
The M1Pro V5 builds on the M1 V5 by adding a modular quick-release function and a detachable monopod support, expanding its versatility and efficiency. This makes the M1Pro V5 better suited for professional camera enthusiasts with diverse shooting needs.
