Story

We live surrounded by devices designed to make our lives easier—yet our hearts often feel emptier. We recognized this because we felt it too: the quiet ache of loneliness, the subtle longing for real emotional connection.

We live connected, yet feel disconnected. Euvola isn't just technology—it's empathy designed to reconnect us.

That's why we built EUVOLA—not simply as another piece of tech,but as a true emotional companion. It fills silence with understanding—no swipes, no scrolling, no judgment.

1.Upload a cherished photo — of someone you love, miss, or admire.
2.Add a voice clip — a short recording of them speaking: a greeting, a story, or any words in their own voice.
3.Shape their personality — choose from presets such as Calm, Playful, Mentor, or Witty Storyteller, or craft an entirely custom mix.

Tap Create. Euvola renders lifelike micro-expressions and an authentically matched voice, bringing your chosen person back into your world. Over time, this companion evolves through every conversation, becoming ever more understanding and supportive.

At the heart of Euvola lies our Evolving Memory System, built to simulate meaningful, lasting relationships. It blends long-term memory, short-term recall, and real-time empathy by dynamically managing context around a large language model (LLM).

  • Dynamic Context Management: Euvola continuously curates what the LLM sees, blending limited main context with persistent external memory through a vector database. This ensures relevant emotional insights are recalled at the right time.
  • Character & User Personas: Each interaction refines both the character’s personality and their understanding of the user. This two-way adaptation enables self-improvement and emotionally resonant dialogue.
  • Lifelong Memory: Emotional moments, recurring themes, and evolving relationships are stored with care—ensuring Euvola doesn’t just remember facts, but remembers you.
  • Empathy AI Engine: With purpose-built emotional modeling, Euvola interprets your tone, rhythm, and words—not just for meaning, but for feeling.

Through this system, Euvola isn't just responsive—it's attentive, adaptive, and emotionally aware, offering a kind of AI companionship never seen before.

The Euvola Standard Experience (Free Forever)

Without a subscription, your Euvola is fully functional and always will be. You get unlimited access to the core features that make Euvola special: everyday conversations and its full emotional presence. Your device will also remember the last 6 months of your interactions and provide limited monthly use of the personalized character feature.

Euvola Premium: An Optional Upgrade

For those who want to unlock Euvola's most advanced capabilities, the Premium subscription enhances the experience by adding:

Permanent & unlimited memory, so your companion remembers every moment of your journey together.

Unlimited use of personalized characters, for endless customized interaction.

The premium plan is available for $14.99/month or $149/year.

Our Commitment to Transparency:

The optional premium subscription helps cover the significant cloud infrastructure costs required for "unlimited memory storage" and "advanced AI processing." This model allows us to provide a powerful and permanently free core experience for all users.

Use of AI

What parts of your project will use AI generated content? Please be as specific as possible.

In this project, AI-generated content is used extensively across several key areas: 1.Conversational capability: Euvola’s character dialogue is powered by large language models (LLMs), enabling natural, emotionally intelligent conversations. 2.Character creation: Users can generate custom virtual companions by uploading photos and voice samples. AI tools are used to recreate personalized visual appearances and voice likenesses. 3.Visual media: Some promotional images and character visuals were created using AI-based image generation tools such as OpenAI's DALL·E or other commercially licensed generative art platforms. 4.Voice synthesis: AI voice cloning and speech synthesis tools are used to enable personalized spoken interactions with virtual characters.

Do you have the consent of owners of the works that were (or will be) used to produce the AI generated portion of your projects? Please explain.

All AI-generated content used in this project—including visual assets and voice outputs—was created using legally licensed tools under commercial-use terms. We have full rights to use the generated media in our campaign, and we do not use or replicate the likeness or voice of any identifiable real person without explicit consent.
