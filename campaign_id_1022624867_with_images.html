Story

We want Loopii to be the real deal for solving your everyday trash headaches. Here’s what some people had to say about it⬇️.

  • Food waste and leftover fruit always give off an unpleasant smell, attracting annoying little bugs.
  • Current food waste solutions either aren't environmentally friendly or don't truly meet the needs of users.

That's why we created Loopii. We hope to find an effective and eco-friendly way to recycle our leftovers, turning waste into valuable resources.

Step 1: Put in food scraps / kitchen waste.

Step2: Start Loopii with one touch.

Step3: Get clean and odorless reduced-volume material.

Step4: Use for gardening or discard.

I know you've definitely heard of food recyclers! But hold on, Loopii is truly different.

Basically, you've got two kinds of old-school food disposers. One just grinds the food up. The other type tries to dry it out with direct heat. And the problem? Well, let's just say it's... painfully slow😭.

Our goal was simple: quickly turn wet food waste from trash into treasure. So, Loopii uses a fast, efficient combo of heated air and blades to get it done. 
And, not to brag, but we're the first in the world to actually pull it off.
Each cycle uses just 0.86 kWh — or as little as 0.5 in Quick Mode. Now that's what we call clean energy.

A full cycle in Loopii only takes about 2 hours. Basically, it's all finished and waiting for your next round of scraps before you've even started cooking again.

With regular home composting, you're looking at a 3 to 6-month wait. Yeah... not exactly instant gratification. Now, that’s assuming you’re just dumping your food waste in as-is. Give it the Loopii pre-treatment first, and you'll kick things into high gear.

🪄Let’s take a look at Loopii’s shrink-down magic.

Without Loopii, you're hauling out a huge, heavy trash bag daily, hoping it doesn't break... 

But after Loopii reduces it by 90%, all that mess just becomes this small, dry pile⬇️.

So you go from daily trash runs to just one a week. You know what all that saved time gets you? Roughly enough time for a movie night every other week🎬. 

Not a bad trade-off, right?

No more late-night “ugh, I forgot the trash” moments.

Typical countertop food recycler holds about 3 to 4 liters. We did some quick math, though, and this is how much food waste an average family creates in just one meal... So, anything over that 4-liter limit still ends up in the regular trash can. Awkward.

⬆️Redesigned from the inside out — We completely reengineered Loopii’s internal structure to deliver a generous 5.3L capacity, all while maintaining the same compact footprint as a traditional 3L food recycler.

How big is 5.3L, really?

We put it to the test — Loopii can hold up to 10 Big Macs, or 5 full 12-inch pizzas, or 27 hard shell tacos in a single cycle.

We all know how bad food waste can smell—especially in the summer.

Maybe you’ve tried composting, only to give up because of the unbearable smell. With Loopii, even at high temperatures during operation, food waste doesn’t release any unpleasant odors.

Loopii tackles kitchen waste odors at the source. The end result is dry, clean, and completely free of unpleasant smells.

That’s why Loopii has a built-in dual-stage deodorizing system: 

  • The first layer — activated carbon — passively traps large odor molecules. 
  • The second — low-concentration ozone — actively breaks down the ones that remain.

One defends. One attacks.

Based on our tests — and yes, the ozone level is strictly controlled and 100% safe for both humans and pets.

Loopii’s powerful blades and non-stick coating can handle almost any kind of food — from tough chicken bones to sticky rice, no problem at all.

You no longer have to wonder, "Can I throw this in?" — Loopii’s powerful processing system handles it all with ease, transforming waste into dry, sterile, natural fertilizer.

We tested many other food waste processors on the market. Most of them rely heavily on apps — as if that somehow makes them smarter. But in reality, apps often increase the risk of malfunction and overcomplicate something that should be simple.

We say no to software obsolescence.

No data centers. No waste. Just focus on what matters — using resources wisely and doing more with less electronics.

Replacing Loopii’s filter is simple — just press it gently and it pops out automatically, just like an SD card in a camera.

Each Loopii comes with 4 complimentary filters. Based on using it 5 times a week, a single filter should last around 6 months.

Normally, you don't need to clean Loopii. But if you want to, the inner chamber is removable and dishwasher-safe for easy cleaning.

 Every detail in Loopii is designed to make things feel easier, more natural.

Watching your food waste gradually turn into nutrient-rich "black gold" — now that’s satisfying! 

Loopii’s viewing window is treated with an anti-fog coating, so no matter how hot it gets inside, you can clearly see the entire breakdown process.

We’ve optimized Loopii’s motor and grinding system to reduce noise — so it can work hard in your kitchen without disrupting your life. We even compared Loopii’s sound level to common kitchen appliances. Here’s how it stacks up ⬇️
