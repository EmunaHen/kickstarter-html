Story

🌌 Enter the Bound Realms

Across crumbling stonework and forgotten crypts, through fog-drenched forests and scorched desert dunes, a world of ancient power awakens. Buried Beasts & Broken Realms is a curated miniature collection featuring beasts, heroes, and terrain — each sculpted with bold silhouettes, compact construction, and solid bases for seamless tabletop use or stunning display. A forgotten world, sculpted in stone and story.

Build your legends. Shape your world. Bring the adventure to life.

🗿 Miniatures

These miniatures are not just playable assets — they're story anchors. Each sculpt tells a fragment of a forgotten history, a whisper of a myth, or the echo of an unfinished battle. And every piece is ready right out of the box — structurally compact, with clean lines and stable forms ideal for your collection, gameplay, or painting bench.

Mireborn Terror

✅ Ready to Print

We’ve thoroughly tested the models on popular FDM printers (like Prusa, Bambulab, Ender, and Anycubic), ensuring:

✔ Easy to print with supports
✔ One-piece OBJ
✔ No post-processing needed after support removal

🛠️ Recommended Print Settings

✔ Layer Height: max 0.2 mm
✔ Material: PLA or PETG
✔ Bed Size: 220 x 220 mm
✔ Supports: Enabled
