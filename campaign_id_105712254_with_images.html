Story

Backed by sleep science, Sleap™ is your all-in-one solution for the best sleep of your life. Unlike regular headphones or bulky earbuds, our ultra-compact Earbuds are specifically designed for side sleepers, offering all-night comfort while blocking out disruptive noise. Stream whatever helps you relax — from calming music to white noise or guided meditations. And that’s just the beginning. Sleap™ is packed with smart features to help you fall asleep faster and stay deeply asleep until morning.

Sleap™ Earbuds don’t feel like typical earbuds. Their ultra-small size and soft memory foam tip conform naturally to your ear, staying securely in place all night—even if you toss and turn or sleep on your side. Weighing just 2.7 grams, they’re so comfortable, you won’t even realize they’re there.

This earphones design, with a 10.7 mm hardware length featuring a 10 mm outer diameter tapering trapezoidally to 6 mm over 5.5 mm with 1 mm memory foam coverage, ensures a seamless and comfortable fit by conforming to the natural contours of the concha and ear canal with a shallow 4–5 mm insertion, while the initial 10 mm diameter section prevents deep insertion by anchoring securely in the concha, and the centrally placed 3 mm diameter removal plug avoids contact with the ear, enhancing overall comfort during sleep.

Unlike general-purpose earbuds, this design is purpose-built for long-term overnight wear, addressing common pain points like external pressure, ear fatigue, and dislodgment during sleep. Excellent passive noise isolation thanks to their position, working like high-quality earplugs.

Sleap™  was created to block sleep-disturbing noise without the bulk or discomfort of typical solutions. With an estimated SNR of ~27–28 dB, its deep-insertion fit rivals top earplugs like Loop Quiet. It reduces snoring and ambient noise, while still letting you hear alarms — combining real isolation with all-night comfort.

Change your sleep routine with personalised alarms and bedtime reminders.

Smart Sleep Tracking. With a built-in sleep monitor, Sleap™ analyzes your sleep stages (deep, REM, light) and delivers morning insights to help you optimize your rest. By continuously tracking your sleep behavior, the Sleap™ app helps you wake up feeling more refreshed and energized.

Sleap™ Earbuds automatically detect when you fall asleep and seamlessly transition to noise masking or silence. Equipped with the latest and world’s smallest Bosch MEMS accelerometer (BMA530) and a compact 1 mm² SciVax PPG sensor, they precisely track your sleep stages and movements for accurate sleep analysis.

Use 'Find Earphones' to make your Sleap™ Earbuds emit a sound, helping you locate them easily.

The Sonion BA driver in Sleap™ Earphones combines a compact form factor for sleep comfort with energy-efficient performance to extend battery life. It delivers exceptionally clear audio—especially vocals and treble—making it ideal for spoken word content and ambient tracks. Plus, the sealed design eliminates vent holes, significantly enhancing passive noise isolation and minimizing nighttime disruptions.

Sleap™’s sound profile features a soft bass boost, flat mids, and gentle treble roll-off — designed to relax your brain and help you fall asleep faster without ear fatigue.

Risks and challenges

We're fully committed to bringing our earphones to life and helping people experience better rest through sound. But as anyone in hardware knows — building physical products comes with its share of challenges. While surprises can happen, we’ve secured the necessary funding and assembled a trusted team across manufacturing and supply chain to handle any issues that may arise. Tooling is complete, components have been sourced and verified, and key purchase orders are already in place. While unforeseen delays in logistics or parts availability could still occur, we’ve taken great care in choosing partners with a proven track record of reliability. On the software side, we're continuing to refine the app experience and finalize firmware performance. There’s always a chance that tuning or update mechanisms — such as over-the-air firmware updates via the charging case or earphones — may require additional development time. These features are being tested in parallel and are not expected to delay initial shipments. Our engineering partners are working closely with us to validate, test, and prepare everything for production. If all goes smoothly, we’ll move swiftly into mass production and aim to deliver to our Kickstarter backers as early as possible.
