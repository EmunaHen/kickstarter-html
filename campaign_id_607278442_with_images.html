Story

Why AMANO

The AMANO S900 isn't just your commute – it's a statement piece that redefines fashion norms.

The AMANO S900 is a sleek and powerful urban e-bike designed for effortless city commuting. It features a 250W Automatic speed motor rear hub motor with 45Nm of torque, paired with a 360Wh Samsung lithium-ion battery, offering up to 100 km of range on a single charge. Its single-speed JDX carbon belt drive ensures a quiet, low-maintenance ride, while ZOOM hydraulic disc brakes and Integrated headlight provide safety and control. With a fully integrated design, TFT color display, and  lightweight Ultra-light aluminum frame Ultra-light aluminum alloy frame, the S900 blends performance and modern aesthetics perfectly.

The AMANO S900 stands out with its minimalist design and sleek, integrated frame, making it as stylish as it is functional. Available in seven modern color options, it lets riders express their personal style while turning heads on every street. Whether bold or understated, there's a perfect shade to match your urban aesthetic.

The AMANO S900 combines speed, power, and durability for a truly dependable ride. Its 250W motor with 45Nm torque delivers strong acceleration and hill-climbing capability, while the carbon belt drive and Ultra-light Lightweight aluminum alloy frame ensure long-lasting performance with minimal maintenance. Built to handle the demands of daily urban commuting, it’s engineered for both performance and resilience.

The AMANO S900 is equipped with a torque sensors, which delivers smoother and more efficient power output to the motor. This advanced technology ensures quieter operation, better responsiveness, and improved energy efficiency. It enhances the overall riding experience by making acceleration feel more natural and controlled.

The AMANO S900 features a Ultra-light aluminum frame, known for its lightweight strength and excellent durability. This high-quality material offers a smooth, responsive ride while maintaining the structural integrity needed for daily urban use. Its sleek, integrated design adds to the bike’s modern aesthetic without compromising performance.

The AMANO S900’s 3.0” TFT color screen display offers a clear, vibrant interface that keeps all your ride data easily visible at a glance. From speed and battery level to assist mode and distance, every detail is intuitively laid out. Its modern, integrated design enhances both the functionality and style of the bike.

The AMANO S900 features a single-speed JDX carbon belt drive system, delivering a smooth, quiet, and maintenance-free ride. Unlike traditional chains, the carbon belt doesn’t require lubrication and resists rust, making it ideal for daily commuting. Its simplicity and durability ensure long-lasting performance with minimal upkeep.

The AMANO S900 is equipped with Integrated headlights, meeting strict European safety standards for visibility and reliability. These high-quality lights ensure you’re seen clearly in low-light conditions, enhancing safety during nighttime or early morning rides. Integrated seamlessly into the bike’s design, they add both function and style to your daily commute.

The AMANO S900 comes with ZOOM hydraulic disc brakes, delivering strong, consistent stopping power in all weather conditions. These high-performance brakes offer smooth modulation and quick response, giving riders greater control and confidence on busy city streets. Designed for reliability and safety, they enhance the overall riding experience with minimal maintenance.

The AMANO S900 features a convenient removable battery design, allowing you to easily take the battery off for charging or direct charging anywhere, whether at home or work. This flexibility adds to the bike’s practicality, making it simple to keep your e-bike powered up without needing to bring the entire bike indoors. The high-quality Samsung lithium-ion cells ensure reliable performance and long-lasting energy storage.

The AMANO S900 stands out with its minimalist design and sleek integrated frame, which is both stylish and practical. We have added 2 more colors, and the S900 now has seven dopamine colors to choose from, allowing riders to show their personal style on every street, and every street is your catwalk. Whether you love bold colors or prefer low-key colors, there is a perfect color to match your urban fashion aesthetic. And, in order to continue to meet everyone's pursuit of personalized colors, AMANO S900 will be launched in a new color every season in the future, constantly adding freshness and unique charm to your cycling journey.
