Story

Meet the world’s smallest and lightest A4 inkjet printer — NEWYES LD0806 Portable Inkjet Printer, built for life on the move. NEWYES can provide professional-grade printing results anytime, anywhere.

The NEWYES LD0806 Portable Inkjet Printer weighs just 750g /1.65 lbs, equivalent to weighs like a book, and the sized like two stacked cans,making it the world's most compact full-format inkjet printer.

Traditional printers are bulky, tethered by cables, and stuck in one place. NEWYES changes the game. It’s not just an inkjet printer—it’s freedom to print anywhere.

Whether you need professional and clear black and white printing or vivid color documents, NEWYES portable inkjet printer can turn your ideas into reality, ensuring that every detail is clearly visible, and the ink cartridge can be easily replaced in just 3 seconds.

Printing doesn’t have to break the bank.The NEWYES Portable Inkjet Printer offers cost-efficient printing with low per-page costs—up to 500 black-and-white pages and 200 color pages per cartridge. These estimates ensure reliable performance and low per-page costs, giving you more prints for less money.

NEWYES portable inkjet printer can print seamlessly on ordinary A4 paper without the need for special or expensive printing materials. It provides you with professional output effects that thermal printers cannot match.

Enjoy the convenience of multiple connectivity options. This printer supports mobile printing from smartphones and tablets (iOS and Android), as well as laptops and computers (Windows), allowing you to print seamlessly from a variety of devices. Whatever device you use, NEWYES printers fit easily into your workflow.It also supports printing from a variety of file formats, including DOC, PDF, PPT, JPG, and PNG, ensuring you can print all your important documents with ease.

Risks and challenges

While we are incredibly excited about the NEWYES LD0806 Portable Inkjet Printer and its potential, we also want to be transparent about the challenges we may face in realizing this innovative product. Here are some of the potential risks we are aware of and the steps we are taking to mitigate them: 1. Supply Chain and Manufacturing Delays Like any product, unforeseen delays may arise during the manufacturing and procurement processes. We are working closely with trusted manufacturers and suppliers to ensure everything runs smoothly and minimize potential delays. 2. Ink Cartridge Challenge Traditional inkjet printers use long conduits to connect the ink cartridges to the print head, meaning the ink doesn't directly contact the print head. As the smallest inkjet printer in the world, our design integrates both the ink and print head into a compact cartridge, causing the ink to come into direct contact with the print head. This makes it more prone to clogging compared to larger printers. After over two years of development, the black ink cartridge is now highly stable. However, the color ink cartridge, which has three outlets for CMY colors, still presents a challenge. While the performance is good, it’s not yet perfect, and we still have time to refine it further. 3. Software Development Our app is functional but currently quite basic. We plan to add more templates to provide richer content for our users. We are committed to delivering regular software updates and will continue to improve the system based on your feedback. Our Commitment to You Challenges are an inherent part of any groundbreaking venture, but we are confident in our team’s ability to overcome them. Our commitment is to deliver a high-quality product while maintaining open and honest communication throughout the process. If any delays occur due to unforeseen circumstances, we will proactively update our backers and provide full support throughout the process. Your trust and satisfaction are our top priorities.
