Story

Umik Smart Lock is the 6-in-1 solution that combines indoor monitoring & outdoor security cameras, a built-in doorbell, an integrated handle and deadbolt, and six different unlocking methods — all in one sleek device. It’s everything your front door needs, in one smart upgrade.

An integrated doorknob and deadbolt — with a built-in camera for added security.

Introducing the 'King of Compatibility'— crafted with recycled aluminum to power a greener future! This smart lock requires no drilling, seamlessly adapting to 97% of European and US standard door types with installation in just 15 minutes. Military-grade security, long-lasting performance. A perfect fusion of eco-friendly durability and intelligent simplicity !

We offer the most comprehensive and secure suite of 6 unlocking methods—you’d be hard-pressed to find a lock this perfectly engineered on the market today.

We have optimized our locks in six key aspects compared to existing market models. No longer will you struggle with installation hassles. Traditional split-design smart locks often result in mismatched color tones between upper and lower components — a frustrating eyesore. Our seamless one-piece design eliminates such issues entirely.

Umik Smart Lock is compatible with 97% doors used in the US.  Plus, it can be easily installed within 15 minutes with just a screwdriver.

Connect to the Tuya App.
Set custom expiration times for dynamic passcodes and share them with friends whenever you need. Give your family members their own secure access. If someone enters the wrong passcode five times, you'll instantly receive a security alert on your phone.    

When a guest rings your doorbell, your phone app instantly alerts you with a live video feed of their face. Monitor your doorstep in real-time through the smart peephole and unlock the door remotely—all from your phone. Convenience meets smart security, anytime, anywhere.
Beyond Bluetooth — Full Wi-Fi connectivity for seamless control, anywhere.

Dirty hands can stop other smart locks—but not ours.  Got kids who love to play outside? My hands were covered in mud and water—it looked like a mess. But the lock? Still unlocked smoothly, no problem at all.

Whether in 122°F desert heat or -4°F polar cold, this lock operates smoothly. Its alloy casing rapidly dissipates heat to prevent jams, while multi-layer insulation blocks ice formation. Frost-resistant fingerprint scanning works instantly even with numb fingers. You enter, we handle the extremes.

Our company Umik Home Co., Ltd. is a China-based smart lock innovator that focuses on high-security, humanized smart locks for modern homes. Through rigorous quality control and cutting-edge technology, we aim to make smart home security accessible to everyone, without compromising reliability or design.
