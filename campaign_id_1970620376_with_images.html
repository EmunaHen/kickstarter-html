Cardylish is a futuristic smartphone case that can stick firmly to walls, thanks to innovative Nano-Suction technology. Nano-Suction uses countless microscopic suction cups that securely adhere to flat surfaces.

Unlike magnets, Cardylish can stick perfectly to non-metallic surfaces such as glass, mirrors, tiles, etc.

With Cardylish, enjoy seamless, hands-free experiences wherever you are. Let’s Free your hands for smarter selfies, video calls, cooking recipes, or streaming videos, exploring the endless possibilities only by your imagination.

The nano-suction adhesion lasts semi-permanently. If it starts to lose its stickiness, simply wipe off any dust or debris with a damp cloth or sheet, and the adhesion will be restored.

Cardylish utilize the unique sticking feature to attach stylish cards with a mirror backing.These cards, called "Design Cards," are available in a wide variety of designs.

With Cardylish, you can easily change your phone's style to suit your daily mood or outfit.

The mirror-backed cards let you effortlessly check your appearance or touch up your makeup anytime, without having to open your bag.

Your smartphone goes everywhere with you—make it uniquely yours. Choose your Design Card just like you choose your sneakers; match your style, match your mood.

There are two strap holes on each side of the case, allowing you to attach a strap to wear it around your neck.

The case is designed to be shock-resistant, providing peace of mind even if you accidentally drop your smartphone.

The case features a heat-dissipating design that’s gentle on your smartphone.

The case supports wireless charging, making it easy to power up your device.
