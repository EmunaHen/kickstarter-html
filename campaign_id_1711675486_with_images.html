At its core, Popur S7 runs on the Split Litter System. Think of SLS like a mini-split AC—one part handles the job inside, the other takes care of the rest outside. In an SLS, the convertible litter tray manages your cat's business; the exterior waste bin collects it separately. The system directs waste through a fully exposed channel, keeping the interior clean and cats comfy. Built with purpose, Popur S7 SLS is inherently hygienic, frictionlessly refreshable, naturally durable, and utterly safe.

Tilt, launch, smooth.

Gently, precisely, dynamically.

Tilt, whoosh, ahhhh! Popur self-cleaning cat litter box scoops like your neat-freak alter ego—only with robotic swagger. It cradles waste away, flings every last crumb, and the final smooth swirls fresh comfort into place before your cat can say "me-wow."

Popur S7 robo-litter cleans with purpose, not guesswork. Its 3-step process begins with a full 90° tilt, using gravity and litter to wrap and isolate waste. A precision sifter then launches clumps, and even runny poops, into the outer bin through a wide, open channel—minimizing friction and maximizing flow. Clean litter is reintroduced and finally leveled into a smooth, flat bed. The result? Few leftovers, less dust, and no sticky clumps—just a fresh start every time.

Rotary drums, by contrast, spin trash on long, awkward paths and hope it drops through a narrow hole—often leaving behind gunky stains. Worse, it all trails inside the same cramped chamber your cat's fur will brush against. Popur S7 solves this with a lateral dumping motion that's faster, cleaner, and fully external. And while spherical drums form uneven layers—particularly shallow at the edges—Popur's cuboid tray maintains up to 4 inches of depth across the surface. That means better coverage, stronger clumps, and far less mess.

Popur S7 seals trash in an enclosed bin—completely split from the litter area. The liner fits inside-out, so vapor never even touches the lid. Double- or triple-bagging hack reinforces the airtight barrier, trapping odor at the source before it has a chance to rise. Up top, the fully open architecture maximizes ventilation: smells dissipate fast, fresh air rushes in.

Rotary drums don't just hold poop; they live with it. Waste sits in the same stale air your cat breathes. Filters try. Fragrances lie. But the stench always fouls. Truth be told, heat, humidity, and time only brew a stronger stink stew—while no known deodorizing technique has the capacity to neutralize exponential pollution in such a confined form. Popur's split design, purposefully upgradable, allows its dedicated bin to evolve independently—future-ready for auto-bagging and self-flushing.

Popur S7 keeps its brain far away from the filth. The motor stays dry. The circuits stay clean. Its split design locks bacteria and ammonia out, permanently. Every component is modular, handily replaceable, and built to last. The system is fully self-serviceable and even self-refurbishable at one's will—not because it's likely to fail, but because we've made longevity a feature rather than a hope.

Rotary drums instead place everything directly in the chemical zone, feeding a steady dose of corrosive gases. It's not a matter of if—just when a teeny diode or resistor fails. No matter how "sealed" the housing claims to be, vapor always finds a way in: degrading sensors, oxidizing wires, and corroding metals over time. We've seen it firsthand, across every brand. And ask around: even robo-litter engineers admit they don't fully understand. But Popur was built differently—engineered for uptime, not excuses.

Popur S7 proactively preempts, prevents, and reduces maintenance. Waste slips through a fully exposed, gravity-powered channel that is effortlessly wipe-clean. Nothing hidden. Nothing festering. Our patented brim design precisely guides urine flow, completely preventing leaks. When necessary, stretch the 25-pound litter fill and squeeze the deep-pocket bin for 35+ days per cat—even allowing safe overload in mischief. Surface wipe-downs, detachable tray rinses, snap-in modules without tools, and seamless trash bag swaps are simple routines, never chores.

Rotary drums do exactly the opposite. Tunnels hide deep inside: tight, damp, and hard to reach. They become dust traps, clump magnets, and five-star bug hotels where nastiness thrives. You spent $500 on a robo-litter to save five minutes a day scooping, but what's the point if it costs you hours every month on deep cleaning? Popur ensures hassle-free upkeep is a reality, not just a promise.

Popur S7 gives cats literally what they need: a large, deep, cuboid tray designed specifically for real feline movement—digging, turning, stretching—naturally. Measuring a generous 24 inches diagonally, the litter tray provides 100% unobstructed, three-dimensional space. Nothing overhead. Nothing in the way. Technically endless room. The litter layer ranges from 1" to 4" thick, offering a softer, paw-pleasing experience. Shaped like a traditional pan, it's a cat turf your feline instinctively loves and respects their natural moves.

Rotary drums, on the other hand, shrink usable space and bulk up the unit to accommodate a built-in receptacle. Compromise is an understatement. It's like forcing your cat to visit a closet that already hosts a trash can. Tight corners, angled sides, choking overheads, and suffocating walls—cats feel constrained and stressed, leading to awkward postures and frequent misses. At Popur, there is no excuse for not bringing it—so here's a bathroom your cat genuinely enjoys revisiting. When your furkids linger comfortably, you'll know it's the perfect fit.

Popur S7 is categorically, unconditionally, and absolutely safe—200% by design. Yes, 200%. Not a mathematical calculation, but a rhetorical conviction: moving, off, jammed, glitched—even if completely broken—the machine can never harm your cat. The litter tray remains fully open during use, nothing spins under paws, and it stays reliably elevated. Under no circumstances will pinches or entrapments occur. Bin hood opens frictionlessly from within. Even the sifter is featherlight, gently pushed aside by a kitten's paw. Forget "failsafe"—it simply doesn't fail.

Rotary drums rely on opening and shutting doors to transport waste—an inherent loophole. To distract from this issue, manufacturers emphasize sensors, sensors, and more sensors. In this robo-litter sensor arms race illusion, they imply that more sensors equal more safety. Yet, even in 2025, wheels still crush, birds still crash, mechanists still shrug. Humans err; cats rebel. Sensors misfire, timing slips, paws get caught. That's not guarantee. That's gambling.

But a paradigm shift starts from today: Popur S7 SLS completely eradicates these mechanical risks. When we say "rest assured", we mean every character.

Risks and challenges

This isn't our first rodeo. In 2022, we delivered our first product—the Popur X5—within two months of campaign close, fulfilling all pledges in under five months. In early 2023, we identified a major design flaw and an unfortunate connectivity bug. Rather than dodge the issue, we owned it: proactively communicating with the community, shipping a software-update dongle to every user, and providing a redesigned waste bin—all at our own expense. That's our commitment: everyone deserves a product they're happy with. We've learnt from that experience. So, for the Popur S7, it wasn't revealed until it passed rigorous long-term testing and was fully vetted for mass production. After shipping the X5 globally for three years, we've also become seasoned in international logistics. Truth be told, the only meaningful risk lies in the unknown: potential reciprocal tariffs in a second Trump administration. Since our manufacturing hub is in Shenzhen, China, it's impossible to predict what U.S. import tariffs will look like after the current 90-day pause. If new duties are imposed and become unmanageably high, we may need to delay shipments for U.S. backers until a viable solution is found.

Environmental commitments

Visit our Environmental Resources Center to learn how Kickstarter encourages sustainable practices.

Long-lasting design

Popur S7's Split Litter System keeps all sensitive components—motors, wiring, electronics—safely shielded from waste and vapor. The modular design allows for tool-free access, swappable parts, and long-life electronics rated for tens of thousands of cycles. Every component is individually replaceable, so your unit can be renewed, not retired.

Reusability and recyclability

Every litter-contact surface is fully removable and washable for frustration-free upkeep. We actively refurbish pre-owned units to give them a second life with new purrents—and regularly donate Popur boxes to over a dozen shelters and 501(c) nonprofits. Supporting caretakers is part of our mission, and we'll continue to do our part to give back to the community.

Sustainable materials

The main body is made from recyclable ABS plastic, which is more durable and longer-lasting than standard PP. The metal waste bin resists corrosion and odor retention. Popur S7 is compatible with biodegradable, flushable litter, and its dual-layer trash bags minimize odor and reduce plastic waste. We steer clear of toxic coatings and mixed-materials that complicate recycling.

Environmentally friendly factories

We work only with manufacturing partners who meet high environmental standards and enforce responsible waste and emissions management. We favor suppliers that reduce virgin material use and ensure clean, safe working environments.

Sustainable Distribution

Popur S7 ships in a compact, check-in-sized box—over 50% smaller in volume than most automatic litter boxes. Smaller packaging means fewer emissions during freight. We prioritize sea or ground transport whenever possible and are actively exploring carbon offset strategies for the rest.
