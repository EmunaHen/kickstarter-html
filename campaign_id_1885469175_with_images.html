Story

K1 Pro combines classic elegance with advanced technology. Made from premium lightweight materials, it delivers a natural, comfortable fit for all-day wear. Whether in meetings, commuting, or relaxing, K1 Pro offers style and comfort without compromise.

Made with advanced lightweight materials, K1 Pro offers an almost invisible feel. No nose pinching. No ear pressure. Just freedom — whether you're working, walking, or working out.

No buttons. No distractions. Just smart, seamless control at your fingertips and on your voice.  K1 Pro is designed for instinctive interaction — whether you're capturing a moment or summoning your AI assistant, everything responds the way it should: naturally.

Moments move fast — and so can you. One gentle tap is all it takes to capture what you see, exactly as you see it. No friction. Just flow.

K1 Pro isn’t just smart — it’s brilliantly intuitive. Featuring a voice-controlled AI assistant, seamless multilingual translation, and deep integration with leading AI platforms, it’s designed to support you effortlessly, anytime, anywhere.

Need to scan a doc or take notes? Just speak.  With voice-activated control and gesture-based capture, K1 Pro replaces your apps — and frees your hands.

From meetings to meetups, K1 Pro keeps track of your plans with smart reminders and voice-triggered scheduling — so you're always on time.

Fully compatible with OpenAI, Azure, Google Cloud, and Meta platforms, K1 Pro delivers a more powerful, personalized AI experience through the latest in cloud intelligence.

Risks and challenges

The Kanaan K1-Pro project faces potential risks, including delays in production or shipping after Kickstarter. To mitigate this, we will keep backers updated regularly and ensure efficient logistics to meet delivery timelines. As a new product, performance issues might arise, but we are committed to thorough testing and a responsive feedback system in the app to address concerns quickly. Unforeseen manufacturing or shipping costs are also a possibility, but we have set aside a contingency fund and will keep backers informed about any necessary adjustments, especially regarding returns and refunds. We also recognize the risk of lower-than-expected demand, but plan to leverage influencers and media to boost visibility and create an engaged early community. While returns may take time, we’ve outlined a clear process and will provide dedicated support for any issues. With these plans in place, we’re confident in meeting the project’s goals.

Long-lasting design

The Kanaan K1-Pro is engineered for long-lasting performance, featuring a robust construction designed to withstand daily wear and tear. Its high-quality materials and precise craftsmanship ensure resilience against external impacts, making it suitable for both urban environments and outdoor activities. The glasses are built with durability in mind, offering a reliable user experience over time, even in challenging conditions like high usage or extreme sports.

Sustainable materials

The Kanaan K1-Pro incorporates sustainable materials throughout its design. The frames are made with durable, eco-friendly plastics that are both lightweight and strong, ensuring longevity. Additionally, the lenses can be easily swapped for prescription or specialty lenses, promoting reuse and minimizing waste. The product's overall design encourages a circular economy approach by reducing material consumption and increasing product lifespan.

Environmentally friendly factories

The Kanaan K1-Pro integrates eco-conscious design principles, focusing on reducing environmental impact. By using recyclable materials and minimizing waste during manufacturing, it promotes sustainability while maintaining high performance. Its energy-efficient features, like fast charging and low power consumption, help minimize the carbon footprint.
