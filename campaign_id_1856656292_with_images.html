Story

In our daily lives, we often spend a significant amount of time sitting on various chairs, be it office chairs, car seats, or sofas at home. However, have you noticed that, more often than not, these seats don't truly conform to the curve of our backs? Sitting in a suspended position for long periods can put excessive pressure on the spine.

To address these issues, we spent months meticulously designing and continuously optimizing to introduce the GLOM Smart Heated Seat Cushion, dedicated to creating a better sitting experience for you.

GLOM Smart Heated Seat Cushion improves sitting comfort by using smart sensors to automatically detect the position of your lower back and adjust the support accordingly, gently cradling your back. The graphene component heats up quickly, providing soothing warmth to your lower back. With its ergonomic design, the cushion ensures long-lasting comfort, while the soft custom Philips fabric adds an extra touch of coziness.

Equipped with pressure-sensitive air cells, GLOM activates with a simple manual switch. Once turned on, it instantly detects your seating position and auto-inflates to cradle your lower back—filling the gap between your body and the chair, no matter if you’re leaning forward at your desk, reclining in the car, or shifting left/right. The support adjusts in real time with every move, ensuring a perfect fit from the moment you activate it.

GLOM heated seat cushion features an advanced graphene heating module with excellent heating performance. It has two temperature settings available: a high setting of up to 122°F and a low setting of 104°F. Once the heating is turned on, it quickly warms up your seat, making you feel as if you're in a warm little nest, shielded from the cold.

GLOM heated seat cushion is filled with high - density space memory foam. When you sit down, it slowly and accurately molds according to the pressure from your body. Whether you're sitting straight or changing your sitting position, it provides the right support for your hips and thighs. When you stand up and the pressure is removed, it quickly returns to its original shape due to its excellent material properties. Even with long - term and frequent use, it never loses its shape, maintaining stable support. Every time you sit, you can enjoy the same comfort as the first time, and long - term sitting is effortless.

The front of the heated seat cushion is made of high - quality fabric customized by Philips. It has a fine texture, is soft and skin - friendly, providing an excellent tactile experience. Meanwhile, it has outstanding breathability, so you won't feel stuffy even after long - term use. Additionally, this fabric has waterproof and dust - proof functions, so stains and dust from daily use are not easy to adhere to it, facilitating cleaning and maintenance, and keeping it clean and fresh at all times.

The bottom of the heated seat cushion is designed with anti-slip silicone particles that increase friction, keeping it firmly in place even as you adjust your sitting position.

It is equipped with a convenient Type - C interface, which means you can easily charge it. Whether you connect it to a charger indoors or use a power bank outdoors, you can recharge the cushion anytime, anywhere, ensuring that warmth and comfort are never interrupted.

The design of this heated seat cushion takes full account of portability. It's lightweight and easy to carry. From the office chair to the car driver's seat, and then to the park bench, no matter where you are, it can accompany you, creating a warm and comfortable small space for you.
