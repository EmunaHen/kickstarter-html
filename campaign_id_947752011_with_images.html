iCharguard is the core hardware module designed to address the challenges of safe power supply and battery protection for older Phones and Pads during prolonged use.
With the second-generation iCharguard, we’ve made significant improvements—essentially a complete redesign—making it more powerful and versatile.

How iCharguard Works：

--For devices that support real-time battery monitoring via Bluetooth: iCharguard stops charging when the battery is fully charged and resumes when the battery level drops below a certain threshold.

--For devices that cannot provide real-time battery data via Bluetooth: iCharguard uses machine learning to analyze the charging voltage and current patterns of each device. This allows it to dynamically adjust the charging process, ensuring a balance between power supply and energy consumption.
This design prevents overcharging and helps maximize the battery lifespan of older devices, even during prolonged power supply scenarios.
The Ambient LED Strip connects to iCharguard and is fully controlled by it. iCharguard adjusts the color changes of the LED strip by receiving color information from the screen.

Risks and challenges

With the experience and lessons learned from delivering the first-generation iFramix, we are confident in the delivery of the second-generation iFramix. However, if we must discuss risks and challenges, we believe there are two main points: 1. We are confident in delivering all Phone and Pad frames by July. However, for the software component, considering the need to adapt to five different solutions for various older devices, we estimate that 90% of the functionality will be delivered by the end of July. The remaining 10% will be implemented in subsequent iterations as quickly as possible. 2. Risks related to logistics and tariffs caused by trade protectionism. Our backers come from more than 30 countries, and under the current global environment, the trend of trade protectionism and increasing tariffs is a challenge we cannot ignore. While this is beyond our control, we will do our best to select appropriate shipping channels to help backers avoid tariffs or minimize them as much as possible.
