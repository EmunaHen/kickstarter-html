Story

Fear of falling holds so many people back from fully living. Every slight tip of a walker, every uneven floor, every awkward transfer — they all chip away at independence and confidence.

The GEORGE Feedback System was created to change that. More than a walker add-on, it is a vigilant companion that watches over every step, gently guiding safer habits through real-time auditory and visual feedback.

At the heart of GEORGE are precision-engineered distance switches integrated discreetly into the front legs. Calibrated to detect even the smallest lifts (as little as 30 mm), GEORGE acts instantly when misuse is detected — helping the user correct posture and movement before a small mistake turns into a dangerous fall.

This mission started with my grandfather, who inspired me to create a way for him to stay safe, independent, and proud. Today, GEORGE stands ready to help countless others — parents, grandparents, patients, and loved ones — move forward without fear.

When you back this campaign, you’re not just funding a product. You’re empowering a movement dedicated to restoring dignity, freedom, and confidence to the people who matter most. Together, we can make every step safer and every day a little brighter.

Why This Matters

Every year, millions of older adults experience falls — leading to injuries, hospital stays, and devastating loss of independence. As a licensed Physical Therapist Assistant, I’ve seen these stories unfold not only in my patients, but at home with my own family.

My grandfather, Edward George, lived with Parkinson’s and dementia. Like so many, he used a front-wheeled walker (FWW) without proper training, and small missteps — especially tipping backward — led to dangerous falls.

Watching him struggle, knowing I couldn’t always be there, inspired me to create something that could. The GEORGE Feedback System was born to stand in when a therapist or caregiver isn’t present — protecting independence, preserving dignity, and keeping loved ones safe.

Our Solution

The GEORGE Feedback System transforms a standard front-wheeled walker into an intelligent safety companion. Integrated precision distance switches detect subtle tipping or lifting of the walker’s front legs (as little as 30 mm), instantly triggering clear visual and auditory alerts.

These real-time cues guide the user to correct unsafe habits before they become dangerous — reinforcing safe movement and building lasting confidence.

GEORGE isn’t just a walker upgrade; it’s a vigilant coach that empowers safer mobility, even when no one else is around.

Why It Works

Falls often happen during transitional moments — like sit-to-stand transfers — rather than while simply walking. Many older adults instinctively tilt or lift their walkers backward for leverage, a major cause of falls that clinicians constantly struggle to correct.

The GEORGE system intervenes exactly at these critical moments. Developed and tested through a National Science Foundation Phase I STTR grant, GEORGE delivers immediate feedback that helps interrupt unsafe movement and build safer habits.

In early trials:

  • Users self-corrected risky movements mid-transfer after hearing or seeing alerts.
  • Therapists described GEORGE as a “coach attached to the walker” — providing reinforcement exactly when and where it’s needed.
  • Biomechanical analysis showed improved postural control and more stable, intentional movement.
  • Users reported higher confidence and a renewed sense of safety when walking alone.

Validated by clinical feedback and real-world use, the GEORGE system is now lightweight, easy to operate, and refined through direct user input — proving both its feasibility and its life-changing potential.

Risks and challenges

Risks and Challenges Section 1. Regulatory and Compliance Assurance: Risk: The process of meeting stringent FDA regulations and standards for medical devices can be complex and demanding. Mitigation Strategy: We are partnering with a manufacturing company that is already registered with the FDA and has extensive experience in producing medical devices across all class ranges. This partnership ensures that the GEORGE device will be manufactured to the highest standards and will comply with all regulatory requirements, leveraging the manufacturer’s established processes and expertise. 2. Manufacturing and Quality Control: Risk: Maintaining high manufacturing standards and quality control can be challenging, especially when scaling production. Mitigation Strategy: By collaborating with an experienced manufacturer, we gain access to proven production capabilities and quality assurance processes. This relationship not only speeds up the manufacturing timeline but also minimizes the risk of defects and ensures consistent product quality. 3. Market Acceptance and Adoption: Risk: New medical technologies often face hurdles in market acceptance, especially from healthcare providers and patients who are accustomed to traditional treatment methods. Mitigation Strategy: Our go-to-market strategy includes comprehensive education and demonstration programs to showcase the unique benefits and reliable performance of the GEORGE device. We will conduct clinical trials and publish the results to build trust and support from the medical community. 4. Financial Sustainability: Risk: Securing enough capital to support production, marketing, and distribution is a common challenge for new medical devices. Mitigation Strategy: In addition to Kickstarter funding, we are exploring various funding sources, including venture capital, grants, and strategic partnerships. Our collaboration with an established manufacturer also allows for more predictable budgeting and financial planning. The refined device was produced during a National Science Foundation Phase I STTR Grant, also received an Illinois Innovation Vouchers Program Grant, and a Illinois Match Grant (pending). Everything has been utilized for R&D at this point. 5. Technical and Operational Challenges: Risk: Integrating new technology with existing manufacturing processes can present technical and operational challenges. Mitigation Strategy: We are working closely with our manufacturing partner to ensure that the integration of our technology into their processes is seamless. Regular testing and pilot runs will help us identify and address any issues early in the process.

Use of AI

Use of AI The development and refinement of the GEORGE device do not directly involve AI technologies. However, we utilize advanced engineering and design tools such as CAD software to optimize the device's functionality and ensure precision in manufacturing. While these tools incorporate computational algorithms, the creative and problem-solving aspects of this project remain entirely human-driven. Our focus is on delivering a product that enhances safety and independence for its users, and we believe that human creativity, expertise, and empathy are at the heart of this innovation.
