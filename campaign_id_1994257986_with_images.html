Introduction – Unlock the Full Potential of Your Prusa Mini

The Prusa Mini is an amazing 3D printer, but its build volume is limited to just 180mm in height. What if you could double that without buying a new printer?
Introducing the Prusa Maxi – Z-Axis Upgrade Kit, a simple and effective way to increase your Z-height from 180mm to 360mm while keeping the same compact footprint! No firmware modifications required, and installation takes just a few minutes.

📈Why Upgrade?

Most large objects only has one large side/axis — there is no need for a large foodprint. With Prusa Maxi, you can print taller parts without increasing the printer’s footprint.

✅ Double the Z-height – Print up to 360mm tall objects
✅ Keep your Prusa Mini compact – Same X and Y dimensions
✅ Most parts have one large side - Print them with your Prusa Maxi
✅ Cost effective - Start printing big - Without the price for a large 3D printer
✅ Stop choosing between a good and a large printer - Make your good printer large!
✅ Easy installation – Upgrade in minutes
✅ No firmware changes needed – Works with stock settings
✅ Video installation guide included
✅ Bring your Prusa Mini and Mini+ back to life - With new large-scale applications

🖨️What about Print Quality?

Some of you will be wondering whether the print quality will suffer due to the extended z-axis. The answer is simple: no. 
We have had the Prusa Maxi printing large parts for months in continuous operation. From thin sleeves to wide figures. The print quality has always been just as good as before the upgrade.
In fact, we even build larger versions than 360mm to see if the quality would suffer but it did not.
Of course you can always use a z axis support bracket as for your standard Prusa Mini if you need to get more stiffness to the z axis. You can find a lot of prints on Printables.

Have a look at some of the test prints we did and get a feeling for the quality!

🤓How It Works – Simple & Effective

The Prusa Maxi Upgrade Kit replaces your existing Z-axis components with longer, high-quality parts, allowing for double the print height.

💡 No firmware modifications are needed—your printer works as usual but with more space - To be precise, you double your total print volume.

🔧Installation – Upgrade in Minutes!

The Prusa Maxi Kit is designed for quick and easy installation:

🔧 Step 1: Remove the original Z-axis components
🔧 Step 2: Attach the new z axis components, plug in the extension cables
🔧 Step 3: Start printing – no firmware changes needed!

We provide a detailed video guide, making installation a breeze even for beginners.

If you want to convert your printer back to 180mm you can simply swap back the original components. No permanent modifications are required.
