Story


Hi, I'm Nathanial, the founder of Hyperfix Nation — and like many of you, my home was starting to look more like a graveyard of hobbies than a place to live.

One night, while sorting through a pile of half-completed projects — that resin dice kit I swore I'd finish, the clay tools I used once, the paints I forgot I owned — I had a thought:

“What if there was a way to swap these abandoned hobbies with someone else instead of throwing them out?”

And just like that, Hyperfix Nation was born.

I’d dabbled in web design before, but nothing like this. So I did what many of us with ADHD do — I hyperfocused.

I spent night after night, sometimes into the early hours of the morning, teaching myself everything I needed to know. All while working full-time and living on coffee, Red Bull and very little sleep, I slowly built the first version of the Hyperfix Nation website.

After 3 intense months, the site was in beta — a place where ADHDers and hobby hoppers like me could swap, share, or sell their abandoned creative pursuits. And people got it.

The idea started to spread organically across social media — and then something amazing happened.

Daniel Jones, a leading ADHD influencer and creator of The Aspie World, discovered Hyperfix Nation and shared it with his community.

Within days, followers began pouring in. The comments, the DMs, the support — it all confirmed what I already felt in my gut:

This isn’t just my experience. It’s ours.

Now, one year later, the mission is bigger than ever.

We want to take Hyperfix Nation from a website to a fully featured mobile app, so you can:

📱 Swap hobbies on the go

🧵 Find others in your area with the same hyperfixation

🎯 Track your hobby graveyard

🧠 Learn something new, and maybe even sell your creations

But I can’t do it alone.

By backing this Kickstarter, you're not just helping build an app — you're joining a movement. One that embraces the beautiful chaos of ADHD. One that values creativity, connection, and second chances for forgotten hobbies.

Every dollar helps turn Hyperfix Nation into a mobile experience that fits right in your pocket — built by someone who gets it, for people who live it.

Whether you’re here to swap your abandoned candle-making supplies, find your next dopamine fix, or just feel seen — you’re in the right place.

Risks and challenges

Okay, real talk — I’m not a tech wizard. I’m just one person with ADHD and a big idea who taught himself how to build a website by Googling things at 2am. I’ve never built a full mobile app before, and while the vision is clear, turning that vision into a polished app has its challenges. But I believe in learning as I go — and being open with you about what could go wrong, and how I plan to make it right. There are a lot of moving parts: coding, testing, design, bugs, app store approval... things can take longer than expected or cost more than planned. I will work with a leading app development company who share the same vision for Hyperfix Nation as I do. I'm not backed by a team of 50 or funded by a startup accelerator. It's just me. This campaign will help me bring in the professional big guns. This budget includes being able to hire the right team so I can focus on building this incredible community and leading the vision. I'm a very impatient person and delays is something that I don't want to happen. Transparency is key. If somethings taking longer, you will be notified. Delays do happen but you'll always be in the know. I don’t have all the answers, and I’m not pretending to. But I do have: The determination to see this through The support of an amazing, growing community A clear plan (with flexibility baked in) A love for this weird, wonderful idea Your backing helps bring Hyperfix Nation to life — and I promise to treat it with care, humor, and lots of late-night coffee-fueled energy.
