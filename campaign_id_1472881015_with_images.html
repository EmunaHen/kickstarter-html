SmartSnack Systems LLC –Our Story

Imagine a world where convenience isn’t tied to a building — it’s mobile, intelligent, and designed around your needs.

Hi, I’m Justin Spencer, founder of SmartSnack Systems LLC.

The idea for SmartSnack Systems was born from a simple realization:
Vending machines haven't evolved fast enough for today’s fast-moving, connected world.

While traditional vending machines sit still, outdated, and often empty, we imagined something different —
a vending experience that learns, adapts, and travels wherever people are.

Using the latest in AI technology, solar innovation, and mobile design, SmartSnack Systems creates a new type of vending:

Vending that thinks — tracking customer favorites and keeping products stocked smartly.

Vending that travels — popping up at festivals, campuses, events, beaches, and busy hotspots.

Vending that connects — using contactless payments, live updates, and eco-friendly energy.


We believe the future of convenience is on wheels, powered by intelligence, and built for real life.

With your support on Kickstarter, we will launch our first SmartSnack Rover:
A sleek mobile unit ready to roll out, stocked with crowd-favorite snacks, refreshing drinks, and essential everyday tech accessories.

But this is just the beginning.

Together, we can change what vending means — making it smarter, more sustainable, and more accessible everywhere life happens.

Thank you for helping us build the future,
one SmartSnack at a time.

Risks and challenges

At SmartSnack Systems LLC, we believe innovation always comes with challenges. Here are the key risks we’ve identified and how we plan to manage them: 1. Supply Chain Delays: Parts for the AI vending unit, mobile trailer, or payment systems could experience shipping delays. → Solution: We've already sourced multiple suppliers and partners to ensure redundancy. 2. Tech Integration: Integrating AI software, inventory systems, and payment options can be complex. → Solution: We're working with experienced tech consultants who specialize in vending and mobile commerce integration. 3. Licensing and Permits: Each city or event may require unique permits for vending operations. → Solution: We have a legal advisor helping us proactively secure permits and navigate local regulations before deployment. 4. Machine Maintenance and Service: Mobile vending machines experience road wear and mechanical needs. → Solution: We'll implement a preventative maintenance plan and keep spare parts ready to avoid downtime. 5. Scaling Beyond the First Unit: Rapid success might create pressure to expand before we're ready. → Solution: Our initial focus is on one fully-functional SmartSnack Rover, carefully tested and refined, before scaling up.

Reusability and recyclability

At SmartSnack Systems LLC, we believe innovation should be good for people and the planet. That’s why we're making environmental responsibility a core part of our mission: Eco-Friendly Mobility Our SmartSnack Rovers are designed to operate with solar panel assist systems where possible, reducing reliance on fossil fuels. Mobile units are compact and lightweight, minimizing fuel consumption during transport. Energy-Efficient Machines We use low-energy, high-efficiency vending units that reduce electrical demand compared to traditional machines. Smart inventory systems minimize unnecessary cooling cycles and lighting when machines are not in use. Reducing Waste Our AI learning system helps stock products smarter, reducing unsold goods that would otherwise go to waste. We prioritize stocking eco-friendly packaging options — such as biodegradable or recyclable snack packaging — whenever possible. Sustainable Materials Trailers and vending units will incorporate recycled or responsibly sourced materials during builds where feasible. Recycling and Disposal Our machines will include integrated recycling bins at certain event sites to encourage proper waste disposal. > Our goal: To not only revolutionize vending with smart technology but also set a new standard for sustainability in mobile retail.
