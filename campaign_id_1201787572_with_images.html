Story

Introducing CarCine, the world’s first portable cinema set designed specifically for cars with panoramic roofs. It includes a smart projector and an auto-unfolding screen that work seamlessly together to turn any car into a private movie theater in seconds.

CarCine is more than just a screen or a projector. It’s about turning ordinary moments into something unforgettable. You lean back, hit play, and suddenly your car feels like your own little getaway. Whether you're watching solo or sharing it with someone, it's that effortless, cozy kind of experience that just feels right. Because the best nights aren’t always at home. Sometimes they’re right where you’re parked.

Take a look at CarCine in real life. No need to imagine it. You can see it in action.
Watch the screen unfold, the projector power up, and your content play directly from your phone. You’ll also see how it performs on the road, staying steady and smooth throughout the ride.

CarCine comes with a 32-inch self-unfolding screen made from high-contrast fabric for clear, vivid viewing any time of day. A smooth lift system ensures stability, while two heavy-duty suction cups keep it firmly in place on your car’s roof.

The CarCine Screen folds and unfolds with just the press of a button. It runs on a high-efficiency built-in battery that gives you up to 200 full open and close cycles on a single charge. For most people, that means you only need to recharge it once every five to six months.

The CarCine Projector was built with two main goals in mind: 1) to be compact and stay out of the driver’s way; 2) to deliver a bright, high-quality image.

And it was engineered with precision. Combining an lightweight design, crisp 2K resolution, and 700 lumens of brightness, it delivers the best in-car cinematic experience.

The CarCine projector uses a DLP projection system paired with an ultra-short throw lens (0.6:1 ratio), allowing it to project a large image from a short distance. The lens features an aspherical, multi-coated 5-layer glass design that delivers sharp visuals with reduced glare. 

A high-performance digital signal processor (DSP) automatically handles focus. An advanced heat management system keeps the projector running at peak performance, even during longer viewing sessions.

Sound is seamlessly integrated into the projector, engineered to deliver rich, balanced audio throughout your car’s interior. Effortlessly control sound levels with the CarCine mobile app. For a truly elevated audio experience, you can also pair the projector with your car’s sound system via Bluetooth.

CarCine is expertly designed to fit any vehicle with a panoramic roof, regardless of make or model. Its flexible and universal design allows for a precise fit whether your car features a fixed glass roof, such as the Tesla Model 3, or a sliding panoramic sunroof like the Toyota RAV4.

Thanks to the universal suction cups and simple connections, setting up the CarCine system in your car takes just 60 seconds. Just follow these two easy steps.

CarCine is designed with a focus on maximum stability: its precision-engineered mounting system and reinforced structure ensure secure fixation.

After countless tests, design tweaks, and pushing the limits with weight checks on panoramic roofs and simulations of tough road vibrations, we perfected CarCine to deliver unmatched stability and performance.

The motorized screen runs on a long-lasting battery and can be controlled through the CarCine app. When it’s time to recharge, simply use the included USB-C cable.

The CarCine projector draws power directly from your car either through the 12V power outlet in the rear cargo area or the USB-C output on the rear center control.

Take your in-car entertainment to the next level with the CarCine Mobile App for Android and iOS. With just a few taps, you can roll up the screen, adjust the brightness, and customize the setup to fit your ride perfectly. Total control, all from your phone.

Turn every drive into an entertainment experience with CarCine. Whether it’s the latest series, a thrilling game, or your favorite vlog, CarCine brings your content to life on the go. 

The CarCine screen case has built-in LED lights that can be used as extra interior lighting. They turn on automatically with a sensor or can be controlled through the app. The projector also has soft LED lighting that adds to the cinema-like atmosphere.

Everything you need for the full CarCine experience comes right in the box. You’ll get the screen, the projector, a secure mount, and all the power cables and adapters. Just set it up, sit back, and enjoy your own cinema on wheels.

We’re now in small-batch production and will move into mass production in July. Shipping is set to begin in October, right after the crowdfunding campaign ends.

We’ve officially filed a provisional patent application with the USPTO to protect the core innovation behind CarCine. This is just one of the steps we’re taking to bring CarCine to life the right way.
