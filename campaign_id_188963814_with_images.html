Story

Wing45 reimagines what a mechanical keyboard can be. This 40% columnar-stagger, split design combines true wireless freedom with deep customizability. Hot-swappable switches, premium PBT keycaps, programmable rotary encoders, and open-source ZMK firmware let you craft the exact setup you need—whether you’re coding, creating, or gaming.

Experience comfort like never before from using a keyboard. The Wing45 optimizes comfort and minimizes fatigue with 3 incredible design elements: Split, Column Stagger Layout, Tenting Pad.

Split Keyboard

Splitting the keyboard promotes natural wrist alignment. Instead of forcing your wrist to bend towards your pinky, your forearem is aligned with your hands creating a naturally comfortable position.
The split feature also allows you to position each half at shoulder-width or wikder, preventing you from hunching in or reaching across your body. This greatly reduces shoulder and neck strain. 

Column Stagger

In a column stagger keyboard layout, each column of keys aligns naturally with you fingers, cutting lateral stretch and reducing wrist deviation., To put it simply, your fingers will bend in a more natural way so you can type for hours without feeling tired.

Tenting Pads

With the tenting pad, you can rotate each half of the keyboard so your palms face slightly infward, mimicking a handshake and decreasing forearm strain. This funny position actually lets you type in a more natural way, increasing comfrot and decreasing the chances of developig carpal tunnel syndrome. 

Swap encoders between halves, then twist to control volume, backlight brightness, scrolling speed, or custom macros. Haptic feedback options make every turn crisp and intuitive. Each half offeres two different positions for encoder placement, so you can customize based on your preferences. 

The traditional spacebar is a wastes space and efficiency of your dexterous thumbs. With Wing45's 8 quick access thumb function keys, you can boost productivity and unleash the power of your thumbs. Dedicate these to shortcuts like app switching, media control, or custom text snippets. Combine them with ZMK’s layering system to unlock virtually infinite macro possibilities.

Open any web browser and start configuring on-the-fly. Drag-and-drop key maps, adjust encoder behaviors, and update firmware wirelessly—no complex toolchains or command-line steps required.

Plug in the detachable USB-C cable for true zero-latency performance. The keyboard automatically switches to wired mode without extra toggles, ensuring uninterrupted play.

At just 45 keys, Wing45 packs all essential functions into a desk-saving footprint. Tap into three onboard layers to access numbers, symbols, and navigation without shrinking your workspace. 

Although Wing45 comes with a choice of Kailh box (red, white, or brown) switches and a full set of PBT keycaps, we made it hot-swappable, meaning you can customize it with switches and keycaps of your choosing. Clicky, creamy, or silent, you have the freedom to make Wing45 truly your own keyboard. Just remember to use MX switches and keycaps. 

Dial in pure white or full-color RGB effects to suit any environment. Multiple presets and reactive modes let you create a subtle glow for late-night work or a dynamic light show for streaming.

The minimalist housing showcases your switches and underglow, balancing modern flair with clean lines. Choose natural or black tinted options to match your desk aesthetic.

LIR2450 technology delivers up to seven days of typical use on a single charge. USB-C fast charging gets you back to full power in under 30 minutes —perfect for mobile workflows.

At only 222g per half, Wing45 tucks easily into backpacks or laptop sleeves. Quickly reconnect to your MacBook, iPad, or PC and maintain your muscle memory anywhere.

Seamless integration with iOS and iPadOS apps makes Wing45 ideal for video editing, photo retouching, or note taking on the go. Dedicated modifier layers mimic desktop shortcuts for a native experience.

A few years ago, we combined our two passions of bricks and keyboards and tried to launch a fully functional bricks keyboard. Unfortunately, we were not ready for the task and the project was not completed. Since then, we have continued to test different designs, layouts and switches, and the result is the Wing45, a keyboard that can deliver top-tier comfort without sacrificing functionality.
