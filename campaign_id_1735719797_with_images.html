Welcome to GaragX – The Creative Playground for Tech Lovers

Imagine a place where you don’t just dream up ideas—you build them.

Welcome to GaragX: a physical space made by creators, for creators. A tech playground where curious minds and passionate makers come together to learn, build, and innovate.

Whether you're into 3D printing, electronics, product design, or media editing, GaragX is your launchpad. From total beginners to seasoned engineers, everyone’s welcome here. If you can imagine it, we’ll help you make it real.

🔧 What is GaragX?

GaragX is a network of next-gen creative labs—physical hubs loaded with tools, courses, and expert guidance. Think of it as a gym for inventors and tech artists. You come in with an idea and walk out with something you made with your own hands.

Our first location? Calella, Barcelona – and we’re just getting started.

🛠️ We’ve Already Started Building

This isn’t just an idea on paper—GaragX is already coming to life.

We’ve begun construction on our very first location in Calella, Barcelona. Walls are going up, tools are coming in, and the energy is real. But here's the truth: building something this ambitious takes serious resources. And while we’ve made solid progress, we need your help to finish what we started.

With your support, we’ll complete the space, fill it with top-tier tools, and launch a full schedule of workshops and courses for creators like you.

💡 Why GaragX?

Because the world needs more creators, innovators, and tinkerers. Because access to tech and knowledge shouldn’t be locked behind high costs or exclusive circles. Because building awesome stuff is way more fun when you do it together. And because is never too late or too early to start learning

We offer:

💥 Hands-on workshops and courses in 3D printing, design, electronics, media editing, and more.
🧰 Full access to high-end tools and resources for creators.
👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 A growing community of makers, tech lovers, and visionaries.
🔄 Flexible subscription plans to fit your needs and pace.
🌍 The Big Goal

To grow a global network of GaragX spaces—accessible creative zones in every corner of the world. We’re building not just spaces, but a movement. A society of thinkers, builders, and doers who want to create the future, together.

Risks and challenges

⚠️ Potential Risks and Challenges Launching GaragX is an ambitious project—and like any new venture, it comes with challenges. One of the main challenges we face is the regulatory environment. Spain has one of the most complex and demanding sets of laws and regulations for businesses in Europe. This makes the process of opening, certifying, and operating a creative tech space slower and more expensive than in other countries. However, this is not a weakness—it's our strength. If we can successfully launch GaragX here, in one of the toughest environments for new business growth, we are confident we can replicate and scale the model anywhere in the world with greater speed and efficiency. Other potential risks include: Construction delays due to permits or supply issues Higher-than-expected costs for regulatory compliance and equipment Initial community growth may take time as we build awareness and trust in a brand-new concept How we are addressing these risks: We have already started building and securing key equipment to stay ahead of delays. We are working with local experts to navigate regulations efficiently. We have a strategic marketing and outreach plan to accelerate community growth from day one. At GaragX, we believe that big dreams come with big challenges—and overcoming them makes us even stronger. We are fully committed to transparency, adaptability, and smart, sustainable growth.

Reusability and recyclability

♻️ Turning Waste into Wisdom: GaragX’s Commitment to Sustainable Learning At GaragX, technology isn’t just something you use. it’s something you understand, repair, and reinvent. That’s why we proudly integrate used and damaged devices into our learning process. Instead of letting valuable materials go to waste, we transform them into powerful educational tools. Our members learn how technology works from the inside out, gaining hands-on skills in diagnosis, repair, upgrading, and creative reuse. This approach not only builds real-world tech skills—it fosters a culture of sustainability and innovation, where every device gets a second life and every learner becomes a creator. By teaching recycling through action, GaragX is shaping a smarter, greener, and more resourceful generation of tech leaders.
