Story

Introducing Vipera, The Electric Ski. 

Designed to revolutionize your winter adventures. Born from a passion for skiing and cutting-edge engineering, The Vipera empowers you to explore uncharted terrain with unparalleled freedom and sustainability. 

Bring Freedom With You

For those who want to move efficiently through snow-covered terrain, options are limited. They're either slow and time-consuming, or loud and overbuilt. 

Cross-country skiing will always have its place, but it often lacks the speed, range, or excitement many outdoor enthusiasts crave. On the other end of the spectrum, the idea that you need a cumbersome snowmobile to enjoy nature has always seemed counterintuitive. 

Our mission is to take away anything standing between you and the outdoors, whether it be conditions, ability, means, or gear.

Frigid Dynamics is built on an uncompromising dedication to quality and a desire to innovate with agility, freedom, and confidence. Our team consists of people from all over the world, each bringing their unique perspectives and expertise to realize our shared dream of free movement in extreme conditions.

Frigid Dynamics is delivering freedom and adventure with revolutionary engineering. We build sleek, zero-emission, lightweight snow transportation technologies so that you can navigate the wild with innovation, not intrusion.

Efficient Winter Transportation

Design Excellence

Our skis feature a sleek, crystalline design that combines aesthetics with functionality. The removable drive train offers flexibility, enabling effortless transitions between powered and traditional skiing. 

Advanced Engineering

Crafted with aerospace-grade 7000 series aluminum and thermally-hardened stainless steel, The Vipera is built to withstand extreme conditions. Integrated suspension systems ensure smooth rides over diverse terrains, while state-of-the-art, water-resistant lithium-ion batteries provide up to 10 miles (16km) of range on a single charge.

Built on Advanced Fluid Dynamics

User-Friendly Controls: Vipera Controller

Navigate slopes with ease using our wireless controller, seamlessly integrated into the ski pole. This intuitive system allows you to control speed, braking, and monitor battery levels, ensuring a safe and enjoyable experience for skiers of all skill levels.

Included with Every Purchase of The Vipera

Subzero Speed, Electrified

With instant torque and precision control, The Vipera is built to carve through all terrain. Lightweight and modular, Vipera redefines backcountry mobility without sacrificing speed or thrill. Say goodbye to limits and hello to freedom.

Our Athletes

What are we using the Kickstarter funding for?

Frigid Dynamics currently operates a dedicated manufacturing facility, custom-built by our team to produce Vipera systems. In addition, we maintain our headquarters in Boston, Massachusetts, supported by a highly capable and experienced team.

Funds raised through Kickstarter will be allocated toward testing, quality assurance, raw materials, components, labor, machinery, engineering, and other essential expenses necessary to manufacture and deliver final products.

The Kickstarter funding will NOT be spent on bonuses for our executives.  Our goal with this Kickstarter is to build and provide reliable electric skis to users who want or need them.

Risks and challenges

We are redefining winter exploration with sustainable innovation. Our electric skis eliminate the noise and air pollution associated with fossil fuels, preserving the serenity of the outdoors while reducing our environmental footprint.
