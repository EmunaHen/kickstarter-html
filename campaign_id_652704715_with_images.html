Story

Introducing MIDILLI MIDI Router: A MIDI Hub and Filter!

After our first project was fully funded in less than three weeks, we couldn't wait to build more. Now we're back, and this time, it's all about MIDI routing done right.

Meet the smartest way to take control of your MIDI world. The MIDILLI MIDI Router lets you route messages exactly where you want and in any direction you want — between USB, Bluetooth, DIN and TRS (Type A & B) — with per-interface and per-message type filtering that gives you total creative freedom. Compact, powerful, and ultra-efficient, it’s designed for both stage and studio. Only 106 × 55 × 33 mm (4.17 × 2.17 × 1.3 inches) and 115 grams (4.06 oz).

Say goodbye to messy cables and rigid signal chains — and hello to the most flexible MIDI brain you've ever owned.

📦 Product Highlights

MIDILLI MIDI Router is a plug-and-play and versatile tool that works across many platforms and has various connection options for MIDI routing. It's particularly useful for musicians or producers who need a flexible setup for connecting multiple MIDI devices.

We built the MIDILLI MIDI Router from the ground up—hand-programmed and focused on the requirements that true music makers crave. In a world where off-the-shelf products often miss the mark, we've engineered a routing, filtering, and customization system that fills the gaps for musicians, producers, and DJs.

Every feature is a response to your needs: seamless integration across Bluetooth, USB, DIN, and TRS interfaces, a smart matrix routing system, and message filtering to ensure that every note, beat, and control signal arrives just as you intend.

Imagine having the flexibility to create complex signal chains with just a few simple clicks, tailoring every connection to suit your specific performance or studio setup. Our selective routing isn't just about connectivity. It’s about giving you full control over your musical expression. Elevate your live shows, streamline your studio sessions, and push the boundaries of what’s possible with MIDI — all on your terms.

Selective MIDI Routing

Introducing our advanced Selective MIDI Routing—a game-changer designed with musicians, producers, and live performers in mind. With MIDILLI, you enjoy a versatile suite of interfaces including USB In/Out, MIDI In/Out, and Bluetooth In/Out, all seamlessly integrated into one powerful device.

Using our intuitive MIDI routing matrix (or simply by clicking on the interfaces in our Configurator software) you can effortlessly enable or disable signal paths. This means you have complete control over which signals from any input are routed to your desired outputs. Whether you're splitting, merging, or isolating signals, our system lets you craft the exact connectivity your creative workflow demands.

More Than a Simple MIDI Router

Beyond MIDI routing capabilities, MIDILLI MIDI Router also introduces powerful message filtering.

Decide exactly which types of MIDI messages each output should receive. From Clock, Start, and Continue signals to Control Changes, Program Changes, Note On/Off, Pitch Bend, Aftertouch, and even System Exclusive messages — you're in control. No more unwanted data clogging your setup. Just pure, purposeful MIDI.

Don't want to send Clock signals to Bluetooth Out? Just disable it.

✨ Key Features

Multiple MIDI Interfaces:

  • 4x MIDI Out (2x MIDI DIN-5, 1x TRS 3.5mm Type-A, 1x TRS 3.5mm Type-B) 🎹
  • 1x MIDI In DIN 5 🎶
  • 2x MIDI Thru DIN 5 🔄
  • 1x USB MIDI (In and Out) 💻
  • 1x Bluetooth MIDI (In and Out) 📱

MIDI Routing:

  • Forward incoming MIDI messages between MIDI Out, USB MIDI, and Bluetooth MIDI 
  • Enable or disable interfaces for forwarding 
  • Select which message types (e.g., Note, Clock, SysEx) to forward.

Plug & Play:

  • No drivers required! Works out of the box with macOS, Windows, Linux, Android, and iOS*
  • Powered via USB-C with low power consumption.

*iOS: Bluetooth MIDI or with devices with USB-C connector.
