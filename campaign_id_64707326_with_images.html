English Version

Do you sometimes face a mountain of ideas, goals, and tasks and don't know where to start? Do you feel that grand visions seem out of reach? Or are you looking for smart support but find yourself lost in endless AI chats that don't help you exactly where you need it?

I know that feeling all too well. It's frustrating when your potential is blocked by unclear ideas or a lack of focus. Many tools I tried were too rigid, too complicated, or offered only superficial help. This led to my motivation quickly fading, and my goals falling by the wayside.

This is exactly where Journelty comes in. I've developed an app that not only helps you define your dreams but also actively guides you in turning them into tangible successes. Journelty is your smart companion for every step on your journey to personal growth.

Imagine: You have a personal coach who is always there for you when you need them – right where you're putting your ideas into action.

I developed Journelty to enable you to grow beyond yourself and consciously track your progress – like a modern success journal for the future.

Set Goals Right, Achieve Them Intelligently:

AI Feedback on Goals: Start with a clear foundation. My AI gives you instant, precise feedback on your goals even before you begin. Set the right course for your success from the start.

Sub-Goals & Tasks: Break down your grand visions into small, manageable steps. Assign sub-goals and concrete tasks to each goal. This way, every mountain becomes a walkable path.

AI Support – Directly in Your Workflow:

AI Chat in Tasks: No more lengthy searches in separate chats! Do you need a tip for a task or a goal? My AI coach is available directly within the task/goal and provides you with instant, relevant information, strategies, or next steps without interrupting your workflow. Maximum efficiency, minimal distraction.

Reflect & Celebrate Progress:

Diverse Reflections: Capture your thoughts, successes, and learning experiences – whether by text, voice memo, or even with a picture.

Visualize Your Journey: See at a glance how far you've come, recognize patterns, and celebrate your achievements. This keeps you motivated and strengthens your resolve to persevere.

I'm Alex, and I work in the financial sector. From this world, I bring an appreciation for structure, precision, and efficiency. However, I, too, repeatedly faced the challenge of consistently pursuing my personal goals and grand dreams, and despite great efforts, some still seemed unattainable.
