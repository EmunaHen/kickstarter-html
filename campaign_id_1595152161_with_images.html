Story

Meet QROV Ultra Pro—the world’s first dashcam with Q-Track™ technology: track with real-time satellite GPS and Apple & Google Find My network, all packed with next-level, intelligent features built for 2025. It’s a game-changer.

When something goes wrong on the road—or even when your car’s parked—you don’t get a second chance to capture the moment. 

That’s why Qrov Ultra Pro doesn’t just record—it protects. Here’s how it safeguards what matters most.

Car thefts and break-ins are fast. Qrov reacts faster. The moment it detects impact, it sends a live phone alert with GPS pin, 20 seconds video, and incident report. 

You can instantly livestream from front, cabin, and rear cameras or share it with police. It’s more than security—it’s real-time response when seconds count.

Qrov Ultra Pro isn’t just smart—it’s a game-changer. The only dashcam on the planet that taps into Apple and Google’s Find My networks, giving you unmatched tracking power.
If your car is stolen, you’ll know exactly where it is—within seconds.

Parked cars are still vulnerable. Qrov’s AI-Powered Parking Mode is your 24/7 digital guard. Time-Lapse Mode records in compressed format so nothing gets missed. 

Eco Standby conserves battery and wakes instantly if impact is detected—recording and alerting you on the spot. At home or away, Qrov’s always on.

Standard dashcams miss key moments. Qrov covers 510°— 360° via front and cabin cameras, plus wide rear coverage.

 Powered by Sony Starvis and 5K HDR, your footage stays crisp—even at night or in shadows. Whether it's a minor scrape or road rage, you’ll have court-ready proof from every angle.

If you’re in a crash and can’t respond, Qrov’s SOS feature steps in. After detecting a serious impact, it gives you 60 seconds to confirm you're okay. 

If there’s no response after an impact, a trigger message will appear  and with one tap, you can send your live GPS location and crash footage to your emergency contact.
It’s more than just an alert , it’s a built-in witness that acts fast, even when you can’t.

Access live or saved footage anytime remotely via the Qrov app—no need to be near car. It also auto-logs routes, speed, and timestamps. 

In case of theft or disputes, you’ll have GPS-backed proof. It’s more than video—it’s your car’s digital memory and legal backup, always just a tap away.

Letting a loved one drive alone can be nerve-wracking. With Qrov, you stay connected without hovering. Track their location, speed, and surroundings in real time. 

Whether it’s your teen’s first drive or a family trip across town, you’ll know they’re safe—and they’ll know you care. It’s peace of mind powered by technology.

Smarter Than Ever—Because 2025 Demands It. Say goodbye to outdated dashcams. QROV is the future on your windshield. There’s more to QROV than just safety and clarity—it’s also designed to make your everyday driving experience smarter, smoother, and more in your control.

Qrov Ultra Pro supports OBD-II installation, making setup fast, clean, and completely DIY. No tools, no technician—just plug it into your vehicle’s OBD port, and you’re ready to go. 

It’s a simple, 2-minute install that saves you the time, effort, and cost of professional wiring.

Sometimes you need privacy—and QROV gets it. With one tap, the cabin camera and mic shut off instantly. No Cabin video, no audio—just your space, your rules. Whether it’s a quiet drive or a private moment, your car stays your zone.

Powered by a quad-core 64-bit Arm Cortex-A53, and advanced image signal processing, QROV captures crisp, vivid footage in any light. With 5GHz Wi-Fi and a fast 4-second boot-up, you're always ready the moment you start the engine.

Specially designed portable mount which is exceptional secure and stable, even on rough roads or off-road terrain—it simply doesn’t fall off. 

It’s also easily removable, letting you switch between vehicles in seconds—perfect for rideshare drivers or families sharing multiple cars.

QROV supports GPS with advance low-noise amplifier with high signal gain for stronger signal reception and faster, more accurate positioning. Enjoy real-time location tracking with reliable and faster precision—no matter where you drive.

QROV dashcam is thermally engineered for all-weather durability. It features an internal metal heat sink and air-ventilated outer casing to maintain optimal temperature. 

Plus, its built-in supercapacitor ensures stable performance from -20°C to +80°C, making it reliable in both freezing winters and scorching summers.

QROV Ultra Pro combines advanced technology with a sleek, minimal design that’s built around real driver feedback. 

It installs neatly under the rearview mirror, ensuring no obstruction to your view and avoiding any legal or safety concerns. No bulk, no clutter—just powerful tech that seamlessly fits your drive.

QROV Ultra Pro comes with a built-in 4G LTE module, so there’s no need to buy an external 4G module accessory - saving you money and avoiding bulky setups.

It supports global LTE bands right out of the box, delivering seamless connectivity in a compact, all-in-one design.

Important Notes: SIM Cards & 4G Service
It’s easy—just plug in your own SIM card with your preferred plan. There are no restrictions, and QROV ULTRA PRO works globally with any SIM. However, to unlock 4G features like Real-time Event Notifications, Live View, SOS Notifications, Witness Mode, Live GPS location Tracking, there is  a small 4G service fee of $4.95/month

If you choose not to pay the service fee, all non-4G features will remain available, so you can still enjoy the core functionalities of QROV without the 4G features.

Forget cables and card readers—QROV lets you download unlimited footage wirelessly via Wi-Fi or preview instantly over 4G. With the intuitive QROV app on iOS or Android, you get full control in seconds—live view, playback, alerts, and settings. 

No hassle. No confusion. Just seamless access, anytime, anywhere.
