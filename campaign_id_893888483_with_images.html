Story

Twelvetooo 3.0 Firefly Laser Projector is a multifunctional bionic firefly laser lights projector that can create the most realistic firefly light effect. It redefines ambient lighting with two precision-engineered editions:

Home Edition: Perfect for creating enchanting atmospheres in gardens, living rooms, or festive gatherings.

Pro Edition: Designed for professional stage performances, weddings, and large-scale events.

It supports 7-color RGB lighting, which can also dance with the rhythm of music to create a unique light show for you. Twelvetooo has three control methods: APP, remote control, and DMX-512-controlled,so you can choose the appropriate control method according to different scenarios.

Twelvetooo creates a romantic and interesting atmosphere for your surroundings. Suitable for various parties such as Halloween, Christmas, weddings, etc., for your garden, living room, bedroom decoration.

Twelvetooo uses semiconductor laser spectral diffuse reflection and fluorescent ceramic conversion technology to restore the scene of 100,000 fireflies flying freely. Through multiple optical channels, it presents the floating, static, breathing and stroboscopic light effects of fireflies, creating a beautiful scene of real fireflies chasing each other.

You can select white laser through the APP or remote control to create a snowflake atmosphere with white snowflakes flying all over the sky and a white starry sky.

Twelvetooo supports 7-color (blue, orange, yellow, white, purple, red, green) of light cycle display and movement. You can choose the appropriate light color and mode according to the festival or scene.

When festive music plays, you can turn on the voice control mode through the remote control or APP, and the lights will flash with the rhythm of the music, allowing you to enjoy the wonderful singing atmosphere.

The brightness of the light projected by Twelvetooo is 40% higher than that of the current products on the market, making the projected light effects clearer and the visual effects cooler.

Twelvetooo supports APP control, remote control and DMX-512 control. You can choose the appropriate control method according to the distance from the projector.  

Twelvetooo's self-developed APP "Firefly" connects to devices via Bluetooth or WIFI. Exclusively for Twelvetooo (Home Edition).You can remotely control the light color, lighting mode, brightness, and movement speed in the APP. You can control Twelvetooo at a distance of up to 40m.

It supports power on/off operations, as well as the control of light color, lighting mode, brightness, and movement speed. Additionally, it allows you to set a timer for automatic shutdown at 1, 4, or 6 hours. This feature is applicable to both editions.

When Twelvetooo is turned on and the light is on for 6 hours, it will automatically shut down. After shutting down, it will restart in a cycle for 18 hours.

Twelvetooo uses 5W power industrial-grade white laser projection technology, which has a longer service life and a longer projection distance. The optimal projection distance is 5-10 meters (50-100 square meters), and the farthest projection distance can reach 20 meters (150 square meters).

Twelvetooo's one-piece die-cast body is impact-resistant, extrusion-resistant, high-strength and durable, that supports IP44 outdoor waterproofing. It can be safely used in gardens or other outdoor decorations to create a wonderful atmosphere for your parties and festivals.
