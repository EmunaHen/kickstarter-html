Story

INTRODUCING TWINA.                                                                 

The human-centered AI avatar that cares.

Twina is an emotionally intelligent, AI-supported wellness companion — carefully designed to guide users through mood-based experiences, emotional exploration, and daily reflection. But Twina isn’t just software. She’s a character, a creative experience, and a reflection of human empathy, built with care, design, and original storytelling.



This project is born from human creativity — every detail from Twina’s emotional interactions to the visual identity, upgrade options, and shop structure is custom-built. AI is used only to support mood recognition and dynamic responses. All expressions, guidance, and user experiences are handcrafted and guided by real people.



Twina helps users:
• Reflect on their moods and emotional states
• Explore self-awareness and daily wellness
• Interact with a creative character that evolves with them
• Personalize their experience through digital upgrades, avatar expansions, and guided emotional tools



There is no medical claim or diagnostic intent. This is not therapy or treatment — it is a creative tool for connection and expression. We believe emotional wellness deserves thoughtful design, and Twina represents the future of human-AI collaboration where the heart of the experience remains fully human.



Your support will help us launch the MVP (minimum viable product) and bring Twina to life on iOS, with core features ready and a clear roadmap for continued development. We’re here to create something real, beautiful, and shareable — together.

Join us and bring Twina to life.




WHY TWINA MATTERS

We live in a time of constant noise, pressure, and disconnection. Twina is here to change that.

It listens. It responds. It remembers what matters to you. Whether you’re planning your week, expressing your feelings, building creative projects, or just need a moment of grounding — Twina shows up.

Twina is designed for people of all ages — from kids to adults to older adults — each with their own unique avatar experience. It adapts to your mood, supports your emotional state, and even helps you grow.

Every character, interaction, and storyline has been designed by human creators — with AI tools carefully guided to enhance, not replace, our creative process.

How AI is Used in Twina

Twina uses AI for voice generation and personalized mood-based responses — but every element is human-led and designed to nurture connection, creativity, and care.

We use OpenAI’s language model for light emotional dialogue support, guided by original prompts written by the Twina team.

All avatars, scripts, stories, interactions, and design choices are developed by our human creators.

Our database sources are fully licensed, and no copyrighted data is scraped or resold.
