Story

Do you often wake up with snoring?

The night should be a peaceful place for rest and relaxation, but the continuous snoring beside the pillow is like an invisible "noise barrier", repeatedly shattering the expectation of falling asleep. Want to turn over and remind someone, but afraid of disturbing them? Enduring until early morning without any drowsiness?

The Sleepmi Z4 Snore Stopper understands the helplessness of being trapped by snoring, and its thoughtful design helps you block interference and relieve discomfort, allowing you to break free from the exhaustion dominated by snoring every night and return to a peaceful rest.

How does he work?

Sleepmi is an intelligent device built on artificial intelligence technology, specifically designed to improve sleep problems. Unlike common products on the market, it adopts a more accurate and reliable method for snoring detection - not through voice recognition (which is prone to misjudgment due to environmental noise), but by capturing snoring frequency and combining multi-dimensional data such as body movement and sleep detection, through comprehensive algorithms to accurately determine snoring conditions.

The product can continuously monitor the breathing curve, analyze breathing changes, and combine algorithms such as blood oxygen and heart rate to comprehensively determine breathing pauses. When identifying breathing pauses, different intensities of pulses will be used to intervene in breathing pauses, adjust breathing rhythm, and assist users in restoring normal breathing rhythm.

Say goodbye to single voice judgment, and use frequency capture, body movement monitoring, and sleep state algorithm to intelligently perceive sleep dynamics, making assistance more in line with needs.

Covering multidimensional information such as breathing curves, blood oxygen, heart rate, etc., combined with sleep data analysis on the APP, it intuitively presents sleep status and helps understand sleep conditions.
