Story

Introduction

Have you ever wondered who is around you? Nearfy is a creative, community-driven mobile app that helps people reconnect with those nearby in a safe, intuitive, and respectful way. With Nearfy, you'll rediscover the human side of technology, where every notification could lead to a real-world smile.

What is Nearfy?

Nearfy is a real-time social radar. It shows you who’s near, what they’re sharing, and allows you to interact based on common interests — all while giving you full control over your privacy.

It’s not just a map. It’s a tool to turn proximity into opportunity.

The Core Features:

Live Radar Map: See users around you in real time (anonymized until interaction begins).

Geolocated Profiles: Share a short status, a photo, and links to your favorite social platforms.

Privacy First: Go visible, invisible, or use Stealth Mode for temporary invisibility.

No Ads, No Exploitation: Nearfy doesn’t use intrusive ads or sell your identity.

Risks and challenges

Risks and Challenges We know building a social app is no small feat. Here’s how we’ll handle the challenges: Technical Complexity: We’ve budgeted properly for scalable servers and experienced developers. Privacy & Security: Full GDPR compliance and encrypted infrastructure. User Growth: We’ve prepared a community-focused marketing plan for early traction. Funding Gap: If full funding isn’t reached, we’ll release a limited MVP version with core features only. Competition: We focus on proximity and simplicity — not on endless feeds and ads. Some parts of this project make use of AI tools to support creative development and communication. Images and mockups were generated or enhanced using AI tools like Midjourney and DALL·E, based on original concepts created by the Nearfy team. Text drafts and structuring were assisted using OpenAI’s ChatGPT, always under human supervision and review. No sensitive or personal data is used or shared with AI tools. The app itself will be entirely coded and built by human developers, and no automated AI systems will run within the core user experience. Final content, branding, and UI/UX design will be produced manually to ensure full originality and compliance. We believe in using AI responsibly as a support tool — never as a substitute for human creativity or ethical development.
