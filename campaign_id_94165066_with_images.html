ADQWU system information

ADQWU's NAS cloud space page

Files can be transferred between mobile phones and computers, and all files of the other party can be viewed. You can also operate computer files on your mobile phone, such as uploading, copying and pasting, deleting files, viewing pictures, and playing movies. Computers can also operate files on mobile phones.

After uploading a picture on your phone to the NAS cloud space, it will be automatically stored in the computer. The phone, computer, and "ADQWU" cloud space can save this picture or file at the same time, so you never have to worry about losing files.

Currently, this app only offers Chinese and Japanese interfaces. We are developing an English interface. We will eventually set up the APP to support changing the language interface.
