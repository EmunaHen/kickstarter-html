Introducing Voltix 

Voltix isn’t just a charger — it’s your desktop’s new power command center. With a world-first live OLED wattage monitor, Voltix lets you see what’s charging, how fast, and where the power’s going — in real time. No more guessing. No more undercharging.

Whether you're a power user, a tech minimalist, or someone who just needs to know the numbers, Voltix is the charger that finally keeps up.

Watch your devices negotiate for power like siblings fighting for bandwidth. See exact wattage per port (e.g., “MacBook Pro: 100W | iPhone: 18W | Drone: 45W”).
