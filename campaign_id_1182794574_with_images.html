Story

Inspired by lights in nature, this smart sterilizing lamp safely purifies your everyday space with a single light source.

It features harmless sterilization using 405nm visible light, artificial sunlight for emotional relief, and a daylight mode that repels mosquitoes.

The delivery box you touched before washing your hands, the soles of your shoes, even the bed you quietly lie in—bacteria spread throughout your home without you even realizing it.

PURIBAR blocks these invisible paths of contamination and makes your space safe again—using only safe light, with no chemicals

Move around freely, stay light on hygiene—PURIBAR follows you wherever your day takes you.Weighing just 400g, its design sports a slim size that fits right into your bag.Magnetic mounting and USB-C compatibility boost practicality and make installation a breeze.

Three lighting modes provide optimized illumination for a variety of spaces and situations.

Each mode is designed for specific functions—sterilization, emotional relief, and mood lighting—so you can care for both your hygiene and your well-being in your daily life.
