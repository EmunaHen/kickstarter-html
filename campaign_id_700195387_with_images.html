Story

Relying on manual note-taking means constantly splitting your focus between listening and writing. Note-taking apps and AI voice recorders don’t fix that; they often make it worse. 

With low-quality microphones that fail to capture human voices clearly, especially in noisy environments, transcripts end up full of gaps, missed words, and misinterpreted sentences. And when the transcription is flawed, the AI summaries are too, leaving you with incomplete, low-quality content you can’t fully trust.

Flowtica Scribe makes it better.

Powered by AI, it records what’s being said, lets you mark important moments with a tap, and turns it all into clear, structured notes.

And yes — it’s a real pen too.
You can keep writing while Flowtica Scribe records the conversation and summarizes in the background.

Flowtica Scribe recognizes who’s speaking and labels voices automatically, keeping everything organized and easy to review.

It’s like a second brain for your meetings, lectures, and ideas.

Always listening. Always ready when you are.

Flowtica Scribe has dual high-fidelity microphones that capture voices clearly, even from up to 5 meters away.

Poor audio means poor transcription. And poor transcription means summaries that miss the point.

But with clean input, Flowtica delivers accurate transcripts and smart, context-aware summaries that actually reflect what matters.

It’s not just repeating what was said, it’s creating notes that get you.

That’s why the notes feel more thoughtful. More complete. Almost like they “get” you.
