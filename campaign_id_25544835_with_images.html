Story

Introducing Robera NEO, a smart, AI-powered golf cart designed to elevate every aspect of your game. With advanced 3D vision and GPS mapping, it anticipates your every need and streamlines your course experience through real-time obstacle avoidance and dynamic route planning—all wrapped in a robust yet lightweight design.

Choose the version that suits your game: the app-only NEO Vision, or the feature-rich NEO Tablet. Wherever and however you play, Robera brings the future of golf to your fingertips. 

With a state-of-the-art 3D vision system, Robera NEO accurately recognizes your movements and follows you seamlessly. The intuitive gesture control lets you simply wave your hand to activate or deactivate the Follow-Me mode, keeping your focus on perfecting that swing.

Robust navigation comes easy with our Multi-frequency GNSS (GPS/Galileo/GLONASS). Access over 40,000 up-to-date GPS maps and steer the cart either by tapping your destination or issuing clear voice commands. This flexibility ensures that the cart arrives ahead of you, ready when you get to your target.

*Robera NEO’s consumer version navigates within one fairway at a time. Cross-fairway travel requires users to manually set routes in custom map mode and ensure path safety.

The Robera Neo golf cart offers core functions like smart follow, cruise control, remote operation, and easy push right out of the box. For advanced features such as smart navigation, voice commands, course maps, and swing replays, connect via the app or tablet. All modes are fully customizable to fit your playing style.

Robera NEO integrates visual recognition with precise GPS data to detect obstacles in real-time. It automatically charts a course around hazards—such as trees, bunkers, or water features—ensuring a smooth ride from tee to green.

By combining visual recognition with GPS navigation, Robera NEO ensures a smooth and precise journey every time.

Plan your next move effortlessly. Once your ball is hit, mark its new location on the built-in GPS map and let Robera NEO calculate the most efficient, battery-saving route to join you—saving up to 33% of battery power while optimizing your route.

*The Robera Neo consumer version currently supports navigation within a single fairway; the RTK-equipped commercial version supports full-course navigation.

Don't lose time searching for your cart. With a one-button remote summon feature, Robera NEO can be called from a distance of up to 100 meters, navigating through even complex terrain to meet you quickly.

No slope is too steep. Robera NEO’s high-performance capabilities allow it to ascend inclines of up to 25°, ensuring that you can rely on consistent performance, whether you're playing on a hilly course or navigating challenging terrain.

Experience unparalleled portability without compromising durability. Designed with a sleek, compact form and featuring an IPX5 waterproof rating, Robera NEO (including its battery) weighs less than 15kg, making it easy to carry, store, and maneuver under any weather conditions.

Keep playing longer with our advanced battery system. The removable battery offers a capacity of 288Wh, powering up to 27 holes per charge. Plus, with 100W fast charging, you'll spend less time waiting and more time enjoying your game.

For the everyday golfer, Robera NEO Vision offers consumer-focused versions with smart app. The dedicated Robera app facilitates real-time equipment binding and notifications on both IOS and Android platform, while providing OTA updates to continuously optimize performance. 

For enhanced on-course feedback, the Robera NEO golf cart features a built-in camera for swing analysis with frame-by-frame rewind and an high-def touchscreen for exceptional visibility and smooth control.

For commercial applications, Robera NEO delivers a robust management solution with its NEO Drive version. Equipped with RTK and advanced visual technologies, it offers precise full-course navigation. A web-based fleet management system enables real-time cart monitoring, remote dispatching, virtual fencing to designate restricted areas, and one-button summon—optimizing efficiency and elevating the modern golf course experience.
