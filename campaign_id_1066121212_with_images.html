How I Learned Spanish Without a Traditional Curriculum

When I first arrived in Mexico, I didn’t speak a word of Spanish. But I had no intention of sitting through grammar drills or memorizing long vocabulary lists. I wanted something different.

I needed to get around. I had to order food, ask for directions, and talk to locals. So I started with what I cared about. I learned useful phrases for my day using translate English/Spanish like Where is the bathroom? / ¿Dónde está el baño?. Each day brought a new situation, and I picked up the words I needed for that moment. No textbooks. Just real conversations.

At the market, I did the same as mentioned earlier but in different context. Slowly, I built my Spanish through everyday experiences. The more I spoke what mattered to me, the more confident I became.

I realized language learning works best when it fits your life and interests. That idea inspired me to create LingoYak, a tool that helps people learn a language naturally based on what you want. It doesn't follow a strict path. It follows you.

Today, I speak Spanish fluently. Not because I followed a curriculum, but because I learned what I needed, when I needed it. And with Lingoyak, you can do the same way easier and faster.

Risks and challenges

Embarking on the journey of creating LingoYak comes with its share of risks and challenges. While we're excited about the potential of this project, we recognize the need to address potential obstacles head-on to ensure its success. Technology Evolution: As LingoYak relies heavily on cloud infrastructures, we acknowledge the dynamic nature of these technologies. The ever-evolving landscape of cloud services presents a risk of compatibility issues and unexpected changes that could impact the functionality of our app. Version Updates: Our current state is a fully functioning private beta version. However, ensuring that all tools and dependencies are updated to the latest version before the production release is crucial. Failure to do so may result in compatibility issues, security vulnerabilities, or performance degradation. Despite these risks and challenges, the team is committed to overcoming obstacles and delivering a top-notch language learning experience. With dedication, adaptability, and the support of our backers, we are confident in LingoYak's potential to revolutionize language learning in the digital age.
