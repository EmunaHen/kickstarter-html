Unveiling the WURKKOS HD02!

Through in-depth user research, we found a demand for a multifunctional light with white/flood/UV/laser beams for outdoor, home, camping, detection and command use, plus strong/soft lighting, long battery life and rugged metal build. Driven by this, we analyzed scenarios, optimized design and functions, and after 2-3 months of development and testing, created the HD02 to meet users' ideal.

HD02 Conquers All, No Need for Multiple Tools.

HD02 isn’t just a tool; it’s a catalyst for curiosity — empowering you to break routines, explore freely, and see the world through fresh.

Most flashlights focus on single-function brightness, forcing users to carry multiple tools for floodlight, UV, camping light, or laser needs.  Now, we bring 4-in-1 Lighting flashlight: spotlight, floodlight, 365nm UV, and green laser, eliminating the need for separate tools.

The dual-switch system — a magnetic dial for mode selection and a touch button for brightness adjustment — eliminates complexity. No menus, no delays: just instant, instinctual control when every second counts.

One Light. Four Beams.

A triple-head system (floodlight, spotlight, UV light, green laser) paired with dual-side glowing panels creates a 360° lighting ecosystem, adapting seamlessly from urban navigation to wilderness adventures.

3200LM Spotlight

Equipped with a 6500K LUMINUS SFT70 2nd Generation white LED and a 13° TIR lens, it blasts out a maximum brightness of 3200 lumens and reaches an astonishing throw of 330 meters. Whether you're on an outdoor adventure, conducting a long - range search and rescue mission, or in need of emergency lighting, it can clearly illuminate targets up to 300 meters away, banishing the darkness.

365nm UV Light

Featuring a LUMINUS SST08 - UV LED with a wavelength of 365nm, it offers two power levels. The low - power 0.8W mode lasts for 15 hours, while the high - power 3W mode runs for 5 hours. It's not only great for detecting counterfeit money, mineral fluorescence, and bodily fluids but also handy for quickly identifying poison ivy and venomous insects in the outdoors, reducing your exposure risks.

520nm Green Beam

With 0.3mW output and 60h battery life, it features 4-click lock to prevent accidental activation. Ideal for precise constellation pointing or architectural survey marking. Meets Class 2 laser safety standards, safe under normal use with blink reflex protection—avoid intentional direct viewing or use with optical devices.

Folding Wing Lights

Boasting 64 3014 LEDs on each side, it provides stepless color temperature adjustment from 3000K to 6000K. Thanks to the Hall - effect sensor, it turns on as soon as you open the wings and reverts to the previous mode when closed. Use the warm 3000K light for cozy campsite illumination, the cool 6500K light for detailed repair work, or match the natural light for photography fill.

Integrated Heat Dissipation

The HD02 features a single- piece machined aluminum body with no joints or seams. This unified structure minimizes thermal resistance, allowing heat from the LED to transfer directly to the external surface. As a result, the flashlight maintains stable high-output performance without throttling during extended use. 

21700 5000mAh battary

  • 140 hours battery life (low light mode, 10 lumens)

The HD02 is powered by a 21700 5000mAh battery and uses a boost driver for stable, flicker - free output to protect your eyes. On the low - brightness spotlight mode of 10 lumens, it can run for up to 140 hours, reducing the need for frequent recharging. It also supports Type - C bidirectional charging with 5V/2.4A fast - charging, getting a full charge in just 2.5 hours. 

  • Doubles as a power bank

And it can even reverse - charge your phone, GPS, or other devices, seamlessly switching between charging and discharging modes without affecting the lighting function.

Three Placement Options: Adapt to Any Scenario

No matter where you are or what you're doing, the HD02 fits right in. It offers three convenient placement options:

  1. Magnetic Mount

The tail end provides a strong vertical magnetic force, allowing it to stick firmly to any ferromagnetic surface, like car roofs, pipes, or tool racks. Whether you're working on your car or in the kitchen, you can free your hands and let the light do its job.

  • Threaded Mount:

With a 1/4"" - 20 UNC tripod socket at the tail and a magnetic base, it can be seamlessly attached to GoPro or DSLR mounts, instantly transforming into a professional photography fill light. Pair it with a tripod for long - exposure shots or campsite illumination.

  • Clip - on

The back clip features a non - slip, high - grip design that can securely attach to your backpack straps or belt. It's perfect for easy access during hikes or daily carry.

Ergonomic Design: Comfort in Your Hand

The HD02's flat and ergonomic design gives you a comfortable and secure grip. Weighing only 195g (excluding the battery) and with dimensions of 126×41×26mm, it's easy to operate with one hand and stable enough for long - term use. You can carry it around all day without feeling a thing.

1 Meter drop-proof

HD02 is crafted from 6061 aircraft aluminum (oxidized for scratch resistance) and a tempered coated glass lens, surviving drops, impacts.

IPX6 waterproof

The HD02 is IPX6 certified, designed to resist high- pressure water jets from any direction. Its sealed construction prevents water ingress, ensuring operation during heavy rain or exposure to powerful water streams.

HD02 Design & Development

The birth of HD02 has gone through more than half a year of rigorous cooperative research and development and multiple designs. It has been upgraded to a head-mounted triple light source (flood light + spot light + UV light + green laser), double-sided luminous wall lamp, which can be used in multiple scenes. This is not just an ordinary flashlight - it is a battle-tested photon architecture.

HD02's development journey reflects a meticulous commitment to user-centered innovation. From October 2024 to now, our concept design phase dissected diverse scenarios—outdoor adventures, home use, camping, and professional tasks—to craft a dual-module lighting system: a powerful headlight for focused illumination and hidden side panels for soft ambient light. Rigorous testing from April to June 2025 ensured resilience, with prototypes enduring 200+ drop tests, 72-hour salt exposure, and extreme temperature cycles (-20°C to 60°C). Key refinements like shifting UV wavelength from 395nm to 365nm for purer light, upgrading to 30W white spotlight, and integrating hidden magnetic mounts were validated through 3 design iterations, culminating in a product that balances 3200-lumen intensity with 195g portability.
