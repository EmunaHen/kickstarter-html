Long-lasting design

To ensure the longevity of the LiberNovo Omni Chair: Utilize durable materials like 20mm thick foam armrests with 25D hardness for steel-like support and aerospace-grade resilient panels in the chair back. Implement a robust 6-coordinated linkage system designed for repeated use, supporting features like 160° recline and motorized spinal stretch. Conduct extensive stress testing on components, including the hydrophilic sponge headrest and zoned hardness seat cushion, to withstand long-term wear. Design modular parts for easy maintenance or replacement, extending the chair’s lifespan.

Sustainable materials

We will focus on eco-conscious materials, including: Hydrophilic sponge in the headrest, designed for durability and recyclability, reducing the need for frequent replacements. Linen-like elastic fabric for the skin-friendly stretch layer, chosen for its durability and potential for sustainable sourcing. Aerospace-grade resilient panels in the chair back, engineered for longevity to minimize material waste over time. Recycled or biodegradable packaging materials to reduce environmental impact during shipping.

Environmentally friendly factories

When selecting manufacturing partners, we will prioritize: Factories with environmental certifications, such as ISO 14001, ensuring sustainable production processes. Energy-efficient manufacturing facilities that utilize renewable energy sources where feasible. Robust waste management systems to minimize production waste and promote recycling. Ethical labor practices, ensuring safe and fair working conditions for all employees.

Sustainable Distribution

Sustainability will guide our delivery strategy by: Leveraging overseas warehouses in local markets to shorten delivery distances, reduce carbon emissions, and ensure fast, reliable shipping. Partnering with logistics providers using fuel-efficient or low-emission vehicles for last-mile delivery. Minimizing packaging with recycled or biodegradable materials to reduce waste. Optimizing inventory management in regional warehouses to prevent overstocking and streamline eco-friendly distribution.
