Story

Meet P10 — Your All-in-One Smart Handle for Every Adventure

Our lives are surrounded by power banks and handheld devices — mini fans, lamps, flashlights — each one needing to be charged separately to stay ready. Now, P10 introduces a whole new solution: a professional-grade power bank that not only powers your devices, but also lets your handheld devices go battery-free.

As long as P10 is ready — they’re all ready.

65W Input & Output — Full-Speed Power, Both Ways

With 65W input, it recharges itself in half an hour to 80%.

With 65W output, it can charge your iPhone to 80% in just 25 minutes, and power up iPads, MacBook Airs, or even ThinkPads for hours of serious work.

Whether you’re refueling the power bank or your devices, it’s always running at top speed.

Modular Magnetic Charging – Your 3-in-1 Power System, Anywhere

Use it as a standalone power bank, or a full-speed wall charger, or dock it into smart modules for even more functionality.

You can charge two devices at full speed independently. That’s exactly what we all need:  an AC charger and a power bank.
