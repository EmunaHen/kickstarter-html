Simple & Versatile

We believe great tech should be simple. That's why the LDX100 has just one smart USB-C port. No more hunting for the right cable or figuring out what goes where. Just plug in and get power.

This single port is your universal charging solution. It quickly recharges your LDX100 and powers almost anything with a USB-C input: your phone, laptop, drone, smartwatch, portable speaker, VR headset, and more. It supports 5V, 9V, 12V, 15V, and 20V for wide compatibility.

Risks and challenges

Our Promise to Deliver Building a cutting-edge product like the LDX100 is complex. We want to be totally honest about potential hurdles. We've planned carefully, but unexpected challenges can still pop up. Our promise to you, our valued backer, is to face these challenges with dedication, openness, and a laser focus on getting the LDX100 into your hands. Here are the main risks we see and how we plan to tackle them: 1. Manufacturing & Quality Control The Challenge: Moving from prototypes to mass production can cause delays or quality issues. Making sure every LDX100 meets our tough "multi-proof" standards needs careful attention. Our Plan: Experienced Partners: We're working with manufacturers known for high-quality electronics and rugged products. They get our strict quality needs. Constant Checks: We have strong quality checks at every step from parts to assembly to final testing. Each unit will be fully tested for durability and performance before it ships. Built in Buffer Time: We've added extra time to our schedule. This helps us fix any unexpected delays without missing our delivery goals too much. 2. Component Sourcing & Supply Chain The Challenge: Top quality parts like Grade A batteries and Texas Instruments chips can sometimes be hard to get, have long wait times, or change prices, especially in today's market. Our Plan: Strong Supplier Ties: We have good relationships with our main suppliers. This helps us secure key parts early. Backup Suppliers (if needed): For important parts, we've found and approved other suppliers. This means we're not relying on just one source. Smart Planning: We constantly watch global supply trends and order parts smartly to keep things flowing smoothly.

Long-lasting design

Long-Lasting Design: Built to Endure We didn't just build the LDX100 to survive; we built it to last for years. Our goal is simple: make it last as long as possible with the best design and parts. Here’s how the LDX100 keeps going: Indestructible Outer Shell: Made from aerospace-grade aluminum, the LDX100's casing isn't just tough it's nearly unbreakable. This protects it from drops, crushes, and harsh weather for a lifetime. Being waterproof, fire-resistant, and working at -40°C also means less damage inside over time. Top-Quality Inside: We chose Grade A batteries for their long life and steady performance, so your power bank holds its charge for hundreds of uses. Texas Instruments' smart power management charges efficiently, protects the battery, and controls heat—all key for long-lasting parts. Simple, Strong Design: By using just one versatile USB-C port and keeping features straightforward, we've greatly reduced anything that could go wrong. A simpler design is always more reliable and strong, giving you pure, dependable power without any fuss. The LDX100 is engineered for resilience, ensuring it stays your trusted power source through all your adventures.
