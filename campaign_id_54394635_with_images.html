Story

The official sequel to Alive – the legendary Amiga shoot 'em up returns!

Alive was one of the most succesfull games back in the '90 on the Commodore Amiga computer. The game sold more than 10 million units and was declared the best videogame of all times by many famous magazines...

IF THE GAME WAS EVER RELEASED.

In 2024, part of the original Alive team started to work on the sequel, for modern machines, keeping intact the original gameplay and style. Yes, 2d shoot'em'ups can be a sort of retro style, but the game features stunning high-resolution graphics and a pulse-pounding soundtrack, this game delivers old-school intensity with cutting-edge technology.

Relive the thrill of ‘90s arcade mayhem in this modern take on classic coin-op action!
