Food waste shouldn't be smelly, messy, or complicated — but it often is. From sorting scraps and risking fines, to clogged drains and harmful grease discharge, most solutions fall short. Even smart bins usually just compress without true composting.

 Food waste comes from nature — and it should return to nature in a clean, harmless way. That’s exactly what we’re here to make possible.

From vegetable peels, eggshells, and nut shells to cheese, bread, dairy products — even compostable napkins and takeout containers — Wonvita handles it all with ease.
It processes up to 95% of the food waste you generate at home, drastically cutting down on your household trash and keeping your kitchen cleaner, greener, and less cluttered.

Most food waste recyclers come with all kinds of issues — bad odors, no real composting, loud noise, or slow processing times.

Wonvita is truly the fastest, smartest, and cleanest way to turn food waste into compost. It’s not just better — it’s the one you deserve.

The key to achieving true composting in just 8 hours lies in Wonvita’s dual-enzyme system: the decomposer agent and the fermentation agent.

The decomposer breaks down, oils and sugars in food waste into smaller molecules, while the fermentation agent accelerates the microbial fermentation process — making rapid, clean, and effective composting possible in just one cycle.

Why we need to breaks down oils and sugars?

These components are the main reasons food waste becomes smelly, sticky, and hard to compost.

Oils slow down natural composting and can coat other waste, preventing effective fermentation.

Sugars ferment quickly, leading to strong odors and attracting pests.

By breaking them down into smaller, harmless molecules, Wonvita ensures a clean, odor-free process — and produces real, nutrient-rich compost safely and efficiently.

While most food waste processors take 1–2 days to finish a cycle — and traditional composting takes 3 to 6 months — Wonvita completes true composting in just 8 hours. You get real, usable compost overnight — no waiting, no mess, and no smell.

Most food waste processors on the market skip the decomposition step entirely.

They simply compress raw food scraps into dry residue and label it as “compost” — but this material is often undecomposed and not suitable for plant growth. In fact, applying such residues directly to soil can harm your plants, as it may contain excess oil, salt, or undegraded proteins that disrupt the soil’s natural balance.

By using our specially developed dual-enzyme formula, Wonvita ensures a full decomposition process — breaking down proteins, oils, and sugars into plant-friendly nutrients. The result is true compost that helps enrich your soil, nourish your garden, and support healthy, natural growth.

Our enzyme blend includes natural components like amylase, ligninase, and lipase — along with nano-penetration agents, deionized water, and a touch of fragrance. It’s safe, effective, and meets EPA environmental standards and USDA organic compost guidelines.

3 Modes from 2hrs - 8hrs

In addition to its standard composting mode, Wonvita also offers 2-hour and 4-hour quick processing modes.


These cycles rapidly reduce your food waste volume by up to 90%, making it perfect for feeding animals or serving as raw material for traditional composting.

Wonvita uses a four-step odor control system to completely eliminate smells during use — even when you open the processing chamber. It’s a level of freshness no other food waste processor on the market can match.

*Ion-Cooling Chamber Inhibits Decay
Prevents spoilage and extends storage time by slowing down bacterial activity.

*Oil Breakdown Prevents Odor-Causing Decay

*Dual-Layer Activated Carbon Filter Removes the Remaining 20%
