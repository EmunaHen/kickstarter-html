Story

At MILENAS, we believe portable storage should be powerful, private, and effortless. Meet MILENAS—your pocket-sized personal cloud NAS, built for creators, teams, and everyday users who demand secure and seamless access anywhere.

With built-in storage (up to 2TB), automatic USB/cloud mode switching, and support for multi-user private and group spaces, MILENAS transforms file management into a smart, mobile experience. Share, back up, and access your data across devices—whether you’re offline using the built-in Wi-Fi hotspot or connected remotely via SMB/WebDAV.

More than just storage, MILENAS is your always-on, anywhere-access workspace—designed for the way you work, move, and share today.

Traditional storage struggles to keep up in today’s mobile, data-intensive world.
Cloud services become increasingly expensive as your data grows. External drives are fragile and prone to loss or damage, putting your critical files at risk. Conventional NAS setups are bulky, complex, and locked to one location — far from ideal in a fast-moving digital era.

MILENAS, a compact, quiet, and energy-efficient personal cloud storage device, is powered by the MT7628 chip — enabling the following key benefits:

1. Ultra-Compact with High Integration

MT7628 integrates Wi-Fi, USB, and Ethernet controllers in one chip, allowing MILENAS to achieve its truly palm-sized design.

2. Low Power for Silent 24/7 Operation

The chip consumes very little power and requires no fan, enabling MILENAS to stay online quietly around the clock — ideal for home or office.

3. Multi-Device Access Anytime, Anywhere

With built-in Wi-Fi and network port, MILENAS connects easily to phones, PCs, and smart TVs for seamless file access and sharing.

4. Flexible Expansion — Wi-Fi 5 & 6 Ready

USB ports allow connection of Wi-Fi 5/6 adapters, external drives, or gigabit network dongles to meet advanced connectivity needs.

5. Open-Source Friendly, Personal Cloud Ready

Supports OpenWrt and other systems, with SMB, FTP, web UI, and remote access pre-enabled — making it easy to build your secure, private cloud.
