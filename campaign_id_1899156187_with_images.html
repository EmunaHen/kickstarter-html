Risks and challenges

Risks and challenges 1. Shipping: Delivery times will vary depending on the region and location we are shipping to. While we make every effort to ensure prompt delivery to all customers, please be patient and understand that there may be delays that are beyond our control during the shipping process. 2. Legal variances from country to country: Regulations vary significantly across regions and countries, especially for electronic goods. We will take every precaution in ensuring that our product and shipping policies meet with local legislation in all areas, but there could be minor delays in fulfilling compliance requirements. 3. Estimated delivery in Sep 2025: While the product is already finished prototyping and preliminary mass manufacturing stage, we only have the assembly design left, in our experience there is always room for improvement. That is why we believe a Sep shipping date gives us time to ensure the battery pack is the best product it can and should be. 4. Defective products: While we take every measure to ensure each product is perfect, no production process is 100%. If you do receive a defective product, please contact us and we will send you a replacement. 5. Startup: As a small startup company, this is a very busy time for us, during which our team is operating at full capacity. We apologise if we cannot respond immediately, but please be assured we will get back to you as soon as we can.

Long-lasting design

The battery pack is engineered for longevity — using premium components, a durable aluminium chassis, and a silicone cover for the MagSafe facing side that reduces physical wear. By avoiding trends and focusing on timeless design, the product stays relevant and useful far beyond its first charge.

Reusability and recyclability

We’ve used aluminium for the main body, which is not only durable but fully recyclable at end of life. The packaging is plastic-free, and to be widely recycled. Every part has been considered with reuse or recycling in mind — no wasteful extras, just what’s needed.

Sustainable materials

We’ve chosen materials that balance durability with minimal environmental impact. The outer shell is made from high-grade aluminium — not only for its strength and finish, but because it can be reprocessed indefinitely with less energy than plastics. Where possible, internal components are sourced with RoHS-compliant materials to reduce harmful substances. We’ve intentionally avoided unnecessary coatings or finishes to keep the design clean, functional, and lower impact.
