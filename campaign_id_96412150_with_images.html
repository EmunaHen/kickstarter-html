Beyond its tribute to the jellyfish’s evolution, the MK12 Turris blends cutting-edge technology with artistic vision, delivering not just top-tier sound, but an immersive experience. Whether commuting or relaxing, these earphones transform your environment into a sensory journey.

Thanks to the powerful 12mm Dynamic Driver with 12mm Large 91% Pure Magnesium™ M-Shaped Dome Diaphragm, the MK12 Turris delivers deeper bass with improved dynamic response, while the high rigidity of the magnesium diaphragm ensures crisp, detailed highs. MK12 Turris sets a new sound standard, surpassing aluminum-magnesium and lithium-magnesium diaphragms. With superior frequency range, sensitivity, and temperature tolerance, magnesium brings dynamic driver performance closer to electrostatic and planar technologies.

At just 1.74g/cm³, magnesium is lighter and offers clearer highs (12dB reduction at 8kHz), tighter lows (<1ms transient response), and stable performance even at 60°C, providing a more precise, consistent listening experience.

From Sony MDR-Z1R (12Hz-120kHz) to Mercedes-Benz Burmester® systems, 1.5T external magnets lead high-end audio.
