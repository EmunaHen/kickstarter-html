Story

This tiny gadget packs a punch — don’t miss it! At Wavetalk, our mission is simple: empower creators to work without limits. Crafted with a premium zinc alloy shell for superior cooling and durability, Wavetalk fits in the palm of your hand while delivering the power you need to capture, edit, and transfer ultra-high-resolution footage — including Apple ProRes 4K/120fps — without dropped frames or overheating.

No cables. No clutter. No limits. Just plug it directly into any Type-C smartphone, camera, or laptop and instantly unleash your creative workflow, whether you're shooting on a mountaintop or editing in the studio.

WaveTalk’s streamlined, tool-free design allows you to simply plug it in and get to work. It’s built to keep up with your creative pace, letting your inspiration flow without interruptions. TimeLine

Risks and challenges

Creating a compact, high-speed SSD that plugs directly into mobile devices is no small feat. From the start, our goal was to eliminate bulky cables and clunky setups — without sacrificing speed, capacity, or thermal performance. This inspired us to develop a fully self-contained SSD with a direct-plug design and a zinc alloy body for efficient heat dissipation. One of our biggest design challenges was balancing compact size with 20Gbps data transfer and reliable heat management. Through dozens of prototypes, thermal tests, and PCB optimizations, we refined a structure that delivers performance and portability in perfect harmony. While every hardware project comes with potential risks — such as delays in component sourcing or manufacturing lead times — we’ve worked proactively to minimize them. Our team has over 10 years of experience in product development and mass production. We've successfully delivered multiple crowdfunding campaigns and maintain close partnerships with trusted suppliers and assembly partners. Final production timelines have already been mapped out, and our engineering validation units have passed all major functional and thermal tests. We are fully confident in our ability to deliver WaveTalk on time and to the standard our backers deserve.
