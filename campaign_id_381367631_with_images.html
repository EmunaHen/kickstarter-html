Based on SiliconMotion driver technology, PULWTOP is fully compatible with MacBooks powered by M1, M2, M3, and M4 chips. It supports a 5-to-3 video output matrix (3× HDMI 4K@60Hz + 2× DP 4K@60Hz), enabling seamless multi-screen collaboration across platforms. With direct connectivity via Thunderbolt3/4/USB4/USB 3.0, it ensures ultra-low latency and accurate color reproduction—perfect for professional tasks like financial trading, video editing, and software development.

Mac users rejoice: PULWTOP’s SiliconMotion driver overcomes Apple’s native display limitations on M1/M2/M3/M4 chips.

Integrated with PD 3.0 fast charging protocol, PULWTOP features dual USB-C ports that support 22.5W × 2 (45W Max) parallel output—capable of fully powering a 16-inch MacBook Pro. The 7.5W USB-A/C port is specially optimized for low-power devices such as TWS earbuds and styluses. Paired with a 72W GaN power adapter, the system achieves 94% power efficiency and includes triple protection against overvoltage, overcurrent, and short circuit—ensuring stable 24/7 operation. 

Equipped with USB 3.2 Gen 2 (10Gbps) ports—2 × Type-C and 2 × Type-A—PULWTOP delivers theoretical transfer speeds of up to 1.25GB/s, cutting 4K RAW video file transfer time down to just 8 seconds. An independent USB 2.0 port (480Mbps) is dedicated to keyboard and mouse peripherals, featuring interference-shielding technology to ensure ultra-responsive 0.1ms input latency for gaming-grade performance.

Equipped with a gigabit Ethernet chipset, PULWTOP supports the IEEE 802.3az Energy-Efficient Ethernet standard. Compared to Wi-Fi 6, it offers a 300% increase in wired transmission speed, with jitter controlled below 0.2ms—ensuring lag-free performance in high-load scenarios such as 4K video conferencing and cloud server management.
