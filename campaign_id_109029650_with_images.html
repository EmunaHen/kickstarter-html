The Pomodoro Technique

A 2014 study in the Journal of Applied Biobehavioral Research found that alternating periods of focused work and short, mindful breaks, known as the Pomodoro Technique, significantly reduced procrastination and improved time management.

Flow Timer puts this proven method into your hands, helping you focus longer, recharge effectively and stay in control.

Concept sketches & early 3D renders

The idea started simple. We wanted a dual timer that looked beautiful on your desk and worked flawlessly. Something minimal, with a clean form factor and a strong focus on usability. At first, we explored a few directions. The earliest versions were close to what we have now, but they had too many buttons and felt more complicated than they needed to be.

Community feedback & design improvements

As a small team of three, it was a huge gift to be able to gather feedback from people who care about productivity. We tapped into our Instagram audience (+200K followers) and thousands of existing customers to gather feedback on our initial designs. Eventually, this early group turned into a supportive Discord community that was part of the design journey with us.

What we heard was clear. People loved the concept, but it felt too fiddly. So we stripped it back. All the extra switches became one simple settings menu. Just enough control, without the clutter. As Dieter Rams would say, less but better.

Long-lasting design

We‚Äôve designed the Flow Timer to be durable by using a zinc alloy front housing, which provides excellent strength and a premium feel compared to plastic. Together, these materials create a product that feels solid, reliable, and made to last.
