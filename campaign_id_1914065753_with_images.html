Instant Setup for On-the-Go Shooting: Designed for One-Handed Operation

We designed the grip for a seamless, one‑handed setup because during installation one hand is already holding the iPhone, leaving your free hand to attach the grip.

The L³ ProVideo Grip’s Q-Mount allows you to hold your iPhone in one hand securely while installing the grip with the other. Align your iPhone with the slot, press it into place, and lock it instantly with a quick turn of the Q‑Mount Lever. Next, connect the USB‑C cable using the guide clip to finish the setup—no case removal required.*

How to Carry the L³ ProVideo Grip

The L³ Grip is so compact and lightweight, you can easily slip it into your backpack or jacket pocket. You can attach the included strap ring to the bottom 1/4" thread, making it easy to use with a hand or neck strap.

Once you become familiar with the Q-Mount locking system, you'll effortlessly detach the grip with one hand, sliding it into your jacket pocket, while your iPhone naturally returns to your jeans' back pocket — and done. Seamless and simple.

Features

UniBody Grip

The grip body, made from polymer nylon, is designed for optimal elasticity for the Q-Mount system through Computer Stress Analysis. MJF‑printed as a single piece, robust nylon body that integrates with the Q‑Mount system and adapts to various iPhone widths.

FlexFit

The L³ Grip’s FlexFit design, combined with the Q-Mount system, can support four USB-C iPhone models—15 Pro, 15 Pro Max, 16 Pro, and 16 Pro Max—making it easy to share one grip among multiple users.

Q-Mount

The Q‑Mount system features a locking mount slot with an eccentric lever‑shaft design. When you insert your iPhone (0.44–0.48 inch thick with the case), three ball plungers automatically align it. Turning the Q‑Mount lever amplifies the force, pressing your iPhone firmly against the elastic nylon grip and creating a secure hold through the grip’s counterforce.

Built-in 3-port hub

The grip features a built-in USB-C 3.2 Gen 2 3-port hub, allowing simultaneous use of external microphones and an SSD. Its extension cable design provides convenient connections to other USB-C devices.

Magazine SSD & Heatsink

The magazine-style SSD system meets Apple's external storage requirements for ProRes recording on the iPhone 15 Pro and 16 Pro series. It supports M.2 NVMe 2230 SSD, which are widely available on the market. 

The built-in heatsink seamlessly integrates with the grip’s design. When inserted, the SSD interfaces with the aluminum casing via a carbon-copper sheet, ensuring efficient heat dissipation. 
Users can monitor the SSD’s temperature through tactile feedback during operation.

L³ MagFilter Case

The L³ ProVideo Grip is compatible with Apple’s silicone case and standard iPhone cases of similar thickness (0.44–0.48"). 
For professional videography, we offer the L³ MagFilter case with a leather-textured TPU shell, dual MagSafe rings, a 67mm filter mount, two 17mm lens threads, and an internal graphene sheet for enhanced cooling.

*This L³ MagFilter Case is available for iPhone 15 Pro and iPhone 15 Pro Max.
For iPhone 16 Pro and 16 Pro Max, we offer the L³ SnapFilter Case, designed with a Secure SnapFilter mounting system—Each filter mount system has its own pros and cons depending on user preference. Please refer to the Add-ons section below for more details.
 

M2AI (Mini 2-Channel Audio Interface)

Our dedicated M2AI audio interface captures two independent audio channels from dual external microphones. It mounts onto the L³ ProVideo Grip via the cold shoe, operates alongside the Magazine SSD through the built-in hub, and requires no external power.
