Enter the Whitetailed Ruler—our revolutionary take on a traditional tool. This isn't just another ruler. We didn’t take this project lightly. We’ve poured an incredible amount of research, development, design, and engineering into creating something truly extraordinary. We want to redefine what’s possible when it comes to rulers—and with the Whitetailed Ruler, we believe we've done just that.


This isn’t just about making rulers cool again; it’s about creating something that brings pride back to the simplest of tools. It’s time for the ruler to stop being a background player and start being the star of the show. Join us in making the Whitetailed Ruler the centerpiece of your workspace—because it’s not just a ruler; it’s a statement.

Quality Craftsmanship

Precision you can feel-craftsmanship you can trust

The Whitetailed 12-Inch Metal Ruler is more than a measuring tool. It’s a symbol of American craftsmanship, designed to bring accuracy and durability to your workspace. Whether you’re designing, sketching, or teaching, this ruler offers the perfect blend of function and style.

Bigger, stronger, thicker, more durable, more beautiful, and designed to inspire pride in its owner. Introducing the WHITETAILED metal ruler—"The ruler they told you not to worry about."


So, how do you make something competitive at a low price? You take shortcuts, use cheaper materials, cut corners wherever you can. But that’s NOT our approach. Not at all. From the very beginning, we were committed to crafting a superior, well-made ruler. Every step of the process, we sourced only the best American-made materials. U.S. metals, high-standard manufacturing, and a rigorous quality assurance process—backed by skilled engineers and designers. We’re not here to compete on price. Every detail was carefully considered, ensuring no corners were cut.


What makes this ruler American? What makes it stronger and better? We researched those questions and infused the answers into this piece you’re holding. This is a WHITETAILED ruler—crafted with pride, from Illinois, USA.
