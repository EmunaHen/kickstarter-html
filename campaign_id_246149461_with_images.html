Story

🚀 Welcome to FURELY – The Future of Learning

👋 Who Are We?

We are a passionate, global team from different countries, united by one mission: to reshape how people learn and teach in the 21st century.

Some of us are educators, some are students, and all of us share the same drive to make education more engaging, personal, and efficient.

📱 What Are We Creating?

Half of our team has taught various subjects, and we’ve all been through the education system. We saw a gap — a huge niche between how people want to learn today and what tools are actually available.

So we decided to act!

We took our combined experience, researched modern attention spans, behavioral trends, and learning patterns, and built something that’s both simple and powerful.

We’re building an educational platform that feels like your favorite social app but delivers real knowledge.
Imagine an app where you can learn anything, your way — with text, videos, images, quizzes, and swipeable steps that feel like scrolling through your feed.

The idea was born in December 2023, and after over a year of hard work, we're proud to launch this Kickstarter campaign with a functioning MVP and a clear vision for the future.

Let’s face it — classrooms haven’t changed in decades. Meanwhile, medicine, technology, even how we drive has evolved dramatically.

And educational apps today are either:
- Long, boring video lectures, or
- Gamified, flashy tools that don’t provide deep value.

We’re changing that.

Our app brings real pedagogical value in a fast, flexible, intuitive format. Whether you're a student or teacher, it’s designed to adapt to your rhythm and help you grow.

💼 Our Revenue Model

To stay transparent and sustainable, here’s how our platform will generate revenue:

👨‍🎓 For Learners (Users)

Free Plan: Users can access limited content (free courses with ads and time restrictions).

Premium Plan: For just $10/month, users can enjoy:

  • Unlimited access to premium courses
  • No ads
  • Full access to all content by any teacher after purchasing any one of their courses
  • Ability to download and learn offline

👩‍🏫 For Educators (Course Creators)

Teachers and content creators can publish courses on the platform for free.
We take a 20% commission on sales to support platform maintenance and growth.
Early backers who become creators will enjoy discounted commission rates.

Risks and challenges

Development Delays While we already have a working MVP and a clear roadmap, building a polished, scalable educational platform is complex. Technical setbacks, bug fixes, or integration issues may slow us down. How we’ll handle it: We’ve assembled a skilled team and work in agile sprints. We’ll keep our backers updated on milestones, and if any delays happen, we’ll communicate them clearly. Scaling the Team As we grow, we may need to onboard more team members, particularly in development and customer support. Finding the right people can take time. How we’ll handle it: We already have strong hiring pipelines and global connections through our distributed team. Marketing & User Acquisition Even the best products can struggle without visibility. There’s always a risk that reaching a critical user base may take longer than expected. How we’ll handle it: A portion of your support is going directly into marketing and outreach. We have a solid go-to-market strategy including partnerships, early creator incentives, and platform-native promotion.
