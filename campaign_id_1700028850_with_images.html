Long hours gaming? Deep focus work at your desk? You know the struggle. Introducing CtrlChair: Your Chair, Unleashed!

What if your favorite chair could do more? What if it could glide effortlessly wherever you command?

CtrlChair is a universal, remote-controlled base that transforms your regular office or gaming chair into a mobile, responsive system — no special chair required.

Designed to bring movement, interaction, and fun to your workspace, CtrlChair introduces an entirely new category of seated mobility.

With multiple registered patents and a user-centric control system, it’s more than just comfort — it’s control, freedom, and a smarter way to move through your space.

Effortless Movement is Now Yours.

Imagine needing something across the room... but not getting up. With CtrlChair's intuitive remote (choose single-hand or dual-joystick!), you can glide your chair forward, backward, spin 360°, wherever you need to go – all while staying seated!

  • Command from Afar: Powerful 2.4GHz remote with up to 300 meters of range.
  • Instant Repositioning: Perfect for grabbing a drink, adjusting your view, or turning to collaborate.
  • No More Awkward Shuffling!

Your workspace or gaming station just got a major upgrade. CtrlChair lets you use your room like never before. 

  • Reach Everything: Effortlessly access shelves, printers, or different parts of your desk.
  • Seamless Transitions: Move smoothly between different zones (work/play/relax) without breaking focus.
  • Flexible Layouts: Adapt your position instantly for presentations, group chats, or optimal screen viewing.

Upgrade YOUR Chair, Not Replace It! (Easy & Smart)

Why buy a whole new expensive chair? CtrlChair is designed to work with the chair you already own and love! 

  • Universal Design: Fits most standard office and gaming chairs with a single central gas lift mount.
  • Simple Setup: Installs in minutes – just swap your old base for the CtrlChair base!
  • Smart Investment: Get a significant functionality upgrade without the cost of a new high-end chair.

(How It Works - Keep it Simple!) 

Getting started is a breeze: 

  1. Attach: Swap your old chair base with CtrlChair.
  2. Power Up: Turn on the base and the remote.
  3. Pair: They connect automatically! 
  4. Drive! Use the remote to move freely. 

(Safety First!) 

  • Ensure your seat is securely attached before use.
  • Keep loose clothing/shoelaces clear of the base.
  • Avoid strong electromagnetic fields (may interfere with remote).
