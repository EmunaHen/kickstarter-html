Story

MOVA has a semi-open dome, which is perfect for cats from 3.3 to 22lbs and fits seamlessly into homes of any size.

The tech enthusiast Han who feeds 3 cats, mentions that he is impressed by the powerful odor removal effect of MOVA LR10 Self-cleaning Litter Box. 

Sami, the parent of a ragdoll, likes the overall stylish appearance, and the one-pull detach design for rapid cleaning and assembly. 

David comments about the big 11L waste bin that lasts up to 15 days, is ideal for busy schedules or multicat homes. 

Like many cat owners, we've had our share of litter box frustrations——scooping every day, lingering smells, scattered messes, and boxes that barely fit grown cats.

That's why we designed a smarter solution-one that cuts the mess, clears the odor, and gives cats the space they deserve. A better home for both you and your furry companion.

MOVA's dual deodorizing system keeps your home odor-free at all times. The upper chamber features activated carbon that traps and eliminates lingering odors.

While the lower section uses an automatic deodorizing spray to neutralize smells after each use.

Together, they break down odor-causing compounds. So you and your cat can breathe easily every day.

MOVA puts your cat's safety first. During cleaning, it auto pauses when a cat is nearby, thanks to smart radar sensors. The system only resumes when it confirms the area is clear——ensuring your furry friend stays safe at all times.

MOVA has set up a final layer of safety for cats. We use infrared light curtain switches to prevent the risk of cats being injured. If this electronic component fails, the machine will stop working at the same time, eliminating the possibility of cats being trapped.

Keeping your cat's litter box clean has never been easier. The waterproof waste bin and box rinse clean in seconds-no scrubbing, no mess.

Thanks to the one-pull removal design, you can detach the bin, rinse it, and snap it back fast and hands-free.
It's the perfect solution for busy cat parents who want a hygienic space for their pets with minimal effort.
